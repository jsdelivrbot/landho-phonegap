Latsi (Latchi) - Profile

Location

This pleasent, picturesque port lies on the northwestern corner of Cyprus. It is on the eastern side of the Akamas Peninsula; an area of special flora and fauna. It is well-known for its fresh fish. The old carob warehouses have now been converted in to restaurants and travernas.

Position 35°02'32"N, 32°23'55"E (marina entrance)

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry and a convenient place to enter Cyprus is coming from Turkey.

Port Police monitor VHF channel 16.

The is not Customs office here but they will visit from Pafos is necessary.

Last updated November 2015.

Port Authority
Tel:+357 994 68750. ,VHF Channel 16

Docking

This is a busy, crowed harbour. However, the Harbour Master does his best to find space for visiting yachts. It is avvisable to call or email in advance, if possible.

Beware of under-water rocks if berthing on the northern breakwater.

There is water and electricity but no toilets or showers. Fuel is available by tanker and it is possible to haul out here.

Anchoring: The Blue Lagoon provides a good overnight anchorage in about 6m
