Paphos (Pafos) - Profile

Location

This attractive and interesting town on the west coast of the island, is focused around the little harbour whose picturesque open-air fish restaurants line the quayside. This part of the town is known as Kato Pafos.

Position 34°45'04"N, 32°24'30"E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

Call Paphos Marine Police on VHF channel 16 on approach.

Although this is a Port of Entry, but it may be necessary to be stamped in at Limassol or Larnica.

Last updated November 2015..

Docking

This is a sheltered fishing port which may have room for yachts too, but beware that the east of the leading line is very shallow. There is also a submerged breakwater projecting from that side at the entrance. The maximum depth in the harbour is 2m.

The Harbour Master welcomes yachts and does his best to find a space.

The port generally provides good shelter but can be uncomfortable in strong southerlies.

There is water, electricity and toilets (no showers).
