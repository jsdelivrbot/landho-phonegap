Zygi - Profile

Location

This is a small village on the south coast of Cyprus between Larnica and Limassol. It is a well-known fishing port and renowned for its fish restaurants.

Position 34°44′N, 33°20′E.

Docking

This is an inexpensive harbour even though there is a minimum 5 day charge. Currently (Aug 2015) is is not full and so a berth can usually be found here.

Electricity is not available at most berths.

Fuel can be delivered by mini-tanker.

Boats up to 30 tons can be hauled out here.

There some small supermarkets here and many restaurants.

Last updated November 2015.
