Karpaz Gate Marina - Profile

Location

The Karpaz peninsula is a stunning, pristine cruising area, only recently opened up after decades of restriction.

Karpaz Gate Marina, located in Yenierenkoy Harbour on the Karpaz peninsula, is halfway along the north coast of the peninsula, making it a very convenient port of call for those cruising the Eastern Mediterranean. It is however quite an isolated spot - see cruisers' reports adjacent.

Position 35°33.5’N, 34°13.6’E.

Clearance

Karpaz Gate Marina is a Port of Entry and has Duty-Free status.

Clearance formalities can be completed here with Customs and Port Police offices on site. The marina does request a 24 hours notice before departure, which is not uncommon in the eastern Med.

Last updated June 2014.

Docking

The new marina opened in June 2011. It is a five star facility part of a resort with a wide range of services and duty free status. It is now full operational and includes a beach club with associated facilities. The staff are reported to be very helpful.

The marina runs a boatyard with a 300 ton travelift for mono and multi-hulls, dry storage and repair services.

Check out their website for frequent special offers. Their 2014 berthing package means you save more if you stay for longer. The marina was awarded 5 Gold Anchors last year.

Last updated June 2014.
