Larnaca - Profile

Location

This laid-back town is a mixture of the very old and a modern tourist resort. It lies towards the eastern end of Cyprus' southern coast.

Position 34°55'42"N, 33°38'55"E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

All the authorities are based at the marina.

Yachts must call "Larnaca Marina" on VHF Ch16; the working channel is 8.

Crew must remain on board until clearance is granted. Clearance is completed with the Marine Police, Customs and Immigration and, sometimes, the Health Inspector.

If arriving after 14:00 hrs or at the weekend, you may be charged an overtime fee.

Last updated November 2015.

Docking

The marina is to the south of the commercial harbour and generally provides good shelter. There are laid lines and services on the pontoons.

The outer harbour of the marina is not suitable for berthing and is subject to rough seas with Easterly winds. It is no longer used for that purpose.

Hard standing is on the Western side of the outer harbour.

Larnica is a popular over-wintering stop for many yachts, therefore advance reservations are recommended.

Last updated November 2015.
