Engure - Profile

Location

Located on the west coast of the Gulf of Riga, approximately midway between Roja and Riga.

Position 57°10' N; 23°14' E.

Docking

The harbour is surrounded by piers. Approach is no problem.

Stern buoys now laid out for local boats.

Limited space for visiting yachts alongside yacht club or with stern anchor. (harbour fees are charged)
