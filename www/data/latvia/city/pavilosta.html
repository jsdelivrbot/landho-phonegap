Pavilosta - Profile

Location

Once a busy fishing and commercial port, now a sleepy holiday town, which, now has a marina. It is approximately 30NM south of Ventspils, at the mouth of the Saka River.

Position 56°53.2'N, 21°10.2'E.

Docking

56°54 N - 21°10 E

Pavilosta Marina quaysides are on both sides of the river Saka. The Marina office is on the northern bank of the river and also offers a wider range of services. The town is on the opposite bank.

The entrance is very exposed to on-shore winds and even when the proposed wave-screen is in place, is likely to prove a lively berth in such conditions.

The harbour has been dredged and now has a depth of 4m, with 2 to 4m at the pontoons.

A useful contact is Anete Blazevica, tel.+371 26557856
