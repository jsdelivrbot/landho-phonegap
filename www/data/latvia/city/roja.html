Roja - Profile

Location

A convenient and attractive port of call, being the first yacht harbour after entering Riga Bay. It is on the west coast, south of Kolka.

Position 57°31' N, 22°49' E.

Clearance

Customs clearance can be arranged here.

Docking

Roja is situated at a small river with the same name. The estuary is surrounded by piers, and approaching offers few difficulties.

There are good facilities for yachts in the inner harbour, close to the bridge. The approach is straightforward, but the outer harbour is shallow outside of the leading lines. The maximum draft is 3 metres.

There are some good restaurants and provisioning here, as well as diesel fuel. North of the harbour there is a good beach.

The town has pharmacies, banks, post office and provisions.
