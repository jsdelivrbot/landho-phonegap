Latvia - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2. Summer time end of March to start October UTC +3

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Euro (from 1st Jan 2014)

Pre-2014 currency was Lats (Lvl) of 100 santims.

Almost all major hotels and restaurants accept credit cards.

There are plenty of ATM machines for withdrawing local currency.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Latvia is +371.

Low-cost Latvia cell phone service. rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone

Transport

There are on the spot fines for those found travelling on public transport without a ticket or with a ticket which has not been validated. Tickets can be bought from the driver or from shops/kiosks but must be validated by using machines sited within the bus/tram. Additional tickets should be purchased from drivers for large pieces of luggage and/or pets.

You should use a major taxi company such as Baltic Taxi (+371 2000 8500) or Red Cab (+371 8000 13 13). They are generally able to tell you the type, colour and number of the car in advance.
