Pointe-a-Pitre - Profile

Location

This is Guadeloupe's biggest town. It is a lively place with a large, well-protected harbour. It lies on the southern side of the link between Basse-Terre and Grande-Terre.

Position 16°13’N, 61°31’ W. (marina)

Clearance

Check in at the Customs computer in the marina office and check out at the same time if you are not changing crew or staying longer than two weeks. There is no longer any need to see either Customs or Immigration officials.

Last updated June 2016.

Docking

There are two hurricane holes; Port de Plaisance and Riviere Salee. The passage north through the River Salee is tricky but do-able in daylight and good conditions if your draft is less than two metres.

However, the Bridges of the River Salee are closed until further notice due to maintenance.

The yachting services here are some of the best in the Leeward Islands and they are experienced in dealing with many types of yachts. The marina site has many bars and eateries with free Wifi.

Last updated June 2014.
