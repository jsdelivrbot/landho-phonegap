Port Louis - Profile

Location

Port Louis Lies on the NW coast of Grande Terre and is an attractive small fishing port.

Position 16° 25' N, 61° 31.5' S.

Clearance

There is a squad of Customs and Immigration (Police) based here. Take the main road up past the Post Office, then turn right. The office is a couple of buildings along.

This is not an official Port of Entry, although the officials here try to be helpful.

Customs and Immigration Office
Tel:(0) 590 22 9716

Docking

The new port here now caters for both fishing and pleasure boats.

Maximum draft is 1.5m. .

There is an anchorage at the northern end of the town, although is can be subject to swell.

Last checked January 2013.
