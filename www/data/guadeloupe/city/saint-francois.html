Saint Francois - Profile

Location

This small town lies towards the eastern end of the southern coast of Grande Terre, about 20 NM from Pointe-a-Pitre.

Position 16° 14'51"N, 61° 15'24"W (channel entrance)

Clearance

There is a squad of Customs and Immigration (Police) based here.

Last updated November 2013.

Customs and Immigration Office
Tel:(0) 590 884076

Docking

There is a small marina and a well protected anchorage.

Enter in good light as the channel is narrow with reefs on each side.

Last updated December 2014.
