Salelologa Harbour (Savai'i Island) - Profile

Location

The island of Savai'i lies to the North West of Upolu.It is a lush tropical volcanic island.

Salelologa Harbour is the main centre for Savai'i. It is located on the SE corner of the island and the harbour is well sheltered by the fringing reef.

Position 13° 44'18"S, 172° 13'04"W

Docking

The harbour needs to be entered in a good light due to the number of coral heads in the anchorage area. The entrance channel is well marked.

The best dinghy access is by the Bluebird Hardware store, adjacent to the pink shop.

Provisions, banks, hotels and restaurants can all be found here but are fairly spread out.

Last updated April 2015.
