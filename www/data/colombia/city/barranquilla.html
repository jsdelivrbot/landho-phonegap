Barranquilla - Profile

Location

This town lies on the west bank of the river, Rio Magdalena, on the south westernern side of the Isla de Salamanca National Park.

Position 11° 0.4'N, 14° 46.4'W.

Clearance

Full details on the formalities required for clearing into and out of Colombia can be found at Colombia Formalities.

For a list of agents see Clearance Agents.

Remember, an agent is required to clear into and out of Colombia, so no attempt at contacting the Port Captain should be made, except through your nominated clearing agent (Agencia Maritima) on arrival.

The yacht clubs have limited space, and anchoring is dangerous in the busy river. It may be necessary to come alongside the commercial dock.

Marina Puerto Velero opened in 2013 and is a 25 minute drive SW of Barranquilla. Clearance is also possible here and a boatyard is under construction. Anchoring is also possible.

Last updated September 2013.
