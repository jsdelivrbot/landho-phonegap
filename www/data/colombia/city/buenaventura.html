Buenaventura - Profile

Location

This port is located at about the midway point in Colombia's Pacific coast. It has the reputaion of being one of the most violent cities in the country.

SV Sarana have some information provided by the Colombian Navy in their documention: http://www.svsarana.com/free_guides.php#COL

Position 03° 45'N, 77° 15'W.

Clearance

Although this is a Port of Entry, it is not the best place for yachts, except in an emergency.

Full details on the formalities required for clearing into and out of Colombia can be found at Colombia Formalities.

For a list of agents see Clearance Agents.

Remember, an agent is required to clear into and out of Colombia, so no attempt at contacting the Port Captain should be made, except through your nominated clearing agent (Agencia Maritima) on arrival.

Docking

Great care needs to be taken in this bay due to the number of logs and other debris washed into it from the surrounding rivers.

The port should not be approached at night as the large number of un-marked fishing nets are impossible to see in the dark.

There are several marinas here, mainly for sports boats.
