Libya - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 127 V*

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Libyan dinar (LYD).

Libya is mainly a cash society. Credit/debit cards are accepted in very few places.

ATMs are unreliable. Banks are usually closed on Thursdays and Fridays. There are branches of Western Union and Moneygram situated at Tripoli International Airport.

Communications

International dialing code for Libya is +218.
