Location

This is a new commercial port, the western part of which is for militarty use only.

A small fisherman's port is in front of the centre of the city.

It is very close to the Roman city of Leptis Magna.

Clearance

Planned arrival at the port is best advised at least 24 hours prior. Call the Port Authority via VHF channel 16 when within range. You will be advised when and where to berth. Entrance is only permitted in daylight.

Khoms Port Authority
Tel:+218 31 21809 ,VHF Channel 16

Docking

Water and fuel are available by prior arrangements.
