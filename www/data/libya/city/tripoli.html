Docking

Tripoli is the capital and main port of Libya. It is a modern city with a population of about 1 million. Its old city, " The Medina " is at its centre, next to the harbour. Due mainly to its isolation from western tourists, as well as a greater prosperity, there is a pleasant freedom from persistent salesmen not experienced in neighbouring countries.

If the proposed berth is untenable due to swell, the Port Authority will usually allow anchoring in a more sheltered part of the harbour.
