Tobruk - Profile

Location

Tobruk, founded by the Greeks, is one of Libya’s finest seaports and has been an important trade and transportation centre for thousands of years. It lies close to the Egyptian border.

It was the centre of much fierce and prolonged fighting during the 2nd World War.
