Benghazi - Profile

Location

This is Libya's second city. Very little of the old city is now left. It has a large commercial harbour as well as a fishing harbour.

Position 32°7'N, 020°3'E.

Clearance

Advise your shipping agent of your ETA 24 hours prior to arrival and make contact with the Authorities when 12 miles off the coast. You will most likely be escorted in to a berth. Be sure to have at least a dozen copies of your crew list (preferably with a boat stamp) and also many photocopies of the crew passports (translated into arabic).

Last updated March 2010.

Benghazi Port Authority
Tel:+218 61 92017 ,VHF Channel 12, 16

Docking

There are water taps in the port area. Fuel can usually be obtained by prior arrangement.
