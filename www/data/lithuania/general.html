Lithuania - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2, summer time end March to end October UTC +3.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Euro (as at 1/1/2015)

Previously Litas of 100 centas was used. The exchange rate is fixed at €1= LTL 3.4528

Communications

International dialing code for Lithuania is +370.

Mobile phones - There are three mobile phone operators in Lithuania: Omnitel, BITE and TELE 2. About 97% of the country's surface is covered by the standard European GSM 900/1800 MHz network, the remaining 3% are non-walkable forests.

International and roaming calls are expensive. To reduce bills: - Buy "phone cards" for international calls
- Talk over the Internet.

Foreign Embassies in Lithuania

Emergencies

Emergencies - Dial 112
