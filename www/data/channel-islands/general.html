Channel Islands - General Info

Time Zone

UTC, summer time UTC +1 from March to October.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Pound sterling (£) of 100 pence.

The Channel Islands also have their own notes also in circulation.

Communications

International dialing code for Channel Islands is +44.

Mobile phones work throughout the country; the network operators use GSM 1800 networks, which may not be compatible with some dual-band US cell phones.

Internet cafes are available in major towns and cities, train stations and airports.
