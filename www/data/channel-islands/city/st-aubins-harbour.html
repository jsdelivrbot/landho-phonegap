St Aubin's Harbour - Profile

Location

St Aubin (photo by Val Ellis))

A sheltered drying harbour at the head of St Aubin's Bay, just west of St Helier. The small village here is a very attractive corner of the island.

Position 49° 11'14"N, 02° 10'00"W.

Docking

St Aubin's Harbour at low tide (Photo by Val Ellis)

Visiting yachts should only enter up to one hour either side of high water and moor up on the wall immediately to starboard.

Facilities include water, fuel and some repair facilities.

There is a good chandlery on the harbourside.

Last updated July 2016
