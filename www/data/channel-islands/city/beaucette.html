Beaucette - Profile

Location

Situated on the north east tip of Guernsey, about 3 nm north of St Peter Port.

Position 49° 30'10"N, 02° 30'11"W (marina entrance)

Clearance

Clearance must be complete at St Peter Port before visiting this marina.

Last updated July 2016.

Docking

This marina is situated in an old quarry on the NE corner of Guernsey. It is approached via a marked channel from the Little Russell. There are leading lights, but daylight is advisable when entering for the first time.

A quieter option than St Peter Port but some distance from any shops. Taxis, buses and hire cars available.

Last updated July 2016.

Visitors' berths. Call in advance to ensure a berth available. Sill dries at 2.37m.
Full service marina, fuel dock, gas, launderette, WiFi, restaurant and general store, car hire, busses to St Peter Port.
Boatyard with 18 tonne travel lift, shore storage, some repair services.
Accessible 3 hours either side of high water at St Peter Port. If unsure of entrance or buoyed channel, call VHF 80 and boat will be sent to meet you.
