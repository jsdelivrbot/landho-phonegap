Location

With its many sea lochs, islands and wonderful scenery, the Firth of Clyde provides a great cruising area.

Docking

Located on the north shore of the Clyde Estuary, Rhu Marina is accessible at all states of the tide and can accommodate yachts up to 18m in length.

It is also now possible for yachts to berth in the centre of Glasgow.

For the bigger ports in this area, see the separate port entries for Largs and Rothesay
