Watchet - Profile

Location

The attractive little port of Watchet lies on the southern shore of the Bristol Channel, in an area of outstanding natural beauty. There has been a port here for over a thousand years.

Position 51o11’.00 N. 03o19.64'  W.

Docking

With the installation of a lock forming an inner harbour, a marina has now been opened here. The town now provides a most useful, and interesting, port of call.

The tides in the Bristol Channel are fierce and this must be born in mind when approaching the harbour entrance. It is advisable to enter 2 hours before high water. Check the marina website for detailed instructions.

The outer harbour dries.
