Stronsay - Profile

Location

Stronsay lies roughly 7 miles north of Mull Head on the East Mainland. It is about seven miles long by six miles wide overall, but it is deeply indented with bays. Almost 400 inhabitants live on the island, many of whom are engaged in fishing. It has several dramatic rock stacks and many rare plants and birds.

Position 59.1162°N, 2.6165°W.

Docking

Whitehall is the port and is used by the daily car ferry to Kirkwall. This is situated on the Grice Ness peninsula between Mill Bay and Papa Sound, sheltered from the NE by the small island of Papa Stronsay.

There are several sheltered anchorages in Whitehall Harbour or contact the Harbour Master about berthing on the pier, clear of the ferry berth.

There is a resident doctor, a nurse, an hotel, two shops, a Post Office, and a visiting bank.
