Bryher - Profile

Location

Situated just to the east of Tresco, separated by New Grimsby Sound, Bryher is the smallest community in the Scillies.

Position 49° 57'52"N, 006° 21'15"W (N. Entrance to New Grimsby Sound)

Docking

There are no berths for yachts at the fish quay.

New Grimsby Sound Anchor between Hangman Island and the quay. Good shelter except in NW winds.

There are 22 visitors' buoys here.

Close to the quay, there is a public toilet and a water tap by the shed.

There is a pub and cafe/restaurant on the island.

NOTE There is no garbage disposal on this island, but there is on Tresco.

Last updated February 2016.
