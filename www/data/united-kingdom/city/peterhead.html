Peterhead - Profile

Location

Sitting, as it does, on the flat land of the most easterly point of Scotland, Peterhead is generally described as austere. Even so, this is a very useful passage port.

Position 57° 29'44"N, 01° 46'21"W (Harbour entrance)

Docking

The harbour is accessible day or night, at any state of tide and in any weather.

The marina is in the SW corner of this large harbour.
