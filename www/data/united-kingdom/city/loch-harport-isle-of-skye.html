Loch Harport (Isle of Skye) - Profile

Location

This 4 mile long loch lies on the western coast of the Isle of Skye. The main settlement is at Carbost. It is popular with tourists due to its proximity to the Talisker Distillery.

Position 57° 20'22"N, 006° 24'40"W (Loch entrance)

Docking

There are several suitable anchorages in the loch and well as in the surroundings.

Carbost
New visitor's pontoon and toilets and showers are due to be available in summer 2016.

There is a visitor's buoy off the distillery and two more off the village.
