Plockton - Profile

Location

Plockton is a charming village on the southern shore of Loch Carron, about 15nm north east of Kyle of Lochalsh.

It is a National Trust for Scotland conservation village.

Position 57° 20'20"N, 005° 38'35"W

Docking

Shelter is good in the bay but exposed to the north and north easterlies.

There are 9 yellow visitor's buoys and a good anchorage in about 3.5m in the centre of the bay.

There is a dinghy pontoon accessible at all tides except low water springs.

Last updated January 2016.
