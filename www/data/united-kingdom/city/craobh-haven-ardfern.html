Craobh Haven & Ardfern - Profile

Location

Craobh (pronouced croove), is a well sheltered natural harbour on the coast of Argyll, south of Oban. The off lying islands of Jura, Scarba, Shuna, Seil, Luing and Mull all provide a wonderful cruising ground.

Ardfern, at the head of Loch Craignish, south of Craobh, is also a very sheltered spot.

Docking

Good anchorages are to be found E of Eilean Arsa, just S of Craobh Haven and in the area known as The Lagoon, between the island of Eilean Dubh and the mainland, in Loch Craignish.
