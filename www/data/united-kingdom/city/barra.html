Barra - Profile

Location

Barra is a tranquil island located near the southern tip of the Western Isles. It is only five miles across by eight miles long, and forms a compact paradise with stunning coastal scenery and fascinating flora and fauna.

Docking

Castlebay (56deg 56.7’N, 07deg 29.6’W) is a very good sheltered harbour with excellent holding. The best anchorage is in about 8m, NW of the castle. There are now 20 visitors’ buoys and a ferry pier.

For details of the buoys in this area, see Western Isles moorings

An alternative anchorage may be found in Vatersay Bay in about 9m.

Water and fuel are available, as well as provisions. The village also has shops, 2 hotels, a bank, post office and a hospital.
