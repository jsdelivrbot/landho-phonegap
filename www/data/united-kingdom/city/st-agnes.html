Docking

Anchorages:

The Cove. Sheltered from the W and N, except near HW in strong NW'lies.

Porth Conger. Sheltered in S'lies.

Facilities: A shop, post office, restaurants and bar. The Turks Head supplies excellent cornish pasties.

Ferry to St. Mary's.

There is a landing at the quay.

PORTH CONGER Landing place on the bar at The Cove and on the beach below the Turk's Head at Porth Conger but keep clear of the jetty.
