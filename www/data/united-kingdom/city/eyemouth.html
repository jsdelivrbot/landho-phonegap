Location

This small port lies just 6nm north of the border with England on the east coast. It still retains many of the features of its ancient herring fishing port past. It still has a working fishing fleet.

Docking

In recent years the harbour has been deepened and extended and now there are two new floating pontoons for visiting yachts. There is a depth of 0.9m at LW but the bottom is soft mud.

There is water and electricity on the pontoons. Toilets, showers and laundry facilities ashore at the Harbour building.

The harbour is in the centre of this busy the town but the visitors' berths offer a quiet, sheltered mooring.

Last updated April 2014.
