Arbroath - Profile

Location

Arbroath is a useful sheltered harbour when passage making along this exposed coast. It is still a busy fishing and commercial port, but welcomes visiting yacht too. One thing not to be missed when visiting here, are the famous 'Arbroath smokies'

Position 56° 33'13"N, 02° 34'58"W (Outer Harbour entrance)

Docking

The marina here is well-sheltered in the inner harbour. The marina lock gate opens +-3h HW. Beware of the myriad of fishing nets when approaching the harbour. They can be difficult to avoid even in daylight..
