Location

The River Itchen enters the northern end of Southampton Water. The Itchen Bridge is high enough to clear masted vessels. Beyond Kemps Marina, the river is too shallow for most boats.

Docking

There are three marinas here; Ocean Village and Shamrock Quay have 24hr access and Kemps is restricted to 3 hours each side of high water. Beware of the moorings in the middle of the river if entering at night.
