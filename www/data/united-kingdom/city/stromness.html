Stromness - Profile

Location

Situated on the southern side of West Mainland, either of the approaches to Stromness will involve dealing with strong tides. (up to 8kn at Springs).

Position 58° 57.4'N, 03° 17.5'W.

Clearance

Customs
Tel:+44 (0)1856 872 108

Docking

Approach either from the west through Hoy Mouth, or from the south-east through Hoy Sound.

The new marina here is well sheltered and open all year. It is situated just beyond the ferry terminal.

There is also an anchorage west of Inner Holm, clear of the channel.
