Bangor - Profile

Location

Bangor is situated on the southern shore of Belfast Lough and is midway down Northern Ireland's east coast.

There are very pleasant walks along the coast.

Position 54° 40'04"N, 05° 40'24"W (marina entrance)

Docking

A very useful port of call. The harbour is exposed to northerly winds, but shelter in the marina is excellent.

Harbour Master
VHF Channel 11 (24hrs)

Last updated April 2016.
