Location

Situated on the souther shore of the 25 mile wide entrance to the Solway Firth, Whitehaven is one of the more accessible ports of refuge in NW England. It affords a safe entry in most weathers.

Docking

On entering the outer harbour, the marina is entered through the lock straight ahead.
