Westray - Profile

Location

Westray is one of the northerly islands of the Orkneys.

Docking

There is no actual marina here, but a visitors' pontoon within the harbour.

Opening hours: May to September.
Serviced visitor pontoon facility within Pierowall Harbour. Accessible at all states of tide, summer facility, excellent shelter. Showers and toilets on site. Depth varies. Maximum draft 3.0 metres but call for instructions for deep berths. Some shallow berths less than 1 metre at LAT.
