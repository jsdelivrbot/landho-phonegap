Ballycastle - Profile

Location

A useful, sheltered port on the northern coast.

Location 55°12.5'N, 06°14.3'W

Docking

The harbour has been recently developed and is now also a ferry port for services to the Mull of Kintyre and the off lying island of Rathlin.

The marina entrance is sharply to port round the southern breakwater. Visitors berth on the first pontoon. Gate to pontoons and the separate marina facilities block are controlled by pin number. If arriving "Out of hours", ask another boat owner for the number or you may have difficulty re-entering.
