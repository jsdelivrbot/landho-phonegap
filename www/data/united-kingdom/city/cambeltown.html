Cambeltown - Profile

Location

At the head of a deep loch and sheltered by Davaar Island, Campbeltown’s harbour is a useful refuge while waiting for suitable weather to round The Mull of Kintyre (or recovering from a lively passage!).

Docking

There is a single long pontoon and it is often necessary to raft alongside another vessel. Water and limited electric supply on pontoon.

Dedicated showers, washing machine and dryer available for yachtsmen(for a fee) at the swimming pool (Aqualibrium) during opening hours Showers also available for a fee at a local hotel (The White Hart Hotel).

Public toilets in Bolgam Street (400m from head of pontoon) open from 08:30 to 20:30 approx.
