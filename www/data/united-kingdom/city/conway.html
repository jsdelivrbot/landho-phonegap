Conway - Profile

Location

Lying ten miles east of the Menai Strait, is the small town of Conway which has two marinas and is close to the seaside resort of Llandudno.

Position 53°17.5'N,03°50.2'W

Docking

Access to both marinas in the Conway Estuary is limited to about 3 hours each side of HW.

There is a short stay drying pontoon off the town and a waiting pontoon outside Conway marina which is on the western side of the estuary.

Deganwy marina, which is smaller, is on the eastern bank of the river.
