Location

Fleetwood is a former fishing port, but now serves as a quieter tourist resort to the more flamboyant Blackpool 8 miles to the south. It lies on the southern extremity of Morecambe Bay.

Docking

There is now a marina in the former dock and the area has been developed to include over 40 shops and retail outlets.
