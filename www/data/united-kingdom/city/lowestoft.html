Lowestoft - Profile

Location

Lowestoft is a useful port, sitting as it does, on the eastern-most part of East Anglia. It also gives access to that wonderful, peaceful sailing area, the Norfolk Broads.

Docking

The harbour provides good shelter and is accessible 24hr. With a strong ebb tide and an on shore wind, the entrance can be quite lively.

There are yacht berths in the SW corner of the Outer Harbour.

Lowestoft Haven Marina is based in the former Hamilton Dock. It provides complete shelter. Turn to sta’bd once through the entrance of the Outer Harbour and continue N past the fish market in Waveney Dock. The channel is dredged 3m

Lowestoft Haven Marina

Tel:+44 (0)1502 580300 ,VHF Channel 80, 37

Situated to port 1.5 miles beyond the Inner Harbour bridge. 140 fully serviced pontoon berths in deep water each with 16 amp power supply and water within metres. 24 hour access to berths at all states of tide. Waiting pontoon in the outer Trawl Dock. All services, 70 tonne boat hoist, launderette and fuel. Boat storage afloat or ashore. Refuse, waste oil and battery disposal facilities. Onsite marine workshop. Pub/restaurant. New 46 berth marina in Hamilton Dock.
