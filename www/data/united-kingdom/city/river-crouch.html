Location

This river is a popular yachting centre. The outer part of the entry lies approximately 8nm north east of Shoeburyness on the Thames Esturary.

Docking

Due to shifting sands, up to date charts are advisable and care taken to follow the bouyage.

Burnham Yacht Harbour lies on the north bank of the river close to the town and opposite is the Essex Marina. Frambridge Yacht Haven is another 5nm further up river.

The Town Pontoon at Burnham-on-Crouch is also available for docking.

The Town Pontoon at Burnham-on-Crouch, closed since mid-December because of storm damage, has been repaired and is fully operational again.

The best (and safest) anchorages are to be found in the river above Burnham.
