West Island - Profile

Location

Lying south west of Home Island, West Island is the capital of Cocos Keeling although it is the least populated of the islands (less than 150 people). It houses the Government Buildings and the airport, plus the Resource Centre which has the only ATM on the islands.

Position 12° 11'10"S, 96° 50'36"E.

Clearance

Police and other administrative staff are based on West Island generally, but can often be found on Direction Island or Home Island when they make visits.

Docking

No garbage can currently be disposed of anywhere in Cocos Keeling. Yachts must take everything away with them.

Last updated July 2016.
