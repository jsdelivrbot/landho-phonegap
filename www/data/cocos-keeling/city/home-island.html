Home Island - Profile

Location

Home Island lies 1.5NM south of the Direction Island anchorage. It has a population of approximately 600.

The population is mainly Muslim. Please respect their dress code.

It is advisable to follow the channel markers when moving from Direction Island to Home Island as there are numerous shallow areas and coral outcrops along the route.

Position 12° 06'58"S, 96° 53'33"E

Clearance

The FEES payable for anchoring in Cocos Keeling are paid at the Shire Office on Home island.

Docking

No garbage can currently be disposed of anywhere in Cocos Keeling. Yachts must take everything away with them.
