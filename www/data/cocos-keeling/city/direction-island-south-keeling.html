Direction Island (South Keeling) - Profile

Location

Direction Island lies 1.5NM north of Home Island.

It is advisable to follow the channel markers when moving between Direction Island and Home Island as there are numerous shallow areas and coral outcrops along the route.

Whilst Direction Island has nothing ashore, this is where the main yacht anchorage is located.

Position 12°05′16″S, 096°52′55″E.

Docking

Holding in the anchorage is good, there is not much shelter from the SE Winds, but there is little swell in the lagoon.

There are several shelters along the edge of the beach. Two of which have large rain-water butts attached which store the roof run-off. Also, there are toilets ashore, with toilet paper provided.

There is a WiFi router in the main shelter where you can sign up for internet online and pay by credit card. The connection is not fast but is useable from the anchorage.

No garbage can currently be disposed of anywhere in Cocos Keeling. Yachts must take everything away with them.

Mosquitoes are a real problem on Direction Island (although this tends to be seasonal); bring plenty of bug spray!
