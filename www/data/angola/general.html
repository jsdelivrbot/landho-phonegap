Money

Kwanza (Kz)

US$1 = 80Kz (approx 2009)

ATMs exist but do not generally accept foreign cards.

Travellers' cheques are not accepted.

Credit cards only accepted in a few places (hotels, airlines etc.)

US dollars are widely accepted.

Communications

International dialing code for Angola is +244.

There is limited mobile phone coverage and some internet availability.

Transport

Although improving, the roads in Angola are in a very poor state.

Several international airlines provide flights in and out of the country, including British Airways and Air France.

Local minibus transport is unsafe.
