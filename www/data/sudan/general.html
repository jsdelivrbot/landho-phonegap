Sudan - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +3

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Sudanese pound (£S) of 100 piastres (PT). It is illegal to export any £S on leaving. Foreign currency must be declared on arrival and departure.

Communications

International dialing code for Sudan is +249

Sudan City Area Telephone Codes
+249 11 Khartoum
+249 71 Kosti
+249 81 El Obeid



Suakin and Port Sudan harbour have good phone coverage and also along coast between the two ports.

Coverage is a bit erratic as you move north but some Marsas have both good phone and internet coverage.

Marsa Fijab (excellent); Khor El Marob and Marsa Oseif also have good coverage.

Last updated April 2010.

Mobile Networks Operators from Sudan offer 3G services also GSM bands.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Sudan

Emergencies

Police - Dial 999
Fire Brigade - Dial 998

Red Sea Pilot: Aden to Cyprus
By Elaine Morgan & Stephen Davies
Publisher: Imray,Laurie,Norie & Wilson Ltd; 2nd Revised edition (December 2002)
ISBN-13: 978-0852885543
This fully revised 2nd ed. pilot covers the entire stretch of the Red Sea from Aden to Cyprus. Including ports and anchorages on the Red Sea coasts of Oman, Yemen, Aden, Djibouti, Eritrea, Sudan, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, the Gulf of Aqaba, the Suez Canal, Israel and Cyprus. Over 70 anchorages were resurveyed and new ones explored. One key improvement is the coverage of diving sites.
