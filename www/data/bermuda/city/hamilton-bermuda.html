Hamilton, Bermuda - Profile

Location

Position 32° 17'17"N, 064° 47'36"W (harbour)

Docking

Berthing here is restricted to yacht club berths, boatyards and marinas. There is no berthing at any commercial dock except in an emergency.

There is a short-stay floating pontoon for dinghys at Barr's Bay Park.
