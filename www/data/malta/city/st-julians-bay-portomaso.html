St Julians Bay (Portomaso) - Profile

Location

This Bay lies about 3nm north of Valletta on Malta's north east coast.

Position 35°55.1'N, 14°29.4'E.

Docking

This marina is part of an expensive apartment complex. See cruisers comments below. An alternative to using the marina is to anchor in nearby St Julian's bay. Alternatively Gzira Manoel island is not too far away, although the surroundings are less luxurious.

Last updated June 2014.
