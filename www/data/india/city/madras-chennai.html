Madras (Chennai) - Profile

Location

This large commercial port lies on India's east coast.

Position 13.084°N, 80.274°E.

Docking

Contact the Manager of the Royal Madras Yacht Club which is based in the harbour for advice on a suitable, safe berth.

The fishing harbour north of the commercial port is very crowded and not particularly safe.

This is one of India's largest cities and all services are available here.

Last updated April 2014.
