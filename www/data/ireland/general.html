Communications

International Dialing Code for Ireland is +353.

The mobile phone network consists of a number of mobile phone base stations and masts that are located throughout Ireland. Each mobile network can have a different coverage area, depending on the number of mobile towers in use, the local terrain and technology used. Ask your mobile operator if they can provide you with coverage maps that can assist you in determining how well the areas that are important to you are covered.
