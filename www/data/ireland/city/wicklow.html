Location

Located on Ireland's east coast about 25nm south of Dublin.

Docking

There is no marina or any facilities here. Yachts berth on the tidal East Pier. This is not suitable in N/NE winds over F3 and the wall is very rough, but fender boards are provided. Occasionally berths are available on the commercial South Quay or contact the Sailing Club for use of their mooring buoys.
