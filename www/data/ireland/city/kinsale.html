Location

Sitting on the estuary of the Bandon River, Kinsale is an attractive resort 10NM SW of Crosshaven. It is a small commercial port as well as a leisure destination.

Docking

The estuary provides sheltered anchorages as well as two marinas.

Harbour dues are payable by all vessels entering the harbour.
