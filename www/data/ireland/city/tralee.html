Location

Located at the eastern side of Tralee Bay, not far south of the Shannon Estuary.

Docking

The marina is on the north coast at the entrance to the bay.

Fenit Harbour Marina
Fenit Tralee , Co Kerry , Ireland
Tel:+353 (0)66 7136231 Fax:+353 (0)66 713473 ,VHF Channel 16, 14, 37 (09:00-21:00)
http://www.fenitharbour.com , fenitmarina@eircom.net
15 visitors' berths. Good shelter. 120 berth marina accommodating boats up to 15m LOA and benefits from deep water at all states of the tide. All facilities. Fuel, launderette.

Yacht Clubs

Tralee Bay Sailing Club
Fenit , Tralee , Co Kerry
Tel:+353 (066) 7136119(Cluhouse)
http://www.traleebaysailingclub.com , info@traleebaysailingschool.com
Formed at Fenit in 1956, the Club has grown and now boasts a vibrant and expanding membership. In addition to the clubhouse, with changing facilities, showers, kitchen, storage and licensed bar, facilities at Fenit include a sheltered 110 berth marina. Cruiser racing takes place from April to October on Tuesday and Thursday evenings. Dinghy racing is run on Wednesdays and Saturdays. Visitors welcome.
