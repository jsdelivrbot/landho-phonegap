Galway - Profile

Location

Although sheltered from the Atlantic swell by the Aran Islands, this bay is large enough to allow the seas to build up in strong winds. The city of Galway, one of Ireland’s largest, lies on the northern shore and its harbour is well protected by its small off-lying islands.

Docking

There are many anchorages around the bay, but beware of the salmon drift nets.
