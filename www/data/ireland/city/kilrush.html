Kilrush - Profile

Location

Kilrush is located on the northern shore of the Shannon River, on the west coast of Ireland, 16 NM from Loop Head. This attractive, market  town is designated a heritage site.

Position 52° 38' N, 9° 29.7' W.

Docking

This is a safe, sheltered harbour with all-tide access through a lock (Tel:+353(0)65 9052155)

Call the marina in advance for lock arrangements and berthing. Lock opens on demand and at night the outer gates remain open to enable visitors to berth inside the lock until morning.

The town provides all requirements, i.e restaurants, bars, banks, post offices, and very good shops.

Last updated January 2013.
