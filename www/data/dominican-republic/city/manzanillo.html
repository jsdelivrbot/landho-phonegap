Manzanillo - Profile

Location

This large sheltered bay is on the north coast. The town of Pepillo Salcedo, in the southern corner of the bay, has a commercial port and jetty .

This clean, neat town provides a useful stop for some basic fresh provisions and other services.

Position 19° 42'36"N,71° 44'37"W.

Clearance

See DR Formalities for detailed clearance information and fees

This is a port of entry and all the officials are in the town.

Docking

The abandoned Canadian ferry is an unmissable landmark for this anchorage. Give the ferry a wide berth.

Avoid anchoring on the east side of the bay in the National Park as this would be seen as suspicious.  It is better to go to the Saltwater lagoon (Estero Balza) in the South West part of the bay, south of the commercial dock.

This is a quiet saltwater lagoon where you can anchor in about 3m. The small village of Pepillo Salcedo is within walking distance of the anchorage.
