Location

In settled weather this is a good provisioning stop, with fuel and groceries close by.

Clearance

Immigration Office
Dumaguete
There is a local office which can handle clearances as well as visa extensions for yachts.

Docking

It is possible to anchor off the city of Dumaguete with care. The bottom here is gently sloping with very good holding in sand. Yachts can anchor in approx. 5 to 7 mts in a stretch between south of the wharf and the next point, which has many fishing bangkas both moored off and ashore.

There are both steps in the seawall and a small beach for dinghy access.
