Leyte Island - Profile

Location

This small island, 6km in length, is situated off Padre Burgos on the most southerly tip of Leyte. It is noted for its pristine coral reefs and excellent diving.

Docking

There are now orange visitors' mooring buoys on the western side of Limasawa Island, opposite Triana Bay.

Note: A bridge, which does not appear on many charts, now crosses Panaon Straits, between Leyte and Panaon. Its reported position is 10°10'N, 125°08'E and its height, at mean low water, is 46 ft (14 m).
