Location

Located on the far north western coast of Palawan, the scenery around this port (and its close neighbour, El Nido) is spectacular. An increase in the number of tourists visiting the area has improved the facilities available here.

Clearance

This is not a Port of Entry.

Docking

Corong Corong: Anchor in 5 to 12m over sand/mud. Ensure the anchor is well dug in as the holding is not so good in some places.

El Nido : You can anchor here in 4 to 5m but the town is more crowded with tourist. .

Which anchorage to choose will depend on the wind direction.
