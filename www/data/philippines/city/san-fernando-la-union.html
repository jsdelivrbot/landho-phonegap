San Fernando, La Union - Profile

Location

This city lies on the western coast of Luzon, just north of the Lingayen Gulf.

Location 16°37′N, 120°19′E.

Clearance

Clearance is reported to be straight forward.

Last update April 2014.

Docking

This is an anchorage, but a useful stop if cruising further north.

The local people are very helpful and will assist in getting water and fuel.

Some repairs might be possible from various business in the town.

Last updated April 2014.
