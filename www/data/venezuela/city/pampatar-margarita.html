Â Pampatar, Margarita - Profile

Location

Pampatar lies on the east coast of Margarita Island, about 8nm north east of Porlamar.

Clearance

The port captain here has been reported as not very welcoming to yachts.

Docking

Venetur Margareta marina lies in the bay between Porlamar and Pampatar.

Last updated January 2014.

Venetur Margarita Marina
Cayenas Street, Costa Azul, Nueva Esparta
Tel:58 (0295)-808-53-95.
Formerly known as the Hilton marina. Located a few miles east of Porlamar. 231 berths. Basic facilities (water and electricity at some slips). Described as "a big open-ended bathtube with bollards", conditions are basic and rough. Med moor to the quays. Probably most secure berthing option available in the area.
