Puerto Cabello - Profile

Location

This old colonial city is considered to be Venezuela's main commercial and military port, and its houses and streets conserve the original colonial style. It has great beaches.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry. It is the most convenient one to use if wishing to visit Las Aves. Visiting this archipelago without clearing in (and then out again) first, is no longer tolerated.

Obtaining the necessary cruising permit may take several weeks.

For a useful report from a cruiser visiting this port see Puerto Cabello Updates

Last updated February 2012.

Docking

It appears that the Venezuelan government has taken over the marina at Puerto Cabello. This facility is quite near the naval base, which provided some limited haulout facilities.

The marina is reported as being in a poor state of repair, and there are sunken boats (unmarked) in the middle of the channel 2ft below the surface. They have been there since 1999.

Last updated January 2014.
