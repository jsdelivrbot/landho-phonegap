Cumaná (Puerto Sucre) - Profile

Location

Pueto Sucre is the port for Cumana. It lies at at the mouth of the Manzanares River about 50NM east of Puerto La Cruz.

Position 10°28' 30" N,64°8' 25" W.

Clearance

Formalities are reasonable as the officials are used to yachts. Downtown Cumaná is about a mile from the port.

Docking
