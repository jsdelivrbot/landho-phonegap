Clearance

Applications to visit Heard Island and McDonald Island must be made to: The Australian Antarctic Division, Channel Highway, Kingston 7050, Tasmania, Australia. Website: http://www.aad.gov.au Email: information@aad.gov.au
The necessary forms can be downloaded from www.antarctica.gov.au

A permit to visit Macquarie Island can be obtained from: Tasmanian Dept. of Parks, Wildlife & Heritage, PO Box 861J, Hobart 7001, Tasmania, Australia. Email ParksEnquiries@dpiwe.tas.gov.au.

A landing fee is charged per person.

More information about the island can be found at www.antarctica.gov.au
