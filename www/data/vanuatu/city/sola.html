Location

The small settlement of Sola, on Vanua Lava island in the Banks group, is the furthest North port of entry in Vanuatu. If planning to visit the Torres and Banks Islands, this should be the last port of call in order to clear out of Vanuatu.

Clearance

This is one of the four official clearance ports for Vanuatu.

For a long time the office here was unmanned, however Vanuatu Customs announced on August 11th 2015 that the office here has now re-opened for yacht clearance.

See Vanuatu Formalities for detailed information on Clearance.

There is a bank in Sola, but it will only change foreign bank notes, no travellers cheques or credit cards, so it may be better to arrive with some vatu.

Last updated August 2015.

Docking

On arrival anchor off the small wharf in the SE corner of the bay. The dinghy can be left on the beach either side of the wharf. The anchorage can get rolly in E-SE winds.

Yacht Clubs

Sola Yacht Club - Meet friendly Robert & Serah at the small yacht club.
