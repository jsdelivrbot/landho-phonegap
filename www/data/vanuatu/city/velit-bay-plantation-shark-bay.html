Location

Velit Bay Plantation (formerly Shark Bay on some charts) is located at exactly latitude 15 degrees South on the East Coast of Espiritu Santo, Vanuatu.

It has good beaches, snorkeling and a 'blue hole' for swimming and is less than half an hour's drive from Luganville.

Docking

The bay is open to the SE trades but is calm in winds below 20knt.

The estate here used to offer free moorings for visiting yachts and a restaurant etc. however this is currenty in the process of being sold and the restaurant and bar are shut.

Visiting yachts are still welcome to use the anchorage, beach and showers.
