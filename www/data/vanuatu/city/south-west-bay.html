Location

The village of Labo comes recommended by cruisers.

Clearance

Not a port of entry. Make sure you check in/out from one of the 4 ports of entry in Vanuatu on Efate, Santo, Banks and Tanna Islands.

Docking

SW Bay is well sheltered from the tradewinds (open to infrequent westerlies) and enormous (it can harbour over 100 yachts). Anchor in good sand in 10 to 40 feet. You can land your dinghy ashore in front of the different villages in the Bay and you can also dinghy into the lagoon.

Yacht Clubs

South West Malekula Yacht Club
See Chief Esrom inside the lagoon. 40 yachts inaugurated the yacht club in 2008 and the local villagers are very keen on welcoming cruisers into their community.
