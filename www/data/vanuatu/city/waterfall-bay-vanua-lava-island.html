Clearance

Clearance is at Sola, which is situated on the SE of the island. There is only a trail to there, so the best way is to sail there if needed.

Docking

This anchorage on the West coast of the island and is better protected from the swell than Vureas bay, 7 NM south. It is sheltered from NE & SE tradewinds (but open to infrequent westerlies).

Anchor in black sand in about 40 feet. You can land your dinghy on the small black sand beach in front of the Yacht Club (look for the channel through the coral reef especially at low tide).
