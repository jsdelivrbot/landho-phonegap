Cole Bay - Profile

Location

Cole Bay is tucked into the southern-most corner of the Simpson Bay Lagoon.

Position 18° 02.2'N, 63° 05.2'W.

Clearance

See Simpson Bay for clearance information.

Clearance can also be arranged at the Yacht Club Port de Plaisance marina.

Docking

Cole Bay is just to the SE of Simpson Bay. There are a good number of yachting services based here. There have been reports of thefts from yachts berthed here, so the usual security measures should be taken.
