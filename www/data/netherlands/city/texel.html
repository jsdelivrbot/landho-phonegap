Location

Texel is the most western of the Frisian Islands and is well known for its great beaches. The port lies in the centre of its southern coast.

Docking

The Veerhaven is the commercial port on the south western corner of the island. Visitors should berth in the yachthaven at Oudeschild, further east along the south coast.
