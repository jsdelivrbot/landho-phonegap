Almere - Profile

Location

This very new city is tucked into the southern corner of the IJsselmeer.

Position 52° 20'15"N, 05° 07'43"E (marina)

Docking

The yacht haven is to port, just before the road bridge.

There are several good beaches close by. It is also close to the train station with services to Amsterdam.

Last updated October 2015.
