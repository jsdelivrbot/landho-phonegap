Hoorn - Profile

Location

Hoorn lies on the IJsselmeer, about 20nm north of Amsterdam. It is a very attractive historic town based around its harbour.

Position 52°39′N, 05°4′E.

Docking

There are several docking options here. In addition to the inner and outer harbours, there are the Grashaven and Vluchthaven harbours. In the Schelphoek, a new marina is being built. With 800 berths, it is called the 'Marina Kaap Hoorn'.

There is anchoring on the Buitenhn.

Last updated September 2015.
