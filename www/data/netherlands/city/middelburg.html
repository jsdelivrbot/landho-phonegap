Docking

There is no mooring in the canal here, but there are several yacht basins in the town.

Call on VHF channel 22 for locks and bridges.

Binnenhaven
Through the Spijkerbrug opening bridge
Tel:+31 (0)118 627180 ,VHF Channel 22 for the bridge

Voorhaven
On the north side of the canal, near the fuel dock. Suitable for a short stay.

Yacht Clubs

De Kemel (WV Arne)
in the Binnenhaven
Tel:+31 (0)118 627 180
The clubhouse is situated just south of the Spijkerbrug bridge. It has a convivial bar and provides good meals. There are also showers and toilets as well as a launderette.
