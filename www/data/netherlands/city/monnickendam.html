Monnickendam - Profile

Location

A town in the province of North Holland, Monnickendam is located on the coast of the IJsselmeer. It isn't far north of Amsterdam and has a rich history - 74 buildings, mostly in the town centre have national heritage site status.

Position 52°27'45" N, 05°02'33" E (marina entrance)
