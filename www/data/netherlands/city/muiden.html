Muiden - Profile

Location

Lying just about 8nm SE from the centre of Amsterdam, Muiden is the site of the most remarkable medieval castle in Holland. It is a World Heritage site.

The port is on the western shore of the Ijsselmeer, at the mouth of the river Vecht.

Position 52°20′N, 05°04′E.

Docking

There are two small marinas here, overlooked by the castle.

The small village is atmospheric with a busy lock and cafe adjacent.  Good bread/ coffee shop for breakfast by lock and across bridge to two supermarkets both small and not designed for major provisioning.

Last updated September 2015.
