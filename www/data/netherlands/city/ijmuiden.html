Location

Ijmuiden lies at the North sea entrance to the 15 mile long canal which leads to the city of Amsterdam. It is a busy port and a good watchout should be kept when entering.

Clearance

For clearance formalities see Netherlands/Formalities.

Docking

There can be a strong tidal stream at the entrance. The Seaport Marina IJmuiden is located at the south pier. There is good shelter here in the marina.

There are 4 locks here. Most yachts use Kleine Sluis. It operates 24/7 on request. Call on VHF channel 22.

Last updated September 2011.
