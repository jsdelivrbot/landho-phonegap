Location

This small town lies on the western shore of the Westerschelde, opposite Vlissingen (Flushing), to which it is connected by ferry and a tunnel. It is easily identified by its distictive black and white lighthouse.

Docking

There is a yacht harbour here and it is also possible to anchor off the yacht haven in settled weather. Sheltered except ing N and NW winds.

Last updated January 2011.
