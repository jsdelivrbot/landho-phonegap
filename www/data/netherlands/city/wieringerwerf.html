Wieringerwerf - Profile

Location

Wieringerwerf is a Dutch town in that is a part of the municipality of Hollands Kroon. It  is situated about 21 kilometres southeast of Den Helder.

Location: 52°52'24" N, 5°6'16" E

Docking

Marina Den Oever is ideally located for access to the Wadden Sea and Ijsselmeer.

Sheltered and cosy harbour situated on the Ijsselmeer, by the Wadden Sea and North Sea. Visitors berths available. Local fishing village accessible on foot or by bike. Boatyard has a 30 tonne boat lift and winter dry storage in insulated sheds. Marina Den Oever offers full maintenance/repair service.
