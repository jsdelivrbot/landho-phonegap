Vlissingen Sea Lock
VHF Channel 22
This lock operates 24hrs but does not open if the tides are above or below a certain level. It is advisable to contact the lock directly.

Docking

The port is entered through a set of lock gates which are permamently closed form the end of October to the beginning of March.

There is a marina close to the lock, and another, the Michiel De Ruyterhaven marina, in the town centre.

If navigating the Scheldt in fog, call the MCSAD (Maritine and Coastal Services Shipping Assistance Division ) on VHF channel 14 and they will kep a radar watch for you and warn you of other vessels' positions. At other times, keep a listening watch on VHF 14 and advise MCSAD of your intentions.
