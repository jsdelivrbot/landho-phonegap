Location

This city lies on the southwestern tip of Korea and is a busy ferry port for the various islands in the Yellow Sea. The harbour is close to the picturesque Yudal mountain.

Clearance

Clearance can be carried out here.

Docking

The marina here is one of the best in Korea. It is owned by the university, but welcomes visitors. Water and power are available and there is a 40 ton crane for lift-outs. The office staff do not speak English but are very helpful.

Marina office hours are Monday to Friday 8 am to 5pm.

Many local boat owners speak English.

It is a good and safe place to leave the boat.
