Location

Cheju is an island off the southern coast of South Korea. The port of Cheju lies in the middle of its north coast.

Clearance

There is an Army check point just inside the harbour entrance, where one should stop for inspection. Formalities are completed inside the main harbour where yachts sometimes tie up to one of the coastguard boats. Arriving yachts are visited by Quarantine, Customs and Immigration.

Docking

There are two small marinas in Cheju. One is Kimnyoung and the other is Dodoo marina. Both are located close to Cheju city.
