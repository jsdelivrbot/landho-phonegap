Time Zone

UTC +9

Money

Won of 100 chun.

Up to US$500 can be reconverted on departure, but not outside of South Korea.

ATMs are widely available but only those marked 'Global' accept foreign cards.

Communications

International Dialing Code for South Korea is +82.

Incheon: 032
Daejeon (Taejon): 042

Gyeongsang Nam Do (South Gyeongsang): 055

GSM roaming phone services operate. It is also possible to use a local SIM card or rent a local mobile phone.

Internet cafes and other internet access points are widely available. Free Internet and computers are available at most Post Offices in Korea.

Officals do not often use VHF radio and may not understand its proper international usage.
