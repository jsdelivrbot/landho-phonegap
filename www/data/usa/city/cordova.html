Cordova - Profile

Location

Cordova is a hidden gem in the midst of lofty peaks located on eastern Prince William Sound. Commercial fishing has been the mainstay of the city for several decades. Wildlife abounds in this area and the backdrop views of the Chugach Range will keep any photographer happy.

Position 60° 32.5'N, 145° 46'W.
