Port Angeles - Profile

Location

Located on the northern coast of the Olympic Peninsula on the southern side of Strait of Juan de Fuca, Port Angeles lies just 20nm from Victoria, Canada.

It provides an excellent base for exploring this amazing area with its northern rain forest and mountains.

Position 48° 07"43'N, 123° 22"49'W. (Harbour entrance)

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

It reduces the waiting time if prior notice of your arrival is given.

Docking

Port Angeles Boat Haven is in the heart of downtown and within easy reach of shops and restaurants.

The John Wayne Marina lies about 15nm east of Port Angeles, in Sequim Bay.

There is an anchorage south of Pitship Point in Sequim Bay (be careful of the tidal range).  Mooring buoys are available at the Sequim Bay State Park, south of the marina.
