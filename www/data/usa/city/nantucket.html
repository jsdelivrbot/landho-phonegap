Nantucket - Profile

Location

This former whaling port, with its pre-civil war building beautifully restored, forms a very attractive destination. It lies about 30nm south of Cape Cod and 10nm east of Matha's Vinyard.

Position 41° 18'11" N, 70°06' W (harbour approach)

Docking

There are two free dinghy docks located on the town dock.

Toilets and showers are located at the town dock.

Free pump-out at the town pier, or call the Harbour Master.

Last updated March 2015.
