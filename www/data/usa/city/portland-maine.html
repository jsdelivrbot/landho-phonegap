Portland (Maine) - Profile

Location

Portland is Maine's business, financial and retail capital and is the largest city in the state. It is perched on a peninsula, jutting out into island-studded Casco Bay.

Historic architecture blends with the new along the working waterfront and in the cobbled streets of the restored Old Port. This area is both a working waterfront and a chic shopping, dining, and entertainment district.

Position 43°40′N, 70°16′W.

Clearance

It is necessary to call or fax the CBP office 2 hours prior to arrival to arrange a time for inspection. Nobody must leave the boat until clearance is complete. Inspections are done during normal working hours.

Berth at the 'State Pier'. Ask for directions as it it unclear exactly which pier this is. There are several west of the main ferry terminal along the Old Port waterfront.

Last updated November 2014.

Docking

There is a very large number of marinas and berthing options in and around Portland.

For the best boatyard facilities, it is necessary to go away from the downtown area, but there are several within easy reach.
