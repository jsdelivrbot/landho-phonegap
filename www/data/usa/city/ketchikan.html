Ketchikan - Profile

Location

This city is known as a centre of Native Culture and has the largest collection of totem poles in the world. It is also known as "The Salmon Capital of the World."

Position 55°21′N, 131°40.2′W.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry and the reporting station is Ketchikan Casey Moran Harbor.

Last updated November 2014.

Docking

Bar Harbor marina is the main berth for transient boaters but for short stays and clearing in, the Casey Moran pontoon in the heart of downtown is very convenient.
