Annapolis - Profile

Location

This city is one of Americas foremost sailing centres. It is home to the United States Naval Academy, and hosts the East Coast’s major boat show each year as well as many national and international races and regattas.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Call the CBP reporting office and moor at the Reporting location.
