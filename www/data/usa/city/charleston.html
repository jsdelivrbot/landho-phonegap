Charleston - Profile

Location

The city of Charleston is located where the Ashley and Cooper Rivers converge, forming a large harbour which then empties into the Atlantic through a 2km wide entrance. It is a major tourist destination and has a well preserved architecture.

Position 32° 44'40"N, 79° 51'36" (harbour entrance)

Docking

Charleson docking facilities suffered serious damage during Hurricane Matthew. It is recommended that you contact the marina in advance to confirm that visitor berthing is still available before stopping here.

Charleston City Marina
17 Lockwood Drive, , Charleston , S.C 29401
Tel:+1(843)723-5098
http://www.megadock.us
N 32° 46.717 W 079° 57.217
The marina itself is private, but they do welcome transient yachts. There is also a free anchorage in the vicinity, although foul ground has been reported. There are also moorings able to take boats up to 45ft. There is a public dinghy dock, accessible at high tide only. The marina allows dinghies to moor at their dock for a $5US daily charge. Boats berthing here can enjoy a free shuttle into town and the grocery store.
