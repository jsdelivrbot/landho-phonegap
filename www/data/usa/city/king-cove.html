King Cove - Profile

Location

King Cove is located 600nm SW of Anchorage on the southern coast of the Alaska Peninsula. It is home to a large fish processing plant.

Position 55° 03'06"N, 162° 19'10"W

Docking

Contact the Harbour Master on VHF channel 16.

Yachts can moor in the old harbour.

Showers and toilets are in the Harbour Master's building.

Water, electricity and fuel are available.

There are two supermarkets here, one by the wharf and another in the town.

Last updated April 2016.
