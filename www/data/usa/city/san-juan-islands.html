San Juan Islands - Profile

Location

This group of west coast islands is the part of the archipeligo spanning the border between the U.S A. and Canada. On the Canadian side of the border they are called The Gulf Islands.

The principal islands in this group are Lopez Island, Shaw Island, Orcas Island, and San Juan Island. There are many other smaller islands and a large number of uninhabited islets.

Friday Harbor lies on the east coast of San Juan Island, while Roche Harbor is on the north west coast.

Clearance

Both Friday Harbor and Roche Harbor are Ports of Entry.

At both harbours, formalities are completed at the marina.

If registered with the NEXUS program, tel. 1-800-562-5943 on entering US waters.
