Hampton (Virginia) - Profile

Location

Situated on the western shore of the entry to Chesapeake Bay, this old American city has miles of attractive waterfront along the Hampton River. it is also the site of Fort Monroe.

Newport News is about 10 miles south west of Hampton.

Clearance

Non-US flagged boats must clear through a port of entry with a face-to-face meeting with Customs and Border Protection. See Noonsite/USA/Clearance for full details.

The closest Port of Entry is Norfolk News. CBP telephone is (757) 245-6470 (24 hr Preferred Number).

Last updated October 2010.

Docking

There are a huge number of mooring options in and around Norfolk, Portsmouth, Newport News and Hampton, as well as in the rivers feeding into this southern end of Chesapeake Bay. The Hampton River is well marked and there is little current. Together with Sunset Creek, the river provides several marinas.
