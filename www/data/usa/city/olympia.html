Olympia - Profile

Location

This port is tucked away on the south eastern side of the Olympic Peninsula about 100 miles SSW of Seattle (in a direct line). The route to Olympia lies down Puget Sound, under the Tacoma Narrows Bridge before winding your way around many islands.

Position 47° 03"49'N, 122° 54"28'W.

Docking

This is a very useful port as a base for exploring the wonders of the National Park on the Olympic Peninsula.
