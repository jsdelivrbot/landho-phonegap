Baltimore - Profile

Location

Baltimore is a charming city with a lively waterfront and top-rated attractions such as the National Aquarium with its world-renowned dolphin show, rainforests and magnificent coral reef.

It is situated at the northern end of Chesapeake Bay.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Call the CBP reporting office and moor at the Reporting location.
