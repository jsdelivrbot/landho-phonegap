Martha's Vineyard - Profile

Location

As an Island, Martha's Vineyard is well accustomed to visitors arriving by sea. Many towns offer mooring, marina, launch and fuel service and are situated close to shops, restaurants and other services. Several places have retained their victorian buildings.

The island lies just 5nm south of Cape Cod.

Position 41° 27'27" N, 70°35' 41"W  (harbour entrance)

Docking

Vineyard Haven, on the north coast, and Edgartown, on the eastern side of the island, are the main ports. Oak Bluffs, just east of Vineyard Haven, provides both slips and swinging moorings.

Oak Bluffs is the largest marina in Martha's Vineyard and is owned by the town. It has both moorings and slips.

The harbour provides good shelter except in very strong NW winds.

Last updated March 2015.
