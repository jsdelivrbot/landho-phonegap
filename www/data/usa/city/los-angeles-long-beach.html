Los Angeles (Long Beach) - Profile

Location

This internationally famous city need no introduction!

The Port of Los Angeles has been a major commercial port for over 100 years, and today, is still of vital economic importance to Southern California.

Position 33° 45'N, 118° 11'W. (Long Beach)

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Call the reporting station in advance of arrival. The CBP office is at the entrance to Long Beach Harbour which is just west of the huge Long Beach Shoreline marina.

Last updated November 2014.
