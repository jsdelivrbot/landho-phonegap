Southwest Harbor (Mount Desert Island) - Profile

Location

Mount Desert Island lies approximately 150nm south west of the Canadian border. Southwest Harbor lies on its southern side at the entrance to Somes Sound. It is less well-known than its neighbour, Bar Harbor, on the eastern side of the island, but it is a quaint New England town.

Position 44°16′19″N, 68°18′W (harbour approaches)

Clearance

Clearance can be arranged by the marina office.

Last updated March 2015.

Docking

The habour is sheltered except in strong easterly or southeasterly winds when better shelter can be found in Somes Sound. There is a marina as well as moorings here.

There is an anchorage midway between Greening Island and the southern shore in depths of 34 to 50 feet. Smaller vessels can anchor farther in the harbour.

Somes Sound also provides 10nm of water with many sheltered anchorages.

Last updated March 2015.
