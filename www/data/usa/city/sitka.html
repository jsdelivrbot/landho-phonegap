Sitka - Profile

Location

This busy fishing and cruise port on located on the western coast of Baranof Island. It is notable as the place where the agreement was signed for the purchase of Alaska from Russia in 1867.

Position 57° 02'52"N, 135° 19'40"W

Docking

There are several options for dockage here. One of the most convenient is the Crescent Harbour in the centre of the town.

New Thomsen Harbour and Old Thomsen Harbour are both north of the bridge (clearance 15m). The Harbour Master's office is here.

Eliason Harbour is just south of the bridge, across the channel on Japonski Island. There are showers here.

Last updated April 2016.
