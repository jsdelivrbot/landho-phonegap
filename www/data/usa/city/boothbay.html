Boothbay - Profile

Location

Locate about 50nm northeast of Portland (Maine), this large, natural harbour makes a great stopover when cruising along this coast. The small fishing town is attractive with its 18th century buildings and flower-lined streets.

Position 43°50′50"N, 69°38′10"W.

Docking

This large, sheltered harbour provides many options for berthing, mooring and anchoring.
