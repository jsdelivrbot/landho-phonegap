Stuart - Profile

Location

This is a popular fishing and sailing resort about 50 miles north of West Palm Beach on Florida's eastern coast.

Position 27° 12'17"N, 80° 14'45"W.

Clearance

This is NOT a recognized Port of Entry by the US Customs and Border Protection.

See USA Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of the USA.

If you are arriving from abroad, customs clearance and immigration procedures must be conducted elsewhere before proceeding to this port.

Docking

There are several marinas here to choose from.

It is propsed to site 51 moorings in the anchorage located in the Indian River Lagoon south of the Jensen Beach Causeway and east of Indian River Drive at Intracoastal Mile 982. The mooring field, would include a dinghy dock, shower and laundry facilities on shore.

There are public docks in the downtown area.

Last updated June 2011.
