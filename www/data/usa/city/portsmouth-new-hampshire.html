Portsmouth (New Hampshire) - Profile

Location

Portsmouth lies on the southern bank of the Piscataqua River which divides New Hampshire from Maine. It lies about 80nm north of Boston. It is an attractive city with many historic buildings.

Position 43°4′32″N, 70°45′38″W.

Clearance

It is necessary to call or fax the CBP office 2 hours prior to arrival to arrange a time for inspection. Nobody must leave the boat until clearance is complete. Inspections are done during normal working hours. Berth at the Town Pier.

Last updated November 2014.

Docking

This historic harbour offers almost everything from state-of-the-art marinas to full service boatyards and quiet anchorages. The US Naval shipyard is based on the island opposite the town and a less than a mile to the east, across the Piscataqua River, is Kittery. Here there are more marinas.
