Seward - Profile

Location

Seward is located 125 miles south of Anchorage at the beginning  of the Seward Highway. It provides easy access to Exit Glacier and Kenai Fjords National Park. It is a popular destination for outdoor adventurers.

Position 60° 06.5'N, 149° 26'W

Docking

The Small Boat Harbour is situated on the northern edge of Resurrection Bay. It generally remains ice-free although the occasional heavy snowfall can cause problems for boats remaining in the water during the winter.

The port provides haulout and repair services, as well as other facilities required by boaters.
