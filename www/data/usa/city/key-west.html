Key West - Profile

Location

Key West is the southernmost city in the continental United States. This small island is the last one that you can drive to in the chain of islands off the southern tip of Florida, known as the Florida Keys.

Position 24°33′33″N 81°47′2.51″W

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

See USA Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of the USA.

Cruisers (either US or foreign) may encounter a problem here if arriving directly from Cuba. We have a report that one foreign cruiser's Cruising Licence was cancelled on arrival and they were instructed to leave US territory. This situation is changing recently as travel restrictions on Cuba are relaxing; however, be aware that this situation has occurred.

Last updated May 2016.
