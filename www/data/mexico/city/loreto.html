Loreto - Profile

Location

One of the earliest settlements on the Baja California Peninsula. It is a popular 'sport fishing' tourist destination.

Position 26º00' N, 111º20' W

Clearance

Immigration and Port Captain are within walking distance of the waterfront.

Docking

Yachts can anchor off the town in settled weather and dinghies can be left in the small fishing harbour.

There is now a new marina 10 nm south of the town at Puerto Escondido.
