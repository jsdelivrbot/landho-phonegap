Majahual (Mahahual) - Profile

Location

This normally sleepy little town lies about 30nm north of the Belize border on the Costa Maya, although when the cruise ships are docked it get flooded with tourists. It is a useful port of call if wishing to visit the many lesser known Mayan ruins in the Yucatan.

Position 18°42'36"N, 87°42'24"W (pass through the reef)

Docking

There is a dock suitable for yachts, approached through a gap in the fringing reef, about 2kn south of the main cruise ship dock.

Many of the shops and other services are only open when there is a cruise ship in port.

Last updated September 2014.
