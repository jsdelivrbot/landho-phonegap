Manzanillo - Profile

Location

There is a small marina in Manzanillo, but not to be missed is the Marina Puerto de la Navidad 25nm NW of Manzanillo at N 19° 11.77' W 104° 40.96'.

Clearance

Yachts anchor outside Las Hadas Hotel or stern-to one of the docks inside the jetty. The offices of Port Captain and Immigration must be visited, in Manzanillo town. Their offices are in the large concrete building situated on the sta'board side of the channel leading to the inner harbour, on the southern side of Sandiago Bay.

Docking

There is a small marina on the southern side of the Santiago Peninsula as part of the Las Hadas Hotel complex. They have a dinghy dock for which anchored boats can pay a daily/weekly rate to use (see comment posted at bottom of page for details).

It is also possible to find other sheltered anchorages close to one of the peninsulas which project into the Manzanillo and Santiago Bays.

Twenty five miles NW of Manzanillo is a new marina, Marina Puerto de la Navidad, at Barra de Navidad. Reported to be an excellent hurricane hole.
