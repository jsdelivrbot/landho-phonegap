La Cruz de Huanacaxtle - Profile

Location

This small fishing port, with its large new marina, lies about 15nm northwest of Puerto Vallarta on the north coast of the large Bahía de Banderas. It is a popular destination and the primary spot from which most cruisers travel by land to other places around the Bay.

Note: Local Time in La Cruz de Huanacaxtle is the same as Puerto Vallarta time even though it is offically in a different time zone.

Position 20° 45'N, 105° 24'W.

Clearance

If berthing in the marina, then they will handle the clearance for you.

If anchored outside, visit the Port Captain's office. This is located in the offices betweeen the Mercado del Mar and the shipyard on the starboard side as you enter the Marina.

As you head in, via your Dinghy, hail the office on VHF 16 to announce your arrival.

The marina's dinghy dock fee is not required to be paid if just visiting the Port Captain's Office.

Last updated February 2012.

Docking

The marina here is generally known as Marina de La Cruz.

There is an anchorage outside and, for a fee (now a $50 MXN fee per day), it is possible to use the dinghy dock inside the marina.

The dinghy dock is now Dock #1 by the Marina Offices.

To reach this, proceed into the marina, passing the fuel dock to starboard and then take a turn to port and it is off to the starboard side at the end of the fairway.

Last updated December 2012.

Marina Riviera Nayarit
Marlin No. 39, La Cruz de Huanacaxtle , Riviera Nayarit, , México
Tel:52 (329) 29-555-26
http://www.marinarivieranayarit.com , harbormaster@marinarivieranayarit.com
A large, modern marina with all facilities. Fuel, pump-out, laundry, WiFi, yacht club, boat yard with 150 ton travel hoist and repairs, restaurant, provisions, 24hr security.
There are a variety of events hosted by the Marina, including movie nights in the outdoor amphitheater on the breakwater, swap meets, etc.
