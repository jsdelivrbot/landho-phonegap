Puerto Vallarta - Profile

Location

Puerto Vallarta is an authentic Mexican town as well as a world-class resort, completely geared up for tourists and with ample services for yachts.

It is reported to be the only safe harbour in the area during the hurricane season.

Clearance

Marina Vallarta is a convenient place to complete formalities, as is Paradise Village Marina.

Port captain and customs offices are in the terminal building by the cruise ship dock. Immigration is in town.

Obtaining a Temporary Import Permit here, if this is your first Mexican port, is reported to be straightforward.

Docking

There are three marinas here, Marina Nuevo Vallarta and Marina Vallarta have both been recently renovated.

The only anchorage area inside the harbour, is off the creek mouth. Anchoring is prohibited elsewhere (including in the Los Arcos underwater park).
