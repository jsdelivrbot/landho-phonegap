Islas de Revillagigedo - Profile

Location

There are three Revillagigedo Islands: Socorro, Clarion and San Benedicto plus the smaller Roca Partida and several adjoining rocks.

Position 18° 20'27"N, 114° 43'46"W (South coast Isla Clarion)

Clearance

The group has been declared a marine reserve and it is patrolled by the Mexican Navy which has a garrison on the main island of Socorro. There is also a Navy garrison established in Clarion. Clearance should be completed with them. The islands are occasionally visited by scuba and fishing expeditions. There are few anchorages and no facilities for yachts in transit, other than the help that the Mexican Navy provides in case of emergencies.

Docking

There are many sheltered bays around Socorro. These islands are very popular with divers.
