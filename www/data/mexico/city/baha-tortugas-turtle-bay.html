Baha Tortugas (Turtle Bay) - Profile

Location

Situated on the end of a peninsula approximately halfway down on the west coast of Baja California Sur, this useful port provides a welcome stop along this fairly inhospitable coast.

Position 27°38.5'N, 114°54.0'W.

Docking

This is a well protected natural harbour. There is a tall wooden pier in the NW corner of the bay. Approach the ladders there with care.
