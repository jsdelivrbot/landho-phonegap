San Blas - Profile

Location

San Blas, a small fishing village 20 miles north of Santa Cruz, has a variety of attractions such as beaches, colonial architecture, history, many species of exotic birds, a thick tropical jungle, estuaries and a navigable river. It was was an important Spanish port from the late 16th to the 19th century.

Whilst the "no see-ums" can be a problem here, it's worth applying lots of insect repellent as the town and people are worth a visit.

Clearance

Check into and out of San Blas is now done by VHF Radio (Channel 16) to the Port Captain (most likely in Spanish).

Docking

The entrance channel here, constantly moves. Call Norm Goldie on VHF channel 22 for advice. There is often a 3 knot current through the harbour anchorage.

Last updated April 2012.
