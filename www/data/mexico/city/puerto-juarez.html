Puerto Juarez - Profile

Location

Located just 5 miles across the channel from Isla Mujeres.

Position 21° 10'55"N, 86° 48'17"W (wharf)

Clearance

See Noonsite/Mexico/Clearance page for full details.

This is a better option for clearing into Mexico. You can ask to tie up temporarily at Marina Hacienda del Mar, just to clear in. It is located about a mile from the a big round CIS office, part of the Puerto Juarez Capitania near the ferry terminal. Clearance takes an hour or so. Charges are about $20, the tip is about $50. You don't need an agent. They're very friendly, speak English and take credit cards.

Last updated November 2012.
