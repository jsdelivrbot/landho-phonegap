Puerto Aventuras - Profile

Location

Puerto Aventuras is an upscale resort located in the heart of the Mayan Riviera. It is known primarily for its marina, which is the only one to be found along the Riviera. It is also the centre of sport fishing for Mexico's Caribbean coast.

Position 20° 29'46"N, 87° 13'30"W (marina entrance)

Clearance

See Noonsite/Mexico/Clearance page for full details.

Although officials have to come from Playa del Carmen and an agent have to be used to arrange this, Puerto Aventuras is a Port of Entry. This can take several days and cost about US$450.

Last updated January 2011.

Docking

The marina provides a good hurricane hole.

There is no secure overnight anchorage.

Last updated January 2011.
