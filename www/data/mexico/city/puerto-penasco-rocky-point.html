Puerto Penasco (Rocky Point) - Profile

Location

Located near the northern end of the Gulf of California on the eastern side of the Gulf. It is a popular beach resort for Americans being just 1 hour's drive from the border. It is also an active fishing port with shrimping being the main activity.

Position 31°19′00″N, 113°32′13″W.

Docking

This sheltered harbour is accessed via a narrow entrance. In it are located the marina, fishing boat docks as well as a navy dock.

Being a tourist destination, most shore services can be found here.

There is a new cruise ship terminal being built near Penasco, as well as a new marina.

Last updated November 2015.
