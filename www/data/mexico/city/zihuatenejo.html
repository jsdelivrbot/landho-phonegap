Located 4 miles from Zihuatanejo. Slips for boats upto 70' LOA in a sheltered basin behind the west end of Playa Ixtapa. Their entrance channel is occasionally blocked for dredging. Seasonally, the depth varies from 5' to 12' in the channel. Water, electricity, fuel dock (diesel & gas), showers, laundry, and a restaurant. Dock staff speak English. Danger of crocodiles here so swimming not advisable. New Boatyard 2014 with 100 ton travelift. Limited hard standing.
