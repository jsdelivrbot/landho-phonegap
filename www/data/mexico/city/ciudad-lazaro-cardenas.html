Clearance

Before entering this busy port, it is essential to call Port Captain on VHF Channel 16. You will then be given a schedule for entry. An official will come in a launch to check the papers.

Yacht skippers must hire a local agent to handle their port clearance. A list of shipping agents can be found on the Port Authority website, http://www.puerto-lazarocardenas.com.mx/comercio/a_navieras_e.htm
