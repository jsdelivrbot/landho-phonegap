Baja Asuncion - Profile

Location

This attractive, small fishing port lies about 70NM SE of Turtle Bay (Bahia Tortugas).

Position 27°8'26" N, 114°17'48" W.

Docking

This is really an anchorage, but there is a pier here.

People are VERY friendly and welcoming.

Contact a retired whale researcher Sherry (goes by Sirena) on VHF 16. She is always very helpful to cruisers.
