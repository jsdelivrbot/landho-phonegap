Santa Rosalia - Profile

Location

This useful port of call is about halfway up the east coast of Baja California.

Position 27°19.5’N, 112°15.3’W.

Clearance

Port Captain's office is inside the big ferry terminal at the southwest corner of the harbour. Immigration is half a block south.

The dockmaster at the marina can arrange for clearance.

Docking

The old marina is located by the bright turquoise building in the NE corner of the municipal harbour, past the ferry terminal.

There is also a new marina here, with all facilities.
