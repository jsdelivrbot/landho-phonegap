Ukraine - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2, UTC +3 summer time end March to end September.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Ukraine grynia (UHA), also spelt hryvnia.

Exchange kiosks offer the best rates. US$ and Russian roubles are the easiest to exchange, now being quickly followed by the euro.

ATMs are found everywhere.

VISA, MasterCard / EuroCard are accepted in major hotels, restaurants, casinos, stores, and ATM's.

Owners of American Express will experience problems when using their cards even in regional centers.

Communications

Cell phone coverage is good and pre-paid cell phones are readily available in Odessa and Yalta.

There are no private post operators in the Ukraine.
Ukrainian post (http://www.ukrposhta.com/www/upost_en.nsf) is inherited from the USSR and no-one speaks English. There are alot of branches.

Wi-fi is not wide spread, but one can use 3g connection from one of the biggest cell providers MTS (http://www.mts.com.ua/eng/main.php) and Kievstar (http://www.kyivstar.ua/en/)

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Ukraine

Emergencies

Fire - Dial 01
Police - Dial 02
First Aid/Ambulance Service - Dial 03
