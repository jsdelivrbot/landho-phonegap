Yevpatoria - Profile

Location

Inside a large bay, Yevpatoria is a bustling summer resort with two blue flag beaches. The old town is close to the harbour and well worth a visit.

Position 45°11.2’N, 32°22.8’E.

Clearance

Currently this port is closed to international shipping.

This was recorded as a Ukrainian a Port of Entry, but the current situation is not known.

Call Port Authorities of VHF channel 16.

Last updated February 2016.

Docking

This harbour offers shelter from W winds. Visiting yachts can either berth on the commercial pier if space is available, or anchor.
