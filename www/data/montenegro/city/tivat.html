Tivat - Profile

Location

Situated in the central part of the Boka Kotorska Bay, Tivat has an impressive display of tropical plants and unusual trees.

Position 42˚26.03’N, 018˚41.33’E.

Clearance

This is now a Port of Entry.

See Montenegro Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of the country.

Clearance can be completed at the marina. The Customs dock is on the port-hand side as you enter the marina. Clearance reported to be slow but officials very accommodating.

Last updated July 2014.

Docking

Docking here is now much improved with the opening of the new Porto Montenegro Marina in 2009. This is now classed as a "superyacht" marina and plans to be the biggest in the world by 2020. All yachts welcome up to 170m.

In addition, Porto Montenegro has teamed up with Adriatic Shipyard Bijela to develop a repair and refit facility in the Boka Bay with a travelift.

Last updated February 2015.
