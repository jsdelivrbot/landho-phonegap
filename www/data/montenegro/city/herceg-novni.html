Location

Situated at the entrance of the Gulf of Kotor (Boka Kotorska), this is a busy ferry port and tourist town. The harbour is noisy and suffers from the wash of the many tripper boats.

It is town known for its Mimosa trees and numerous steps. Herceg Novi looks like one big botanical garden.

Clearance

After clearing in at Zelenika, yachts can then proceed to Herceg-Novi.

Docking

The harbour is exposed to the south.

Berth on the inner side of the harbour wall where there is space, water and electricity included in the daily fee.

It is also possible to anchor west of the harbour but a bright anchor light is necessary due to the large number of ferries which arrive late into the night. Good holding in 5 to 6m in sand and mud.
