Budva - Profile

Location

A popular tourist centre and party town with an attractive old town, sitting on a peninsular with excellent beaches. It lies approximately 15NM NW of Bar. Also popular with yachts cruising the Adriatic coast.

The marina here was taken over by Camper & Nicholsons Marinas in 2014.

This town is reported to be very noisy with late night bars.

Position 42°16'N, 18°50'E.

Clearance

See Montenegro Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of the country.

The Marina is an international port of entry with a seasonal border control which is open from May 1st through October 1st (from 8am to 10pm daily).

Last updated October 2016.

Budva Customs Dock
Position 42°16.748'N, 18°50.345'E. Depth 3.5m. Alongside berthing.

Docking

The harbour is in the western corner of the large bay fronting the town. It is composed of a marina and a town quay; both operated by the marina. Mooring is med-style and expensive.

It is possible to anchor off the town, but the bay is open to the south, although the holding is good. The only place to get ashore with a dinghy is at the Marina, who charge a daily fee for anchored yachts to do so.

Last updated October 2016.
