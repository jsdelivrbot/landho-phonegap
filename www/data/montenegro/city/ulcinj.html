Location

At the southern end of the coast, just 8 miles from the Albanian border. A small town with good beaches and a rebuilt old town. A popular holiday resort which includes loud disco music!

Clearance

Call the Harbourmaster on VHF 16 on approach.

This no longer a Port of Entry. The nearest alternative is Bar and if you arrive here first before clearing in you will be fined.

Last updated June 2011.

Docking

Reasonable shelter, but open to the south. Some swell, even in calm conditions.

Generally anchor in the bay which has good holding in sand.

If it is calm, berth at the quay, keeping clear of the ferry.
