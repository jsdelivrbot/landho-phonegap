Zelenika - Profile

Location

Located at the entrance to the large Gulf or Kotor (Boka Kotorska), which is about 15NM south of the border with Croatia.

Position 42°26.994N 18°34.278E

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

See Montenegro Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of the country.

Zelenika Customs Dock is an 8 metre-long concrete dock with large black rubber bumpers and cleats spaced very far apart. Plus there is no onshore assistance with docking.

Check in and out here is quick and efficient with no charges. Not a suitable place for yachts in an onshore breeze.

Last updated January 2016.
