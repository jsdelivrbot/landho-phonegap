Risan - Profile

Location

A small, quiet town at the northern end of Kotorski Zaijev in the Gulf of Kotor. Worth visiting to see the Roman mosaics close to the harbour.

Position 42° 30'48"N, 18° 41'33"E.

Docking

Berth in the harbour, which lies in front of the gardens. Depending on the wind direction, berth where possible on either side near the entrance as it shallows further in.

It is possible to anchor outside in 6 to 8m, but the bottom is very soft mud and the anchor may not hold..
