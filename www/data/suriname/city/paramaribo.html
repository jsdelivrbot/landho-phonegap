Paramaribo - Profile

Location

The capital city, Paramaribo, is considered a jewel of Caribbean architecture. The old plantation houses and historic centre of town were placed on the Unesco World Heritage list in 2002, and much has been renovated since then.

Clearance

See Suriname Clearance for full details on how to complete clearance here, plus river navigation advice.

By new governement rule all visiting yachts are to proceed to Domburg. It is not possible to anchor near the town unless special permission has been granted. Once settled in Domburg, clearance can be done the next day or two in Paramaribo.

Last updated July 2015.
