Lohusalu - Profile

Location

This little port lies about 30nm west of Tallinn on the east side of the Lohusalu Peninsula. It is a popular holiday resort, especially for water-base activities.

Position 59°24.2'N, 24°12.6'E.

Clearance

This is an official Port of Entry.

Docking

The marina here is protected from all wind direction. Visitors berth in the northern basin where there are 10 berths available..

Last updated December 2015.
