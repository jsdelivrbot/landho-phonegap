Saaremaa Harbour - Profile

Location

This is a new super-yacht harbour on the north coast of the island. It is fairly remote from any village or town.

Position 58°32.4'N, 22°14.4'E

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry but is more familier with cruise ships.

Docking

There is a small floating pontoon for yachts inside of the cruise ship berth.

Last updated November 2013.
