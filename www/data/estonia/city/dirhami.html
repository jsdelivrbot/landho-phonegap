Dirhami - Profile

Location

This port lies on the north west corner of Estonia's north coast, opposite the island of Osmussaar. Due to the area being out of bounds for many years, it is now an interesting haven for wildlife and fauna.

Position 59°12’41.00”N, 23°30’03.05”E. (harbour entrance)

Docking

It is essential for follow the leading marks when approaching this harbour due to dangerous rocks.

Shore storage is available at the port.

There is a summer beach cafe here and a well stocked local store (open daily).

Last updated January 2016.
