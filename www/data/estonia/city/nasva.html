Nasva - Profile

Location

A fishing village with a sheltered harbour at the mouth of the Nasva River. Boat and yacht building are also industries here.

Position 58°12.80'N, 22°23.76'E.

Docking

There is no longer a public marina here.

There is a slipway and limited repair facilities which it may be possible to use in an emergency..

NOTE: (2009) there are no lateral marks and the leading line is out of order.

Night entry is not recommended.

Last updated December 2015.
