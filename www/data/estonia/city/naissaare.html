Naissaare - Profile

Location

This small island lies in the Gulf of Tallinn. about 5nm from the coast.

Position 59°33'28"N, 24°33'7"E.

Docking

Principally a ferry port but also with room for yachts on floating pontoons.

Limited water and electricity (solar power).

Last update December 2015.
