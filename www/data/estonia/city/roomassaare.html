Roomassaare - Profile

Location

This is a very convenient port of call, lying just 3nm south of Kuresseaare at the very tip of a peninsula.

Position 58°12'55''N, 22°30'16''E.

Clearance

Clearance is available here.

Last updated December 2015.

Docking

This large harbour offers plenty of room. It is located just off the souther end of the airport. There is no village nearby and is rather remote but a useful stop for clearing in or out of Estonia.

Photo from www.visitestonia.com

There are some repair facilities, a pharmacy, provisions at the port and  a bank and post office a mile away.

Last updated December 2015.
