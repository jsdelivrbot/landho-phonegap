Narva - Profile

Location

Located on the Narva River which marks Estonia's border with Russia.

Position 59°23.01' N, 28°12.38' E.

Clearance

Due to its position, it is assumed that Clearance can be carried out here.

Docking

Narva town yacht harbour is situated 14 km up river from the entrance. There are no official charts for Narva River, but it is safe if you keep to the middle and to the outside of the bends. The depth varies between 3.4 and 8.7 m. The frontier between Estonia and Russia is along the river, so it is wise to avoid approaching too closely to the eastern shore.

There is good, all-round shelter here.

Last updated December 2015.
