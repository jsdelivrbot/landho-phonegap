Lehtma - Profile

Location

A large fishing and commercial harbour. Located on the NE corner of on Hiiumaa, Estonia's second largest island. Its Kõpu lighthouse, completed in 1538, is still in use today.

Position 59°03.94' N, 22°42.14' E.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Passport Control and Customs open from May 15 - September 15, 8 am to 10 pm.

May be possible to make reservations for other times.

Last updated December 2015.

Docking

This harbour is exposed to S and SE winds.

There are 10 berhs for visiting yachts here.

Last updated December 2015.
