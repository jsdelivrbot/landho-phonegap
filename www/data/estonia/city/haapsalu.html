Haapsalu - Profile

Location

This is an attractive spa town about 50 miles from Tallinn. It lies on the west coast of Estonia, opposite the island of Hiiumaa.

Position 58°57.50'N, 23°31.61'E.

Clearance

Call the Harbour office on VHF channel 12 on approach before entering the harbour.

Passport control is based here.

Last updated December 2015.

Harbour Office
Tel:(+372) 472 9880 / mob (+372) 565 2887 ,VHF Channel 12 'Grand Holm'
Contact the Harbour Master for Passport control and Customs clearance.

Docking

There are two berthing options here. The Veskiviigi marina provides better shelter, but guest berthing is limited.

The harbour is exposed to easterly and NE winds.

There is a security gate in place on the pontoons.

A useful harbour map shows the location of the yacht harbours.

Be very aware of the depth on approach. It should be 3m but can be much less at times.

Last updated December 2015.
