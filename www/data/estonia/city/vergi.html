Vergi - Profile

Location

Vergi is situated on the north coast, approximately midway between Tallinn and the Russian border. It is an attractive and rocky part of the coast.

Position 59°35.84'N, 26°06.18'E.

Clearance

Passport Control is based here.

Last updated December 2015.

Docking

It is best to approach this harbour in daylight, along the leading lines. There are shallows on the W side of the breakwater.

It is well sheltered and has berths for 20 visitors in 3m.
