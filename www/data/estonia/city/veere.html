Veere - Profile

Location

A fishing harbour in the Bay of Tagalaht, on the north coast of Saaremaa Island, with room for a few yachts. It usually remains ice-free in the winter.

Position 58°27.67'N, 22°03.12'E.

Docking

The bay is deep, making the approach to the harbour straightforward.

It can be exposed in easterly and southerly winds. Also, a swell enters the port in strong NE'lies.

There are 6 berths here.

Last updated December 2015.
