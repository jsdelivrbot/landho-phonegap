Kardla - Profile

Location

Kardla is the only town on the island. It is small enough to explore on foot and admire its beautiful wooden architecture.

Position 59°00' N, 22°45' 18"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

This is a well sheltered, new harbour. There are no berthing fees.

Last updated January 2016.
