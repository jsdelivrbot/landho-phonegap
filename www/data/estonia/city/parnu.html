Parnu - Profile

Location

Pärnu is in the south western part of Estonia on the coast of Pärnu Bay, an inlet of the Gulf of Livonia. It is a popular summer resort.

Position 58°23.16'N, 24°29.20'E.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

There is Passport control is based here.

Last updated December 2015.

Docking

Due to the number of fishing nets in the vicinity, it is advisable to approach using the official shipping lanes. There are leading lights and marks into the Parnu river.

The yacht harbour is on the southern bank before the bridge, about 2km from the river entrance.

The approach to the marina is along the Silla leading line.

The marina offers complete shelter.

A useful harbour map

The city has all the usual facilities and services.

Last updated December 2015.
