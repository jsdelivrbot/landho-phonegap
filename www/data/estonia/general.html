Estonia - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2, summer time end March to end October UTC +3

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V :

Frequency: 50 Hz

Opening Hours

Normal shopping and banking hours are 09:00-18:00.

Supermarkets remain open until 23:00 on weekdays.

Money

Euro (as from 1st January 2011).

Credit cards are widely accepted.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Estonia is +372.

Mobile phone - Call your cell phone provider to make sure that they offer phone coverage in Estonia. Ask to be upgraded to international roaming.

The Estonia SIM card provides a simple, convenient, and affordable and will require a SIM-unlocked GSM cell phone that supports the 900 and 1800 frequencies.

There are public WiFi zones all around the country and internet access in libraries and post offices.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Estonia

Emergencies

General Emergency number - 112
Police only - 110
