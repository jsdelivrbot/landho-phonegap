Euro

Communications

International dialing code for Madeira is +351.

European GSM mobile network will work in Madeira phone. There is good coverage on most of the island. There is no coverage for non-EU mobile phones.

Events

The Festival of the Atlantic takes place in June, with the Madeira Music Festival, the International Fireworks Competition and performances by philharmonic bands in Funchal city centre.

For more information, go to: www.madeiratourism.org
