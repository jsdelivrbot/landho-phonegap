Location

Being at the western end of the island this is the sunniest part of Madeira. This port, for some reason, does not appear to feature in many crusing guides, but it is a Port of Entry and has a marina for about 300 yachts. It is situated about 14NM west of Funchal.

Clearance

Customs, Immigration and Maritime Police are all based here.

Docking

Shoreside facilities include 4 bar/resturaunts, a chandlery, a fishing tackle shop, a hotel, showers but no laundry. There is also a very good supermarket adjacent to the marina.

Last updated November 2009.

This marina is the 2nd largest in Madiera. All facilities including waste disposal. Yacht club. The staff here are reported to be excellent. Care is needed when leaving the marina as some of the concrete defences have fallen into the south side of the channel. Keeping to the starboard side means keeping to the centre of the channel especially where the concrete defences meet the mole.
