Porto Santo - Profile

Location

This tiny island lies approximately 30nm north east of the main island of Madeira. The port is on the southern side of the island and at the eastern end of a beautiful white sandy beach. The harbour wall here is decorated with murals and insignia of the many yachts which visit here.

Position 33°03'24"N, 16°17'04"W (harbour entrance)

Clearance

Formalities are normally completed during office hours. The captain should report to the Port Captain's office on the north side of the harbour. Even if arriving from Funchal and already possessing a transit log, one still has to report to this office.

Permits for visiting the outlying islands can be obtained here.

Last updated October 2015.

Docking

There is a marina and a boatyard in the NW part of the harbour and it is also possible to anchor in this large, sheltered port. There is a charge for anchoring but use of the facilities in the marina is included. The holding is excellent.

Water and electricity are laid on to the pontoons and there are showers at the end of the dock.

Last updated October 2015.

Opening hours: Marina services available daily from 09h to 18h, Monday to Sunday.
The Porto Santo Marina is identified by conspicuous sandy coloured storage tanks to the west of the entrance. Call the marina office on Ch. 16 on arrival for assistance.
160 berths on floating pontoons with water and electricity plus 40 moorings available. Services include Wi-Fi, laundry, car rental, fuel & gas refill. Permits for visiting the outlying islands can also be obtained here.
The boatyard is located next to the marina office, inside an area not accessible to the public, and can store up to 80 boats in metal cradles. Water, electricity & toilets on site. Living on board and own work in the boatyard is permitted.
A new promotional package has been launched, with attractive rates for: Marina + travelift + Boatyard. The travelift can haul out boats up to 25 tons, 4.35mts beam, and 2.8mts draft. (price for haulout Sep. 2013 for 11m boat 217Euros each way). Boatyard services include life raft inspections, boat repairs, mechanic & electricity, sail repair, and other customised services upon request.
