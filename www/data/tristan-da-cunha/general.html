Tristan da Cunha - General Info

Time Zone

UTC

Opening Hours

All Government Departments, including the Island Store, are open: Mon/Thursday 08:00-12:30 & 13:30-16:00 (08:30-16:30 in winter), Friday 08:00-13:00 (08:30-14:30 in winter).

Money

GBP £ Sterling.

US$ and SA Rand can be exchanged; cash or travellers cheques only.

Credit cards and personal cheques are not accepted.

Communications

International dialing code for Tristanda Cunha is +290.

TS2 provides Tristan da Cunha new mobile services. BGAN deliver seamless network coverage across most of the world's landmass. Users are able to get broadband wherever they go, not just in major cities or airports. BGAN is currently accessible in Tristan da Cunha, Europe, Africa, the Middle East, Asia, North and South America.
