Playa Herradura - Profile

Location

Located on the Pacific coast about 3 miles north of the town of Jaco. It is a major destination for sports fishermen as well as a popular tourist destination.

Position 09° 38'53"N, 84° 39'48"W (marina)

Clearance

There are no clearance facilities here.

It is necessary to have cleared into Costa Rica before visiting this port. See Cost Rica main ports for a list of clearance ports.

Last updated March 2016.

Docking

This marina will not admit sailing vessels unless you have already checked into Costa Rica. It is primarily a sport fishing marina and not cruiser-friendly (see comments from cruisers below).

Last updated September 2016.
