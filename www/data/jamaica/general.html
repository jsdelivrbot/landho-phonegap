Jamaica - General Info

Time Zone

UTC -5

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency 50 Hz

Money

Jamaica dollar (J$). It is advisable to shop around for better rates as those offered by banks and exchange places can vary widely.

Major credit cards are widely accepted throughout the island.

Most of the city bank branches throughout Jamaica have 24-hour ATMs. They can also be found at fuel stations.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Jamaica is +1 876.

Mobile phone networks are available. A prepaid Jamaica SIM card with an international cell phone is the most convenient and economical solution while in Jamaica. All incoming calls while in Jamaica are FREE, regardless of where they originate. Pay the local rate for local calls and use a cellular phone in Jamaica

Internet cafes can be found in the main towns and resorts, and access is also available from most hotels and parish libraries.

Northwest Caribbean Net, 1400 UTC 6209 USB, alternates 6212 and 6516, 7 days/wk. Generally covers Providencia north to Mexico on the Carib side, including Providencia, San Andreas, Honduras, Guatemala, Belize, Mexico, Cuba, Jamaica and the Caymans. They often follow boats all the way home to Key West through Galveston on the US coast, and sometimes boats heading south will continue to check in down to Panama, but not often.
