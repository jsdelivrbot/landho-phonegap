Oracabessa - Profile

Location

Oracabessa is a small town on the north coast of Jamaica, 10 miles east of Ocho Rios. It is a useful port of refuge.

The late Ian Fleming's house is here and several scenes from the 1962 James Bond film Dr. No were filmed at James Bond Beach.

Position 18°24′N, 76°57′W

Docking

The harbour is a man-made basin close to a big resort. It is reported to be well protected from the prevailing winds with good holding.
