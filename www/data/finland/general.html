Finland - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2, summer time UTC +3 from April to October.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Opening Hours

Banks are open Monday to Friday 10:00-16:30.

Money

Finland uses the EURO.

ATMs in Finland are called OTTO, and they are easily recognised by their orange coloured signs.

Credit cards are accepted everywhere.

Communications

The International dialing code for Finland is +358.

Mobile phone - Call your cell phone provider to make sure that they offer phone coverage in Finland. Ask to be upgraded to international roaming.

Consider renting a cell phone or SIM card to use with your personal cell phone if your cellular company does not offer coverage in Finland. rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Finland

Emergencies

Emergencies - Dial 112.
VHF Channel 16 should be used to contact the coastal rescue services.
