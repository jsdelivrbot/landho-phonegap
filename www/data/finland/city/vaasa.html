Vaasa - Profile

Location

Located about 50nm south of Jakostad. It is both an industrial city and a university town with many cultural activities. It is reputed to be the sunniest place in Finland.

Position 63° 05.5'N, 21° 35.2'E.

Docking

There is a guest harbour here.

New port added March 2013.
