Mariehamn - Profile

Location

The town is on the large peninsula projecting from the southern shore of the principal island of Aland. It is an attractive place of old wooden houses and pavement cafes. It has an ancient seafaring tradition of which it is justifiably proud.

See also the Aland Islands page.

Position 60° 05.87'N, 19° 55.67'E.

Clearance

The Aland Islands are not part of the EU Customs territory.

Docking

There are guest harbours on both sides of the peninsula, which, although only a mile apart by land, require an early decision about which to head for when approaching by sea.

The main ferry docks are on the western side of the peninsula, as it the ASS Guest harbour.
