Lappeenranta - Profile

Location

Situated at the southern edge of the Saimaa lake system, Lappeenranta has a colourful, 350-year-old history. It is a safe city, very accustomed to foreigners, and is the second most popular destination in Finland.

Docking

There are several options for berthing.

Kasinonranta
50 moorings, toilets, showers, water, electricity, restaurant.
Linnoituksen laituri
61 ° 3'57 "N 28 ° 11'13" E
220 m long dock for boats over 15 metres, water, electricity. Linnoitusniemi services are also available here.
Linnoitusniemi -Guest Harbour
20 moorings, water, electricity, toilets, showers, fuel, pump-out, launderette, chandlery, provisions, cafe. Suitable for small and medium sized boats.
Maalaistenlaituri
10 moorings. The Kasinonranta services are available here. Restaurants. This marina can be used free of charge for a short time (max. 4 hours). Overnight stays are charged as other marinas.
