Loviisa - Profile

Location

A small seaside town, approximately 65NM east of Helsinki. It is well known for its fortifications and the wooden houses in the Old Town.

Position 60°27.7'N, 26°14.7'E.

Clearance

Harbour Master
Tel:+358 019 515 163 ,VHF Channel 12, 13, 14, 16

Docking

The town is at the head of a long narrow inlet and has a guest harbour to the SE of the town centre. This is an excellent marina with all facilities including fuel.

There is a boatyard in Valko, located on the West Bank of the Loviisaviken, some 2.8nm South of Loviisa.

Last updated September 2012.
