Pietarsaari (Jakobstad) - Profile

Location

This is a commercial port on Finland's west coast, in the Gulf of Bothnia. It is also the home of the well-known Swan sailing yachts built by Nautor, as well as a large number of other boat building businesses.

Position 63°42.5'N, 22°41.4'E

Docking

There is an attractive guest harbour here.
