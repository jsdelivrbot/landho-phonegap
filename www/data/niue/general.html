Niue - General Info

Time Zone

UTC -11

Money

Currency - New Zealand dollar (NZ$) is the legal tender. There are no restrictions on the import or export of local or foreign currency.

There is no ATM on the island. It is best to bring NZ$.

Bank of Pacific does not accept MASTERCARD at present. Converting $US is the easiest and cheapest option.

Communications

See Pacific List of Radio Nets

International dialing code for Niue is +683

To get useful information regarding the approach to Niue, moorings availablability, procedures for lifting out your tender and Customs,Quarantine and Health information, email as you approach Niue.
The address is query@saildocs.com
Leave the subject line empty and in the body of the essage just type:-
send niuedotmoorings

Cell phone coverage is now available. A SIMM card will need to be purchased from Niue Telecom.

Post office 08:00-15:00 Monday to Friday. Mail closes on Fridays at 9:00 to meet the one Air NZ flight out a week.

Niue's only internet cafe has relocated. Rocket Systems is now in Alofi right next door to Nur Bakeries, opposite the Tourism Office. It is open from 9am to 3pm and wireless connections are also available. Cost is NZ$5 per hour.

There is a $25NZ hook-up fee for the open access WiFi service available from the moorings. Ask for instructions from the yacht club on arrival. This is a one off payment where you can have WiFi 24/7. Depends on your radio antennae and place in mooring field. Scan for WiFi nodes and strength on arrival.

Last updated August 2013.

Transport

Airport
Hanan International Airport is 2km south-east of the main town of Alofi.
Transport can be arranged if NYC is notified ahead of arrival.
Airlines
Air New Zealand flies from Auckland on Saturday morning, arriving Niue Friday 14:00, with a flight time of just over 3 hours.
Bike Rental
Niue is a good place to cycle, with a 68km ring road around the upraised atoll. Mountain bikes for hire for around NZ$15/day.
Buses
Local people will often stop and offer visitors a ride.
Car Rental
An attractive local Niue Photo Driving Licence is required (NZ$23).
