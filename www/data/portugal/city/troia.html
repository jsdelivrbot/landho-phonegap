Troia - Profile

Location

A useful stop for yachts in transit on the west coast of Portugal, as it lies 38nm from Cascais and Sines and 107nm from Lagos.

This is a new development on the northern tip of the Tróia peninsular, and consists of an enormous holiday resort which includes hotels, swimming pools, tennis courts, a casino and a golf course as well as the marina. Care has been taken to preserve the local ecology and history.

This narrow sand-strip 47 km south of Lisbon is unique. The wonderful sandy beach extends for a total of 18 kilometers.

Position 38°29'41"N, 08°54'16"W (marina entrance)

Docking

Although the entrance to the Rio Sado is wide, in wind over tide it can be dangerous. The main channel is well marked.

The marina is immediately to sta'bd on entering the river. Berth at the reception pontoon.
