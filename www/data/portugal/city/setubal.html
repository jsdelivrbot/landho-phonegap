Setubal - Profile

Location

Setúbal, located on the northern bank of the Sado river estuary, is just 10 miles NE of Sesimbra. Since the decline of its sardine fishing industry, the town has become principally a tourist resort.

Position 38°31.2'N, 08°53.1'W.

Docking

On approach, keep to the buoyed channel. The marina is in the third basin, after which, there is 6 miles of commercial quays. Watch out for the ferries which also dock here.

The marina, adjacent to the town, is usually full of local boats and rarely has room for visitors.

A better option is the new marina just 2nm away on the opposite bank, at Troia. It lies at the extreme northern tip of a long, thin peninsula. There is a regular ferry service to the town from here.

Anchorages
At Setubal, in 13m SW of the fishing basin (the first basin).

Various places within the estuary.

A shallow bay, Portinho da Arráriba, 1.5 nm NNW of the river entrance, in 2.5m. Should only be used in settled weather.
