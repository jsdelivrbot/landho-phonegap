Sines - Profile

Location

This port is a useful haven, being halfway between Lisbon and Cape St. Vincent. Although a small industrial town with a commercial port, the marina is adjacent to a clean sandy beach nestled below the delightful Old Town. It´s quite a hike up, but well worth it for the views.

Position 37°57'N, 08°52.1'W.

Clearance

Whether at anchor or in the marina, take passports and ships papers to the office to complete the usual forms.

Docking

If approaching from the north, beware of the submerged outer breakwater marked with a red buoy.

The marina can be found in the NE corner of the large commercial harbour inside the Porto Pesca (fishing harbour). The shelter is good, however the marina is small and tight with berthing for one or two larger boats only. The marina has new offices and is well-equipped, clean and safe.

It is also possible to anchor in the inner harbour, close to the marina in 4m, although the holding may not be too good.

Last updated August 2015.
