Leixoes (nr Porto/Oporto) - Profile

Location

Located 4nm north of the entrance to the Douro river, Leixoes is the artificial harbour built to serve Porto (Oporto). It is one of Portugal's most important ports, however not the most attractive port of call. Now that the new Douro Marina has opened in Oporto, visiting yachts have an alternative should they prefer to be much closer to the city for exploring.

Position 41° 11.6'N,8° 41.5'W.

Clearance

Visit marina office with passports and ships papers to fill in necessary paperwork, whether berthed in the marina or anchored off.

Last updated December 2015.

Docking

The marina at Leixoes is in the NE corner of the commerical harbour; downwind of the oil terminal! It is run by an association of the three yacht clubs based there.

This port is not easy to enter at night as the shore lights hide the identity of the navigation lights.

Beware of anchored ships near the entrance and the traffic of fishing vessels morning and evening.

This port can be closed is strong westerlies.
