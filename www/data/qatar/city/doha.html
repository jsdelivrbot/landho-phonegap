Doha - Profile

Location

This rapidly expanding city lies on the eastern coast of Qatar and is the country's capital. It is the planned venue for the 2022 Football World Cup competition. It's marina facilities are also expanding fast although mainly aimed at the luxury yacht market.

Position 25° 22"43'N, 51° 37"22'E. (West Bay marina district entrance)

Docking

Several large marina developments have been built in the West Bay Lagoon area, which is about 10 miles (15km) north of Doha city centre.

The Ritz Carlton Doha marina, Lusail City marina and the Pearl-Qatar marinas are all in this area.
