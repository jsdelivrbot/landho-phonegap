Choiseul Island - Profile

Location

This island lies southeast of Bougainville Island, which is part of Papua New Guinea, and north of Noro on the New Georgia islands. It has several small satellite islands and a larger one, Rob Roy Island, off its southern end and separated from it by the narrow Nggosele Channel.

Position 07° 26'15"S, 157° 28'58"E (S entrance to Nggosele Channel)

Docking

There are many sheltered anchorages along this narrow, dog-legged passage between the main island and Rob Roy island but, be warned, you may be approached by local residents for a charge for passing through. See email report from October 2011.
