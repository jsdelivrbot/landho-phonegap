Yandina - Profile

Location

Yandina lies on the NE corner of Mbanika Island, which is, itself seperated from Pavuvu Island to the north, by a narrow channel. The whole group of the Russell Islands is NW of Guadalcanal.

Position 09° 04'30"S, 159° 13'15"E.

Clearance

For full details on clearing into and out of these Islands see Solomon Islands Formalities.

This is an official Port of Entry.

Docking

Yandina has one of the best deep water anchorages in the Solomon Islands, although it is likely care needs to be taken to avoid snagging any of the WW2 debri lying on the seabed.

There are also has basic services, including a store, post office and airport here.

Last updated November 2011.
