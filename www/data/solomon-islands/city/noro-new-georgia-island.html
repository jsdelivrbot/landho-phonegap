Noro (New Georgia Island) - Profile

Location

Noro is on the western side of the island of New Georgia, which itself lies in about the middle of the Solomon Islands chain.

Position 08° 14'S, 157° 11'45"E.

Clearance

For full details on clearing into and out of these Islands see Solomon Islands Formalities.

This is a Port of Entry and the necessary officials for clearing in and out are based here (Customs, Immigration, Health and Quarantine).

It has been reported by cruisers that Noro is now the preferred port of entry into the Western Province as Gizo has been experiencing reports of theft, and the Immigration officer is extremely uncooperative and rarely available.

The Immigration officer at Noro is the most senior in the Solomon Islands. He is called Billy and can be contacted on +6777495010. Cruisers recommend this is a good telephone number to have if you leave your yacht in the Solomons and have to return with only a single ticket. You won't be allowed on the flight, but a call to Billy will get you on.

Advice is to begin check in early as it takes most of the day and is a lengthy process. Clearance fees are charged according to the boat length and are quite high.

Last updated July 2016.

Docking

This port has several safe anchorages and is a very useful port for both clearing in and out.

The nearest repair facilities are at Liapari on the island of Vella Lavela.

Last updated February 2015.
