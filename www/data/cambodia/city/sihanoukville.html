Sihanoukville - Profile

Location

This port city is a growing Cambodian urban centre, located 185 kilometres (115 mi) southwest of the Cambodian capital, Phnom Penh.  It has become a popular beach tourist destination and attracts divers to Koh Tang, one of the Sihanoukville islands where the major battle to free the SS Mayagüez took place. Divers can see two shipwrecks 40 metres (130 ft) down.

The first marina in Cambodia - Marina Oceania - is now open.

Position 10° 38.3'N, 103° 29'E.

Clearance

This is now the only clearance port for visiting yachts in Cambodia.

See Cambodia Formalities for full details.

Last updated June 2015.

Docking

Marina Oceania opened in November 2013 and is well protected by a breakwater. It is located in the inner harbour of Sihanoukville Seaport (see photo provided by the marina).

The marina is keen to provide a professional service to visiting yachts and hopes to expand to include a shipyard and yachting services at some point in the future.

The marina is small (20 boats) and built with equipment from Europe. Transfers to the city by car and ferry are available (10-15 minutes to the city center and the main shops and markets).

Small repairs are possible in the marina, such as underwater hull cleaning, ultrasonic scraper etc. Repairs on the beach are possible by two local shipyards nearby.

Last updated June 2015.
