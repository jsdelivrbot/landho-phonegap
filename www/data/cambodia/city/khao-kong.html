Khao Kong - Profile

Location

Khao Kong lies a short distance south of the border with Thailand (not to be confused with the island of Kho Kong a few miles south of the town). This is a relatively undeveloped part of the country and the city previously had the reputation of being a 'wild west' type of town. To the south lies the Peam Krasaop wildlife sancturary.

Position 11° 36.4'N, 102° 58.2'E.

Khao Kong fishing village © angkor7thtravel.com

Clearance

In 2015 rules have changed for visiting yachts and it's no longer possible to clear into or out of Cambodia here. All clearance must take place in Sihanoukville.

Docking

One option is to anchor off the delightful Cafe Laurent,  which is owned by ex sailors who are happy to let you use their dock. The anchorage is in 8m and about 500m below the bridge.
