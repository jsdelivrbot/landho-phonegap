Time Zone

UTC +7

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Note - Some outlets are a combination of type A and C and can accept either type plug. Plug G may be found in some hotels.

Money

Riel.

Most transactions are conducted in cash (US Dollars)

ATMs are available in Phnom Penh, Sihanoukville and Siem Reap.

Credit cards are not widely used.

Communications

International dialing code for Cambodia is +855.

Mobile phone coverage in Cambodia is patchy at best. In urban areas coverage is fine, but rural areas its pretty poor. Phone coverage in Angkor Wat is very patchy.
