Ambergris Cay - Profile

Location

Ambergris Cays are located in the sparsely populated southeastern quadrant of the Caicos Bank. The area is full of sea life and is commercially fished for conch, spiny lobster and many varieties of fish. South Caicos fishermen voyage daily to these flats.

Big Ambergris is a private island under development as an upscale resort. The Turks and Caicos Sporting Club is based here.

Landing on the Cay by transient sailors is not permitted. There are no customs facilities on the island and it is not a Port of Entry.

Location 21°18'N,71°37'W.

Updated November 2008.

Clearance

It is understood that Customs and Immigration will soon be based here but at present there are no customs facilities and it is not a Port of Entry.

Last updated November 2008.
