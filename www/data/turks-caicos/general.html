Turks & Caicos - General Info

Time Zone

UTC -5

Money

United States dollars (US$). Some Turks and Caicos coins are used.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Turks Caicos is +1 649.

Digicel update December 2014:
Anyone cruising using Digicel should be aware that if you purchase a SIM card in one country, it cannot be topped up in different one, as previously possible. So if you wish to use the sim bought here while cruising in other countries, you should either put on a large enough sum to cover your needs, or set up an account and do it online.
You can top-up Digicel at https://online-top-up.digicelgroup.com/

Transport

There are scheduled flights from Miami, Florida, to Providenciales by way of American Airlines. Turks & Caicos Island Airways can get you around the island group, or to Providenciales or Grand Turk from Jamaica, Nassau, Haiti, the Dominican Republic and Puerto Rico.

The Turks and Caicos Guide
By Stephen J Pavlidis
Publisher: Seaworthy Publications Edition: 2nd (2002)
ISBN: 1892399113
This is the original in depth cruising guide to the Turks and Caicos region. It includes key harbours on the northern coast of the Dominican Republic. The book contains colour charts, GPS waypoints, piloting instructions, approach routes, marinas and anchorages. Also included are dive sites, radio weather broadcasts, tides and currents, and evn history, holidays and customs. In addition, the book contains information on Puerto Plata and luperon in the Dominica Republic with charts and photos.
