King Edward Point (South Georgia) - Profile

Clearance

Report to King Edward Point by VHF. Customs and Immigration formalities will be carried out by the Officer in Charge or the Harbour Master who will board the yacht.

There is a landing fee of £100 (2006) per paying passenger, in the case of charter yachts.

Yachts are expected to be self-sufficient and there are no facilities available, except perhaps in an emergency.

Last updated April 2014.

Docking

Docking for yachts and tenders is available at Grytviken (Tijuca jetty). Water is also available here.

Due to biosecurity concerns, yachts are not normally permitted to use the jetty at King Edward point.

Last updated April 2014.
