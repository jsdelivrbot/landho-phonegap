Location

Located at the entrance of the Sungai Muar, about 20nm SE of Melaka on Malaysia's west coast.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

For full details on clearance into and out of Malaysia see Malaysia Formalities.

Clearance procedures here are reported to be simple and friendly.

The Jabulan Laut are based at the ferry terminal on the South side below the bridge.

Last updated October 2010.

Docking

The new marina here is not yet complete but the pontoons are in place although there are no facilities or services (2010). The approach is shallow, so best entered close to HW.

It is also possible to anchor further into the river, towards the northern side, opposite the ferry terninal. There is good holding here.
