Location

The second largest town in Sarawak, Sibu is situated on the confluence of the Rajang and Igan Rivers, some 60 km from the sea. Even at this point, the river is over a mile wide.

Clearance

Port of Entry.

For full details on clearance into and out of Malaysia see Malaysia Formalities.

When arriving here from places other than a port in Sarawak, a boat must clear in with the authorites. Similarly on departure for a destination outside Sarawak.

Note the new update to the restrictions on visas. See Malaysia/Immigration.
