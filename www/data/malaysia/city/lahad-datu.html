Lahad Datu - Profile

Location

This is a mainly commercial port on the northern side of Darvel Bay on the far eastern coast of Sabah. A deep water port facility for the export of palm oil is being developed here.

It is in an area of disputed territory and has been subject to recent Malaysian anti-terrorist attacks, as well as the kidnapping of foreign tourists from various islands south of Semporna.
