Location

Not the prettiest of towns, having many concrete buildings, but friendly and easily to explore on foot. The mosques give the town a more exotic feel and the colonial-style Courthouse and Handicraft Centre add interest to the old town.

Docking

There is a sheltered anchorage in the river and plenty of space between the moored fishing boats.
