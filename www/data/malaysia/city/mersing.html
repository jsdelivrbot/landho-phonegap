Â Mersing - Profile

Location

Formally a small, sleepy fishing village on the east coast of the mainland opposite Tioman Island. Now grown into a rather busier town, acting as the main gateway for the island.

Clearance

Authorised entry point by sea.

For full details on clearance into and out of Malaysia see Malaysia Formalities.

Docking

No yacht moorings but possible to raft up to a fishing boat in the river. The anchorage at the entrance is subject to swell.
