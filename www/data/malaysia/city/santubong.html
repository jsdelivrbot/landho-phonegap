Santubong - Profile

Location

This is a small, quiet village at the entrance to the Santubong River. To enter the river go close to the starboard mark and follow the channel markers.

Position 01°43.05'N, 110°19.6'E.

Clearance

Clearance can be carried out at Pending, just outside Kuching. See Port Clearance/Kuching for the details.

Docking

There is a anchorage (no berthing) at the mouth of the Santubong river with access to a private marina where you can leave your dinghy and get water, fuel and laundry services. Avoid the area near the biggest jetty as the ground is foul.

Last updated October 2013.
