Time Zone

UTC +9

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Note - A UN correspondent reports "power is poor in the country with frequent brownouts and blackouts. I suspect that surges are frequent as we go through a lot of surge-protecting power bars." Further he reports than Type I is common as much construction is done by Australians; type C is common in building built during Indonesian occupation; type E is less common; type F is common in offices but not hotels.

Money

The currency is US dollar, nowhere now accepts Rupiah (Indonesian currency).

There are 3 banks in Dili with ATMs.

Communications

International dialing code for East Timor is +670.

Timor Telecom offers mobile GSM services.
