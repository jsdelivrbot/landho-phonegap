Mutsamudu - Profile

Location

This is a deep water port located in the centre of the Anjouan's north west coast. It is the largest city on the island. (Photo courtesy of SV Totem - September 2015).

Anjouan is known for its cloves, the ylang-ylang flowers (the base for many perfumes) and vanilla.

A famous island nobody knows - BBC Travel article by circumnavigator Diane Selkirk.

Position 12° 10′S, 44° 24′E.

Clearance

This is the main port of entry for Comoros. See Comoros Formalities for more details.

An agent called "Maketse" has been serving yachts since 1991 here. See Clearance Agents for details.

Last updated September 2015.

Docking

After clearance it is best to anchor off and to always have somebody on board. Maketse can provide a reliable guard. When going ashore, it is advisable to lock the boat and just leave food and water for the guard.

Even in calm weather, there is a slight swell in the anchorage here.
