Moroni - Profile

Location

Moroni is the capital of the Comoros and is on the west coast of Grande Comore (Ngazidja), which is the largest island in the group.

The town lies close to one of the largest and most active volcanos in the world.

Position: 11° 42'S, 43° 15'E.

Docking

There is a harbour and anchorage here, however it offers little protection. The port deals with small cargo vessels and it is unknown whether yachts can berth here.

The town has several shopping malls.
