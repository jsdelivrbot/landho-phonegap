Fukuoka (Hakata) - Profile

Location

This large city is the most important centre on Kyushu. The port is call Hakata and it lies on the NW coast of the island.

Position 33°35'35"N, 130°18'84"E (Odo yacht harbour)

Position 33°35'60"N, 130°19'37"E (Marinoa marina)

Clearance

See Japan Formalities for detailed clearance information.

This is a Port of Entry and either of the marinas will notify the officials of your arrival. They will then visit the boat to do the paperwork.

This has to be done even if you have already cleared into the county.

Last updated March 2016

Docking

This port is well marked and can be entered at night.

The two marinas here are on the western side of the city, in the Nishi district. The newer one, Marinoa marina is more secure but berthing fees are higher. It is easily identified by the ferris wheel. The Fukuoka City Yacht Harbour is just a little further west, round the headland.

Fukuoka is a vibrant city and most goods and services are available.

Last updated March 2016.
