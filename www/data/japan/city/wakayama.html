Wakayama - Profile

Location

Wakayama lies just south of the entrance to Osaka Bay, on the main island of Hoshima.

Location 34° 09'38"N, 135° 10'31"E (marina entrance)

Clearance

See Japan Formalities for detailed clearance information.

Full clearance can be obtained here, including arrangements for any visas required. See the marina manager.

Last updated July 2013.

Docking

The marina here is new and the manager (Ms Tomoko Koura) extremely helpful. On arrival, berth at the fuel dock and visit the Harbour office, then move to the visitor's pontoon.

The marina is part of a large tourist complex - Wakayama Marina City - and has hotels, restaurants, bathhouses, local products markets and shops (no supermarket).

Last updated April 2016.
