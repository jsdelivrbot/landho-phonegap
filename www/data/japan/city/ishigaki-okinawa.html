Ishigaki (Okinawa) - Profile

Location

The island of Ishigaki lies about 200nm east of Taiwan and is part of the Yaeyama Island group. The main island of Okinawa is about 400nm north east. The climate here is more tropical than on mainland Japan. The port of Ishigaki lies on the south west corner of the island.

Position 24° 20'N, 124° 08'E.

Clearance

See Japan Formalities for detailed clearance information.

This is the most southern port of entry on Okinawa. Permission to enter MUST be obtained 24hrs before arrival. A fax sent before leaving your previous port is the best way.

Last updated May 2015.

Customs
All clearance officials will visit the boat. Customs must be advised of any narcotic medicines on board.

Docking

This is a well-sheltered harbour with several outlying breakwaters.

There is a 500ft floating pier with 3.5m at LW. If there is no space here, it is possible to use the harbour wall, but it is advisable to use a breast anchor to keep off it.

The harbour is safe in a typhoon, but plenty of rope is needed.

Locally, there are good mechanics and plenty of hardware stores and supermarkets.
