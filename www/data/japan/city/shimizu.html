Shimizu - Profile

Location

An excellent stopover roughly 100 sea miles west of Yokohama. Shimizu is well-placed as a base for land travel with a JR Train station and easy transfer to the Shinkensen in nearby Shizuoka. On clear days nearby Mt. Fuji dominates the skyline.

Location 35°6′N, 138°54′E.
