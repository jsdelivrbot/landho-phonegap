Tannowa - Profile

Location

This port lies just inside Osaka Bay on the eastern side. It is just a small village with only a supermarket, a post office with an ATM and a train station.

Location 34° 20.2'N, 135° 11'E.

Clearance

See Japan Formalities for detailed clearance information.

This port is not able to carry out Quarantine clearance, although it has Customs and Immigration. A better option is the marina at Wakayama.

Last updated July 2013.

Docking

The manager at the marina is very helpful, but with so few facilities and an automatic train station which will completely confuse a foreigner, there is little reason to visit.

Last updated July 2013.
