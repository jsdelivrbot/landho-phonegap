Awaji Island - Profile

Location

Awaji Island (Awaji-Shima) is the eastern boundary of the Seto Inland Sea and is connected both at the northern and southern ends by impressive suspension bridges.

Sumoto lies on the eastern side of the island, about 10nm across Osaka Bay from Wakayama.

Clearance

Although there is both Customs and Immigration facilities here, there is no Quanantine clearance possible. Wakayama is the nearest Port of Entry for foreign yachts.

Last updated July 2013.
