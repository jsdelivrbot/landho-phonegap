Monaco - Profile

Location

This is THE place to see and be seen! Port Fontvielle-Monaco is a private port and there is not usually any space available for visitors.

Position 43° 44'13"N, 07° 26'E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

Customs and the Harbourmaster are both at the NW end of Quai Antoine.

Customs
Tel:+377 9397 970245

Docking

Call Port Control on VHF channel 12 for instructions.

There are two ports actually in Monaco, The Hercules Port and Fontvieille Port, and another, Port de Cap d’Ail, immediately to the west of the boarder, in France.

The shelter is good in all the marinas.
