Monaco - Formalities

Clearance

Call port control on Channel 12 or 16.  A berth must have already been assigned.

Last updated March 2015.

Immigration

Immigration requirements are the same as for France

Last updated March 2015.

Customs

The import of fresh meat and milk products from non-EU countries is prohibited.

Monaco is not a member of the EU, but has close economic and political ties with France and is part of the European Union customs zone.

Customs regulations in Monaco are the same as in France.

Last updated March 2015.

Pets

The Pet Travel Scheme (PETS) applies to dogs and cats going in and out of Monaco. They require a Pet Passport, to be microchipped and have a current rabies vaccination certificate and to have had recent blood test. Monaco require Bi-lingual EU Form 998
