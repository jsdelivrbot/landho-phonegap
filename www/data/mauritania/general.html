Money

Ouguiya (U) of 5 khoums. The export of local currency is forbidden.

Foreign currency must be declared on arrival. Ouguiyas cannot be exchanged outside Mauritania.

Credit cards are not accepted as the country is not connected to the international banking system.

Travellers cheques can be exchanged at banks and are best in US Dollars.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Mauritania is +222.

Telephone: Outgoing international calls must go through the operator. Mobile phone.

Roaming agreements exist with some international mobile phone operators. Coverage is limited to urban areas.

Internet: There are several Internet cafes in Nouakchott.

Post: International postal facilities are limited to main cities. Airmail to Europe takes approximately two weeks.
