Nauru - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +12

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 240 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Australian dollar (Aus$)

There is one ATM on the island located at the Menen Hotel, but it is often out of cash.

Credit cards have very limited acceptance in Nauru.

Communications

See Pacific List of Radio Nets

International Dialing Code for Naura is +674.

To use your mobile phone, it must have a frequency compatible with territory you’re heading to and your home operator must have a roaming agreement with Digicel.

Simply switch on your mobile phone when you arrive in Nauru and your phone will automatically connect to the Digicel network. If not you may have to select it manually.

Internet is available but certain sites, including some social media sites like facebook are blocked by law.
