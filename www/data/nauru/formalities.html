Clearance

It is essential that permission to enter is obtained before making an entry here. Permission may not be given.

The Port Captain monitors VHF Channel 16 and will advise on the best procedure. If a large ship's mooring is available, he may advise a yacht to tie up to it.

Entry formalities are completed ashore.

Some officials reported to be very unfriendly and unhelpful. Not a port which appears to welcome yachts.

Last updated August 2015.

Immigration

Visitors arriving by yacht may given a 30 day visa on arrival. There is likely to be a $100 AUS charge.

Health

Medical facilities in Nauru are very basic and medical evacuation by air ambulance to Australia is necessary in most cases.

Nauru is subject to periodic outbreaks of typhoid. Dengue fever also occurs.

There have been reports of visitors being bitten by stray dogs. Be vigilant, particularly on the beach. If you are bitten seek medical attention immediately.
