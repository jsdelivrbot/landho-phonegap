Walker's Cay - Profile

Location

This cay lies at the northern end of the Abaco Islands. It is a convenient stop before using the northern-most ocean pass onto the Little Bahama Bank.

Position 27° 12'N, 078° 25'W

Clearance

This is not currently a Port of Entry as the Customs post relocated to Little Grand Cay some years ago.

Updated April 2016.

Docking

Ignore the stakes marking the narrow channel to the old marina. It has not been maintained for many years.

The best approach is that to the east, between Walkers Cay and Gully Rocks.

The area around Walkers Cay the area is difficult. The ground consists of hard rock and coral lumps and you can easily run aground. Be careful, keep a good lookout.

Since the area was destroyed by a hurricane in 2004, all businesses at Walkers cay have closed.

Last updated April 2015.
