Clarence Town - Profile

Location

Clarence Town lies at the southern end of Long Island, on it eastern coast. This island, like Eleuthera and Cat Island, has an ocean coastline to the east, which offers deep-sea fishing and one or two anchorages in suitable weather, including Clarence Town. To visit this attractive settlement, consult the cruising guides, as it can be uncomfortable.

The west side offers shallow water cruising with interesting settlements, creeks and bays to explore.

Position 23° 06'21"N, 74° 57'28"W (Flying Fish marina)

Clearance

See main Bahamas Clearance page for more information.

Clearance facilities are available at the Flying Fish Marina, but 24 hours notice is required. The officials have to visit from the airport at the other end of the island.

Last updated August 2016.

Customs and Immigration
Based at the airport
Require 24hr notice.

Docking

The Flying Fish Marina can accommodate boats up to 200 feet.

The Clarence Town anchorage offers better protection against the elements then you might expect, as the coral reef even dries at LW. No surge or swell.

Note that the closest grocery store for provisioning to Clarence Harbour is 6 miles away.

Last updated August 2016.
