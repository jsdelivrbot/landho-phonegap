Cape Eleuthera Marina
Cape Eleuthera
Tel:1 (844) 884-1014 Fax:(242) 334 8507
http://www.capeeleuthera.com , reservations@capeeleuthera.com
This is an official port of entry. Located on a 9 mile private peninsula at the southern end of Eleuthera. This is a large marina with 12-15’ deep water entry. Able to take mega yacht dock. All facilities, fuel, laundry, WiFi, provisions yacht management, service and repairs. Restaurant, BBQ and grill area. Accommodation. Security.
Davis Harbour Marina
Location 24º 43' 773" N,/ 76º 15' 147" W. , Wemys Bight, South Eleuthera, Bahamas
Tel:+242 334 6303 Fax:+242 334 2280 ,VHF Channel 16
http://www.davisharbourmarina.com/ , info@davisharbourmarina.com
This marina lies midway between Powell Point and Eleuthera Point, at the southern end of the island. 24 slips. All facilities, fuel, laundry and is a useful stop-off. Restaurant(to re-open later in 2015). Some repairs can be arranged to be done there. There is little else around it and it is a long way from anywhere. Rock Sound is the nearest place for provisioning, hardware and LPG.
