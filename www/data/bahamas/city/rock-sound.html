Docking

On approach keep a sharp lookout, for scattered rocks, bombies and shifting sand bars. Head for the conspicuous Batelco tower and anchor off the village in 2m sand and grass - good holding and all round shelter.

The developement here has halted for now.

It is possible to berth at the Government dock for customs and shopping etc.
