Staniel Cay - Profile

Location

Half-way down the Exuma chain, Staniel Cay offers the opportunity to do modest reprovisioning (see comment below on missing mail boat) and charter flights can be arranged here.

Staniel Cay has been used in lots of movies, most notably the James Bond film “Thunderball”, and is a very popular tourist destination.

Don't miss the Thunderball Grotto.

Position 24° 10'N, 76°26'W.

Docking

There is a government dock at 24° 10.11'N, 076° 26.69'W. This is convenient for accessing two of the stores on the island.

The third store which is is on the other side of the inlet  (at 24° 10.11'N, 76° 26.50'W) has its own dock. It also has some hardware and marine items.

An excellent anchorage can be had on the west side of Big Majors Spot, towards the northern end, just south east of
Fowl Cay Resort. The holding is good and there is shelter from NW through N to SE. You will need a fast dinghy to visit Staniel Cay from there.

The anchorage in the south east corner of Big Majors Spot, opposite Big Rock Cut has good holding except for a patch to the south of this area. This is very sheltered and out of the current.

Anchoring close to Staniel Cay is possible if you position yourself just out of the channel, but the tidal stream is fierce.
