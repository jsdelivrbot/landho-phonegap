Frazer's Hog Cay - Profile

Location

Frazer's Hog Cay is located on the northern end of Chub Cay, in the Berry Islands. Having the advantage of being sheltered by Bird and Whale Cays with their inshore sand banks, Frazer’s Hog Cay is protected from the prevailing easterly winds.

Position 26° 25'25"N, 77° 50'09"W

Clearance

Review main Bahamas Clearance information.

The Chub Airport is close to Frazer’s Hog Cay and is a Port of Entry.

Customs and Immigration are on on duty and can clear in yachts at the marina.

Last updated September 2016.

Docking

The marina offers the best shelter, otherwise there is little protection from southerly winds.

Take care if anchoring as the sand banks around Frazer's Hog Cay are constantly shifting.

last updated September 2016.
