Governors Harbour - Profile

Location

This is the capital of Eleuthera and the largest town on the island. It lies approximately half way along the western coast. It is a quiet, attractive little town with steep, narrow streets.

Position 25° 11.8'N,  076° 14.9'W.

Docking

The holding in this anchorage can be indifferent, but it is a useful place to provision. The best holding is near the old Club Med dock.

A good anchorage (that offers protection in W to N winds) is a good mile NW of the town anchorage, at 25°12,5’N 076°15,7’W.

There are moorings off the town, but without any marker buoys. Ask the port authority on VHF 16 before you dive down to find one!

There is a small, (and probably shallow) marina about 3 miles south at Runway Bay, Palmetto.

Last updated January 2015.

Location 25° 8' 40"N, 76° 10' 9"W , Rogue Cove , South Palmetto Point

Only suitable for very shallow draft boats, although the entrance channel is said to be 10ft in depth.
