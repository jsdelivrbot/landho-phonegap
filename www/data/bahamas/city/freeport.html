Freeport - Profile

Location

This big commercial port is geared to dealing primarily with commercial vessels, so visiting yachts might find it more convenient to clear in at nearby Lucaya, Sunrise or Xanadu Marinas.

Be very street-wise here. The crime rate is high (not only for theft), so take care where you walk, especially at night, and secure all your property as best you can. Locking your boat securely at night and when away from it is a must, as is locking your dinghy and outboard when tied to the boat and ashore at a dinghy dock.

Review main Bahamas Clearance information.

Docking

Freeport Harbour itself is a commercial harbour with no place for yachts except in an emergency. The marinas to the east (Xanadu Beach, Sunrise and Port Lucaya) provide a more comfortable port of call.

The Lucaya area is a big development, after Nassau probably the biggest in the Bahamas. If you like hustle and commerce you’ll find much to your liking.
