Sampson Cay - Profile

Location

Unfortunately, the marina here closed to visiting yachts in 2013. Its billionaire owner has decided to make the island and facilities private for use by his family and guests.

It is however still possible to anchor in the outer harbor, good holding but open to the west.

Position 24° 12.5'N, 076° 28.5'W.

Last updated May 2015.
