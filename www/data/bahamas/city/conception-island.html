Conception Island - Profile

Location

Conception Island is a national park with no inhabitants or facilities. Anchoring and landing is permitted but absolutely nothing must be taken or left.

Position 23° 51'06"N, 75° 07'12"W (anchorage)

Docking

Take care on the approach to this island as there are extensive reef surrounding it. Good light is essential.

There is an anchorage on the west side - close to the north west end of West Bay - however, shelter is limited and it is advisable to leave before anything in the western semicircle starts blowing.

Another anchorage can be found around the corner at Booby Cay. Keep a good look-out for there are a lot of bombies around. This anchorage gets rolly in eastern winds and it's advisable to leave the anchorage when strong easterly to southwesterly winds are expected.

Watch the weather closely and get out of here before you get trapped in these anchorages.
