Hope Town, Elbow Cay - Profile

Location

Located towards the northern end of Elbow Cay, Hope Town is easily identified by its red and white striped lighthouse. This is a famously attractive little town where no cars or golf carts are allowed.

Position 26° 32'38"N, 76° 57'44"W (Hope Town harbour entrance)

Docking

Hope Town Harbour is a well-protected and popular harbour, so much so that finding a space to anchor may be difficult (and may not be liked by the Harbour Master). Hope Town Marina, Abaco Bahama Charters or Lighthouse Marina all have moorings for rent; contact them on VHF Ch 16. Reservations however may be necessary. Dinghies can be landed at the foot of the spectacular Hope Town lighthouse.

It is also possible to anchor outside the harbour, between Parrot Cays and Elbow Cay in about 2m with good holding, but you are exposed to the north and south. This is however a beautiful anchorage and lovely for swimming.

Last updated July 2016.
