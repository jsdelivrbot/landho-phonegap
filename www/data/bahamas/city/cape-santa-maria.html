Cape Santa Maria - Profile

Location

Cape Santa Marina lies at the northern tip of Long Island. The reefs off this cape extend a significant distance off shore. Be sure to give it a 3 or 4 mile clearance.

This is a remote and very peaceful area of the island.

Position 23° 33.3′N, 75° 16.4′W (Stella Maris Marina)

Clearance

See main Bahamas Clearance page for all information.

Clearance can be arranged here by requesting a meeting with the Customs and Immigration officers at Stella Maris airport.

Last updated August 2016.

Docking

The small marina at the Stella Maris Resort, lies about 15nm south of the northern end of the island, on the west coast. It offers very good shelter. There are several secure anchorages in the area if great care is taken entering them.

Last updated August 2016.
