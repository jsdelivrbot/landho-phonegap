Grand Cay (Little Grand Cay) - Profile

Location

The Grand Cay group consists of five main islands; Grand, Big Grand, Little Grand, Rat and Felix.

Little Grand Cay lies south of Walker Cay and is one of the main island in the Abacos group. It is identified by a tall radio tower at its western end.

Position 27° 12.75'N, 078° 18.8'W.

Clearance

The Customs post re-located here from Walker's Cay and can be found at Rosie’s Place on Little Grand Cay.

Currently there are no known plans to move it back to Walker's Cay.

Last updated April 2016.

Docking

The entrance to the harbour is shallow but cheerful help with guidance may be had by calling on VHF channel 16.

There is a small marina here, but also an anchorage. The holding near the shore is reported not to be good, so beware of your anchor dragging.

Facilities ashore are limited, but it is a wonderful, quiet spot for snorkeling and fishing. Not to be missed, is 'Rosie's Place'.

An alternative anchorage can be found at Sandy Cay, Double Breasted Cays. It is protected from all directions except the south. Be aware of the tidal flow between the barrier rocks.

Last updated April 2015.
