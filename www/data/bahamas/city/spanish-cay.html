Spanish Cay - Profile

Location

This attractive, privately run, island was severely damaged by hurricane Floyd but has been rebuilt and is flourishing again. Well worth a stop.

Position 26° 56.36'N, 077° 32'W.

Clearance

Review main Bahamas Clearance information.

There is an addition $50 charge for clearing in here if not staying in the marina.

Customs and Immigration are on call 24hrs.

Last updated January 2015.

Docking

The marina pay to provide clearance services here.

Last updated January 2015.
