Treasure Cay - Profile

Location

This purpose-built resort is located mid-way between Coopers Town and March Harbour, on the east coast of Great Abaco Island. It is a popular destination for visitors from the USA in winter.

Position 26°39'49"N, 77° 17'24"W (entrance to the bay)

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

See the main Bahamas Clearance page for information.

Docking

There are is an excellent marina here and a recently built resort on the peninsula at the approach to Treasure Cay marina which also accepts transient visitors.

The moorings at Treasure Cay Marina have pretty good monthly rates and are checked annually.

Anchorage: Yachts can anchor also, however there is a nightly charge. The anchorage (in 2m) is immediately inside the bay, opposite Clipper Cay.

Recommended by cruisers as a good place to leave the boat for a while.
