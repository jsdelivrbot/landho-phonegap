South Bimini - Profile

Location

This sparsly populated island, just 150 yards from North Bimini, is now being more developed, but there are still very few facilities here.

Position 25° 42'37"N, 79° 18'06"W (marina entrance)

Clearance

Review main Bahamas Clearance information.

The Bimini Sands Resort & Marina houses the Customs and Immigration officials.

Last updated January 2015.

Docking

Do not attempt to enter this marina when the wind is blowing out of the wrong direction (everything from S over W to N).

There is a small boat repair shop close to the airport and some provisions can be obtained from Morgan's Drive Supermarket or Big Willie's grocery store. This last store also sells liquor and is a launderette!

Last updated September 2016.

Bimini Sands Resort & Marina
P.O. Box 21, South Bimini
Tel:+1 (242) 347 3500 Fax:+1 242 347 3501 ,VHF Channel 68
http://www.thebiminisands.com/?page_id=1049 , reservations@southbiminisands.com
A resort complex situated about halfway along the west coast of South Bimini.
Depth 8 ft. Water/ice, electricity, fuel, showers, laundry, WiFi, b-b-q grill, swimming pool, restaurant and bars, gift shop. Limited marine supplies. Accommodation.
It is a Port of Entry and the Customs office is within the marina.
