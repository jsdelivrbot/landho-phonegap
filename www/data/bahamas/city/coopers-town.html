Coopers Town - Profile

Location

Located in the Central Abacos.
This is a good place to stock up with several grocery stores and a few restaurants as well. There is a Shell fuel dock and an Esso dock further south. In case of an emergency there is a small hospital here too.

Position 26°53’N, 077°30’W

Docking

Approach is simple and straightforward. If weather permits you can throw a line to one of the docks, but beware it shallows quickly. If the wind is less favourable you can drop anchor in sand and dinghy ashore.

This is an open anchorage and the holding is good. However, it can get very choppy and uncomfortable here in a matter of minutes. Always watch the weather.

Nearby, Powell Cay is a good anchorage to seek shelter.
