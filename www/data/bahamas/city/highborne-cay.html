Docking

This is a private island and only visitors to the marina (in the south) are allowed to walk about the island. Crews from anchored yachts can come to the dinghy dock or to get fuel and are charged for garbage disposal.

The bay shelters you from anything but the N, but any westerly component makes it uncomfortable and rolly in here. The bottom is sand and very good holding.

Last updated December 2008.

A small marina on the south side with 16 slips. Often full so call ahead to reserve a slip. As a visitor in the marina, you are able to walk around the island. Prices are high. Electricity, water, fuel, WiFi, b-b-q area and grocery store. Showers and laundry charged at extra. The beach is plentiful with nurse sharks.
