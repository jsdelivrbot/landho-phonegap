Morgans Bluff - Profile

Location

Morgan's Bluff at the north end of North Andros, just north of Nicholls Town. Not much goes on here except for the regatta held in June. This is when it really comes alive - for one wild weekend!

Position 25° 10'29"N, 78° 01'45"W (entrance)

Clearance

Review main Bahamas Clearance information.

This is a port of entry.

Docking

The entrance is wide and deep. The water tankers, which used to go in and out every day, are no longer needed and have ceased operating.

Once you’re in the bay it stays quite deep (4m) up to a certain point.

The anchorage is good holding in sand and grass and protection good except from the north.

The government dock is off-limits to private boats.

Shalom is the unofficial harbourmaster and loves to help with anything a cruiser may need.

The bar on the inner harbour is open afternoons on and has WiFi when open.

There is fuel and ice here.

Last updated February 2012.
