Fresh Creek (Andros Town) - Profile

Location

Located on East coast of Northern Andros, it is not much visited by tourists.

Position 24º 44'04" N, 77º 45' 60" W (pass through the reef)

Clearance

Clearance is possible here.

See the main Bahamas Clearance page for information.

Last updated August 2016.

Docking

Fresh Creek has the only marina on Andros and limited docking and anchorage space. The marina is to port, before the bridge over the creek.

Coakley Town is on the northern bank.

Beware of the fast tides in the channel.

Last updated September 2016.
