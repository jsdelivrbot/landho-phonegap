Schooner Bay - Profile

Location

Located at the southern end of Great Abaco Island. This is new development and provides a useful harbour.

Position 26°09'53"N, 77° 10'33"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

This sheltered harbour provides slips, fuel, provisions and other services. It is part of a new village being built here.

Last updated April 2015.
