Congo Town - Profile

Location

Congo Town (or Driggs Hill) lies on the east coast of South Andros. It is rarely visited by yachtsmen.

Position 24° 12'51"N, 77° 35'58"W.

Clearance

Review main Bahamas Clearance information.

Congo Town is a port of entry.

You can clear in here at the Customs and Immigration of Congo International Airport.

Last updated January 2015.

Customs
Tel:+242 369 2640

Docking

Anchor on the West side of the basin in 4m sand, do not hinder the freighters. It’s not particularly nice, but a very good shelter if you need one, and there’s the commercial airport nearby.

There are several anchorages around the cays at the northern end of the island.

Last updated January 2015.
