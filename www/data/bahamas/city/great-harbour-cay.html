Great Harbour Cay - Profile

Location

Great harbour Cay lies at the northern end of the Berry Island chain. It is the largest island in the group. It also has the only harbour at this northern end of the Berry Islands.

Position 25° 44'50"N, 77° 52'06"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

Careful navigation is required to reach the settlement of Bullocks Harbour on Great Harbour Cay.

The marina is well protected, tucked in the south east corner of the harbour.

From the harbour entrance, channel is well-marked to the entrance of the cut which leads to the outer basin, where the fuel dock is located. Follow the markers to the inner part of the marina (remember - red to sta'bd). Channel depth is around 2.5m at mean low tide.
