Chub Cay - Profile

Location

Chubb Cay lies at the western end of the Berry Islands chain, and north west of Andros. It is a useful port of call.

Position 25° 24'38"N, 77° 54'37"W (marina entrance)

Clearance

Review main Bahamas Clearance information.

Chub Cay is a private club but yachts are welcome to visit and use the clearance facilities.

Customs and Immigration are located at the airstrip.

Last updated September 2016.

Docking

The channel to the marina is dredged and well marked and lit.

There is space for anchoring just in front of the beach. It is pretty shallow with 1.6m (LW) and as it is sand over rock, the holding isn’t particularly good.

Although the marina is bankrupt, it is still open and yachts can berth here. See recent comments below from cruisers who have visited.

Last updated April 2016.
