Fox Town (Little Abaco Island) - Profile

Location

Located at the western end of Little Abaco Island, this is described as a quaint, friendly little town. It merges into the settlement of Crown Haven on the southern side of the island.

Position 26° 54'23"N, 077° 49'03"W (Crown Haven)

Docking

This is quite a shallow anchorage close to the shore (about 1.5 to 2m over sand) but the town has provisions, fuel, a clinic, restaurants and other services.

There is a dinghy landing at the government pier and at Fox Town Shell fuel dock.

Last updated April 2015.
