Olbia - Profile

Location

Olbia is located on the Costa Smeralda on a beautiful stretch of the NE coast. It is a popular holiday destination, having both a wild and rocky coastline and beautiful sandy beaches.

Position 40°55′N, 09°30′E.

Clearance

Port Authority

Docking

Yachts can use the (disused) ferry quay for free for one night in the harbour (no electricity or water).

Anchoring in the harbour is also free.

There are also several marinas in the vicinity including the newer Marina di Olbia, at the SE end of the harbour.

Previously there have been incidents of anchored  boats being broken into. It may be advisable to leave someone on board if leaving the boat during the evening. Yachts moored against the wall were not affected.

Cruisers also report anchoring comfortably and for free in the large bay to the south of Porto Romano (to port as you approaching Oblia).

Last updated October 2016.
