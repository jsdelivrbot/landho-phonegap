Porto Santa Margherita (Caorle) - Profile

Location

Located about 25nm NE of the entrance to the Venitian Lagoon, this holiday complex is based around the traditional fishing port of Caorle.

Position 45°35.2'N, 12°52'E.

Docking

For Marina 4, turn to port through the lock and follow the channel right to the end. Use your fog horn to request the foot bridge to be opened, if it is closed. Visitors' berths are opposite the fuel berth.

For Consorzio Darsena Orologio, go further up stream and turn to sta'bd.

Complete shelter here.
