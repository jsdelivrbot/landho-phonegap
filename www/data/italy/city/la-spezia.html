Docking

The Gulfo La Spezia provides a wide range of mooring and anchoring options.

If approaching at night, be careful of the many mooring buoys located inside the Gulf of La Spezia embankment.

At the head of the gulf, at Porto La Spezia, visitors berth to the north quay, which is on piles, allowing a continuous swell from the ferries and commercial boats to affect the yachts moored here.

The Porto Turistico Mirabello is the new marina just SW of the quay.

Inside the embankment, Porto Lotti lies on the east bank and marina di Fezzano on the western side.

La Grazie harbour is close to the western pass of the embankment and Muggiano opposite the eastern pass.
