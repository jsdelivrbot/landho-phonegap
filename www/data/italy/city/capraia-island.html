Capraia Island - Profile

Location

Not to be confused with Capri!. This small island lies east of the northern tip of Corsica and NW of Elba. It is part of the Arcipelago Toscano National Park. Apart from the port area, this mountainous island is uninhabited.

Docking

In addition to berths in the port, there are 20 new visitors' buoys just outside this popular destination.

It is also possible to anchor here.

Shelter is good except from the east.

Last updated June 2011.
