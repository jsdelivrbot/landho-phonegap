A sheltered marina with all the usual services, but the entrance can be difficult in strong north westerly winds as it is both narrow and shallow. 700 berths, 60 visitors berths. Mooring in "boxes" which provides good protection from sea and other boats. Fuel, water, electricity, 50ton travel hoist, slipway, repairs, chandlery, provisions, Post office, bank, pharmacy and yacht club.
