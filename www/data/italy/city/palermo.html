Palermo - Profile

Location

This is Sicily's capital, and is a busy port situated on the north-western coast of the island. Its large natural harbour has been a trading centre for thousands of years and the many different cultures that have occupied it have left a facinating legacy.

Position 38° 8.35' N, 13° 22.2' E. (marina at Acquasanta).

Docking

Acquasanta is a modern yacht harbour for visitors, just north of the two commercial harbour entrances.

There is a yacht harbour (Cala) in the southern corner of the commercial harbour. There are six different organisations providing moorings here on floating pontoons.
It is reported that security of both people and boats here can also be rather doubtful and extra care is advised. Also there may be a problem with rats coming onboard.

The fuel station also has a few mooring moorings (about 40 euro/night) with water and electricity.

On the northern edge of the city is the small port of Arenella, the old part of which is used by fishing boats.
