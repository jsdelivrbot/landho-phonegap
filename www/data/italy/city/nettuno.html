Nettuno - Profile

Location

This attractive town is about 30nm south of Fiumicino, the port for Rome. It is a popular tourist destination and has a well-preserved old town, the Borgo Medievale. This area has mediaeval streets, small, attractive squares and a 15C Fort.

Position 41° 27'04"N, 012° 39'33"W (marina entrance)

Docking

photo from www.marinanettuno.com

The large marina here is composed of several basins. It is well protected by breakwaters.

Contact by VHF prior to entry. Advisable to reserve a berth in advance.
