Porto Cetrero - Profile

Location

A useful port of call along this part of the southern Italian coast. The old town of Cetrero lies about 2 miles south of the port and is up the hill from the coast.

Position 39°30′N, 15°57′E.

Docking

The port is about 3kn north of the town and the berthing cost in the marina reasonable, especially since they offer free use of an electric car for provisioning (need to book it).

Last updated August 2014.
