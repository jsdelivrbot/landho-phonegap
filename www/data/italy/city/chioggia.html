Chioggia - Profile

Location

Chioggia is a long narrow island, forming part of the chain of shallows and islands separating the Venitian lagoon from the Adriatic itself. It is a popular tourist destination.

Position 45°13.4'N, 12°17.7'E.

Docking

The channel to the marinaa and public berths from the southern entrance into the Venitian lagoon, is marked.

The main options for berthing here is in Canaletta Morin (Sporting Club Marina di Chioggia) or in Darsena Mosella at Sotomarina.

Shelter is good in most berths.

Last updated March 2010.
