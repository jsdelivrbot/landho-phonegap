Vibo Valentia - Profile

Location

Situated on the shores of the Tyrrenhian Sea at the toe of Italy, this delightful town has a marina and a friendly atmosphere.

Docking

The Marina Stella del Sud is a small family owned marina situated in the middle of this pleasent little town. As long as you don't require a lot of other cruisers for companionship, this would be an ideal place to spend the winter.
