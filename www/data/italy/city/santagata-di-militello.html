Sant'Agata di Militello - Profile

Location

This large harbour lies on the northern coast of Sicily, about 80nm east of Palermo. It is a useful departure point for the Aeolian Islands.

Position 38° 04'32"N, 14° 37'38"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

There is a small marina in the north west corner of the harbour, which is safe to enter in all conditions.

Last updated June 2016.
