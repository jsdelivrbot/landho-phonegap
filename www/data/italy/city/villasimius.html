Location

A very useful marina on Sardinia for a passage to or from Sicily.

Located on the Eastern side of the bay of Cagliari. Slightly open to the north, a relatively small fetch but some swell can make its way in.

Pretty, but reported to be quite unfriendly. The high cost for overnight berthing coupled with the poor facilities make this a useful place a quick rest, stopover or shelter only. The marina is a long walk from anything.

Clearance

It is necessary to complete all formalities at this marina.

Docking

Cruisers that visited this facility in April 2012 found most marina buildings to be closed, either permanently or seasonal.

If anchoring, be aware that the holding near the marina is not good.

Last updated February 2014.
