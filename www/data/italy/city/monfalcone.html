Monfalcone - Profile

Location

This is a busy commercial and industial port tucked into the northern corner of Golfo di Trieste. The commercial part of the harbour is not for yachts but there is an area west of the main port, Bacino di Panzano, which has a marina and also a yacht club, in pleasent surroundings.

Position 45°47'N, 13°33'E.

Docking

Use the main port entrance and turn to port through Bacino di Panzano and follow the channel through to the marina.

Berth in the maina as directed.

An alternative is the harbour at Sistiana, about 5nm SE of Monfalcone. Here you berth on the quay in the SE corner.

Good shelter in the marina.

Last updated March 2010.
