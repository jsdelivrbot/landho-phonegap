Mazara del Vallo - Profile

Location

A good stopover en-route to Sardinia (although not as close as Trapani), Mazara is the largest fishing port in Italy. It is home to the statue of the Dancing Satir (important Hellenistic sculpture found by a fisherman in 1998) and displayed in a church close to the marina.

Position 37° 38'30"N, 12° 35'15"E (port entrance)

Docking

If you have problems contacting the marina, call the Harbour Master on VHF Ch. 13 who will arrange for a marinero to assist with tying up, day and night.

There are 2 marinas here, Lega Navale Italiana and Adina Marina. Both have 24hr security.

No English spoken here (except for the Harbour Master), but locals reported to be very friendly.
