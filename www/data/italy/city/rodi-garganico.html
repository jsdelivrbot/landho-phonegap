Rodi Garganico - Profile

Location

Situated 200km up the coast from Bari on the East Coast of Italy, this is reportedly the closest marina to Croatia. Brand New in August 2009, there was still considerable construction work to complete on opening.

Position 41° 55' 53" N, 15° 53' 20" E.
