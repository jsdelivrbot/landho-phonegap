Rimini - Profile

Location

This large city is a fashionable holiday resort and has all the facilities of a tourist destination.

Position 44°04.6'N, 12°34.5'E.

Clearance

All officials are here. The Harbour office is on the E quay.

Docking

A berth may be found in the river but it tends to be crowded and can also be affected by the sudden flood which can happen here.

The marina is large and offers a more comfortable berth. Moor where directed.

Good shelter in the marina and in the inner part of the harbour.

Last updated March 2010.
