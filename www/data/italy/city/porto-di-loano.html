Porto di Loano - Profile

Location

This interesting port lies 50nm SW of Genoa.

Position 44° 7'N, 8° 15'E.

Docking

The port here has now been developed as a marina and provides good shelter. It is about a 5 minute walk from the centre of the town.

Last updated November 2011.
