Alghero - Profile

Location

This is a useful port, on the northern end of the west coast. The harbour is in the heart of the city, so all services are close by. The area NW of the town is well-known for its caves, both above and under the water.

Position 40° 33.5' N, 8° 18.5' E.

Clearance

Port Authorities
Tel:+39 079 953 174

Docking

Beware of a badly lit fish farm in the approaches.

There is usually a reception boat at the harbour entrance which will guide yachts to an appropriate berth.

There is a huge number of berths here, both transit and longer term moorings are available.

Berths on the town quay, in the northeastern part of the port, are all equipped with tailed moorings, water and electricity. Mooring here is free of charge for up to 5 days.

The newest marina, Marina Di S. Elmo, is in the quieter area on the southern side of the port.

Visitors' berths can also be found at the Banchina Dogana and the Banchina Sanità which are also in the southern part of the harbour.

Fertilia, just north of Alghero, offers very good shelter and an ecomonic place to over-winter.

Last updated October 2014.
