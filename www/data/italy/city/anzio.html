Anzio - Profile

Location

A very pleasant town about 30nm south east of Fiumicino (Rome). Home to a sizable fishing fleet.

Position 41° 26.8'N,  012° 38.3'E.

Clearance

The authorities are likely to visit the boat even if you are at anchor and request to see your paperwork. They are friendly and helpful.

Last updated February 2014.

Docking

This harbour may be subject to silting, so take care at the entrance if there is any swell.

Currently, a better option is the marina at Nettuno about a mile to the east. However, there are plans to install marina pontoons in the harbour.

There is a good anchorage just outside the harbour in 8 ft over sand. The yacht club, located at the north corner of the harbour past the inner break wall, is happy to allow you to tie the dinghy up there. Do not anchor in the channel.

A fish farm off Capo D'Anzio has been established. It is marked by four yellow buoys and forms a serious hazard to yachts visiting this coast.

Last updated January 2016.

Within the port area you can access to dry dock area equipped with travel lift, ramp and various technical services
