Fano - Profile

Location

Located on Italy's Adriatic coast, this fishing port and holiday town lies approximately halfway between Rimini and Anconia.

Position 43°51'N, 30°01'E.

Docking

Berth in the marina. The rest of the harbour is too crowded to offer any moorings.

Good shelter.

Last updated March 2010.
