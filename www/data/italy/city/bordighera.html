Bordighera - Profile

Location

This town is just 15nm east of the border with France and is known for its mild climate. It is popular with artists and is known as the City of Flowers as the surrounding area cultivates them.

Position 43° 46'52"N, 007° 40'41"W (marina entrance)

Docking

This harbour is often very crowded, so it it possible there may not be room. There are plans to extend it.

The berths at the entrance (where visitors are most likely to be) can be subject to swell.

There is good provisioning in the town and also restaurants near the port.

Last updated January 2016.
