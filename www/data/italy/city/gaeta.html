Gaeta - Profile

Location

Located in Gulf of Gaeta, 50 nm north of Naples, this is an attractive town of narrow winding streets, surrounded by green hills and mountains. It is situated about halfway between Rome and Naples. It has a US navy base as well as prosperous fishing and tourist industries.

Position 41° 12.83’ N, 13° 34.53’ E.

Clearance

Port Police and Customs are based in Gaeta.

Last updated February 2016.

Docking

Care needs to be taken approaching the marinas in this bay due to the large number of unlit mooring buoys. Also beware of the mussel beds.

Take care to keep clear of the military installations if berthing at the Flavio Gioia marina.

An alternative berth is the marina at Formia, about 3nm NE across the bay from Gaeta.

There is an anchorage in the bay just north of the fishing boat pier.

Last updated February 2016.
