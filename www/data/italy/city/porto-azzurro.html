Location

Porto Azzurro is situated in a deep indentation on the eastern side of the island. It is an attractive place to visit.

Clearance

This is the only port of entry on the Island of Elba.

Docking

Berthing is in the port. Check which quay to use as one is cheaper than the other. The harbour can smell of sewage.

Anchoring outside the harbour in the bay is possible and free. There is a good anchorage just outside the main harbour, to the SE of the Western point. It has good holding and is an easy ride into the port.

There are also many sheltered bays on both the north and south coasts which are worth visiting.

The harbour itself has two quays, one is operated by the village adminstration and is cheaper, the other one adjacent is private and is very expensive (100 euros/night for 12m).
