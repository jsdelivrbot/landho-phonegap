Le Castella - Profile

Location

Located in the province of Crotone on the Ionian coast of Calabria, this is a useful haven in bad weather. It lies approx. 55 miles NE of Rocella Ionica and 15 miles SW of Crotone.

The fortress here dating back five centuries was fully restored in 2001 and is well worth a visit (picture taken by Peter Ter Haar).

Docking

The harbour has a southern basin reserved for fishing boats and a northern basin for leisure craft (see photo left taken by Peter Ter Haar, entrance to the right of picture). It is well maintained and run by local government.

There is no longer a sluice gate in the eastern part of the yacht basin. This closed 8 years ago. Cruisers visiting in 2013 report that depth is a minimum of 3 metres. Shelter is good.

Fees are based on the width of your boat (e.g. 50€/night for a 3.95 metre wide boat). This scale runs from 3.61 up to 4.5 metres regardless of boat length. There is one place for a 17 meter boat, alongside the former day trip boat berth.

The northern basin has water and electricity. There is a guard / marinero and security cameras. This basin is very well lit both night and day.

Yachts are permitted in the southern basin, but only when the fishing boats are out and the pier is fully occupied in the evening with fishing boats.

There is a good supermarket 200 metres from the harbour and a small chandlery close by.
