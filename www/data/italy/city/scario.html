Scario - Profile

Location

This small port is tucked into the north west coast of the Gulf of Policastero. It is in the Cilento National Park and is a popular summer holiday destination.

Position 40°3′9.83″N, 15°29′28.75″E.

Docking

It is also possible, in settled weather, to anchor south of the main breakwater. Holding is good in the sand.

Last updated August 2014.
