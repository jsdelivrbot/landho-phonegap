Milazzo - Profile

Location

A useful port of call on Sicily's northern coast, opposite the Aeolian Islands. It is dominated by a huge medieval fortress. Whilst rather industrial, Milazzo has three marinas with plenty of berthing and facilities.

Docking

The main port/commercial harbour is exposed to the SE.

Marina del Nettuno lies in the NW corner of the commercial harbour, tucked up right in the top corner. Marina Poseidon and Porto Santa Maria Maggiore are both situated off the long sandy coast approx. a mile to the north of the port entrance, Porto Santa being the marina farthest away from the commercial harbour.
