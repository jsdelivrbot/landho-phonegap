San Remo - Profile

Location

San Remo is about 20 miles east of Monaco, on the Cote d' Azur. The Old Town of San Remo is very interesting with building from the Roman period onwards.

Clearance

All the authorities are at Marina Porto Sole.

Docking

The marina of Porto Sole is very well protected and has every modern facility. The old harbour, Porto Vecchio, is more crowded but has a wonderful Italian atmosphere.

Ten miles further east is the port of Maurizio. A new marina is being built here, but the existing port provides good shelter and services. It is also an ancient town with old streets and little winding alleys.
