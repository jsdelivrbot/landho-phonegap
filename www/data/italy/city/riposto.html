Riposto - Profile

Location

Riposto is located on the East coast of Sicily, 30 km north of Catania, and is the only good stopping place as you pass through the Straits of Messina.

The town has several notable churches and monuments. Mt. Etna forms a striking backdrop to the port. This mountain is the highest active volcano in Europe (3,320 m) and is also one of the most active in the world.

The Etna dry red and white wines are produced in the vineyard area around here.

Position 37°44'N, 15°12.7'E.
