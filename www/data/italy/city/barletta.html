Barletta - Profile

Location

The old part of this town is a very attractive medieval fishing port. It has now expanded into a large modern city on the landward side and is Iocated just south of the promontory of Gargano.

Unfortunately however, it now appears this port is closed to leisure boats (see comment at bottom of page - September 2015).

Position 41°19.9'N, 16°17.5'E.

Clearance

The Harbourmaster and Customs have their offices and the base of the western mole.

The Police office is in the town.

Docking

Shelter here is very good, and whilst there are restrictions as to where leisure vessels can go (see comment at bottom of page), it is still possible to anchor here with some shelter.

Last updated September 2015.
