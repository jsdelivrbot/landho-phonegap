Genoa - Profile

Location

This large, busy city is the provincial capital of the region, and the main commercial port for Italy. It also has a thriving shipbuilding industry. The Old Port area, though, has retained its charm amid the urban sprawl of the modern metropolis.

Position 44°24.6’ N 08°55.5’ E.

Clearance

All the clearance services are here.

Docking

There is a very large choice of berths here. Most are close to the Old Port area which has been revitalised and provides all the shops and services required.

Last updated February 2016.
