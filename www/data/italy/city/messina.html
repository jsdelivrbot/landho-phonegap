Messina - Profile

Location

An attractive stop on the extreme eastern end of the island. The modern marina has greatly improved the yacht facilities at Messina.

Position 38° 12'53"N, 15°14'57"E (port entrance)

Clearance

See Italy/Formalities section for details.

This is a Port of Entry.

Last updated August 2016.

Docking

The visitors' berths are to port once through the entrance in the outer breakwater. Beware of shallow water on the port side of the entrance.

It is essential to contact Messina Marina on VHF before entering.

This port is popular with cruise ships and marina berths do suffer from the wash/surge caused by their props. Tie your boat up with care.

Good passage port, but not for winter lay up.

Anchoring
Note that no anchoring is permitted in the Strait of Messina. If anchoring in case of emergency, be sure to report where you drop the hook to avoid large fines. Read the VTS Messina Strait User Manual for more information.

Last updated September 2016.
