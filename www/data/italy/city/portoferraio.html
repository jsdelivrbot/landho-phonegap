Location

Elba is a most attractive island, just 15 miles long. It lies 5 miles off the Italian mainland and 30 miles east of Corsica.

Portoferraio is on the northern side of the island.

Docking

There is a sheltered marina here - visitors berth on the first pontoon inside the entrance. It is also possible to berth alongside the harbour wall in the port, which also has water and electricity.
