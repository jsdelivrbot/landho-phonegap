Ventimiglia (Vintmille) - Profile

Location

This ancient town is just 5 miles from the French border, in the Gulf of Genoa, on the river Roia. It has the remains of a Roman theatre as well as many other interesting buildings. The medieval city centre is on a hill overlooking the modern one.

Position 43°48.4' N, 7°35.5'E.

Docking

The harbour lies at the entrance of the river which divides the town.
