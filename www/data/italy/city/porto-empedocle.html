Porto Empedocle - Profile

Location

Port Empedocle is a small town but an important fishing port. It lies on the south west coast of Sicily.

Position 37° 16' 50"N, 13° 31' 39"E.

Docking

Yachts can berth in the small marina, which is conveniently away from the busy ferry quays and offers good all-round shelter.

There is also an anchorage near the beach wall, however be wary of anchoring as the coast guard have been known to levy hefty fines for anchoring here (see comment at bottom of page).

Just 3nm south of the port is the harbour of San Leone, where there is a larger marina with very helpful staff.

Last updated August 2014.
