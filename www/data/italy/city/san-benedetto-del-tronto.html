San Benedetto del Tronto - Profile

Location

This is an attractive holiday town on the Adriatic coast about 50nm north of Pescara.

Position 42° 57.5'N, 13° 53.5'E.

Docking

The harbour is usually quite crowded with fishing boats but now there is a new marina in the southern part of the harbour.

The sailing club also has a few berths in the SW part of the port.

Berth in the marina as directed.

The shelter is good except in strong N or NE winds which send a swell into the harbour.

Last updated March 2010.
