Savona - Profile

Location

This town is rather industrialised, and its port has commercial docks, but it does have some interesting buildings in its historic centre.

Docking

The port of Savona has an easy approach and is very sheltered.

The bridge across the entrance to Darse Vecchia opens for 5 minutes each hour at H+ 30 (Mon - Sat), H+15 and H+45 (Sun and Hols) and midnight to 6 am, it is left open.
