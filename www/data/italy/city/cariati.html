Cariati - Profile

Location

Lying on Italy's southern coast, on the western side of the Golfo di Taranto, this quiet and picturesque town makes a good alternative to Crotone, which lies about 50nm to the south. The town, which is on the hill above the marina,  is still completely surrounded by the original medieval walls.

Position 39°30'N, 16° 57'E.

Docking

This marina is very well sheltered from SE and NW, with no swell in winds from either direction. Keep close to N breakwater for a least depth of 3.5m.

Last updated September 2015.
