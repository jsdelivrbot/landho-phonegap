Porto San Giorgio - Profile

Location

This is a fishing harbour about 25nm south of Ancona which now has a marina.

Position 43°10.2'N, 13°48.5'E.

Docking

Call the marina on VHF channel 14 on approach for instructions on where to berth.

There is complete shelter in the marina.

Last updated March 2010.
