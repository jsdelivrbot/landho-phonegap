Porto Cervo - Profile

Location

Situated on the NE corner, Porto Cervo is one of those ports where the super-yachts come to see and be seen! Consequently, it is not a cheap place to stay. It is an attractive town with narrow streets and interesting shops.

Clearance

Port Authorities
Tel:+39 0789 94498

Docking

There are mooring buoys as well as a marina here, but beware of the cost (for a 10-12m boat - €105/night on a mooring and €400.00 in the marina).

This is also a useful place for repair work (if you can afford to stay!).

Last updated February 2014.
