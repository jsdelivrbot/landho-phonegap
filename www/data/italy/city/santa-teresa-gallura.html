Santa Teresa Gallura - Profile

Location

This harbour is on the most northern tip of Sardinia, just 11 miles from the southern coast of Corsica. Sited on a long narrow bay which is fringed by attractive coves.

Position 41° 14'42"N, 09° 11'50"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

This port is a very reasonably priced place to stay. Contact the port captain on VHF channel 12 before entering.

This is a very well protected harbour and is well lit for night entry.

Last updated February 2012.
