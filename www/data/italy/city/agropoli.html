Agropoli - Profile

Location

A delightful town with helpful, friendly people.

This is a good place from which to visit the remains of the city of Paestum, a major ancient Greek city.

Position 40° 21'N, 014° 58'W

Docking

Photo courtesy of Berthold Notthoff - May 2015.

Visitors berth on pontoons at the SW end of the harbour.

The free 48 hour tie up is on the right just inside the breakwater, south of the club at the end. There is room for about 5 boats. If you are very early in the season you may be able to stay longer.

Most of the pontoons have different operators.

The bottom is mud. The harbour is well sheltered but is open to the NE.

Last updated January 2016.
