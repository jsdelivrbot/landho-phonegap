Gela - Profile

Location

This port lies on the south western coast of Sicily. It is principally a Port of Refuge.

The town has a large number of interesting ancient archaeological sites, but otherwise, is not a particularly attractive port of call. The oil refinery, built here after WW2, and which disfigures the local landscape, has now been closed.

Position 37° 3' 48"N, 14° 13' 47"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

The port is often full of local boats.  There are 3 floating pontoons and Pontoon no.1 is designated for public mooring.

There is a fuel berth in the port, as well as toilets and showers.

There is also a 45 ton mobile crane, slipway and a good range of repairs.

The town has a bank, post office, provision shops, some medical facilities and pharmacy.

Last updated August 2016.
