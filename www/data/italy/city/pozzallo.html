Location

Located halfway along the short southern coast of Sicily, this is another useful port.

Docking

The harbour is reasonably protected except from the north. Beware of the sandbanks. Maximun depth 2.5m.

The port provides water, electricity, slipway, crane, fuel berth, a boatyard and some repairs.

The town has a post office (but no bank), pharmacy, provisions, bars and restaurants.
