Laguana di Mariano - Profile

Location

This is a large lagoon lying about 30nm east of Venice and is similar to the Venitian lagoon being being an area of relatively shallow water interpersed with many islands. The resort of Lignano Sabbiadoro lies at the western end and Grado and the eastern end of the string of islands which protect the lagoon from the Adriatic.

There  are several marinas at western entrance to the lagoon where there is a dredged canal which leads to Marino Lagunare. Further east there is another canal, Canale di Portobuso which leads to Marina Sant'Andrea.

At the eastern side is tourist destination of Grado.
