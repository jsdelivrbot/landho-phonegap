Numana - Profile

Location

Located 10nm south of Ancona, this small port sits at the base of a cliff, on top of which is the main village.

Position 43°30.7'N, 13°37.5'E.

Docking

The harbour must be entered using the southern entrance.

Moor in the marina as directed.

This port is sheltered, but in strong winds with any east in them, as swell will penetrate the harbour.

Last updated March 2010.

Marina Numana
All facilities, fuel berth.
