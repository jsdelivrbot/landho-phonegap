Naples - Profile

Location

Naples is the largest city in southern Italy and has a lot to offer travellers interested in history. Security can be a problem here, though.

Position 40° 40'N, 14° 09'E (entrance to the bay)

Docking

There are several marinas to choose from, although not all of them offer all facilities and good security. However, the major problem is finding a free berth, especially during the high season.

There are two marinas on the Isla Procida, 10NM west of Naples, from which there is a ferry to Naples, and another at Pozzuoli on the mainland. This is a similar distance (by sea) west of the city.

A new, large marina (Marina di Pinetamare) is under construction at a site a few miles north west of Naples. Work is still in progress Jan 2016.

Anchoring in the Bay of Naples tends to be uncomfortable due to the frequency and speed of boat traffic.

Last updated January 2016.
