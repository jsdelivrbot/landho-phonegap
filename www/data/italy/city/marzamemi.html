Marzamemi - Profile

Location

This attractive fishing town lies on the south east corner of Sicily. It is a popular destination for day trippers and has some interesting buildings.

Position 36° 43’59' N, 15°07’53' E (harbour entrance)

Docking

Photo from Sydney Stapleton

On approach, beware of the number of fish farms, they are not easily seen at night.

The harbour is well sheltered but can be subject to swell in strong easterlies.

There are two marinas in the harbour. If requested, either one will send a boat out to guide you to a berth. Just ensure you are clear which one you are talking to (they both listen on VHF channels 06 and 09).

Provisions and fuel are not easily obtained here.

There are plenty of restaurants.

Last updated June 2016.
