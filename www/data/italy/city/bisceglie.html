Bisceglie - Profile

Location

This is one of those town which hides it charms until you venture beyond the waterfront. It is also backed by a modern developement which provides all city services.

Position 41°14'47"N, 16°30'39"E.

Clearance

The Harbourmaster is based in the market building on the SE side of the harbour.

The Customs office is on the first floor overlooking the SW part of the harbour and the Police are in the town.

Docking

This harbour is not safe to enter in strong onshore winds which cause breaking seas at the entrance.

Good shelter, but some swell enters in strong east and north east winds.

Berth in the marina in the southern part of the harbour. Visitors are made very welcome here.

It is possible to anchor close north of the marina in about 2m.

Last updated July 2010.
