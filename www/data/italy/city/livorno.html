Livorno - Profile

Location

In spite being Italy's second largest port and a busy cruise ship terminal, this town has many attractions. Its old city with it maze of canals is not to be missed. In addition, it is an ideal stopver if planning to visit Florence.

Position 43°18.1’ N, 10°29.1’ E.

Docking

The principal berthing for yachts is in the Porto Mediceo in the heart of the town.

The is an anchorage NE of the southern breakwater, off Porticolo Nazano Saura in about 2 - 3 m. Suitable only in settled weather. The marina of darsena Toscana is also here, but it is not known if they accept visitors.

A quieter alternative is the modern marina at Rosignana, south of Livorno.
