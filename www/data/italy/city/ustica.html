Ustica - Profile

Location

Ustica is small island lying between Sicily and Sardinia, approximately 35NM north of Palermo (between which a regular ferry service runs).

The island is a marine park and a popular diving destination.

The harbour on the north east side of the island gives good shelter from the prevailing westerly winds.

Position: 38° 43′ 0″ N, 13° 11′ 0″ E

Docking

There is room for between 5-7 yachts on the concrete wall with laid moorings, however the berthing administrator will most likely shout at you to "use your anchor". The bottom is good holding in sand.

The berthing administrator also runs the closest shop. He is reported to be very friendly (limited English) and will help you with your lines, rents out scooters, has a dive shop etc.

A small square is walking distance from the harbour with restaurants and bars. There are a few local shops but better provisioning a taxi/car ride away.

There is a fuel dock opposite the berths although check depths as it is reported to be shallow here.
