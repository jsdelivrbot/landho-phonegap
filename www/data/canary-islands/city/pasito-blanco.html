Pasito Blanco - Profile

Location

This is a holiday resort on the south coast of the island, approximately halfway between Arguineguin and Maspalomas.

Position 27° 44'47"N, 15° 37'14"W (marina)

Docking

The approaches and entrance to the marina are straightforward and Pasito Blanco Marina (Club de Yates de Pasito Blanco) keeps a 24-hour listening watch on VHF channel 6. On arrival, the skipper should report to the port office during office hours.

Water and electricity is included in the charges. There are showers and toilets on the outer wharf and showers are also available in the boatyard. Long term rates being considerable lower than the daily rate and there is a 30% discount for stays over 6 months. The marina can provide a caretaker service for yachts laid-up afloat.

The marina is fairly isolated, which for some people is its main attraction. The gate into the resort is guarded 24 hours a day, making it a good place to leave a boat unattended for long periods. There are now floating pontoons (with cleats) around most of the quay wall allowing stern lines to rise and fall with the tide (2m tidal range).

Last updated August 2016.
