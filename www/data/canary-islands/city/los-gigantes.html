Los Gigantes - Profile

Location

Position 28° 14'57"N, 16° 50'28"W (marina)

Clearance

The marina monitors VHF channel 9 permanently and skippers are advised to call up the harbour before entering. The port office is in the control tower on the port side as one enters, at the end of the inner mole by the reception dock. Yachts can clear in during office hours, 0800-1900 seven days a week, all formalities being completed in the control tower. Outside of hours, yachts should wait at the reception dock.

Docking

Puerto de Los Gigantes is dwarfed by the sheer cliffs of Los Gigantes.

Beware of constantly moving sands inside the harbour. For this reason it is not advisable to enter outside office hours as they are then unable to advise you on a safe approach to the visitors' berths.
