Puerto Carmen - Profile

Location

Position 28° 55'N, 13° 40.4'W

Docking

The port is busy with excursion boats and with the remaining space largely being taken up by local boats on their own moorings, it is best avoided by yachts.

In settled weather it is possible to anchor just outside the breakwater and take the dinghy inside if wishing to visit the resort.

There are no yachting facilities but the main road running through the huge holiday resort of Puerto del Carmen has many supermarkets, banks, bars and restaurants.
