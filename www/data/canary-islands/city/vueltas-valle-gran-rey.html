Vueltas (Valle Gran Rey) - Profile

Location

The small town on the west coast of La Gomera is called Valle Gran Rey but, confusingly, its port is called Vueltas.

Position 28° 04'40"N, 17° 19'46"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

Due to a large breakwater,  the old commercial port now has space for visiting yachts.

It is also possible to anchor in the bay south of the harbour in 8 to 12m, in sand. Open to the west and subject to gusty winds caused by the high cliffs.

The port office is in the building by the harbour gates (Tel. 922 805476). There is water on the quay, also showers in the Cofradia building.

Last updated 2007.
