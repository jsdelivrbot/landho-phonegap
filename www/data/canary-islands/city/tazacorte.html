Tazacorte - Profile

Location

The quiet town of Tazacorte is situated on the west side of La Palma and claims to be the warmest place on the island. Puerto de Tazacorte is the fishing port and has a long promenade, a black sand beach, narrow streets lined with colourful old houses and many bars and seafood restaurants.

Tazacorte village is about 1km away, and is the main centre with a town hall, post office, shops and supermarket.

Position 28º 38.3'N, 17º 56.3'W.

Docking

The harbour is now protected by a large, new breakwater making the entrance much safer, even with a large westerly swell. This breakwater also minimises any the swell entering the marina.

There is now a modern marina inside this harbour.

When closing with the harbour beware of the extensive fish farms to the NW of the entrance.

On arrival, report to the port office.

Last updated October 2011.
