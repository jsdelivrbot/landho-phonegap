Puerto de las Nieves - Profile

Location

A busy ferry port on the NW coast of Gran Canaria.

Position 28° 05'51"N, 15° 42'38"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

There are three pontoons, most of them occupied by local craft. Visiting yachts may be able to find a space alongside the main quay.

Friendly harbourmaster. No showers. Electricity and water on the wharf.

Toilets at the ferry terminal. WiFi also possible here.

Anchorage outside the harbour when the wind is in the eastern quarter.

Last updated March 2011.
