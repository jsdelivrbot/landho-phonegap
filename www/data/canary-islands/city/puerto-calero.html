Puerto Calero - Profile

Location

Puerto Calero is situated just SW of Puerto del Carmen on Lanzarote's south east coast.

A preferred base in the Canaries for leaving boats unattended, standards are high and the marina is well-kept and maintained. Despite many restaurants and a fully-equipped yard, the area is quiet and relaxed.

The airport is 15 minutes drive from the marina and car-hire, taxis, bike-hire and a bus stop can all be found on site. There is a market on twice a week and Endeavour Sailing, the local RYA centre, offers many courses for cruisers.

Discounts are offered for sailing visitors in Cabrera Medina car hire; the well-equipped Mc2 Action gym and Amura restaurant. Mention “Noonsite” for a 10% discount off short stay berthing.

Position 28º 54'55"N, 13º 42'25"W.

Clearance

Ships papers, passports and yacht insurance must be produced when checking in.

Docking

There is a full service marina here and arriving yachts should tie up at the reception dock, located to port upon entry, opposite the control tower.

Security is very good and this is a good place to leave a yacht unattended.

Last updated April 2016.
