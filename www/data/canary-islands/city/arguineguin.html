Arguineguin - Profile

Location

A fishing harbour on the south coast of Gran Canaria. A useful port for both repairs and provisions.

Position 27° 45'33"N, 15° 41'08"W (marina)

Docking

Rarely any moorings for visiting boats.

Visitors must anchor outside and take stern lines to the inner breakwater, or anchor in the well-protected area between the harbour and the beach. The holding here seems to be reasonable.

Skippers are advised to enquire if there is space before they arrive, otherwise visiting yachts berth at the main wharf until a place is found for them.

There are a few moorings for yachts to come stern-to the outer breakwater which is fronted by large rocks.

Last updated March 2011.
