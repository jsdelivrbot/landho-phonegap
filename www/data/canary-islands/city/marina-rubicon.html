Marina Rubicon - Profile

Location

Marina Rubicón is the southernmost marina in Lanzarote, situated between the natural monument of los Ajaches and Playa Blanca. The marina sits in the heart of the tourist centre of Playa Blanca.

Position 28° 51'30"N, 13° 49'10"W (marina entrance)

Clearance

Arriving yachts should contact the marina on Channel 9 before entering the port. An attendant will wait on the reception dock and allocate a slip and help dock the boat with an inflatable dinghy. Ships papers, passports and yacht insurance must be produced when checking in.

Full service marina on the south coast of Lanzarote with 90 ton travel-lift, all repair facilities, WiFi, fuel berth, 24hr security, ice, gas, supermarket, chandlery, restaurants, shower blocks and laundry. Storage lockers. Pump out service for grey waters provided by a barge that calls at individual berths.
Prohibitions - wind generators must be blocked, no dish aerials, no laundry drying, no use of chains for mooring to pontoons, no sanding or painting jobs, no cleaning of fish, no fishing or swimming inside the marina. Marina also provides free access to outdoor swimming pool adjacent to marina.
