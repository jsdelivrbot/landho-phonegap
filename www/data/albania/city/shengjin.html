Shengjin - Profile

Location

This small fishing port is only 10 miles south of the border from Montenegro. There are shoals off the headland to the north of the harbour. It is busy for a short time during the summer, but for the rest of the year it is very quiet.

Position 41°48'47''N, 19°35'12''E.

Clearance

See Albania Formalities for detailed Clearance Information.

On approach, contact the Port Authority on VHF channel 16 and 71. They are not used to yachts and may allocate an unsuitable berth.

Visiting yachts need to use an agent for clearance here. See Albania/Clearance Agents for a list.

Last updated May 2015.

Docking

This harbour now has been dredged, so depth is not a problem. A new quay has been built, although the large ship fendering may be cause difficulties for smaller vessels. The port provides good shelter.

Security is good, but Note that the gates to the port are locked at 2200hrs and no entry or exit is permitted after that.

Water and fuel can both be delivered by tanker. Berthing fees can probably be negotiated.

Last updated June 2015.
