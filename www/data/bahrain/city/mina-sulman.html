Mina Sulman - Profile

Location

Mina Sulman is on the promontory just south of the capital, Manama.

Location 26° 11' 55" N, 50° 36' 43" E.

Docking

There are 3 marinas within a few miles of the Customs Dock; Bahrain Yacht Club (where most sailing boats are based), The Marina Club (which has facilities for larger boats) and The Al Bandar Resort.
