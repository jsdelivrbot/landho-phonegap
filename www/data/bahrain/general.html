Time Zone

UTC +3

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230v (Awaku 110v)

Frequency: 50Hz (Awali 60Hz)

Opening Hours

Friday is a holiday.

Generally most businesses are open Saturday to Thursday, although many close early on Thursday.

Government offices are open Saturday to Wednesday. 07:00-14:15 (Saturday-Tuesday), 07:00-14:00 (Wednesday).

Banks - 07:30-12 noon (Saturday-Thursday), selected branches also open a few hours some afternoons.

Shops - hours vary, but typically 08:30-12:30 and 16:00-20:00 (Saturday to Thursday). Many shops open half or full day on Friday as well.

Money

The currency in Bahrain is Dinars, each divided into 1000 fils.

Communications

International Dialing code for Bahrain is 973
