Barillas Marina - Profile

Location

Barillas is located on the Bahia de Jiguilisco, the entrance of which is about 75NM from Cutuco. This quiet Bahia is one of the best cruising grounds on the West Coast of El Salvador and a haven for wildlife.

Position 13° 15.7' N, 88° 25.2' W.

Clearance

Clearance is straightforward and can be made with Customs and Immigration at the marina who will come to the boat to do all the paperwork.

Last updated May 2016.

Docking

The marina is approximately 10NM from the entrance of Bahia Jiquilisco.

Call the marina one hour in advance of your arrival at the channel entrance - by phone is the best bet. A panga will come out and accompany the yacht over the bar up the channel to the marina - daylight hours only. The service charge is included in the first night's mooring fee (see comments below for latest reported fees).

Provisioning can be done in Usulutan which is 20 minutes driving away. The marina provides a free van service on Tuesdays and Fridays. Marine parts must be obtained from San Salvador.

Last updated May 2016.
