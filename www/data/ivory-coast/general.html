Ivory Coast - General Info

Time Zone

UTC

Money

West African franc CFA (Communauté financière africane). Franc=100 centimes.

All the major Credit Cards are widely accepted. Travellers cheques can be readily exchanged at banks and are accepted in many hotels, restaurants and shops.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Ivory Coast is +225.

Mobile GSM services are available around the main towns and cities.

There are Internet cafes in Abidjan.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Ivory Coast

Emergencies

Emergencies

Police - Dial 110, 111, 170
Fire Department - Dial 180
