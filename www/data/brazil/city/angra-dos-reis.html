Angra dos Reis - Profile

Location

Lying about 60 miles south of Rio de Janeiro, it is in the Baia da Ilha Grande. (See separate entry for details of this bay). This port is a growing yachting centre with good repair facilities.

Clearance

Read Brazil Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of Brazil.

Customs clearance no longer needs to be done in Rio - and anyway - international clearance in Rio for pleasure yachts is very difficult and many cruisers give up and come here.

There is now a customs office here (Receita Federal), near the port. Street: Lgo Lapa 35 - Downtown. Open 10:00 to 15:00 hs.

The Policia Federal: on the quay west of the fisherman's wharf.

Capitanio dos Portos: corner of Castelo Branco & César de Noronha.

Both the Policia and Capitanio dos Portos are reported to speak English.

Last updated June 2016.

Docking

A lively fishing harbour, also sporting two marinas, a yacht club and a great number of private docks.

There is also a marina at Port Bracuhy with some of the best repair facilities in Brazil.

It is also possible to anchor in front of the city wharf.

Last updated June 2016.
