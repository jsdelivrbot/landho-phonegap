Paraty - Profile

Location

Located approximately 25nm south west of  Angra dos Reis,  Paraty is an attractive historic city with great walks, waterfalls and landscape. It is also a popular sailing centre.

Position 23º 13’ S,  044º  42’W.

Last updated June 2013.

Docking

There are several marinas here and numerous sheltered places to anchor in the bay and in the vicinity of the town.

All the normal services provided in a tourist destination are to be found here.

Last updated July 2015.
