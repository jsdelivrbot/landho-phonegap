Sao Sebastiao - Profile

Location

The island of Sao Sebastiao is about 45 NM north of Santos. It lies just 2 miles off the coast, opposite the town of the same name.

Position 23°50'S, 45°22'W.

Clearance

Read Brazil Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of Brazil.

All the necessary authorities are on the waterfront in the town of San Sebastiao.

Docking

The only possible mooring on the mainland is in the small basin behind the ferry termnal. Here you berth among the fishing boats. There are no anchorages this side of the channel.

On the island of Sao Sebastiao there are several options of swinging moorings on the west coast, anchoring in front of the village, or in various anchorages around the north and east coasts.

There are several yacht clubs here and most offer a few days free mooring to visitors.

For repairs, supplies, chandlery or banking etc. it is better to moor on the island and take the ferry to the mainland.

Last updated January 2010.
