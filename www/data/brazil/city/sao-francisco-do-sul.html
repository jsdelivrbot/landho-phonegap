Sao Francisco do Sul - Profile

Location

This interesting, attractive town was, in the past, a busy whaling station and is one of the oldest cities in Brazil.

Position 26°14'S, 48°38'W.

Clearance

This is a port of entry.

Read Brazil Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of Brazil.

Docking

It is possible to anchor in front of the jetty on the waterfront and also off the jetty by the museum in the downtown area.

Further into the bay, there are pontoons at the yacht club at Joinville and one at the Iate Clube de Capri. The channel to which is quite shallow and approach need to be made above half tide in most yachts.

Last updated January 2010.

Iate Clube de Capri
Avenida Brazil 14 - Capri SC , 89242-000 Brazil
Tel:+55(47) 3444 7247 ,VHF Channel 16, 68 (not Wed)
http://www.capriiateclube.com.br
Advisable to call the club for help in the approach. The club has good facilities, showers, restaurant and wifi, but the pontoons are on the Island side, so to get to the club you have to take the club launch or your own dinghy.
The first 3 nights are free, after which it was 60R/night.
The town is some distance and the bus is the best way there, there are not many from the club but if you walk to the main road there are plenty.
