Paranagua - Profile

Location

This bay is formed by the estuaries of several rivers and is surrounded by mangrove forests. An intriguing place to visit.

Position 25°37.5'S, 48°16'W.

Clearance

This is a port of entry.

Read Brazil Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of Brazil.

Docking

On approach, take care to locate the seaward end of the channel and then following it carefully as it is borderd by sand banks until inside the bay.

There is a useful anchorage just inside the entrance if waiting for daylight or the tide. It is to port, by some conspicuous towers. The depth is 6m wih good holding.

There are pontoon berths at the yacht club's main base and swinging moorings at their base on the island of Ilha da Cotinga. The yacht club runs a ferry to this out base 4 times a day.

Last updated January 2010.
