Communications

See Pacific List of Radio Nets

International dialing code for Norfolk Island is +672.

There are 2 internet cafes in Taylors Road.

For telephone and fax services, Norfolk Telecom is open Monday to Friday 09:30am - 10:30 pm. Saturday and Sunday 09:00 am - 12 noon and 3pm-6pm.

Mobile phone service is available on Norfolk Island by purchasing a local sim card.

Postal and fax services, phonecards and a Philatelic Bureau are available at Norfolk Post in Burnt Pine. Open Monday to Friday.

Norfolk Island has its own stamp and postal service.

Transport

There is no public transport but taxis and car hire are available. A current driver's licence is required and the miniumum age for renting a car is 21 years.

There are 170km of road around Norfolk. The speed limit is 40km/h in town, 25km/h in the national park and along the waterfront at Kingston. Everywhere else is 50km/h. Warning - wandering cattle have the right of way.

Flights to Norfolk Island - Air New Zealand www.airnewzealand.co.nz - Norfolk Jet, Australia - Tel 617 3221 6677.
