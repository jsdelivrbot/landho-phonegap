Atafu - Profile

Location

This is the smallest atoll and it surrounds a lagoon approximately 3nm by 3nm square. It is the most traditional of all the islands and dugout canoes are still made here. The main village is on the northwestern corner.

Position 8° 35' S, 172° 30' W.

Docking

The whole of the reef around the lagoon is exposed at low tide.

It is possible to anchor off the more sheltered NW side of the lagoon in about 20m, but the seas generally break with some violence against the reef.
