Ottley Hall - Profile

Location

This small cove lies on the south western shore of St Vincent, just NW of Kingstown.

Position 13° 09'35"N, 61° 14'50"W.

Docking

There is a very useful shipyard here with a dry dock, shore storage  and docking for larger yachts.
