Blue Lagoon - Profile

Location

Blue Lagoon is surrounded by palm trees and this area, with its lovely beach, provides a very pleasant anchorage. The marina and hotel have been completely refurbished during 2014 and is due to be fully operational by the end of the year.

Approach - currently boats with a draft of 6.5ft or more cannot enter the marked channel at low tide.  The marina management are looking at addressing this in the near future.

See latest comments from cruisers at bottom of page.

Position 13° 07'40" N, 61° 11'40"W (marina)

Clearance

This is now a port of entry with Customs and Immigration on site at the marina.

Clearance office open form 13:00-18:00 daily.

Last updated March 2016.

Docking

This bay offers excellent shelter.

Moorings are sometimes available at the marina. There is also plenty of anchoring space here too and the marina dinghy dock is free of charge with security.

There is a bus stop just outside the marina entrance with buses into Kingstown ($2EC any journey). Here there is an excellent Saturday morning market.

Dinghy accessory thefts were reported here in April 2015 (fuel tank etc.) Be sure to secure all your valuables as you would anywhere in the world.

Last updated March 2016.
