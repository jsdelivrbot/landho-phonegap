Britannia Bay , Lovell - Profile

Location

This small island, lying south of Bequia, is just 3 miles long. It is wooded and hilly, and has several white sandy beaches. A favourite holliday spot for the rich.

Position 12° 52.7'N, 61° 11.4'W.

Clearance

Clearance is carried out by Customs and Immigration at the airport.

Docking

The only mooring allowed in Mustique is at mooring buoys in Britannia Bay (fees are paid to the Mustique Company). There is a landing jetty here.

Mooring fees for Mustique are:
Yachts up to 70 feet: 200 EC$ or 75 US$
Yachts 71 to 85 feet: 300 EC$ or 110 US$
These fees are one night only, with two free consecutive nights.

Anchoring is may be possible if all the moorings are occupied  but it is prohibited elsewhere in the island.

There is no chandlery and no apparent electrical or mechanical services.

Sometimes, when celebrities are on the island, freedom of movement is restricted to Britannia Bay. In such cases, it is even impossible to rent a minimoke or a taxi.
