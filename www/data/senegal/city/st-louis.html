St Louis - Profile

Location

This port is situated right on the border with Mauritania, at the mouth of the Senegal river. Its colonial architecture and its island site has granted it World Heritage status.

Position 16°02′N, 16°30′W.

Clearance

There is a dangerous bar at the entrance into this port.

Clearance formalities can be completed here, but the breaking bar may pose problems to a keeled yacht.
