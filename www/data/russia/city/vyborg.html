Location

This once elegant city is, nowadays, rather neglected. It is located in the north east corner of the Gulf of Finland, close to the Finish border.

Clearance

It is now possible to clear in here and this allows sailing the sheltered waters of the Bjorke Channel to St Petersburg.

Moor to the Immigration dock where an official will then take you to the Customs office for more form filling. The staff here are used to (Russian) yachts clearing in and out are are very helpful and friendly.

Last updated September 2010.

Docking

It may be possible to remain at the Immigration dock overnight (there is a guard here).

Opposite there is a marina where it is possible to stay.
