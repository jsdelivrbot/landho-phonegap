Sochi - Profile

Location

Sochi is the most southern commercial Port of Entry. It lies about 25nm NW of the border with Georgia.

Position 43°34.6’N, 39°43.0’E.

Clearance

The chances are high that a yacht will not be permitted to enter Russia here.

Call Sochi Port Control on VHF channel 16 when 5 miles off and be prepared to be boarded at sea.

All yachts, even if coming from another Russian port, must go to Sochi harbour and complete formalities before going on to the yacht club. this must also be done on departure.

Docking

The yacht harbour has basic facilities and the yacht club are welcoming to visiting yachts.

Try calling yacht club (VHF Channels 16 and 69, 08:00-20:00 only in summer). Yachts normally moor stern or bow to the quay.
