Location

Situated on Grand Terre, opposite the clearance port of Dzaoudzi. There is a frequent ferry service between the two ports. Mamoudzou has better shoreside facilities, chandleries/repair service companies etc. however Dzaoudzi is better protected from a berthing point of view.

Clearance

For full details on clearance go to Mayotte Formalities.

The marina here (see Frederic who is in charge) can organise entry and departure clearance. Weekend clearance is also possible.

Docking

The marina here is full of local motorboats, as well as a few sailboats on moorings in the not-so-protected bay, so visitor berthing is unlikely. One can however come alongside for water.
