Bergen - Profile

Location

Bergen lies about 100nm north of Stavanger on Norway's North Sea coast, protected by a string of off-shore islands.

Position 60° 24'06"N, 005° 17'16"E (entrance to Vågen)

Clearance

Port Control
Tel:+47 55 56 89 80/8

Docking

There are several marinas around Bergen and on the nearby islands. There are also berths at the head of Vagen on the north side. This is close to the city centre.

See this useful website for details of guest havens in the long fjord, Hardangerfjord, to the south of Bergen.

Bergen Vagen
This is the main harbour for non-commercial vessels, and is very central. Subject to some wash. Excellent facilities available using a key card from the Tourist Information Office behind the fish market. Toilets, showers and launderette. Berthing is on the quay wall to port at the far end of the harbour, by the fish market. It can be noisy.
