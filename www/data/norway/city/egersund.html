Egersund - Profile

Location

This well-sheltered harbour on Norway's south west coast, provides a very useful refuge and an attractive port of call.

Location: 58° 26' 56"N  5° 59' 13"E.

Clearance

On entry, fly the 'Q' flag if coming from a non-Nordic country or from outside the Schengen area, or if carrying dutiable goods.

The is no longer a Customs office in Egersund. If a Customs inspection is required, Tel: 55 57 37 00. The officials will probably have to come from Stavanger.

Last updated January 2014.

Customs (Tollvesenet)

Docking

There are moorings for visiting yachts in the inner harbour.

The Town's web site provides information in English and German as well as Norweigen.
