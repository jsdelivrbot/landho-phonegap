Rosendal - Profile

Location

This attractive village is now a most convenient port of call when transiting the coast between Stavanger and Bergen. For music-lovers it would probably serve as a destination on its own, as during the summer it features many classical concerts and other musical attractions. In addition to the attraction of its being a centre of arts and crafts.

It lies at the entrance to the Hardanger Fjord.

Position 59° 59'08"N, 006° 00'E.

Docking

The visitors' berthing has recently been considerably extended with new floating pontoons which can now house up to 60 yachts as well as improved berthing at the quays around the harbour.

There is provisioning and restaurants in the town.

Last updated February 2016.
