Tromso - Profile

Location

Located 200 miles (350km) north of the Arctic Circle, this is the Land of the Midnight Sun (from mid May to Mid July). It is a busy vibrant town with an active night life.

Position 69° 38'55"N, 18° 58'12"E.

Clearance

This is an official Port of Entry.

Last updated February 2016.

Docking

Protected harbour with sheltered pontoons in the city centre.

Guest pontoon with fingers off Rica Ishavshotel (+showers, washing machines).

The Smabathaven just north of the bridge on mainland welcomes visitors, makes no charge and has diesel but is a 30-min walk over bridge into town.

As at 2015/2016 major redevelopment of the waterfront will make weekday berthing here a noisy place.

Last updated February 2016.
