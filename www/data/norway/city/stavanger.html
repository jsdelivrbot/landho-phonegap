Stavanger - Profile

Location

This is an important cruise ship and ferry port as well as a centre for the North Sea Oil industry. It provides a useful departure point for the crossing to Scotland.

Located at 58°58'N, 05°4'E.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Docking

There are now three marinas here, The Vagen which is exclusively for visitors, another on the north side, just before the bridge and the new one by the Oil museum.

The Vagen is subject to serious wash from passing commercial vessels.

The Oil Museum has three berths alongside on South side of marina. These are quieter and better protected but more expensive.

Last updated February 2016.
