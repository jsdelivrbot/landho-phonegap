Moss - Profile

Location

Moss is on the eastern bank of the fiord leading up to Oslo.

Position 56° 25'29"N, 010° 39'02"E

Docking

You can tie up to the bridge on the east side of the canal, or enter the yacht harbour, Moss Gjestehavn. Look for green signs, indicating availability. It is on the east-side of Jeloy, some 5 cables SW of the canal. This option is only suitable if your draft is less than 2.3m and loa less than 45 feet (14m).

Better and quieter are berths N of bridge, via Mossesund from the north.

The bridge across the canal does not open.

There are provisions available, and 2 chandlers. Also restaurants, as well as nice beaches, are all to be found a short walk from both harbours.
