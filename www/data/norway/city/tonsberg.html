Tonsberg - Profile

Location

Tønsberg is a vibrant city with a lot going on in the summer. It is one of Norway's oldest towns.

Position 59° 16'15"N, 010° 23'44"E

Docking

You can practically tie your boat up to any one of several outdoor restaurants or go to the guest harbour a bit further west for peace. The guest harbour can get a bit uncomfortable, but not dangerous in a strong SW. Avoid the outside of the pontoons if possible.

There are also many new local marinas on the approaches to the canal.

The new bridge opens 5 minutes before the old bridge for E-bound and 5 minutes after old bridge for W-bound vessels.

The marked channel SE of Tønsberg canal continues through a huge expanse of quite shallow water. There are extensive charted anchorages south of this marked channel at Træla.

Last updated February 2016.
