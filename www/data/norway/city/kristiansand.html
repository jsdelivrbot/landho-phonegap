Kristiansand - Profile

Location

This popular boating centre is on Norway's southern coast and is not to be confused with the town of Kristiansund on the west coast north of Alesund.

This is one of Norway's main ports catering for small boats with good marina facilities.

Position 58° 08'30"N, 008° 00'17"E (Guest berths)

Docking

This is a very large, sheltered harbour, but the visitors' berths are close to the town centre. Mooring is bows to jetty with stern buoys. Outside berths subject to swell. A few berths alongside, no doubling up.

Tokens for showers and laundry from kiosk.

It gets very full but Harbourmaster is most helpful.

Last updated February 2016.
