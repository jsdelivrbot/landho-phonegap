Fredrikstad - Profile

Location

On River Glomma (longest in Scandinavia) which divides in the town to flow SW into Oslo Fjord and SE into the Indreleia.

Position 59° 12'N, 10° 57'E.

Docking

There are 3 bridges over the Vestereleva (West River). Be aware that the river can flow at up to 2kts.

There are 60 guest berths (20 with water and electricity) between the two southern bridges.

Berthing fees and access card to the facilities (which are in the Town Hall, 30m from quay) from Harbour Master.

The best place to moor is 100m N of the most southerly bridge (to avoid noise from traffic and from the early morning bridge opening).

Last updated February 2016.
