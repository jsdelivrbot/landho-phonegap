Lillesand - Profile

Location

This attractive town is located on Norway's SE coast.

Position 58°15'N, 08°23'E

Docking

The surrounding islands provide shelter, although the harbour is somewhat exposed to the south.

The pontoon moorings now give better protection from South and increased capacity.

Visiting yachts can lie alongside wavebreaking pontoon or moor bows-on with stern anchor.

Electricity (two pin Norwegian socket - adaptors available from electrician off main square) and water on pontoon.

Free WiFi. Bread rolls distributed to each boat at breakfast time by harbour office during summer season!

Last updated February 2016.
