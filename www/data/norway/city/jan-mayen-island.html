Jan Mayen Island - Profile

Location

This volcanic island is occupied by a small number of Norwegians who man the radio station and a few armed forces personnel. There are not usually any polar bears here but occasionally one may be seen in the summer. The island is a nature reserve.

Position 70°55'N, 08°34'W.

Clearance

Permission must be obtained in advance of a visit to the island.

See the http://www.jan-mayen.com/jan-mayen-information.html website for information.

Last updated February 2016.

Docking

There are no ports or harbours and landing may only be permitted at Båtvika (Boat Bay) or Kvalrossbukta. Visitors are not normally allowed into the area of the nature reserve.

Permission to climb the volcano has occasionally been granted.

Last updated February 2016.
