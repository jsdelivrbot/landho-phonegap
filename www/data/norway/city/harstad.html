Harstad - Profile

Location

This important northern town, and is a centre for the oil industry. Since the sun does not rise here between the beginning of December to mid January, it is a good place to see the aurora borealis (northern lights).

Position 68°46.42'N, 16°29.44'E.

Clearance

This is an official Port of Entry.

Last updated June 2011.

Docking

The harbour is open to the north east.

There are plenty of guest berths on the pontoons here. The town berths have showers and laundry facilities.

There is also more berthing to the south at the head of Harstadbotn including a sheltered private pontoon with space for two yachts to overwinter.

The town has good shops.

Last updated February 2016.
