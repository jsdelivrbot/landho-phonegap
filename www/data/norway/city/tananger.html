Tananger - Profile

Location

Tananger is a most useful port of call if wishing to berth in the vicinity of Stravanger. When making a coastal passage, is save a considerable detour as it is right on the North Sea coast of Norway.

Position 58° 55. 6’N, 5° 35.6’E.

Docking

This is a large, busy harbour as it services the North Sea oil rigs.

There are plenty of berthing options for yachts.
