Maloy - Profile

Location

Stratigically placed at the southern end of the open water passage of Stadt when cruising up to Alesund.

The Redningsselskapet (Norwegian Sea Rescue Service) provides a convoy service across this stretch of coast to Alesund during the summer months.

Position 61º 56'N, 005º 07'20"E

Clearance

This is an official Port of Entry.

Last updated February 2016.

Docking

Facilities were improved when the Tall Ships Race called here. Moor to the concrete pontoon near the town centre.

Facilities block with laundry. Water, electricity available.

Asmundvågen (61º59'.19N 5º11'.06E) A small mole harbour at the top of mainland peninsula East of Ulvesund, 4nm North of Måløy. Also anchorage in 3m, in pool to the south.

Last updated February 2016.
