Communications

International dialing code for Hong Kong is +504.

Hong Kong operates on the GSM cellular phone system. Having a cell phone is strongly suggested to you as a visitor to Hong Kong. Cell phone service is no longer the huge obstacle in Hong Kong that is once was.

Arrange service before leaving your home country or rent a GSM phone with SIM pre-paid card which saves both time and money. Incoming calls are free.
