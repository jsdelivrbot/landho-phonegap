Stockholm - Profile

Location

This important city lies on the south east coast and is the capital of Sweden. It is spread across several islands and has an interesting 'old town'.

Position 59°19′46″N, 18°4′7″E.

This marina lies just to the west of Stockholm city centre on Lake Malaren near the E4/E20 motorway and reached from the archipelago by a canal and lock (Max mast height 15.5m) or via the Sodertalje ship canal from the west.
Winter season is from 1 November to 31 March.
Storage under cover ashore and afloat. Lift to 50 tonnes. Storage under cover is in great demand but vessels can remain the the water because of a "bubbler" which is installed to disperse ice. All repair services.
