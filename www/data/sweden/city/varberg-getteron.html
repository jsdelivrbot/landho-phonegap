Varberg Getteron - Profile

Location

This is an interesting town with a castle, a museum and very good beaches. It is situated south of Goteborg, in the Kattegat.

Docking

The old commercial harbour is convenient for the town and the train station, but the marina at Getteron has most of the services including chandlery, boatyard and engineers.

There is a ferry link between them.
