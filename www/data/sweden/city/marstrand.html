Marstrand - Profile

Location

This very boat-friendly port of call is approximately 35NM north of Goteborg. It is a charming, traffic-free town on the east coast of the island and is renowned for its beautiful wooden architecture.

Position 57° 53'N, 11° 35'E

Docking

There are a large number of docks here but no information on visitors' berths.

Last updated September 2015.
