Ornskoldsvik - Profile

Location

Located midway up the Gulf of Bothnia, Ornskoldvik is at the northern end of an area known at the High Coast.

A sheltered natural harbour at the far end of a fjord called the Havsfjärden. It is a major port for the export of pulp and wood products. It is in an area of the Gulf of Bothnia

Position 63° 17.2' N, 18° 42.7' E.

Docking

The re-developed old port is now the guest harbour and is in the heart of the town.

Last updated December 2013.
