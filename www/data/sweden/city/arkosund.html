Arkosund - Profile

Location

A rather isolated port, about 60NM south of Stockholm.

Docking

There are several marinas here, one of which is run by the yacht club. Facilities are generally more limited, with coin operated toilets and outdoor showers.

Mosquitoes can be a problem in this area.
