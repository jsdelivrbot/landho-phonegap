Prickly Bay - Profile

Location

Prickly Bay is the unofficial HQ of the annual cruiser community with lots of activites. The bay is completely open to the south so some continual rolling due to swells can be expected.

Note: The concrete channel markers here are poorly maintained and may not be lit, so great care should be taken if navigating at night.

Position 11° 59'38"N, 61° 45'48"W (bay entrance)

Clearance

See Grenada Formalities for detailed information on clearing into and out of Grenada, including downloadable forms.

Customs and Immigration in Prickly Bay are in the group of buildings adjacent to Prickly Bay Marina. The office is open 7 days a week, 0900 to 1600 hours, and the officers are reported to be friendly. Note that Saturdays and Sundays are classed as "overtime" and extra fees are charged.

Customs and immigration require payment in EC dollars and it can be a long walk to the ATM, which is actually closer to Spice Island boatyard in the NW corner of the bay.

Last updated June 2015.

Docking

There are several good anchorages in this bay, though you may need a long scope in places. There are also plenty of swinging moorings available.

The bay is completely open to the south so some continual rolling due to swells can be expected.

Security

July 2016: An overnight an attempt was made to steal an outboard from a dinghy in the water. The outboard was locked to the dinghy which was locked to the yacht.

Dinghy thieves do operate in this area. Whilst noonsite has received reports in the past about unlocked dinghies being stolen, these were however subsequently recovered. Cruisers are urged to be vigilant and to report any theft to the Coast Guard and the Police at once.

No dinghies have ever been reported as stolen from Prickly Bay Marina dock or jetty. The dinghy dock here is lit at night and has 24/7 security. The marina has an emergency tender called "Rescue 1" in service 24/7 (call vhf ch.16 or 415 1132/415 1182) that can assist on stolen dinghy recovery or any other emergency.

Last updated July 2016.
