Petit Martinique - Profile

Location

Lying less than half a mile south Petite St Vincent (PSV), it is a mirror image of its smaller neighbour. It is a friendly, and welcoming island. It is one of the original Caribbean Spice Islands.

Position 12° 31'32"N, 61° 23'19"W.

Docking

The anchorage is deep (7-11m and rock bottom). The best holding is east of the docks. A better anchorage is across at PSV in front of the resort on a white sand bar in 3-5m.

On this small island, there is a bank, a post office, a health centre, a gas station (B&C Fuels for duty-free fuel), and a few grocery stores, all in close proximity to the main pier.

The ferry service to Petite Martinique is the Osprey High-Speed Catamaran Service, which makes daily trips from Grenada at 09:00 and 17:00 or from Carriacou at 11:00 and 20:00. (except Wednesdays).

If dinning there, there are buoys in front of the Palm Beach restaurant.
