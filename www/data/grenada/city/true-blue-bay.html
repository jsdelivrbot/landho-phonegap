True Blue Bay - Profile

Location

This is a very small bay just west of Prickly Bay and is primarily a charter base for Horizon Yachts.

Position 11° 55'55"N, 61° 46'10"W (bay entrance)

Docking

The bay is totally open to the south and rolly. Dockage is very limited.
