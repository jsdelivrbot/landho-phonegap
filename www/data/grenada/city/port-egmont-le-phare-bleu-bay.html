Port Egmont (Le Phare Bleu Bay) - Profile

Location

Lying just north of Calvigny Islandand east of Woburn Bay, Le Phare Blue Bay is approached via a marked channel.

Position 12° 00'07"N, 61° 43'25"W (marina)

Clearance

See Grenada Formalities for detailed information on clearing into and out of Grenada, including downloadable forms.

There are Customs and Immigration facilities at the marina.

Last updated March 2016.

Docking

La Phare Bleu Marina is at the head of the bay. There is a buoyed channel leading to the marina.

The marina is modern and the owner/operators are Swiss and they brought over a retired Swedish Lightship "Vastra Banken" as their theme piece for this marina.

It is possible to dinghy through the cut to Clarkes Court Bay and visit Hog Island, Clarkes Court Bay Marina, Whisper Cove Marina and Lower Woburn to catch the bus to downtown.

Last updated March 2016.
