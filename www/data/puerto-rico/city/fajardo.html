Fajardo - Profile

Location

This small city is on the eastern coast of the island. It is a major boating centre with a number of large marinas and facilities.

Position 18°20’N, 65°38’W.

Clearance

This is a convenient Port of Entry.

The designated site for clearance inspection is  the Municipal Pier In front of the Custom House.

It is possible arrangements can be made with the dockmaster at Marina del Rey and SunBay Marina, for the authorities to visit the boat to complete clearance formalities there.

See main Puerto Rico clearance page for full details.

Docking

It is home to several large marinas.

Do not think about leaving your dinghy at the mainland pier - it is unsheltered and theft is common.

August 2014: Yachts in dry storage at Puerto Del Rey Marina reported attempted break-in. See CSSN report.

Last updated August 2014.
