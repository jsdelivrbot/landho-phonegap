Puerto Real - Profile

Location

This small fishing village lies just 10 nm south of Mayagnez on the southwestern corner of Puerto Rico. It is tucked into the northern corner of the bay of the same name.

Position 18° 04.2'N, 67° 11.1'W.

Clearance

Although this is not an official Port of Entry, it is possible to berth here first and then contact the CBP office in Mayagnez. They will either request the whole crew to go to their office in Mayagnez (the marina can help arrange transport) or the officials will visit the boat.

If the boat is part of the Local Boaters Scheme, then just a phone call might be all that is required.

Last updated November 2014.

Docking

There are buoys marking the channel into the bay.

The marina here has been recently upgraded and the owner is very helpful. Call on VHF channel 68 if help is required.

The anchorage here is sheltered and secure with a bottom of thick mud. Anchor SW of the marina in about 3m.

Last updated December 2015.
