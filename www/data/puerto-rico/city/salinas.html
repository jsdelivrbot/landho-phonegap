Salinas - Profile

Location

This fishing port lies on the southern coast of Puerto Rica, about 20nm east of Ponce. It is a good base from which to explore the country and is known for its seafood restaurants.

Position 17°57’27"N, 66°17’33"W.

Clearance

This is NOT an offical Port of Entry.

Docking

This provides a well-protected anchorage in a lagoon, the entrance to which is sheltered by mangrove swamps. The anchorage bottom is mainly mud and sand. Holding is mediocre.

Security in the anchorage is good and at the marina.

Dinghies can be landed at the marina in the transient dinghy area. Also, there is a dinghy dock available at the Waterfront Bar and Restaurant called Sal Pa’ Dentro, which is just west of the marina.

Note that this is a Manatee Zone at 5mph.

Last updated March 2015.
