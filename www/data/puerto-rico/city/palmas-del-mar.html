Palmas del Mar - Profile

Location

This is a new resort complex on the east coast of Puerto Rica, about 2 NM north east of the commercial port of Puerto Yabucoa.

Position 18° 04.59'N, 65° 47.7W.

Docking

Care needs to taken in the approach as there are reefs each side of the entrance.
