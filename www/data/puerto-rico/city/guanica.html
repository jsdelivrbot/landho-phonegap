Guanica - Profile

Location

Located on the southern coast, this deep bay lies approximately 25nm west of Ponce. The town is surrounded by the largest remaining tract of tropical dry coastal forest in the world and also the Punta Ballena Reserve mangrove forest.

The company town of Ensenada, on the west side of the bay, is reported to be interesting as a time capsule. Students of 19th century industry will enjoy the ruins of a late-model sugar mill.

Position 17°58’N, 66°55’W.

Clearance

This is not a Port of Entry.

Docking

Cruisers report that this is an excellent harbour which offers good shelter. The entrance is narrow, but then the bay widens and extends another 2 miles to the town.
