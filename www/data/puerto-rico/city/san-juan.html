San Juan - Profile

Location

Old San Juan is most attractive with its cobble streets lined with pastel Spanish Colonial-style houses.

Position 18°28’N, 66°07’W.

Clearance

Arrival can be made at San Juan Bay Marina, Club Náutico de San Juan or Cangrejos Yacht Club.

This is the preferred option rather than yachts going to the Customs House, where the officials are busy with cruise ships and commercial vessels. They prefer to come and clear yachts at the clubs or the marina.

See main Puerto Rico clearance page for full details.

Last updated November 2014.

Docking

There is a designated anchorage, just West of the San Juan Bay Marina, in the San Antonio channel. However, large cruise ships dock on the South side of this channel and encroach into this area so there is not much room. In addition, there are also a number of resident boats on moorings. Holding in soft mud at 10 metres is only fair.

Dinghies can be left at the marina for a small daily fee.

Buses run frequently to town from the nearby bus stop. Buses also run to Plaza Las Americas, a huge shopping complex. There is a good small supermarket within walking distance of the marina.

Last updated June 2015.
