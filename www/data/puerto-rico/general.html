Cruising Guide to Puerto Rico (inc. the Spanish Virgin Islands)
By Stephen Pavlidis
Publisher: Imray 2nd Edititon (2009)
ISBN: 978-1892399328
This book is perhaps the most comprehensive guide ever written covering Puerto Rico and the Spanish Virgin Islands. Its 50 full colour charts containing extremely accurate date are based on independent surveys personally conducted by the author using a computer hydrographic system. This book also contains detailed piloting instructions, GPS waypoints, photos, approaches and routes, anchorages, services, dive sites etc.
