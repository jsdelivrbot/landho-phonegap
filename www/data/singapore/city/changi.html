Changi - Profile

Location

Changi is on the eastern end of the main island of Singapore. The sailing club (and moorings) are on the north east corner of the island.

Position 01°23'36"N, 103° 58'46"E (sailing club jetty)

Clearance

See Singapore Clearance page.

Docking

Those on a limited budget usually anchor as close to the Changi Sailing Club as they can, as the Club rarely has any spare moorings. Do not anchor in the mooring field.

Changi is popular as it has a village atmosphere, a fresh produce market, restaurants, good shopping and other facilities within walking distance.

There is no marina here, but cruisers report that The Changi Sailing Club (CSC) is very welcoming to yachts in transit. CSC facilities are superb and the staff are helpful and friendly.

Last updated August 2016.

Changi Sailing Club
32 Netheravon Road , Singapore
Tel:(65) 6545-2876 Fax:(65) 6542-4235 ,VHF Channel 77
http://www.csc.org.sg/contact-us/visiting-yachts , enquiry@csc.org.sg
Self-christened as Singapore’s “secret hideaway", the Club is located on Serangoon Harbour on the NE shore of Singapore Island.
This is not a marina, but swing moorings are available for visiting yachts with a LOA of up to 50 ft. Email a mooring request before leaving your previous port.
The mooring fee is S$21.40 per day and visitors pay a daily membership fee. The club also runs a daily ferry service (call VHF ch 77).
There is a floating pontoon for boats to come alongside to load/off-load and take on water. The Club has full amenities including restaurant, bar, cafe, a swimming pool and chalets.
