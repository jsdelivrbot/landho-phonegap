Kettering (Oyster Cove) - Profile

Location

This port lies at the heart of the well sheltered Oyster cove and is just 20nm south of Hobart at the northern end of the D’Entrecasteaux Channel. To the east, is the long mass of Bruny Island.

Position 43° 07'35"S, 147° 15'09"E (marina)

Docking

An attractive position and marina staff friendly and helpful.

Last updated April 2016.
