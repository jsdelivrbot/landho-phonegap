Adelaide - Profile

Location

Adelaide is the capital of South Australia and lies approximately 400nm north west of Melbourne.

Position 34°47′27″S,138°27′31″E (river entrance)

Clearance

To clear in at Adelaide, contact the Outer Harbour control tower on VHF Ch 16 and proceed to a berth as instructed.

For full details of clearance requirements, see Australia/Formalities.

Last updated January 2014.

Docking

There are several marinas here, as well as three yacht clubs. Some in the Outer Harbour and other up the river, in the Inner Harbour. They all lie about 5 miles(8km) north of the city centre.

The North Haven Harbour, which houses several marinas, lies half a mile south of the Outer Harbour entrance.

Glenelg lies about 3 miles south west of the centre of the city.

There is also a marina 40 miles south of the city at the Wirrina Cove Resort, but it is not possible to clear in here.
