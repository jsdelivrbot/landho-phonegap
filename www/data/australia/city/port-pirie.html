Port Pirie - Profile

Location

Located on the Pirie River, Port Pirie is an exporter of grain and minerals with the world's largest lead smelter. It is located on the eastern shore of the Spencer Gulf.

Position 33°11′9″S, 138°1′1″E.

Clearance

Not a Port of Entry for yachts.

Docking

The Royal Port Pirie Yacht Club sails mainly off the beach classes and is situated adjacent to the silos and boat ramp.

No real facility for extended visits by cruising yachts.

Last updated January 2014.
