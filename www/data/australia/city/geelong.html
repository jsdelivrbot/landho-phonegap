Geelong - Profile

Location

Geelong lies in an inlet call Corio Bay, on the western shore of Port Philip Bay, about 40nm south west of Melbourne. It is a major port and centre of industry. It has many parks and an attractive waterfront.

Position 38°09′S, 144°21′E.

Docking

The large marina run by the yacht club is the principal mooring facility here. It is located in the heart of Geelong.

A new marina at Rippleside, north of the centre, is planned but not yet built.

Last updated January 2014.
