Yamba, Clarence River - Profile

Location

Yamba is located at the mouth of the Clarence River along the tropical New South Wales coast, about 120 miles south of Brisbane and 60 miles north of Coffs Harbour. It is a popular boating area as the Clarence river system provides an attractive and sheltered sailing area.

Location 29° 26'S, 153° 20' 45"E.

Clearance

Yamba is not a clearance port. The nearest authorised clearance port is Coffs Harbour, 60 nm south.

Docking

In addition to the maina, there is a small public wharf at the far end of Yamba Channel (29°,25.994'S 153°,21.577'E) which is suitable for shallow draft vessels and tenders.

There is a pontoon by the Sedgers Reef Hotel.

It is possible to anchor at the northern end of the bay.

If continuing up the river, there is a marina at Maclean.

An excellent guide to the river is the Sailing and Cruising Guide.
