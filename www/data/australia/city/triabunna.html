Triabunna - Profile

Location

Locate about 50nm north east of Hobart, Triabunna is a quiet fishing village in the shelter of Spring Bay. It is surrounded by eucalyptus forests and has great beaches.

Position 42°30′42"S,147°54′51"E.

Docking

There are med-style berths along the waterfront.

Last updated April 2016.
