Hastings (Westernport) - Profile

Location

Hastings is an attractive fishing port lying on the western side of the large tidal bay in the Bass Strait, called Westernport.

It is a great mecca for serious fishermen. It is also a popular holiday spot for those who like the quiet life. It is approximately 50 miles south of Melbourne.

Position 38° 18' S, 145° 11' E.

Docking

The berthing here is very well protected in this sheltered bay.

There are also moorings and an anchorage north of Hasting Pier.
