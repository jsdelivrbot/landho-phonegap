Port Huon - Profile

Location

This small port is tucked away up the Huon River and is a great place from which to explore the wilderness of southern Tasmania.

Position 43°09.5'S, 146°57.5'E.

Docking

Port Huon : ©/www.marinasguide.com.au

To reach the marina, follow the buoyed channel  from the the D'Entrecasteaux Channel. Kermandie Marina is positioned at the junction of the Kermandie and Huon Rivers.

If draft allows, it is possible to go further up the river to the Wooden Boat Centre and Franklin. It is possible to anchor here.

Last updated April 2016.
