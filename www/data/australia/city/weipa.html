Weipa - Profile

Location

This dedicated mineral exporting port is on the NW coast of the Cape York Peninsula at the northern tip of Queensland.

It exports aluminium ore from the Comalco mine.

Position 12°37′S, 141°52′E.

Clearance

There is no clearance for yachts here.
