Port Hinchingbrook - Profile

Location

This port is located midway between Cairns and Townsville, at the northern end of the Coral Sea. This strait separates Hinchinbrook Island National park from the mainland.

Position 18° 31' S, 146° 23' E.

Docking

The marina here is currently reported to be closed due to silting problems. Phone (07) 4066 2000 for an up to date situation.

Some distance from the town and services.

Last updated January 2014.
