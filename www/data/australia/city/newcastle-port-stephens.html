Newcastle + Port Stephens - Profile

Location

Newcastle is approximately 70 miles north of Sydney and Port Stephens another 30 miles beyond that.

Port Stephens harbour is a large natural harbour with a narrow entrance to the sea. It is surrounded by several small, interesting towns and villages. It has great sandy beaches and wonderful scenery.

Docking

Newcastle has one marina, run the the local Cruising Club, and Port Stephens has several. It is also an excellent and attractive area in which to spend the cyclone season.

Neither port has a sand bar (unlike many along this coast) and can, therefore be entered in almost any weather.
