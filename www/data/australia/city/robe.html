Robe - Profile

Location

Robe is a small, old crayfishing town on the southeast coast of South Australia. Situated on the Guichen Bay approximately 100nm south of Adelaide. It is an attractive little port.

It is a convenient port if caught on this coast in the strong northwest to southwest winds.

Position 37°09′S, 139°45′E,

Docking

Lake Butler, Robe :©www.marinews.com

It is not recommended you approach this port without good charts as there are many reefs around this coast.

The harbour is located in an area of very sheltered water called Lake Butler.

There is a marina here in the Lake. It is also possible to anchor off the town to the south of the harbour entrance in settled weather.
