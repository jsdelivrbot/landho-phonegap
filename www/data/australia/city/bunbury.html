Bunbury - Profile

Location

Bunbury lies approximately 120 miles south of Perth, on the eastern side of Geographie Bay.

Position 33°19'S, 115°39'E.

Clearance

The boarding station for arrival is the anchorage in Koombana Bay within 100m of disused wooden wharf/jetty.

Docking

Bunbury boat harbour : ©Steve Olsen

Casuarina Harbour is located within the southern portion of what is known as Bunbury's "Outer Harbour", which form the commercial port. Access to the Boat Harbour is through the Outer Harbour.

There are two jetties with slips. There is room to anchor in this harbour but we have no information on the holding. There are also swinging moorings here.

About 30nm SW of Bunbury, in the centre of Geographie Bay, is Busselton, where there is a marina (Port Geographie).

Last updated February 2014.
