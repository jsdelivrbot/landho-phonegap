Port Hedland - Profile

Location

This was once a pearl fishing port, but now it is given over to iron ore exports. It is situated at the western end of Eighty Mile Beach which stretches along the coast between Broome and Port Hedland.

Position 20°19'S, 118°34.5'E.

Clearance

Contact Port Control Tower on Marine Ch 16 prior to entering the channel.  The operator will provide instruction re berthing. The primary berth is the town jetty, located opposite Customs House outside the commercial wharf area. (Temporary berthing only).

Note Port Hedland Yacht Club is tide restricted and is not a designated Customs Clearing point.

Last updated March 2014.
