Stanley - Profile

Location

Located on the eastern side of a peninsula on the North west corner on Tasmania. It is an historic town with many preserved buildings. It is a fishing port.

Position 40° 46'09"S, 145° 18'04"E (harbour)

Docking

photo from www.mast.tas.gov.au

There is a boat harbour here but beware of the cross currents at its narrow entrance.

Ask the Harbour Master where to berth.

Water is available on the main jetty.

Fuel can be obtained from Stanley Fish Tel: 61 (3) 6458 1153 or Arnolds Fuel Tel: 0419 580 729.
