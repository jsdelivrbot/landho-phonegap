Tin Can Bay and Hervey Bay - Profile

Location

Tin Can Bay lies on the Queensland coast opposite the southern tip of Fraser Island, which is the site of the Great Sandy National Park. The port lies approximately 65nm south of Bundaberg.

The harbour at Hervey Bay, Urangan lies about 25nm north of Tin Can Bay and well known for its whale watching.

Position 25° 48.2'S, 153° 03.2'E. (entrance to Tin Can Bay inlet)

Clearance

Not a Port of Entry.

Docking

There are several marinas located within the harbour at Hervey Bay.

Tin Cay Bay is tucked away at the southern end of the Great Sandy Straits and provides both a marina and sheltered anchorages.
