Onslow - Profile

Location

This is a holiday town, with an excellent climate, fishing and beaches. It lies about 300nm west of Port Hedland on Australia's north west coast and is a supply base for the oil rigs.

Position 21° 39' S 115° 7' E.

Clearance

This is not a Port of Entry.

Docking

There are moorings in Beadon Creek.

Last updated March 2014.

There is short-stay berthing on the public wharf. Basic facilities include water, electricity, toilets and cold showers. Longer stay mooring is on piles in the creek (known locally as 'the sticks').
