Townsville - Profile

Location

An attractive and vibrant holiday destination for visitors to the Great Barrier Reef. Great beaches, landscaped waterfront and, of course, the diving.

Position 19°15′23″S, 146°49′6″E.

Clearance

For full details of Clearance see Australia/Formalities.

On arrival clearly display the 'Q' flag and call The Breakwater Marina on VHF channel 10. Berth at the fuel dock located immediately inside the marina entrance on the Starboard side. It is imperative that vessels clearing customs DO NOT secure to the fuel dock until Customs officers are onsite. Vessels arriving after hours will be required to stand-off in the breakwater area outside the marina entrance.

Last updated January 2014.

Docking

The Breakwater Marina is situated close to the city centre with supermarkets and all amenities.

There is also a marina at Magnetic Island, just 5nm north of the town.

The Duck Pond offers a free anchorage sheltered against the prevailing winds.

It may also be possible to obtain a berth in the old marina in the Ross River. This is approached via the commercial port and past the ferry terminal.

Last updated January 2014.
