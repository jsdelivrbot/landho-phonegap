Devonport - Profile

Location

This port is the major gateway for arrivals by sea of both goods and people into Tasmania. It is on the north coast of the Island, facing across the Bass Strait.

June 2016: Mersey Yacht Club suffered significant storm damage sadly losing their marina pontoon berths and most of their boats, which were washed down the Devonport River. See news item http://www.mysailing.com.au/latest/mersey-yacht-club-marina-collapses-boats-lost-during-storm. The Club have confirmed they will be closed for 2 months for repairs.

Position 41° 09'56"S, 146° 22'06"E (river entrance)
