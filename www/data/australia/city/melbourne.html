Melbourne - Profile

Location

Melbourne is the capital of Victoria and lies at the head of a very large bay (20nm wide and 40nm long), Port Philip Bay, the entrance to which is only 2nm wide.

The commercial Port of Melbourne lies to the east of the Yarra River which leads into the heart of the city.

Position 37°48′49″S, 144°57′47″E.

Docking

There is a large range of berthing options here. Either at the entrance to the Yarra River at Williamstown or right up the river to the heart of the city at Docklands. Visitors can berth where there are green caps on the marina piles.

There is a large anchorage area in Hobsons Bay, off Williamstown.
