Kingscote, Kangaroo Island - Profile

Location

Kangaroo Island lies about 8nm off the coast, approximately 80nm south west of Adelaide.

Kingscote is the largest town on Kangaroo Island and is situated in Nepean Bay east end of the island.

It is a popular tourist destination and home to a colony of the smallest penguins in the world, the little penguin.

Position 35°39′11″S, 137°38′03″E.

Clearance

There are no clearances facilities here.

Docking

The wharf is not a suitable for mooring for yachts, so it is necessary to anchor off. The bay is open to the south.

Last updated January 2014.
