Mourilyan Harbour - Profile

Location

This is a natural harbour about 120 miles south of Cairns.

Position 17°34′S, 146°03′E.

Clearance

This is not a port of entry.

Docking

Although a large part of this harbour is shallow, the area closer to the entrance, south of the commercial sugar port, provides a good sheltered anchorage.

Last updated March 2014.
