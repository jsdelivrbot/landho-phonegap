Manly - Profile

Location

Manly is located south of the Brisbane River entrance along the western shore of Moreton Bay. To the south of Manly and Moreton Bay lies the Gold Coast, connected by several miles of channels navigable by boats to 2m depth and 22m height.

Position 27° 27'S,  153°11' E. (harbour entrance)

Clearance

Yachts can no longer clear at Scarborough or Manly as there is now a dedicated clearance facility at the Rivergate Marina on the Brisbane River. See Brisbane Port Clearance page.

For port facilities see also Scarborough and northern Moreton Bay, as well as the Gold Coast

Updated April 2015

Docking

Manly harbour contains several marinas as listed below. It provides excellent shelter and the town on Manly, though small,  has all services as well as convenient links to Brisbane.

East Coast Marina
570 Royal Esplande , Manly Boat Harbour , Brisbane, Queensland 4179
Tel:+61 (0)7 3393 3811 Fax:+61 (0)7 3393 4252
http://www.eastcoastmarina.com.au/ , info@eastcoastmarina.com.au
Located in Manly harbour. All facilities, laundry, pump out facility, internet. Fuel in the harbour. Cafe (am only). Range of marine services. Pontoons reported to be in poor condition (2015). Max. length 20m. Friendly staff. Berths are a long walk from the showers.
Good public transport links to Brisbane city and shopping. Easy access to Moreton Bay.

Modern marina with all facilities, laundry, fuel berth, use of clubhouse with restaurant, boatyard with 30 ton travel hoist. Visitors are unlikely to get a berth here.
There is a week-end market in the park next to the marina.
