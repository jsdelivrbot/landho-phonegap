Port Walcott - Profile

Location

This is another commercial port for the export of ore. it comprises of a long jetty projecting out from the shore, with a deep water loading berth at the end.

Position 20°34'S, 117°11'E (Cape Lambert)

Clearance

This is not a Port of Entry.
