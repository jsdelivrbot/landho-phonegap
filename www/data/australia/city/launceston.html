Launceston - Profile

Location

This one of Tasmania's princial cities. It is situated some 60nm inland, up the attractive Tamar River. It is known for its lavender growing as well as its long and interesting history. It is the hub of all communications on the Island.

Position 41° 26'02"S, 174° 07'50"E (seaport marina)

Docking

The best option is a berth at Beauty Point Marina, Low head. This marina is operated by the Tamar Yacht Club.

There is a public pontoon outside the marina and it is also possible to anchor here.
