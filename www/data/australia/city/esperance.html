Esperance - Profile

Location

This holiday centre, about 300 miles east of Albany, is well-known for its brilliant blue water and perfect white sand beaches.

Position 33°52'S, 121°54'E.

Clearance

The Boarding Station for arrival is the anchorage 100m off the Taylor Street jetty in the NW corner of the harbour.

Docking

The yacht club pontoon with slips is on the north west side of the harbour.

The Bandy Creek Inlet is approximately 7 miles east of Esperance and has two floating pontoon with slip. The entrance is well protected by stone breakwaters.
