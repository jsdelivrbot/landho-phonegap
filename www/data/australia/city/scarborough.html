Scarborough - Profile

Location

Scarborough lies just at the southern limit of the cyclone belt and the harbour is well sheltered. It is located to the north of the Brisbane River along the western shore of Moreton Bay in Deception Bay. It is a popular day trip destination for visitors from Brisbane.

Nearby marina coverage includes: Brisbane and the Brisbane River, Manly and Gold Coast.

Position 21° 12'17"S, 153° 06'37"E

Clearance

Scarborough Marina no longer has a clearance facility for yachts arriving from overseas. Yachts could be fined for arriving at the marina from outside Australia without prior customs clearance.

For clearance, yachts can use either Rivergate Marina, Brisbane or Bundaberg Port Marina.

Last updated January 2014.

Docking

There are three marinas with the Scarborough Boat Harbour. Newport marina is south west of the Boat harbour and through the narrow canal which also leads to a marina housing complex.

Last updated January 2014.
