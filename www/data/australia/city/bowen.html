Bowen - Profile

Location

Bowen lies on the northern arm of a 10nm wide bay about 40nm north of Airlie Beach. It has an unusually dry climate. It is a useful place from which to reach the less explored northern end of the Great Barrier Reef.

Position 20°01′S, 148°14′E. (Bowen)

Position 20 16' S, 148 43' E. (Airlie Beach)

Bowen Boat Harbour © Claire Raborar

Clearance

No clearance is possible here.

Docking

The boat harbour here is extremely well sheltered, but small. It is necessary to call ahead regarding availability of a berth or a pile mooring.

There is no anchoring in the harbour, although it is possible outside.

The marina at Airlie Beach provides more facilities, including a fuel berth.

Last updated January 2014.
