Mooloolaba - Profile

Location

Mooloolaba Harbour lies north of Moreton Bay at the mouth of the Mooloolaba River. The harbour is protected from the offshore swell by nearby Point Cartwright. It is a popular tourist resort as well as home to a large fishing fleet.

Nearby coverage includes: Scarborough and northern Moreton Bay,Tin Can Bay and Bundaberg.

Position 26°40′55″S, 153°07′05″E.

Clearance

Mooloolaba is not a clearance port.

Docking

The entrance is well-buoyed and lit and provides an all-weather harbour,

Visiting boats are permitted to anchor in the lagoon at the western end of the harbour. The shelter is excellent and the holding good in a mud bottom.

There are several marinas here.

Redcliffe/Scarborough to Mooloolaba - beware of sandbar entering Mooloolaba because it does move and has to be dredged - check VMR/VCP on current condition.

Mooloolaba to Wide Bay Bar - check Tin Can Bay VMR for bar conditions and the latest GSP waypoints.

The latest information on the river bar at Mooloolaba can be found at www.mooloolabamarina.com.au/mooloola-river-bar-survey
