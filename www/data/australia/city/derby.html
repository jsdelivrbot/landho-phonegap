Derby - Profile

Location

The town of Derby is tucked into the SE corner of King Sound on Australia's NW coast where the Indian Ocean meets the Timor Sea. It is the gateway to the Kimberley region and its dramatic gorges.

Position 17°23′0″S, 123°41′0″E.

Docking

Due to the tidal range here, anchoring overnight near the town of Derby is not possible unless the boat can take the ground.

The maze of islands to the east of the entrance to King Sound is more likely to provide a sheltered anchorage as long as care is taken with the tidal height calculations.

There is a jetty but no other yachting facilities.

Last updated February 2014.
