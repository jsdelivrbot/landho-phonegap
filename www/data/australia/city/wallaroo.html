Wallaroo - Profile

Location

Wallaroo is situated on a very open bay on the eastern side of the Spencer Gulf, about 50nm due south of Whyalla. It has a long jetty for handling grain exports. It is also a ferry port. Just north of this is the entrance to the very sheltered harbour of Copper Cove.

Position 33°55′S, 137°37′E.

Docking

The marina in Copper Cove offers complete protection.

In settled weather, it is possible to anchor off the beach north of the wharf.

Kadina is only a mile or so inland from Wallaroo. Both towns have a range of services which serve the fishing fleet.

Last updated January 2014.
