Gove (Nhulunbuy) - Profile

Location

Gove (Nhulunbuy) is the largest town in Arnhem Land in Australia's Northern Territories. It was built in the 1970's to service the aluminium ore mine. It is home to about 5 thousand people.

The aluminium smelting plant is on the northern coast of the bay but there are many attractive beaches and islands nearby.

Position 12° 12.5'S, 136° 41'E (Gove Harbour entrance).

Clearance

Yachts should contact Gove Yacht Club on VHF Channel 16.

Visitors' permits to visit nearby aboriginal recreation areas can be obtained from Dhimurru Land Management Aboriginal Corporation, Tel. 61 8 8987 3992, dhimurru@taunet.net.au

Docking

Yachts can anchor off Gove Yacht Club, located in Gove Harbour. Nhulunbuy is 5 km to the east.
