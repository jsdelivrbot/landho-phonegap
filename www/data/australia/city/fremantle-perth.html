Fremantle & Perth - Profile

Location

Freemantle lies at the entrance of the Swan River which is towards the southern end of Australia's western coast. it serves as the port for Perth. It is a regular stop-over for many round-the-world rallies and races.

Position 32°04'S, 115°44'E.

Docking

Facilities for yachts improved in Fremantle when it hosted the defence of the America's Cup.

Challenger Harbour is situated to the west of the Fishing Boat Harbour. There are some short stay berths here, as well as some administered by the Fremantle Cruising Yacht Club.

The Aquarama marina is situated up the Swan River and also has visitors' berths.
