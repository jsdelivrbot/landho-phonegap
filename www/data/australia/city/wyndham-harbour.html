Wyndham Harbour - Profile

Location

Wyndham Harbour lies on the west coast of Port Phillip Bay, midway between Melbourne and Geelong, about 30nm south west of Williamstown. The closest town is Werribee.

This will be a useful safe port of call when completed.

Position 37° 58'08"S, 144° 42'41"E (marina entrance)

Docking

Breakwaters, dredging, land reclamation and services infrastructure (including electricity, water, sewer and Wi-Fi) have been completed, with only the berth installation and services connection remaining to be completed.

These works will facilitate a fully integrated marina, creating a safe harbour for recreational craft.

Last updated October 2015.
