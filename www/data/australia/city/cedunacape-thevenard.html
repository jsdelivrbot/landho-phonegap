Ceduna(Cape Thevenard) - Profile

Location

The twin towns of Thevenard and Ceduna at the eastern end of the Great Australian Bight have a large grain and gypsum loading complex at Thevenard and anchorage’s in Murat Bay. Ceduna is a substantial and attractive town and its sandy coves, sheltered bays and offshore islands in the bay make it a popular holiday destination.

Position 32°07′S, 133°40′E (Ceduna).
Position 32°08′S, 133°39′E (Thevenard).

Clearance

There is no clearance for yachts here.

Docking

It is possible to anchor in Murat Bay and alongside the Ceduna town jetty, but keep a sharp eye on the weather.

The yacht club is primary a dinghy club, but welcomes all cruising boats.
