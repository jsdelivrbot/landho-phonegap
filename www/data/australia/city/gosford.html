Gosford - Profile

Location

A holiday town about 80km North of Sydney. It is also the main commercial centre for the Central Coast area.

Location 33° 26' 05" S  151° 20' 23" E

Docking

The harbour area is known as Brisbane Water and lies on the western side of the town.

There are very many places to anchor in this huge area of sheltered water.

In addition to the Sailing Club marina, there is a wharf to the north of it where yachts can med-moor.

Last updated August 2013.
