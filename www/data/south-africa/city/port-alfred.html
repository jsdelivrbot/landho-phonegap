Port Alfred - Profile

Location

Port Alfred lies half way between East London and Port Elizabeth and has been developed into a Marina of "Port Grimaud" style.

Position 33.36,3 S,  26.54,3 E.

Docking

Unfortunately the entry has silted up badly. It is now a dangerous on-shore approach with unreliable 1.6m depth and only 40 ft wide. Local sailors advise anyone to only use Port Alfred in absolutely settled weather and ideally with local knowledge.
