Peros Banhos - Profile

Location

In the northwest of the archipelago, the Peros Banhos atoll consists of about 30 islets and reefs.

Useful Navigation Notes can be find at this report by SY YOLO, posted July 2015.

Clearance

See BIOT Formalities for full details on applying for a permit and clearance procedures.

Docking

Most anchorages offer little protection. The following anchorages may be permitted for visiting yachts:-

Peros Banhos, Ile Diamante

Northwest of a line stretching roughly the full length of Diamante.

N (Diamante) 5 deg S 15.17 / 71 deg E 46.63

S (Diamante) 5 deg S 15.94 / 71 deg E 45.56

Peros Banhos, Ile du Coin

Southwest of a line from mid Anglais to two-thirds down Ile du Coin.

N (Anglais) 5 deg S 26.30 / 71 deg E 45.52

Mid (Ile Coin) 5 deg S 26.67 / 71 deg E 45.76

S (Ile Coin) 5 deg S 26.87 / 71 deg E 46.46

Peros Banhos, Fouquet

South of a line from mid Mapou du Coin to East end of Fouquet.

Outer W 5 deg S 27.60 / 71 deg E 47.79

Outer E 5 deg S 27.40 / 71 deg E 48.75
