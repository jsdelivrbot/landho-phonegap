Les Anses d'Arlet - Profile

Location

Located on the south west corner of the island, about 8nm south of Fort-de-France.

Position 14°29′26″N, 61°04′50″W.

Les Anses d'Arlet : ©patrickk

Clearance

See the main Noonsite/Martinique/Formalities page for full details.

Clearance can be made using the terminal at the internet cafe on the front in the bay marked Petite Anse D’Arlet. Their opening hours may be irregular.

Last updated October 2015.

Internet cafe whose computer terminal can be used to clear in.

Docking

There are two bays here. Both are anchorages and both have a pier where it is possible to land with a dinghy.
