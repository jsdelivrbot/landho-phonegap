Havre de la Trinite - Profile

Location

This small town, on the east cost of Martinique, is a centre for the sugarcane and pineapple industries.

Position 14° 45'30"N, 060° 57'33"W (entrance to harbour)

Clearance

There is a Customs Office in the town.

Docking

Stay in the centre of the harbour as it shallows along the shores.

Last updated March 2016.
