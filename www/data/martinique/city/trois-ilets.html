Trois Ilets - Profile

Location

Trois Ilets lies due south of Port-de-France on the opposite side of the bay. It is a major tourist area and well known as the birthplace of Empress Josephine Bonapart.

Position 14°33'N, 61°02'W (anchorage)

Clearance

It is not possible to clear in here.

Customs
Ponton du Bakoua , Pointe du Bout, Trois Ilets
Tel:05 96 66 10 10
Opening hours: 08:00-12:00,15:00-18:00(daily)

Docking

Beware of the many shoals in this area.

The small bay off the town provides an anchorage on the western side.

The pontoon at the head of the bay no longer exists and just the concrete footings remain.
