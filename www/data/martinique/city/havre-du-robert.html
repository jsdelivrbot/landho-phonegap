Havre du Robert - Profile

Location

Position 14° 40'48"N, 060° 52'37"W (Bay entrance)

Docking

This is a very sheltered harbour. The entrance is well marked.

Beware of the coral in the inner part of the harbour.

There is a small marina in the south west corner for fishing boats where it is possible to get jerry jugs of fuel.

There is a market and some provisioning is possible.

Last updated March 2016.
