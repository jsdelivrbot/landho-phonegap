Flensburg - Profile

Location

This attractively situated border town was once one of the most important trading ports for the ships of the West India fleet. It lies between wooded hills at the end of the Flensborg Fjord. The northern shore marks the Danish border.

Position 54°43.3'N, 09°26.2'E.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Ask at the Sonwik marina for clearance details as it is frequently used by international visitors.

Last updated March 2012.

Docking

Flensborg Fjord also offers lots of mooring havens.

A popular stop is the Sonwik marina just less than 2 miles from the city to which there is a ferry service.

Marina Minde at Rendbjorg is just 6nm away on the Danish side.

Last updated March 2012.
