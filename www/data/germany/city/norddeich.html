Norddeich - Profile

Location

Located about 20nm north of Cuxhaven and is an important ferry port serving Juist and Norderney.

Position 53° 37,5’ N, 07° 09,4’ E.

Docking

Good shelter in the harbour. Beware of the training walls in the approach, which cover at HW.

Berth in the W harbour on pontoons.

Last updated December 2013.
