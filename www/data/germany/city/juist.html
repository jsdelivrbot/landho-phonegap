Juist - Profile

Location

This long, narrow sandy island lies between Norderney and Borkum. It is a quiet place as only the fire and medical services have motorised vehicles. Bikes and horse-drawn carts are the normal form of transport. There are two villages on the island; Juist, in the centre  and Loog, a mile to the west.

Position 53° 40.15N, 6° 59.5E.

Docking

The approach to the harbour requires careful timing and pilotage. The lighthouse is merely ornamental and does not serve a navigational purpose. If the ferry is in the fairway to the port, wait until it is either berthed or clear before approaching.

The harbour at Juist : © Christiane Schnee

The harbour dries so only boats which can take the ground can remain more than an hour or so.
