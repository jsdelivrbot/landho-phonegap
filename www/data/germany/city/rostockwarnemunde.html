Location

Warnemunde is the holiday resort and ferry terminal at the entrance of the river leading the 6 miles up to Rostock.

Clearance

Port Authority
Tel:+49 (0)3816 703100 ,VHF Channel 10, 16, 73
Vessels must maintain a listening watch on VHF ch. 16 or 73.

Docking

The entrance is possible both day and night, being well marked with no off-lying dangers.

The large modern marina of Hohe Dune lies to the east of the river entrance, on the opposite side of the river to Warnemunde and is rather remote from the town.

The western side of the entrance leads to the yacht harbour and also the fishing harbour (Alter Strom) at Warnemunde. This is close to the town centre

The channel up to Rostock is well marked although many buoys are unlit, so a daylight passage is advisable. There are a number of small yacht harbours on the east bank at Gehlsdorf but these are not so conveient for the city. Futher on there are a number of berthing options on the south bank, including the town quay (Stadthafen). The facilities here are being rapidly improved.
