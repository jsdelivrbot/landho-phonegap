Brunsbuttel - Profile

Location

Brunsbuttel lies on the northern bank of the River Elbe at the entrance to the Kiel Canal.

Position 53° 52' 59" N, 9° 7' 59" E.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Harbour Master
Tel:+49(0)4852 8850.

Docking

Good shelter in Alter Hafen (Old Port marina), which is a drying harbour with access 3hr +/- HW. It is just north of the locks.

A lock gives access to the canal and the Kanal-Yachthafen which is on the north side of the large Neue locks. It gets very crowded in season and due to the amount of canal traffic, it is not a peaceful berth, but useful.

There is a second yacht haven about half a mile further along the canal.

There are no moorings in the waiting area for the canal.

The town has a hospital, banks, post office, provision shops and a train station.

Last updated December 2013.
