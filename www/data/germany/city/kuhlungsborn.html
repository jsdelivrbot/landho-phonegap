Kuhlungsborn - Profile

Location

Lying about 45nm east of Travemunde, this very pleasant seaside town and a popular summer destination. It is a useful staging post along this section of coast.

Position 54°09.21'N, 011°46.38'E.

Docking

The marina is just east of the pier.

Last updated March 2012.
