Rugen Island - Profile

Location

Germany’s largest island boasts long fine sandy beaches and wonderful expanses of beech woods. In recent years its elegant spar resort buildings have been beautifully restored.

Docking

There is a maze of small yacht harbours and quiet anchorages in this archipelago of islands.
