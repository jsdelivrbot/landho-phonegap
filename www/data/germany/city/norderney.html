Norderney - Profile

Location

Another of the Frisian islands. Separated from Juist by 3km, Norderney is a spa town and a popular tourist destination..

Docking

Moor bows-to to the floating pontoon or tie up on the west harbour wall. There are facilities on the quay. Shelter is good, but it gets very crowded in high summer.

The town has provisions, post office, bank, bar and restaurants.

Fresh bread can be ordered from the harbourmaster for the next morning.

Showers and toilets are new. The key is obtained from the harbour office.

Yacht Harbour
24hr access, but tricky at LW depending on draft. At the NE end of the harbour. Crowded in high season. Water, electricity, fuel, 10 ton crane, LPG, some repairs. Yacht club.
