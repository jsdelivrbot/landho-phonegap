Wilhelmshaven - Profile

Location

This useful port lies on the west coast of the Jadebursen (River Jade), about 20 miles directly west of Bremmerhaven. It provides access to the Ems-Jade canal.

Docking

A busy commercial port. There are yacht havens in Nordhafen and in Grosser Hafen. Both of which offer good shelter.

A good place is a small yacht club (MSWHV) right behind the lock on the portside.

There are a good number of yacht clubs here and the city offers all facilites as well as providing an excellent place for getting repairs done.

Last updated December 2010.
