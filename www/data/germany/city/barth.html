Barth - Profile

Location

If time allows, expoloring this attractive, medieval little town would be well worth it. It is on the southern shore of a large, very sheltered cruising area, known as the Bodden. The entrance lies just west of the island of Rugen.

Docking

Enter through the gap in breakwater opposite the church.
