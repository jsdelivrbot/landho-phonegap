Stralsund - Profile

Location

A charming old town with cobbled streets, an attractive port and lively markets. It is on the west bank of the Strelasund, the narror waterway between the mainland and the island of Rugen.

Docking

The harbour is accessed through the twin road and rail lifting bridges. If needing to wait for them to open, there is a yachthaven on the western bank.

Beware that the direction of the buoyage changes at the bridges if proceeding beyond Stralsund, either north or southward.

There are plenty of anchorages in the channel as well as yachthaven berths in the town, slightly outside, or on the island of Danholm.
