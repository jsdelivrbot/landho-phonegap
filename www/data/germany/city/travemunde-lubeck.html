Travemunde (Lubeck) - Profile

Location

Travemunde is the port for Lubeck, which is 11 miles further upstream. Lubeck is a world heritage site and definately worth visiting. The old city is a mass of historic buildings and attractive lanes.

Travemunde is a seaside resort as well as a large ferry port.

Position 53°57.3'N, 10°53.2'E.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Docking

Although it is possible to berth at the Historic-Ship habour (the Hansahafen) in Lubeck, there are no facilities there.

A more pleasant option is to use one on the marinas at Travemude, or Herreninsel or Schlutup, both of which are about halfway to Lubeck.

At Travemude, Passat Hafen is the first marina just inside the river entrance on the southern bank.

The fishing harbour lies further in on the northern bank. It now has water and electricity at the box berths with toilets and showers ashore.

Next is the boatyard of Bobs and then Marina Baltica, both on the northern side of the river.

Beyond this, the river is rather industrialized.

There are good public transport connections into Lubeck.

Last updated February 2016.
