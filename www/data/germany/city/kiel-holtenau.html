Kiel-Holtenau - Profile

Location

This is a major port with locks into the Kieler Forde. Yachts usually use the Old lock. Shipping is heavy in this area, so a good lookout is required.

Docking

On the N bank, just E of the lock, there is a yachthafen. It has all facilities and there is fuel close by.

Just 4nm further north east is the marina used for the Olympic games, Olympiahafen Schilksee.
