Greifswald - Profile

Location

Greifswald lies at the mouth of the Ryck on Germany's Baltic coast. It is an attractive, quaint and old hanse town, south of the island Rügen.

View of the town; © Wikipedia.org

Location 54° 5' 42", 13° 26' 48".

Docking

The port is very sheltered as it is some 3 miles inland, up the river Ryck. There is berthing in the marina and alongside the wharf in the river.

There is a full range of marina services here in addition to winter storage.
