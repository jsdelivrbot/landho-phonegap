Hamburg - Profile

Location

This lively and popular tourist city is on the river Elbe, about 60 miles from its entrance.

Docking

This is a very busy commercial port with several marinas. There are two marinas for visitors. One is 12 miles from the city and the other (City Sporthafen) is in the city centre.

All the services of a major city are to be found here.
