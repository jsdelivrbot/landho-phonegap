Koper - Profile

Location

This is an attractive town of narrow, cobbled streets with a strong Venetian influence. It is the nearest Port of Entry to Italy with connections to Trieste airport.

Position 45°32.9'N, 13°43.6'E.

Clearance

The Authorities are available here all year.

To clear in, tie up to the short pier in the old harbour, making sure not to obstruct the ferries.

Docking

There are two harbours here. The old harbour (to the west of the town) is the one used by yachts, the other being the commercial port.

Old Harbour

Although sheltered, a surge can enter the old harbour in strong winds from the west, southwest and northwest. There are stern-to berths on the pier on the northern side of the old harbour, but watch the depth.

It is also possible to anchor south of the short pier in the old harbour, but this is rather exposed.

Marina

There is also a marina to the north of the centre of the old city.

When approaching the marina, beware of the swimming area to sta'bd (marked by buoys) close by the entrance.
