Portoroz - Profile

Location

This is a holiday town close to the border with Croatia. Whilst the town itself is nothing special, there are good marina and repair facilities here. In fact Marina Portoroz was the first marina built in Slovenia back in 1974. It is very large, with more than 1000 berths.

Position 45°30.5'N, 13°35.7'E.

Clearance

There are customs and immigration in Piran.

Docking

The marina for visiting yachts is to sta'bd as you come through the entrance. Not an easy place for a multihull to fit in. Berthing is stern-to with a pile for the bow-line set off the dock. Careful manoevuring is required.

Anchoring is not permitted here.

This is a well-sheltered spot and a safe place to leave a boat during the winter.

Last updated May 2016.

Large modern marina (50 visitors berths) with all facilities. Fuel berth, pump out, laundry, chandlery, no internet at berths but hot spots nearby, supermarkets, swimming pool, yacht club and yacht charter, restaurants.
Boatyard with 3 travel lifts up to 60 tons and crane, mast lift, hard standing, hanger storage, all types of repair. Mechanical workshop on site authorised to service Volvo Penta, Besenzoni and Climma, Yamnar, Mercury, Mercruise etc.
