Clearance

Call Port Control when within radio range. A pilot will be sent to escort you into the port. It is necessary to employ an agent. If one has not already been arranged, port Control can suggest one.

The will be many forms to complete.

It will be nessesary to have a guard on the boat if leaving it overnight to travel inland.
