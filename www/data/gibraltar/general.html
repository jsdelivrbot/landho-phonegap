Gibraltar - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +1. Summer time UTC +2 April to September.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 240 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

The Gibraltar pound is (suppose to be) equal to the pound sterling, (which can also be used).

ATMs dispense either Gib pounds (NatWest) or pound sterling (Barclays). None dispense euros.

Euros are accepted in many shops.

No VAT is charged on goods here.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Gibraltar is +350.

Mobile phone - Call your cell phone provider to make sure that they offer phone coverage in Gibraltar. Ask to be upgraded to international roaming.

Consider renting a cell phone or SIM card to use with your personal cell phone if your cellular company does not offer coverage in Gibraltar. rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone.

Emergencies

Police/ambulance - Dial 199 / 112 (mobiles)

Fire - Dial 190

Royal Gibraltar Police - Dial 72500
