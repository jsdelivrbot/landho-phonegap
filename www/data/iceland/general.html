Iceland - General Info

Time Zone

UTC

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Icelandic krona (ISK) of 100 aurar.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Iceland is +354.

GSM networks and roaming agreements with most international mobile phone companies. Iceland Telecom rents mobile phones to visitors.

There are numerous Internet cafes around the country.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Iceland

Emergencies

Emergencies - Dial 999 (112 mobile)
