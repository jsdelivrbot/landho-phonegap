Port Marmara (Marmara Adasi) - Profile

Location

Port Marmara lies on the SW corner of the large, mountainous island of Marmara Adasi. White marble is mined on the northern side of the island and is well-known for its purity. Unfortunately this does mean that the area surrounding the port is an open pit mine and not a pretty sight.

Location 40° 35.1'N, 27° 33.7'E.

Docking

It is a very crowded harbour and yachts are usually directed to berth on the northern quay, where care must be taken to avoid the underwater rubble.

There is excellent shelter here.

Anchoring is also possible in the bay, good holding and shelter from all winds except Northwest.

Last updated June 2011.
