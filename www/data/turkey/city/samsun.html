Samsun - Profile

Location

This ancient port has a large, busy commercial harbour with a purpose-built yacht harbour and marina operated by the Samsun Sailing Club. It lies about 31 miles SE of Bafra Burnu and with direct flights to Istanbul is an excellent choice for crew changes.

The marina is a secure place to leave your yacht if planning inland excursions.

Position 41° 19.4’N, 36° 20.4’E (Yacht Harbour).

Clearance

Port of Entry.

All officials are based in the commercial harbour, near the ferry dock.

Samsun Sailing Club staff can also assist with formalities.

Docking

Be aware of the commercial shipping using this port and the number of ships at anchor in its vicinity.

Berth in the sheltered marina at the yacht harbour, where space is always found for visitng yachts. It is north of the main commercial harbour and about 3 miles from the town.
