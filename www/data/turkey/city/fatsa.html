Fatsa - Profile

Location

A useful, sheltered fishing harbour tucked into the SW corner of Fatsa Korfezi. Whilst there are limited facilities for yachts in the harbour, the town is popular with local tourists and has good shopping and services.

The countryside around Fatsa is very pretty and worth exploring with a visit to the mineral water springs of Ilıca. Other attractions include the Pontus era monastery of Göreği, 5 km west of Fatsa, and the ruins of the Bolaman Castle in town.

Position 41° 02.8’N, 37° 29.5’E.

Docking

There is a lot of room to anchor here but the holding is reported to be poor.

It may be possible to berth stern-to the quay in the southern part of the harbour.

There is water on this quay and a fuel station in town.

The town is approximately a mile south of the harbour.

Last updated January 2009.
