Guzelce - Profile

Location

Located on the northern shore of the Marmara Sea about 25nm west of Istanbul.

Position 40° 00'  N,  28° 30.3' E.

Clearance

The marina can arrange clearance both n and out.

Last updated February 2014.

Docking

There is no other docking except at the marina, which provides good shelter except in SE winds.

Last updated February 2014.
