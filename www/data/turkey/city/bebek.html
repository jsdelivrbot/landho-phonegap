Bebek - Profile

Location

A sheltered bay on the European side of the Bosphorus, roughly half way between the two suspension bridges. Position 41°04.7'N, 29°02.9'E.

Docking

Although afected by wash from passing vessels, this bay provides good shelter. Visitors will usually have to anchor although there may be a berth on the quay available.

There are regular buses to old Istambul which takes about half an hour, depending on the traffic.

An alternative stop might be at Isinye, a norrow inlet 2 miles N of Bebek, if a free berth can be found.
