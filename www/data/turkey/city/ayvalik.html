Location

The town, archipelago and peninsula of Ayvalik lie immediately to the E of the Greek island of Lesvos.

This is a Port of Entry.

The marina office can process the paperwork, or you can visit the offices in the town. Customs and Immigration are at the ferry terminal and the harbourmaster is close to the marina.

Last updated March 2015.

Docking

The archepelago and peninsula provide a large number of good, sheltered anchorages.

If not requiring the services provided by the large marina accross The Lake on the mainland, the small harbour on the southern side of the island of Alibey can make an attractive alternative destination. The water here however is not clean and definitely not for swimming.

Moor to the southern breakwater or, if there is room, on the northern quay close to the village. Cruisers report prices here to be high.

Last updated September 2014.
