Docking

Mudanya now has three ports where it is possible for yachts to berth. One in the centre, one in the village of Burgaz (1nm to the east), an another 3 miles to the west, at Trilye. In addition there is an open anchorage usable in calm weather.

The pier is used by local fishing and cargo boats.

Water, fuel and power are all available.

The town has all services, banks and good shopping.
