Bandirma - Profile

Location

Bandirma Harbour is a large noisy, commercial harbour (Bandirma Korfezi), but there is good all-round shelter here. It is useful as a port of call if the requiremnts of a bigger town are needed.

Docking

Berth alongside the eastern quay in the basin, or, alternatively, stern-to in the NW corner of the harbour.
