Port Atami - Profile

Location

Located on the northern side of the Bodrum peninsula, Port Atami is on the west side of the long inlet, Ilica Buku. Position 37°07'N, 27°25'E.

Docking

As well as moorings in the small marina here, the bay offers several good anchorages with good holding in the mud.
