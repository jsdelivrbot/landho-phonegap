Gerze - Profile

Location

A fishing harbour and resort town that "wakes up" at the weekend. Fifteen miles south of Sinop.

With the building of much-needed breakwaters, this former anchorage is now a useful, sheltered harbour.

Position 41° 47.8’N, 35° 12.15’E.

Docking

Yachts can anchor either side of the central quay, or berth stern-to at the north western quay.

Last updated September 2011.
