Bozburun - Profile

Location

This port, is tucked into the NE corner of Yesilova Korfezi, which is the large bay east of the Greek island of Simi.

Previously it was a centre for the sponge fishing industry, now gulets are built here for the tourist industry.

Clearance

NOTICE 08 July 2014: Bozburun remains closed for checking in to Turkey. Closest alternatives are Datca or Marmaris.

For the full details on Turkey Formalities go here.

Customs are based here and it is now a Port of Entry.

To check in, first visit the port police whose office is inline with the east jetty.They will get you an agent (compulsory ) and the agent will get everything for you while you wait in their office.

Last updated July 2014.

Docking

Berth stern-to where there is space in the harbour, or, alternatively anchor just east of the harbour. The bottom is sand and mud and is good holding. The dinghy can be ties up NE of the harbour.

Shelter is good but strong southerlies can be uncomfortable.

There are toilets and showers available.

Last updated April 2013.
