Location

This island lies roughly 18 miles south of the Dardanelles and is about 3 miles off the mainland. The harbour is on the NE of the island.

Clearance

The Customs office is by the pier. Port Police are also based here. Some parts of this island are subject to military restrictions, so care should be taken when anchoring away from the harbour area.

Docking

Berth stern-to or alongside the north breakwater, which now has a proper quay. Water and electricity. Good shelter.
