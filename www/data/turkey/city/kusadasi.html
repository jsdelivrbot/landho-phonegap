Kusadasi - Profile

Location

A large tourist town on the mainland, just north of the Greek island of Samos. A popular destination for cruise ships.

Position 37°52'N, 27°15.6'E.

Clearance

For the full details on Turkey Formalities go here.

Customs and Immigration are at the Marina. The Harbour Office is in the town by the pier. Officials are reported to be very helpful and efficient and no agent requried here for clearing out.

Last updated June 2015.

Docking

An attendant will direct you to a berth in the large marina. There are laid moorings. Shelter is excellent, although some swell can penetrate the marina in very strong winds. A useful place to winter the boat.

Last updated March 2014.
