Location

Saraylar is on the NE coast of Marmara Adasi (Marble Island). Its claim to fame is its marble breakwater!

Docking

The downside of this, is the fact that marble is still loaded from here, causing a lot of dust and noise.

The best mooring is on the new marble quay on the east side of the harbour.

An alternative option is to anchor in the bay, to the north of the town or in the cove to the east.

There is good shelter here.

Water is very limited and fuel is not always available either.

A few provisions are available in the village.
