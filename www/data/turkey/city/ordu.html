Ordu - Profile

Location

A new fishing harbour constructed 3nm north of the town of Ordu. It is in an area famous for its hazelnut production.

Position 41° 01.2’N, 37° 51.5’E.

Docking

The harbour offers good shelter, but anchoring is probably the best option.

The surrounding countryside is green and pleasant but the harbour is a long way from the town.

Last updated September 2011.
