Kemer - Profile

Location

An increasingly popular summer tourist destination, especially for the "gulet" trips to Phaselis and Olympus.

This is a good port to use for airport transfer.

Clearance

For the full details on Turkey Formalities go here.

Kemer is a port of entry and all the officials are within the marina vicinity. The formalities will be completed by the marina office staff with no service charge.

It has been reported that Immigration Officials here insist that a yacht departs immediately passports have been stamped, which means leaving after 0830hrs and before 1700, whether or not this is suitable for the passage planned.

Docking

The marina here is one of the most popular in Turkey, especially with long-term cruisers and live-aboards. The winter period is a whirlwind of social activity! It is advisable to book in advance.

It is also possible to anchor with both good holding and shelter on the east side of the headland.

Good shelter in the marina.
