Altinkum (Didim) - Profile

Location

This booming resort town lies on the northern coast of the large Gulf of Korfezi. It lies close to the 6th century BC Temple of Apollo.

Position 37°20.21'N, 27°15.83'E.

Clearance

This is now an official Port of Entry.

Clearance can be made at the marina.

Last updated March 2014.

Docking

The anchorage off the town is not the quietest place to be, but the marina (completed in 2009) just to the SW is very comfortable and offers all services. It is now completely operational with an excellent yacht club and discounts on offer.

Last updated July 2012
