Kurucasile - Profile

Location

Kurucasile is a quiet town, believed to be the place where the first boat was built. The town still continues to build the most beautiful schooners.

Position 41°50.9’N, 32° 43.6’E.

Docking

This harbour provides good shelter.

On the eastern side of the harbour it may be possible to go alongside the quay if there is space.

There does not appear to be any water or electricity on the quay here.

The western side of the harbour is shallow.

Last updated September 2011
