Ekincik - Profile

Location

This quiet little bay lies about 20nm east of Marmaris. If looking for a peaceful berth from which to enjoy unspoilt Turkey, then this is the place.

Position 36° 49'N, 28° 33.9'E.

Docking

The bay is open to the south, but the small marina is tucked into a sheltered corner on the eastern side.

Last updated March 2014.
