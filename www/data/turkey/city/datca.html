Datca - Profile

Location

This sleepy little port on the southern shore of the Dorian Promontory (Datca Yarimadasi) is a very pleasent place to stop and to stock up with provisions.

The Harbour Master is on the south breakwater.

Docking

The harbour has been improved, and a pontoon covers the shallows in front of the quay wall. Berth stern-to where possible. Shelter is good, although southerlies can cause a surge at the berths, when anchoring off is a better option.

In the summer, this is a crowded harbour and it may be necessary to anchor off anyway, either in the north or south bay. Good holding in sand and mud.

The pump-out station here is reported not to have the correct fittings for small yachts to pump out.

Last updated August 2016.
