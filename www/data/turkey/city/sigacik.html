Sigacik - Profile

Location

Tucked into a small bay on the eastern side of Sigacik Korfezi, this is an interesting sheltered harbour with a small walled village.

Position 38°11.8'N, 26°47'E.

Docking

This well-sheltered harbour is now a yacht marina. Berth where directed.

In settled weather, it is possible to anchor in the bay. There is plenty of quay space in front of the town to berth a dinghy.

Last updated March 2014.
