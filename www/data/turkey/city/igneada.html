Igneada - Profile

Location

Igneada is 68nm NW from the Bosphorus, close to the border with Bulgaria. The harbour is 2 miles across the bay from the town, tucked in below Kuri Burnu.

Position: 41º 53'N, 28º 02'E.

Clearance

This is no longer a port of entry, but the Sahil Güvenlik (Customs) are likely to visit your yacht and request ship’s papers.

Docking

This purpose-built fishing harbour provides excellent shelter.

Anchor in 5m in the southern part of the harbour, or stern-to where there is space on the NW quay. Beware of the under water rocks here.

The harbourmaster is friendly and welcoming to visiting yachts.

Last updated January 2009.
