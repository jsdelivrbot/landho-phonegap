Oren - Profile

Location

This small village lies about 30nm east of Bodrum on the northern coast of the Gulf of Gokova. It is easily identified by the tall chimney of the power station.

Position 37° 1.42'N, 27° 59.6'E.

Docking

There is a new marina here due to open in May 2014.

The bay is too deep for anchoring and is subject to strong down gusts.

Last updated March 2014.
