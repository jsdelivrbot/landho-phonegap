Inebolu - Profile

Location

Inebolu is a typical Black Sea port town with many fine examples of traditional Turkish architecture in the Ottoman style. It's large, multi-purpose commercial harbour, can provide shelter for visiting yachts.

Position 41° 58.9’N, 33° 46.7’E.

Clearance

Port of Entry - all officials located in the S of the harbour.

For the full details on Turkey Formalities go here.

Docking

This is a good, sheltered harbour, however watch out for swell in easterly winds and wash from the commercial traffic.

If space is available, alongside berthing is possible on the northern quay (closest to town) at the western end, but beware of the underwater rubble in the corner. It tends to be noisy here with the lorries coming and going.

Alternatively, anchor in mud in the southern half of the harbour, clear of the coatguard's quay. The holding is uncertain.
