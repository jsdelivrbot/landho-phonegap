Gulluk - Profile

Location

Located north of Bodrum, on the eastern shore of the Asin Korfezi. It is an expanding tourist town.

Be aware of the many fish farms in this area.

Position 37° 14'20"N, 27° 35'41" E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

For the full details on Turkey Formalities go here.

All the Authorities, including the Harbour Master are based on the waterfront.

Last updated August 2016.

Docking

This harbour has very little space for visiting yachts. If able to do so, arriving to clear in in the morning calm will allow you to temporarily berth at the shore end of the cargo pier. Later in the day, with the "meltemi" blowing, this berth becomes untenable. Shelter can then only be found tucked behind the breakwater of the inner harbour.

A more sheltered option may be found in the new marina about 1 nm south of the harbour.

Last updated August 2016.
