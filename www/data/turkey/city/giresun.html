Giresun - Profile

Location

This area is rich in agriculture, growing most of Turkey's hazelnuts. Although this is a large commercial port, it is not very busy and it makes a very pleasent stop.

Position 40° 55.1’N, 38° 22.4’E.

Docking

The fishing harbour is very busy with the fishing fleet but the security here makes this a more desirable option than the commercial harbour.

Yachts are best to anchor in the outer fishing harbour.

In the commercial harbour, the Giresun Yacht Club (now appears closed) has a small dock near the end of the inner breakwater. With permission, visiting yachts may be able to use this. This mooring is less secure than in the fishing harbour.

As this is a commercial port, wash can sometimes be a problem.
