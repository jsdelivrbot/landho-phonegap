Karacasogut, Gulf of Gokova - Profile

Location

This very attractive and sheltered little bay (also known as British Harbour) is tucked away on the southern shore at the east end of the gulf of Gokova Korfezi. It has become a place to over-winter now that a small marina has been established here.

Docking

Berth stern-to at one of the jetties.

It is also possible to anchor and take a long line ashore. The depths fall off quickly into the middle of the bay.

Good shelter but with occasional brief gusts.

Ther are 3 places to moor:

1) Belediye pontoon
This is new and a “proper” pontoon on the SW side of the bay, with water and electricity.

2) Karacasogut Jetty
On the SSE side of the bay, this is owned and operated by the restaurant  it leads to. A sign in the restaurant (which only opens during the sailing season) reads: "Owner is Torga, mobile 0539 885 6653". Water, electricity, toilets and showers.

3) Global Sailing School
This hotel, spa, swimming pool and restaurant is located to starboard when entering the bay. Offer year-round, winter, summer and intermittent mooring.
