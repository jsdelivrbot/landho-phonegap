Location

Erdek Harbour is located in the East corner of the Gulf (Korfezi in Turkish) of Erdek on the Southern shore of the Sea of Marmara, opposite Marmara Island (Adasi). It is an attractive town with shady cobbled streets.

Docking

Erdek Harbour has a designated naval anchorage and a breakwater where small coasters and fishing boats moor.

Berth stern-to where directed. The holding is good in the sandy bottom and the harbour offers good shelter from the wind.
