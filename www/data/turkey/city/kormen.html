Kormen - Profile

Location

Located on the northern side of the Datca Yarimadasi. This small harbour is on the S side of the bay by Ince Burnu, surrounded by mountains.

Position 36°48.2'N, 27°37'E

Docking

Care need to be taken when making the harbour entrance in a swell.

Berth at the quay, leaving the northern end free for the ferry, or moor stern-to on the northern breakwater. The holding is generally good.

Currently a new marina is under construction 5 km. away from the centre of Karaköy village, on the north side of the Datca Peninsula. It is due for completion in 2016. When open, the ferry dock will also be here.

Good all-round shelter.

Last updated September 2015.
