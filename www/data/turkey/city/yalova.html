Yalova - Profile

Location

A brand new Setur marina opened here in August 2010. It is a convenient place to berth if wanting to visit Istanbul but not pay the high prices of the marinas there. A frequent ferry into Istanbul leaves from next to the marina.

Last updated June 2011.

Docking

Cruisers report the marina is virtually empty and that special rates are currently available to attract visitors. These work out to about 60 percent of the large marinas' rates closer into Istanbul.
