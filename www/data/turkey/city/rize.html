Rize - Profile

Location

Rize lies 16nm east of Yeniay and Çamburnu, close to Piryos Burnu. Industry and shipping here centres around tea grown in the surrounding area. Bright green tea plantations abound, as well as avocado and kiwi orchards. Don't miss the tea festival here in the last week of June.

Position 41° 02.4’N, 40° 30.9’E.

Clearance

This is a port of Entry.

Docking

Yachts are best berthing in the fishing harbour, which lies just to the south of the commercial harbour and nearer the centre of the town.

In the Commercial Harbour berth as directed by the Harbourmaster or failing that, med-moor to the small stone jetty in the SW corner of the harbour.

The Fishing Harbour is more comfortable and sheltered. On entry ask permission to anchor or pick up a mooring and tie back to one of the boat sheds that line three sides of the harbour.

This is a provincial capital, therefore everything is available in the city.

For repairs, ask at the port office in the commercial harbour.
