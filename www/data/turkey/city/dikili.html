Dikili - Profile

Location

Situated on the mainland, opposite the southern end of the island of Lesvos. The place is said to resemble a construction site.

Clearance

For the full details on Turkey Formalities go here.

All the Authorities required for entry formalities are based here.

Docking

A small harbour often crowded with fishing boats, but space can usually be found. Berth stern-to on the western breakwater or raft alongisde a trawler. The holding is good and so is the shelter from all directions. Might be wise to avoid if they are loading coal!

Harbour fees 2010 were 50 Euro (115 Turkish Lira), no water and no power.
