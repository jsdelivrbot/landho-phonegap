Colombo - Profile

Location

Comombo is the largest city in Sri Lanka and a very busy commercial port. It lies on the country's west coast.

Position 06° 58' N, 079° 50' E.

Clearance

Arriving yachts should contact the port authority on VHF Channel 16 before proceeding into the harbour.

Yachts are treated on the same lines as commercial shipping; it is not recommended to use Colombo, but if it is necessary to go there, it may be advisable to use an agent.

A naval security inspection will be carried out before entering the harbour. Clearance is done with Customs, Immigration and Health.

See Sri Lanka/Clearance Agents for a list of agents.

Last updated March 2015.

Docking

There is no known dedicated yacht berths here.
