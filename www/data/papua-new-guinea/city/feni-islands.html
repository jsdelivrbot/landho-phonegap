Location

The Feni Islands lie to the east of the main island of New Island. The largest one in the group is Ambitle. They are about 80nm north west of Nissan Island.

Docking

The Salat Passage is the route between Ambitle Island and Babase island.

This is a message from the islanders there:-

For the attention of cruising yachts in PNG
" To every sailing vessel that passes through the Salat Passage off Feni Island, PNG. We, the Bafi Theatre Group invite you to join us ashore to enjoy sightseeing, cave exploration, sacred sights, hot springs, snorkelling, diving and surfing. We organize traditional war dances, basket weaving classes, coconut palm climbing demonstrations, artefact making, traditional storytelling, swimming in natural spring waterholes. We provide home-stays and traditional food i.e. lobster, tropical fruits, vegetables and local produce."
