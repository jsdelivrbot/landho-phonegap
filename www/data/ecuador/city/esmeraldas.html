Esmeraldas - Profile

Location

Esmeraldas lies at the mouth of a large shallow river about 80nm west of the Colombian border.

Position 01° 00'N, 79° 38'30"W (river entrance)

Docking

Although Esmeraldas is not considered a safe harbour (for security reasons), yachts can anchor in the fishing boat section of the artificial harbour, opposite the port captain's office. It is more convenient to come to the Navy dock if there is space.

An alternative option is at the Stella Maris Yacht Club (00° 51.6'N, 79° 55.4'W), in a new marina south of Esmeraldas. This is part of a new resort development.

Last updated October 2016.
