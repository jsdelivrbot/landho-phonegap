Manta - Profile

Location

This fishing port is located about 150nm north of Salinas (La Libertad). Since most of the Ecuadorian fishing fleet is based here, good repair facilities are available.

Position 00° 55'S, 80° 42'W

Clearance

Clearing in here is 400USD with the use of a mandatory agent who cruisers report provides rather poor service.

Docking

It is not advisable however to moor in the fishing harbour as this is unsafe for yachts and unfortunately the yacht club has very limited space.

The Marineros from the yacht club are reliable and can arrange for Diesel.

Last updated March 2015.
