Green Turtle Cay Marina - Profile

Location

Located approx. 35 miles west of the San Blas Islands and 6 miles east of Isla Grande, position 09.35.86 North / 79-26.33 West, at Punta Macolla, Playa Damas and Nombre de Dios.

Green Turtle Cay Marina has been under construction for some years. After partly opening in September 2011, cruisers report that much of the site has been put on hold and what has been finished has started to fall into disrepair.

The marina is accessed via a short canal with a depth of 10 feet, however one of the 2 orange balls to guide you in is reported to be missing. In strong easterlies this entrance can be challenging, however, once inside the marina is extremely sheltered from all directions.

Last updated June 2012.

Docking

Read Panama Formalities for detailed information on procedures for clearing into and out of Panama.

Cruisers recommend this marina for it's good service and friendly staff, however, some facilities have still to be completed.

The travelift and boatyard is due to be opened in December 2012.

The entrance channel is well marked with red and green buoys.

Last updated July 2012.
