Puerto Obaldia - Profile

Location

This is a convienient port of entry for Panama if coming from Colombia.

Note that as of of May 2016 it is now closed to Colombians, Africans and Cubans, in an attempt to stem the migrant flow from Colombia. See news article for more details.

The anchorage here is often untenable and should not be relied upon.

Puerto Obaldia has a large military/police presence because inland, the Darien border between Colombia and Panama is a major drug smuggling highway.

A convenient place from which to visit the memorable town of Sapzurro Colombia and the southern, very unspoiled portion of Guna Yala.

Position 08° 40'N, 77° 24'W.

Clearance

See Panama Formalities for full details on clearing into Panama.

There are Immigration and Customs offices here.

The next nearest port for clearance is Porvenir, Portobelo, or Colon.

Last updated May 2016.

Docking

The anchorage at (08°39.84N, 77°25.33W) is a bay offering no protection when waves are from the NW. Your boat needs to be under constant watch because of the swell. The holding is poor. It is recommended to talk and listen to the locals, they have seen some boats stranded.

Last updated August 2011
