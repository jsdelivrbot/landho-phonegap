Almirante - Profile

Location

A new haulout facility opened here in 2013 (http://www.bocasboatyard.com) just a quarter of a mile outside of town. Reports on the cruising forums are positive in respect of safety, Management, price and service. The Management of the facility is in fact the same management as Bocas Marina, a short ferry ride away.

Almirante town has shops and markets and it's a twenty minute water taxi ride to Bocas Del Toro where you can get a flight to Panama City, or San Jose, Costa Rica. Alternatively catch the bus across the mountains, four hours to David on the Pacific side.

Clearance

Read Panama Formalities for detailed information on procedures for clearing into and out of Panama.

Customs, immigration and agricultural authorities are located here and they will process the documents necessary to clear in and out of the country. Commercial port not really suitable for yachts, reported to be very dirty.
