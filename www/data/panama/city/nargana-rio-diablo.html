Nargana (Rio Diablo) - Profile

Location

The island of Nargana has a population who have decided to give up the traditional Kuna ways, and the village here is very different. There is a bank complete with armed guard, a police station and even a jail.

Position 09° 26.65'N, 78° 34.975'W.

Clearance

Check in at the police station, but note that this is not an official Port of Entry for Panama.

Last updated June 2012.

Docking

It has been reported that canoes and small boats with engines speed around the anchorage and that at night generators run most of the time for the village's street lighting.

Shops here sell a little more, you can get beer and coca-cola, fresh fruit, vegetables and chicken.
