Cebaco Island - Profile

Location

This is a beautiful, well-protected anchorage with a pristine wide beach (both at low and high tide). It is however not protected from any kind of Southerly winds.

The vessel Cebaco Bay is moored here year round and supports fly-in fishing charters.A useful stopover for boats heading north or south to replenish fuel and water supplies without having to check in or go up a river.

Note: Cruisers have reported dirty fuel from here.

Docking

Cebaco Bay Sports Fishing Club is located at the southern end of Isla Cebaco (07deg 29.3'N, 81deg 13.3W).

A 110ft mothership "Cebaco Bay" is moored in the southern cove Ensendada del Naranjo (Caleta Cayman) year round and supports fly-in fishing charters.

They provide fuel (March 2014: $6.50 per gallon (no tax)), have fresh spring water piped in from a deep water spring on the island (potable), supplies, moorings, a restaurant and accommodation.

This is a well run operation with very friendly staff, although of course there is quite a premium for this service. They monitor VHF Channel 6 and it's easy to pull alongside.

As well as the moorings for rent, you can drop your anchor anywhere outside the mooring area. It can be a bit rolly, especially during the night.
