Syria - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Syrian pound (S£) of 100 piastres.

Syrian pounds are not convertible and export is prohibited.

The are a few ATMs in some cities.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Syria is +963.

There is good mobile phone coverage in urban areas, and many networks have international roaming agreements. Internet access is limited, but is available in Damascus.

You can buy a local SIM card which then has to be activated at a local head office to allow calls abroad. Call rates are good.

There are some internet cafes, but they are not always easy to find.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Syria

Emergencies

Police - Dial 112
Fire Service - Dial 113
Medical Emergency Services - Dial 110
