Tartous - Profile

Location

Tartous is a small city and is Syria's second biggest port. Formerly known as Antaradus and Constantia, it has developed rapidly over the recent years. Quite a few ancient sites lie nearby, such as Amrit, Arwad, Krak Des Chevaliers and Al Marqab, all of which can be visited in a day trip.

Position 34°51.4' N, 35°51.6' E.

Docking

There is a very small marina here with very basic facilities. Maximim length is 10m and max. draft is 3m.
