Seychelles - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +4

Electrical Supply

220-240 volts AC 50 Hz.

Seychelles uses the British standard square three-pin, 13 amp electric plug.

Money

Seychelles rupee (SR) of 100 cents.

Credit cards are widely accepted.

ATM facilities exist at major banks on Mahé, Praslin and La Digue and at the airport on Mahé and Praslin. These machines provide cash in local currency, Seychelles Rupees (SCR).

Communications

International Dialing code for Seychelles is +248.

rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone.

Internet cafes can be found around central Victoria, in the Beau Vallon area on Mahé, as well as on Praslin and La Digue.

Postal services are available  in Victoria  and on Praslin and La Digue.

Transport

Driving is on the left-hand side of the road.

Car hire is available only on Mahé and Praslin.

Bicycle hire is readily available on La Digue and Praslin.

Air Seychelles operates a 15 minute shuttle service between Mahé and Praslin 20 time a day.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Seychelles

Emergencies

Fire, Police or Ambulance - Dial 999.

Central Police Station: Dial 4 28 80 00

Medical emergency - Dial 151

Seychelles Hospital: Dial 4 38 80 00
Praslin Hospital: Dial 4 23 23 33
La Digue Logan Hospital: Dial 4 23 42 55

Indian Ocean Cruising Guide
By Rod Heikell
Publisher: Imray,Laurie,Norie & Wilson Ltd (2nd edition 2007)
ISBN-13: 9780852889725
A good general cruising guide covering the routes from Europe to Australia and points in between. The 'Indian Ocean Cruising Guide' covers all the usual points of interest for cruising yachtsmen, from history to climate, weather patterns, formalities, route planning and so on. This second edition has been thoroughly revised and contains a lot of additional information on Malaysia and Thailand, the Seychelles and the Chagos archipelago

Seychelles Nautical Pilot
By Alain Rondeau
Publisher: Praxys Diffusion - 2nd edition (2007)
ISBN-9782916083063
Covering the achipelago of the Seychelles this guide contains descriptions of ports and moorings on the following Islands, including coloured chartlets and colour photographs. Mahe, Silhouette, Praslin, North Island, La Digue, Fregate, Bird Island, Amirantes Isalnds, Far Away Islands. There is also information on Climate, Winds, GPS Positioning, Charts, Tides, Sea Conditions, Cyclones, Bouyage, Lights and History.
