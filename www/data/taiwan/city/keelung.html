Keelung - Profile

Location

Keelung is located in the northeast of the island and is Taiwan’s largest seaport. One of the main attractions here is the Night Market. A new marina opened here in 2015.

Position 25°09' N, 121°44' E (marina)

Clearance

The Security Dock is at the north end of the harbour. Clearance is possible at the new Bi-Sha Marina in Keelung.

Port Authority
VHF Channel 16

Docking

In Bi-Sha fishing harbor (Bisha) next door to the large Keelung port, a new marina has now been completed (see photo).

There is also a marina with floating pontoons at Kang-tzu. Position 25°09' N, 121°44' E.
