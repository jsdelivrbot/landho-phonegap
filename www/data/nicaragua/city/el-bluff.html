El Bluff - Profile

Location

El Bluff is the small port for the city of Bluefields, which is about 8km away at the mouth of the Escondido River. The peninsula of El Bluff forms the northern arm of Bahia de Bluefields.

Position 11°59.5'N, 83°41.3'W.

Clearance

Yachts should dock at the customs wharf for clearance, where customs and health officials will board.
