Ras al-Khaimah (Mina Saqr) - Profile

Location

Al Jeer Port sits on the UAE-Oman border about 70 kilometres from mainland Iran, in the Strait of Hormuz. It provides a useful jumping off point for exploring Oman's Musandam peninsula, a popular tourist destination known for its diving and fishing.

Yacht Clubs

Ras Al Khaimah Sailing Association
http://raksa.squarespace.com , raksa.sailing@gmail.com
Opening hours: Fridays
This is an enthusiastic ex-pat sailing club, who warmly welcome visitors. It is based on the northern side of the bay of Khor, under the large radio mast. It has boat shed fitted out with small tools such as jig saws, grinders etc. including a welder, a jetty and mooring buoys. Large sailing cruisers can no longer be accommodated due to a change in the Khor which has placed power lines across the entrance. This may be rectified in the coming years.
