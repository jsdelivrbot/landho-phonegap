Dubai (Port Rashid) - Profile

Location

September 2008 brought news reports that there is an on-going problem here with raw sewage flowing into the harbour. It appears that sewage truck drivers are illegally dumping their tanks in storm drains due to long queues at the official sewage treatment plant.

Clearance

Yachts should call Dubai Creek Port Control on Channel 16 and ask for directions. The Q flag and UAE courtesy flag must be flown. Clearance is carried out at the main port. Clearance from the last port, crew lists, customs declaration and passports, with valid visas, will be requested.
