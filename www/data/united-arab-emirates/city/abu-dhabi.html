Docking

This is a big commercial port, but there are many marinas here mainly catering for power boats, of which there are a large number here.

The area of Al Bateen, to the west of the city centre, has several harbours and marinas and is close to the open sea.

The Abu Dhabi fishing harbour is a colourful, if crowded destination. It lies just south of the commercial docks, to the east of the city.

If wishing to find a quieter location, it is possible to enter the the Abu Dhabi inter-coastal waterway at Mina Zayad, passing under the new Saadiyat bridge.

If you are a motor racing fan, then wending your way for about 8nm to the new Yas Southern marina would be an ideal destination as it is adjacent to the new racing circuit. It is important to follow the marked channel as there are shallow patches. See www.yasisland.ae island-maps-and-directions/ for detailed directions. This marina is now managed by Camper & Nicholsons and has recently added additional berths.

Several more marinas are planned for the Yas cays area of the waterways.
