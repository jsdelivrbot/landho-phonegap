Santo Antao - Profile

Location

Lying just 8nm west of Sao Vicente, this verdent island is very different from its dry neighbour. In spite of the lack of a totally secure anchorage, it is worth visiting.

Position 17° 00'55"N, 25° 03'46"W (Porto Novo anchorage)

Docking

Porto Novo, on the south east coast of the island provides the only reasonable anchorage, but even there, it is advisable to leave a member of the crew on board when exporing ashore.

Last updated June 2012.
