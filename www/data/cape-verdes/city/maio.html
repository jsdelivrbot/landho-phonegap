Maio - Profile

Location

This island lies to the east of Santiago. It is a low, dry island with plenty of fish, birds, turtles and deserted beaches.

Position 15° 08'20"N, 23° 13'00"W (Porto Maio anchorage)

Docking

Visiting yachts are rare here and and theft not a problem.

There is a possible anchorage at Porto de Maio on the SW side of the island.
