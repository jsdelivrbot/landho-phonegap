Fogo - Profile

Location

A spectacular, lush island west of Santago. The fertile soil, particularly inside the crater, allows for the production of wine.

Position 14°55'25"N, 24°30'14"W (port N of Sao Filipe)

Clearance

Reported o.k. to visit here, even after checking out at Praia.

Docking

On the west coast of the island there is now a small port, north of Sao Filipe, where it might be possible to berth or anchor.

There is also an anchorage south of the port in 4m of water, over sand, but the holding is not too good and it is wise to leave someone on board when going ashore.

Last updated March 2016.
