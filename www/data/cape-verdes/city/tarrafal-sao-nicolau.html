Tarrafal (Sao Nicolau) - Profile

Location

This mountainous island lies about 70nm due west of Sal. Tarrafal is located on the west coast of the island. It is one of the only places on the island with a (black) sand beach. The peaks cause sufficient rainfall to support the growing of fruit and vegetables here.

Position 16° 34'07"N, 24° 21'41"W (anchorage).

Clearance

Not offically a Port of Entry, but in practice, the Harbourmaster will clear you in, although with no Immigration Authority stationed here, passports cannot be stamped.

Docking

Pretty anchorage although select a spot to anchor with care; too far south and the swell causes severe rolling; too far north and the bottom is rocky with poor holding. The water is reported to be very clear here, more than 10 metres.

One of the best islands to visit. Friendly people and few security problems. A Dutch ex-pat living on the island is a good contact.

Some shopping is possible ashore at a fresh produce market. Water from a tap (erratic) on the shore. Fuel by jerry jug from the service station.
