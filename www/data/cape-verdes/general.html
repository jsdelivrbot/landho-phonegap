Cape Verdes - General Info

Time Zone

UTC -1

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Cape Verde escudo (CVEsc) of 100 centavos.

Local currency cannot be imported or exported. Foreign currency must be declared on arrival and departure.

Most ATM's accept both Visa, Maestro and MasterCard.

Be prepared, prices for food are comparable to European prices, sometimes even more expensive! A welcome exception is diesel fuel.

 

Communications

International Dialing code for Cape Verdes is +238.

Mobile phone coverage is spotty, but you can normally get a signal in the major tourist resorts.

Internet is available if you buy a 3G SIM card, and significantly cheaper than buying access in for instance Marina Mindelo.

In Escargo and Mindelo there is free internet (KoneKta) and in Mindelo there are several cafe's offering Internet.

Foreign Embassies in Cape Verdes

Emergencies

Emergency numbers in Cape Verde

Police- dial 132
Fire Service - dial 131
Medical Emergency Services - dial 130


Marine Rescue Coordination Center: Tel. +238 324 492
