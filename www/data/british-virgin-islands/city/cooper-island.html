Cooper Island - Profile

Location

Cooper Island lies east of Tortola, just across Sir Francis Drake channel .

Position 18° 23'04"N, 064° 31'50"W (moorings)

Docking

Anchoring in Manchioneel Bay on Cooper Island is not permitted due to the bottom being covered with sea grass.

There are now 30 mooring buoys, which you pay for at Cooper Island Beach Club bar. This bay is very sheltered from the prevailing winds.

This island is certainly worth visiting, but you need to arrive early to get a mooring buoy.

Last updated February 2014.
