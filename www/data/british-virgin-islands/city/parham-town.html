Parham Town - Profile

Location

Located on Tortola's south eastern shore at Fat Hogs Bay.

Position 18° 26'N, 064° 33'40"W

Docking

There is a small marina, Harbour View marina, off the town and another two at the eastern end of the town in East Bay. Hodge's Creek marina is in Maya Cove, which is south of the town and is approached by going round Buck Island (the passage between the mainland and the island is impassable).

Anchoring

There is limited room to anchor at Maya Cove. There is also a good anchorage at the northern end of Fat Hog Bay.

Last updated February 2014.
