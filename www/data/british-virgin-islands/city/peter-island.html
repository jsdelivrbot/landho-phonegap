Peter Island - Profile

Location

Peter Island lies between Norman and Salt Islands, across the channel from Road Town.

Position 18° 21'33"N, 064° 34'39"W (Sprat Bay)

Docking

At Sprat Bay,on the north of the island, there is deep-water marina and a few mooring buoys. The buoys cannot be reserved in advance and are expensive. There is a dinghy dock at the marina.

There are a few moorings in Great Harbor.

There are anchorages at Little Harbor, Great harbor and Sprat Bay.

The anchorages at Deadman's Bay, White Bay and Key Bay are best for day stops only..

Last updated February 2014.
