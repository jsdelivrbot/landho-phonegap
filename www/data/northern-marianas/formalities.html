Clearance

The Port Superintendent must be notified of the ETA of arriving yachts.

Call the Port Captain on VHF channel 16.

Last updated November 2015.

Immigration

Passport must have 60 days validity.

Immigration requirements for Guam and the Northern Marianas are, in general, the same as for mainland USA. See Noonsite/USA/Immigration. This, therefore, requires visitors arriving by yacht to have a US visa in advance of arrival.

From 2015, the new Guam-CNMI VWP (Visa Waiver Program) will allow an authorized period of stay of up to 45 days when arriving by commercial carrier.

Possession of a US visa will permit entry by citizens of most countries, except that people from the following countries, cannot enter the Northern Mariana Islands at all (so also no visa is granted whatsoever):-
Afghanistan, Algeria, Bahrain, Bangladesh, Cuba, Egypt, Eritrea, Fujian Province of China, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Myanmar, North Korea, Oman, Pakistan, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Somalia, Sri Lanka, Sudan, Syria, Tunisia, United Arab Emirates and Yemen.

Last updated November 2015.

Customs

Firearms should have valid licences from the owner's own country.

On entry, licensed firearms will be held by Customs until departure. All non-licensed firearms will be confiscated and destroyed.

The import of narcotics, certain foods (including fruits, vegetables, and meat), plants, plants products and other plant materials, soil, birds, snails and other live animals are prohibited.

Last updated November 2015.

Documents

Yachts wishing to visit some of the outer islands must obtain special permission from the authorities in Saipan. It is advisable also to contact the Fish and Wildlife Office in Saipan, who are extremely concerned that certain plant diseases may be introduced accidentally into the outer islands.

Fees

Dockage fee: under 100 feet, US$18 per day.

Restrictions

Guguan, Asuncion and Maug islands are wildlife reserves and access to them is prohibited.

Pets

There is a 4 months quarantine for dogs and cats for which there is a daily charge.
