Kahului - Profile

Location

Lying on the north coast of Maui, Kahului is a commercial port, but one which welcomes cruising boats.

Position 20°53.4'N, 156°28.2'W.

Clearance

Yachts should berth at the commercial wharf for clearance before anchoring in the harbour.

Docking

This is a good, clean harbour which is well protected.

There is an anchorage for yachts in the south of the harbour, between the turning basin and the beach. Boats are allowed to stay 3 days before moving on.

Report to the Harbour Master, once anchored.

There is no safe or convenient place to leave the dinghy.

Last updated December 2009.
