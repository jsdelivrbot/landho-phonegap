Kikiaola - Profile

Location

Located two miles west of the Waimea River mouth and close to the town of Kekaha on the southwestern shore of Kauai.

Position 21° 53.16'N, 159° 29.585'W.

Docking

This is a fairly shallow bay but it may be possible to find a suitable stop to anchor among the moorings. The holding is good in the sand, but beware if there is a swell running.

There are toilets and showers here.
