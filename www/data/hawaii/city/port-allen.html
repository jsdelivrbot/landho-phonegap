Port Allen - Profile

Location

This small harbour is on the southern coast of Kauai.

Position 21°54.0'N, 159°35.6'W.

Clearance

Although a designated 'Port of Entry', the office based at Lihue Airport has been reported as unable to clear yachts into Hawaii.

Last updated September 2013.

Docking

The small boat harbour is on the eastern side of the port.

It is best to call ahead for a berth as the harbourmaster, who looks after several harbours, may not be on site when you arrive.

Anchoring here is strongly discouraged due to the high chance of the boat dragging ashore.

A swell enters here in southerly winds.

Wahiawa Bay, just to the SE of the town, is a useful, though small, anchorage. The holding is good in mud and sand.

Last updated September 2014.
