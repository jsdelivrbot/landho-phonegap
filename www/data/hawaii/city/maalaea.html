Maalaea - Profile

Location

Ma'alaea lis about 12M SE of Lahaina. As one of Hawaii's favourite surfing spots, it is not surprising to note that this is a windy location.

Position 20°47.58'N, 156°30.6'W.

Docking

There are no allocated visitos' berths or moorings here, but the Harbourmaster may find you a free berth for a day or two.

Beware on the reef which runs North/South through the middle of the harbour.

This harbour suffers from surge even during settled weather and in less benign conditions heavy warps and even chains can be snapped. The constant wind also blows dust and dirt over the boats here.
