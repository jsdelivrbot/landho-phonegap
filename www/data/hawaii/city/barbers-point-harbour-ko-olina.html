Barbers Point Harbour (Ko Olina) - Profile

Location

This man-made harbour is located about 2 miles north of Barbers Point on the south west corner of the island of Oahu.

Position 21°19.3'N, 158°07.8'W.

Docking

Previously only open to commercial shipping, the marina here now offers excellent shelter to crusing yachts.

Call the Harbourmaster in advance to be allocated a berth, if one is available. If arriving after dark, tie up at the fuel berth.

Last updated January 2010.
