Hanalei Bay - Profile

Location

Located on the northern coast of Kauai, this deep bay is one of the best summer anchorages in Hawaii.

Position 22°13.9'N, 159°30.6'W.

Clearance

Foreign vessels are reminded to contact the following departments:-

U.S. Department of Agriculture (808) 245-2831

U.S.CUSTOMS, Port Director (808) 822-5673

Last updated September 2014.

Docking

In the northeast corner of Hanalei Bay, there are 40 Seasonal moorings from May - October. Permits are required. Contact the Kauai District Office before arrival.

There is a large choice of spots to anchor in the bay. The holding is good in the sand. The lifeguard place flags on the beach where dinghies may be landed.

The pier is for fishing and swimming only. No berthing here.

Toilets and (cold)showers ashore.

Last updated September 2014..
