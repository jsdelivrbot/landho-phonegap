Grand Baie - Profile

Location

To the north of Port Louis, Grand Bay offers a good anchorage and many attractions ashore. There is a yacht club, The Grand Bay Yacht Club, with facilities available free for visiting yachtsmen, and an active social and racing programme.

Position 20° 00'22"S, 57° 34'29"E (bay entrance)

Clearance

This is not a clearance port. Port Louis must be visited first.

The Grand Bay Coast Guard will visit you as soon as you come into the bay and may board you to check your papers, as will other Coast Guard units at other ports in Mauritius.

Last updated September 2015.

Docking

The channel leading into the bay is at least 3m at high tide.

There is a good anchorage in front of the yacht club at Grand Baie. A less rolly one can be found further into the bay by the moorings.

Last updated September 2015.

The Grand Baie Yacht Club offers free, temporary membership to visiting sailors for up to 2 months and prides itself on its long tradition of welcoming foreign yatchsmen. The club has good food, as well as cold beer and hot showers. Water and fuel can be obtained from the jetty.
