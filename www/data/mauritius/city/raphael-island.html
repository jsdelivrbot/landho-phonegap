Raphael Island - Profile

Location

Raphael Island is part of the Cargados Carajos archipelago  which lies approximately 200nm north east of the main island of Mauritius.

Position 16°27′S, 59°37′E.

Clearance

Call the Coastguard on VHF channel 16 and they will collect your details and pass them on to their HQ.

Last updated May 2013.

Docking

On Cocos Island (16°50′S, 59°30′E﻿) there is a small fishing lodge and an excellent anchorage in 8m over sand. The keeper of the place, Gabi and his wife Mary are very friendly and helpful.
