Black River (Riviere Noire) - Profile

Location

Located towards the southern end of the west coast of Mauritius, this is an area mountains and wilderness. It is less developed than many other areas of the country.

Position 20° 22'S, 57° 22'15"E (river entrance)

Docking

There is a new marina in the Black River and anchorages off the town of  Grande Riviere Noire, as well as in the river itself.

There is a small commercial centre with shops at the marina.

Last updated July 2016.
