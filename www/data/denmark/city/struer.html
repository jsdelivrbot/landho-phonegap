Struer - Profile

Location

This harbour lies at the southern end of the Limfjord and is a major railway junction. It is also the home of the viking ship, Imme Struer.

Docking

The large yachthavn welcomes visiting yachts.

Note Yachts may not be moored in the East Basin or around the Struer Skibsværft (the Struer Shipbuilding Yard).

Last updated October 2011.
