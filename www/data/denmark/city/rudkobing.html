Rudkobing - Profile

Location

This is a large port located on the western side of langeland, just south of the bridge which connects the island to Svenborg on the island of Fyn. This is an attractive town with its narrow streets and interesting shops.

Position 54° 56'28"N, 10° 42'30"E (marina entrance)

Docking

There are three areas to this port. The marina, with 400 berths, is to the north. The middle one is the fishing harbour, and to the south is the old commercial harbour. In the southern part of which both large and small yachts are berthed. It may be possible to find a guest berth here.

In the fishing harbour there is a slipway and a wooden boat building yard.

The marina has allocated guest berths.

Last updated April 2016.
