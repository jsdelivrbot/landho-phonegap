Location

Rodbyhavn lies on the south western coast of the island of Lolland, and is about 45 miles from Kiel. It is a busy port and has a major ferry link to Germany. Although not particularly yacht-friendly, it is a useful stopping point if making for Copenhagen.

Docking

Visitors berth in the North Harbour, which is to the NE once through the breakwater of the outer harbour.
