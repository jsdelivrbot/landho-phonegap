Ronne - Profile

Location

Ronne is the largest port on the island of Bornholm and lies on its Western coast.

Position 55° 06'N,14° 41'E.

Docking

This is the largest yacht harbour on the island and is 0.5nm north of the main commercial/fishing harbour.

It can be entered day or night.

There are several other yacht havens all around the island's coast.

There are good connections to Copenhagen and a fast ferry service to Sweden.

Last updated march 2012.
