Thyboron - Profile

Location

Lying at the western entrance to the Limfjord, Thyboron is the gateway to a great cruising area, as well as marking the beginning of the shortcut through to the Kattegat, in Baltic.

Docking

Shelter is good in the harbour and yachts can berth either in the yacht haven or in the most northerly of the basins of the main harbour. There is another marina at Lemvig, about 15NM away, on the southern shore of Nissum Bredning.
