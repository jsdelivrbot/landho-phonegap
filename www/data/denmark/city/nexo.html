Nexo - Profile

Location

This fishing port (the largest on the island) lies on the west coast of Bornholm. It is just 7nm south of Svaneke and is also a ferry port. In the season the arrival and departure of hundreds of cyclists is quite a sight.

Position 55° 03'54"N, 15° 08'35"E

Docking

Although mainly a fishing port, there is a small harbour with space for visiting yachts.

Last updated June 2015.
