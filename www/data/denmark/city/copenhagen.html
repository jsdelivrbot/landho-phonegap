Docking

Copenhagen harbour is clearly identified from seaward by three tall factory chimneys.

There are many marinas and havens for berthing here. It is more difficult to identify a particular one. A good, large-scale chart is needed for that!

Tuborg Havn
Location 55°43.6`N, 12°35.2`E ,
(just N of Copenhagen) ,
Tuborg Havnepark 15, 2900 Hellerup

Tel:+45 3314 8787 / Harbour Master +45 20133787 ,VHF Channel 13
http://www.tuborghavn.dk , tlb@kdy.dk
Opening hours: Tues- Thurs 0900-1500 and Fri 0900-1400
Originally the commercial harbour for the Tuborg brewery. A full facility marina; electricity, water, showers, WiFi, fuel. Depths 3-5.5m.
Visitors should tie up on the pier near the restaurant or go alongside on the pier located to port as you enter the harbour. All visitor berths have green signs. BBQ grill area and swimming jetty available with beach nearby.
Tuborg Harbour is also the head office for the Royal Danish Yacht Club, the oldest yacht club in Denmark founded in 1866.
Bus to Copenhagen takes 15 minutes from Waterfront Centre where all shopping can be found.
Bikes available to borrow in the harbour.
