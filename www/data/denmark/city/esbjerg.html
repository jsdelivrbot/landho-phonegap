Esbjerg - Profile

Location

Esbjerg is a vibrant small city on the west coast of Jutland, opposite the Friesian island of Fano. The harbour is a busy commercial and fishing port.

Docking

Yachts berth in the SE of the fishing basin no. 11 (opposite the tall chimney). An alternative is the Nordby Marina on Fano.
