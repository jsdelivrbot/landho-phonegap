Gudhjem - Profile

Location

Located on the east coast of Bornholm, roughtly half way between the northern tip and Svaneke. It is a ferry port serving the island of Christinaso.

Position 55°12.9'N, 14°58.0'E.

Docking

There are two harbours here, both offering visitors berths. The eastern one is the larger. The western one can be very uncomfortable in strong westerly winds.

No fuel berth.

Last updated March 2012.
