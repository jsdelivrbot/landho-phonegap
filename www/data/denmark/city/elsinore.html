Elsinore - Profile

Location

This harbour (written Helsingør in Danish) regards itself as the Gateway to the Baltic. It sits immediately opposite the closest mainland point to Sweden.

The commercial port lies just south of Kronborg Castle (Hamlet's castle). It prides itself on being a centre for traditional wooden sailing boats.

Position 56°02.4'N,12°37.2'E.

Clearance

All authorities are in the port.

Docking

As this is a busy ferry port, vessels must call the Port office on VHF channel 12 before entering or leaving.
