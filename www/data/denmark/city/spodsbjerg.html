Spodsbjerg - Profile

Location

This small fishing harbour is the only harbour on Langeland's east coast.

Position 54° 56''N, 10° 50''E

Docking

The yacht harbour is just south of the ferry port and is protected by a double breakwater.

Anchorage: It is possible to anchor outside the harbour.

Spodsbjerg is just a small village but LPG and some provisions are available and there is also a restaurant.

Last updated April 2016.
