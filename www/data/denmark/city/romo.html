Romo - Profile

Location

One of the Danish Frisian Islands, Rømø lies just north of the German island of Sylt. A causeway links this island to the Danish mainland.

Docking

Access to the harbour, which is on the SE tip, is possible in all tides. Yachts berth in about 4m, on the N side of the inner harbour. It is also possible to anchor outside, just N of the entrance.
