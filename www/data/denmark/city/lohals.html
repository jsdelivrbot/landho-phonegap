Lohals - Profile

Location

This lively harbour lies at the northern tip of the island of Langeland.

Position 55° 08'04"N, 10° 54'02"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

This is an ancient, sheltered harbour which has been extensively renovated. There is live music at the harbour every weekend during the summer. North of the harbour there is a good beach.

The harbour is open from 1st April until the end of November.

Last updated April 2016.
