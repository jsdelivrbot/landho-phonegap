Dagelokke - Profile

Location

Lying about 5nm south of Lohals, on the west coast of Langeland, this is an attractive little harbour.

Position 55° 03'48"N, 10° 51'37"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

A sheltered little harbour with min. depth 2m.

Call the Harbour Master to arrange a berth.

There are attractive beaches both north and south of the harbour.

Last updated April 2016.
