Location

This is the most easterly of the Northern Sporades islands. The southern half is rugged and wooded but the northern half is more cultivated. Burial place of the poet, Rupert Brooke.

Docking

There are many anchorages around the island, but the main port is Linaria, midway down the south west coast.

Linaria
A small, attractive harbour with room for about 12 to 14 yachts.
Moor either stern/bows to or alongside on the quay.
Water and elecricity on the quay and WiFi in the harbour.
