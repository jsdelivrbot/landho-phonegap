Ios - Profile

Location

If you are hankering for some nightlife, then this is the island to visit! It is just east of Sikinos in the southern Cyclades. It has very good beaches. It is claimed to be the burial place of Homer.

Position 36°43′N, 25°16′E (harbour)

Docking

The port of Ios is on the west coast of the island, opposite the northern end of Sikinos, 3.5 NM away.

Moor to the quay, but use plenty of fenders and keep well out as the ferries arrive at speed and create a lot of surge. The port police supervise the yacht moorings on the N quay in the evenings. The yellow buoy marks the mooring chain. Holding is good in mud.

Anchoring in the bay is prohibited due to the many fast ferries serving the island.

Last updated November 2009.
