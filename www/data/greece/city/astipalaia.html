Astipalaia - Profile

Location

This is an almost forgotten island in the Dodecanese chain, lying as it does some 35NM west of Nisiros. It consists of two small mountains joined by a low lying isthmus. The port of Skala is on the southern coast of the westernmost island.

Docking

This island offers many good, safe, anchorages. The main port is Skala Astipalaia.

There is now a small marina here with an extended breakwater, with water and electricity. Moor stern or bow-to to the new quay which you will see on entering. Shelter is now good with the new breakwater, although still subject to strong gusts.

It is also possible anchor in the bay. The bottom is sand and weed. One report says the holding is reasonable, another, that it is good!

Beware of the ferry, which is large, and arrives at 7am every day, during which time it blocks the bay for an hour.

Maltzena lies on the southern shore of the central part of the island and the bay here provides a very protected anchorage. It it is unoccupied, it is possible to berth at the pier here. Provisions are avalable in the town.
