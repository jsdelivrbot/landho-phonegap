Glifadha - Profile

Location

Glifada is situated about 5 miles south of Athens and is the site of a proposed large marina development together with a comprehensive shore-side infrastructure. Currently this does not appear to have happened quite as planned.

Clearance

For full details on clearing into our out of Greece see Greek Formalities.

Docking

There are a series of yacht basins here. The largest and most northerly is no. 4 and this is probably the only one where any space might be found. The other 3 are principally private.

In marina no. 4 (Glyphada), berth stern-to where there is space. The moorings are tailed back to the quay. There is good all-round shelter.

It may also be possible to berth at the Olympic Sailing Centre, about 2 miles north of Glifadha. There are basic facilities here.
