Location

This island lies to the NE of Milos. It is a quiet, very green island. Kastro is the attractive chora of the island and where they are still using donkeys to transport goods within its very narrow streets.

Clearance

Port Police and customs are based here.

Docking

The port of Kamares is on the west coast of the island.

Moor off the quay or alongside the mole in 5m in hard sand, but beware of rocks. Good shelter.
