Â Petriti (Corfu) - Profile

Location

Sitting on the northern coast of the "tail" of Corfu island, this little port is a sleepy place which receives few visitors. The harbour is shallow and fisherman move in and out all night long creating quite a swell. However it's a lovely place for a day visit.

Docking

Berth at the west quay or on the end of the mole where there is room.

Anchor in the bay south of the mole in 2 - 3m over sand and mud. Difficult berthing/anchoring for any boat with a draft exceeding 1.80m.

Good shelter, although some swell can creep round into the bay.

Most provisions available in the village. Water on the quay is only for the fishermen.
