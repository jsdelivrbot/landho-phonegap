Location

Lying to the east of Patmos, this island has a small harbour on the south side.

Clearance

Port police and customs are based here.

Docking

There are several options for berthing here. The inner harbour has been dredged to 3 to 5m but the holding is bad in a mixture of sand, mud and weed.

There is a pontoon in the NW corner and also, maybe, room off the new quay on the SE side, near the water boat.

In strong meltemis, anchoring in the southern part of the bay in 3 to 4m is a more sheltered option. Also, the northside of the entrance to the bay, by the chapel, offers good shelter.
