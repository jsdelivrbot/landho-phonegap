Itea - Profile

Location

Located on the northern shore of the Gulf of Corinth, Itea is the ideal port from which to visit the ancient site of Delphi.

Position 38°25.56'N, 22°25.20'E.

Clearance

For full details on clearing into our out of Greece see Greek Formalities.

Port police and Customs are based here.

Last updated April 2013.

Docking

Berth where space is available. The marina is well sheltered but the quay is susceptible to swell and not protected from the S and SE.

Whilst the marina was opened in 2013, it is still unfinished with limited services. For water, yachts need to go to Galaxidi, 7km SW of Itea.

Last updated August 2014.
