Vouliagmeni - Profile

Location

This is a very attractively situated harbour. It is far enough from both Athens City and the airport to offer a more peaceful refuge than many others along this coast.

Clearance

For full details on clearing into our out of Greece see Greek Formalities.

Docking

Shelter is good in the marina, although a strong southerly wind can cause an uncomfortable swell. Moor stern-to where directed. The bow moorings are usually tailed back to the quay.
