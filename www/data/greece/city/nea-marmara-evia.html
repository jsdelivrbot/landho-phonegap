Location

Located on the SW corner of Evia, this fishing village is a useful stop on your way north.

Clearance

There are Port Police stationed here.

Docking

Moor where there is space on the western side of the harbour. Many of the original buoys are now missing or used by local boats so when dropping the anchor to moor bows or stern to, use a long scope to avoid the old ground tackle. Good shelter from the northerlies.
