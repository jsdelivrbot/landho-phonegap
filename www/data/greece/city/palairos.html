Palairos - Profile

Location

Situated on the mainland opposite the island of Levkas.

Position 38°46.9'N, 20°52.6'E.

Docking

This harbour is crowded in the summer with charter yachts and the pontoon here tends to be completely full, especially at weekends. You may have better luck finding a berth during the week.

The western quay is available for visiting yachts and there are a few water taps here too.

The harbour provides good shelter.

An alternative is to use the charter boat base of Vounaki marina, just south of the town.

Last updated June 2015.
