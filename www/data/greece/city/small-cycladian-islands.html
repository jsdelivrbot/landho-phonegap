Â Small Cycladian Islands - Profile

Location

These three small islands lie between Naxos and Amorgos.

Docking

Koufonisia
A small but safe harbour (high charges in summer but no water). The bay on north east side is an excellent anchorage.

Skoinousa
The harbour/pier at Myrsini provides good shelter. There are bays on the southern side of the island which are good in northerlies.

Iraklia
This island has a nice little harbour. Moor only on the inside pier. Be aware when the big ferries and watership come in.There is a good beach here and several possible anchorages.
