Kiato - Profile

Location

Kiato is situated on the Gulf of Corinth, near the mouth of the river Asopos about 12NM NW of Corinth. The ancient city Sicyon was located 4 km southwest of present Kiato.

Although mainly a commercial harbour, recent extensions to the harbour walls have made this tourist town more attractive as a stopping place for yachts. There is a small harbour here, with bus and rail to Athens and Patras, and good beaches.

Clearance

Port Police and Customs are based here.

Docking

Small protected harbour from prevailing NW winds (but not protected from the East). Mooring stern-to, approx. 3m depth. Bottom is littered with local moorings, so take car when dropping and hauling up your anchor.

Drinking water available, but no electricity. Good illumination at night.

If no space available in small harbour, yachts can moor inside the large breakwater.

Good shopping in town (800m from the outer breakwater there is a LIDL supermarket) and ATMs. In front of the LIDL is also a little harbour, so one can anchor and go by dinghy to the supermarket.
