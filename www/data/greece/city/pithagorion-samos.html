Pithagorion (Samos) - Profile

Location

Samos is a high, pine covered island and this port of entry, Pithagorion, looks across to the Turkish mainland. It is a busy tourist resort.

Clearance

For full details on clearing into our out of Greece see Greek Formalities.

Clearing in here should take about an hour walking between the three offices (Customs, Police and Harbour Master).

Last updated June 2015.

Docking

Moor bows/stern-to the West quay in the inner harbour. The bottom is sand and mud with good holding. Beware of the shallows to port (marked by an un-lit beacon) at the entrance to the inner harbour. With the Meltemi blowing, strong gusts will be experienced in the approaches.

The shelter from NW'ly winds is excellent.

The yacht marina here opened in 2009, located just to the east of the outer harbour. Facilities however are basic and cruisers who visited in 2014 have posted useful comments below.

It is a 15 minutes walk from the town.
