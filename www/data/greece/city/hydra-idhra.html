Location

This port lies in the centre of the north coast of the island of the same name. It sits in a natual amphitheatre surounded by stately 18th and 19th century buildings.

Docking

Moor to the north mole or where there is space in front of the town. This harbour gets very crowded and boats can be moored 3 or more deep, practically filling the harbour completely. Be prepared for a wonderful tangle of anchors!
