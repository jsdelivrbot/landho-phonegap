Platarias - Profile

Location

This quiet little port is on the mainland, opposite the southern end of the island of Corfu. It has good restaurants and tavernas and good food supplies. The nice little village has few tourists, mainly yachties.

Position 39°27.1'N, 20°16.5'W.

Docking

Berth stern-to on the southern breakwater. There is also an anchorage off the beach. Try to find a place in the southern part of the harbour. There is water and electricity, cost per day for water €3 - limitless, €3 for elec. plus €3 for your boat. Very good holding in mud.

Good shelter from the prevailing wind.

As with most town quays, rats can be a problem here, so it is important to take the necessary precautions with any mooring lines.
