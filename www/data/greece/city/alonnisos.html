Location

Lying to the NE of Skopelos, this long, thin island is wooded and hilly. Together with several small off lying islands, it forms the National Marine Part of Alonnisos.

Docking

The main port on this island is Patitiri. Moor stern-to off the NE quay or anchor fore and aft off the beach in the western part of the harbour. Limited space.

There are now new service poles for water and electricity available on the quay. Water quality is reported to be excellent. Unless leaving before 6am, you are charged an extra day (at 15 euro per day).

Cruisers report a nasty surge here (see comment below).
