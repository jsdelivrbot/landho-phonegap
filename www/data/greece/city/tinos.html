Tinos - Profile

Location

Lying immediately to the south of Andros, separated only by a narrow strait, Tinos is a green, wooded and extensively cultivated island. It is dotted with small villages.

The town of Tinos is the main ferry port and twice a year is the scene of the arrival of many pilgrims of the Orthodox faith visiting the shrine in the church above the town.

Position 37°32′N, 25°09′E. (Tinos harbour)

Clearance

Last updated October 2014.

Docking

Moor to the northern quay in the inner harbour taking care to avoid the large chain lying on the seabed there. The holding is good. Do not anchor in the harbour as all the room is required by the ferries when manoeuvering.
