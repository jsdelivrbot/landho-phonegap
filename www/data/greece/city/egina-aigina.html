Egina (Aigina) - Profile

Location

Lying on the western shore of the island, this port is about 25NM from Athens and is a very popular destination for people from the city. The travernas on the waterfront are an attractive spot from which to watch the world go by!

Position 37° 44.5' N, 023° 25.7' E.

Docking

Take care on aproaching the harbour entrance as it is very busy with ferries and hydrofoils entering and leavings at high speed.

Moor to the town quay or in the marina. If moored in the harbour, ensure the anchor is well embedded as there is considerable wash from the ferries. Avoiding the weekends might be advisable, making it easier to obtain a berth here.

Good shelter, although strong southerlies can cause some swell.

It is possible to anchor in 3mt over sand and weed patches south of the marina and main harbour. The dinghy can be landed in the eastern corner of the marina, where you can tie it up next to the small fishing boats.

Last updated March 2013.

Aigina Marina
Many moorings have laid line. Not yet completed, but it is in use. Water and electricity may not be connected.
Apply to the port police for a berth.
