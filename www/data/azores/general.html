Azores - General Info

Time Zone

UTC - 1

Summer time UTC April to October.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220v - (Ponta Delgada 110v to be converted to 220 V)

Frequency: 50Hz

Money

Euro

Communications

International dialing code for Azores is +351.

There is a mobile phone network available on all islands.

Events

Horta: Sea Week (Semana do Mar) starting first Sunday in August, a local festival including yacht races.

Emergencies

Emergency treatment is free to all. EC residents will not be charged for a hospital bed, but non-EC nationals may pay. For non-emergency consultations, a nominal fee is charged. Emergency tel. 112.
