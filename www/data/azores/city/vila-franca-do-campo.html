Vila Franca do Campo - Profile

Location

This port lies on the southern coast of San Miguel, about 20nm east of Ponta Delgarda.

Position 37°42'49"N, 25°25'41"W (harbour entrance)

Clearance

Customs and Immigration are based here.

Last updated December 2015.

Docking

The marina lies to the east of the town and provides very good shelter.

The town provides all normal services.

Last updated December 2015.
