Lajes do Pico - Profile

Location

A small port on the SE corner of the island, once centre of the islands whaling industry.

Position 38°23.9'N, 28°15.4'W.

Clearance

This is not a port of entry.

Docking

Entrance into the main harbour is clear if you stay close to the green light of the jetty. Once in the main harbour, there are dangerous patches of rocks almost everywhere and it is shallow. Care should be taken.

The marina here is tucked into the southern portion of the harbour.

The town has a bank, post office, health centre and a supermarket.

Last updated December 2015.
