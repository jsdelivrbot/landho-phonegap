Corvo - Profile

Location

Corvo Island (meaning the Island of the Crow), is the smallest and the northernmost island of the Azores archipelago, and is the smallest single municipality in Azores and in Portugal.

The village of Vila do Corvo is located in the south of the Island, and the only anchorage is here.

Position 39° 40'2N, 31° 06'5W (anchorage)

Docking

Corvo does not have a marina, just an anchorage which is deep and rocky. Visiting boats should not be left unattended because of the poor anchorage conditions and weather instability.

On many days of the year here, no boats whatsoever (including local fishing boats and ferries) can neither leave nor enter due to the difficult conditions.

There are for facilities for yachts here.
