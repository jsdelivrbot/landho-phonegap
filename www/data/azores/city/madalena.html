Madalena - Profile

Location

Though the majority of cruisers visit Pico by ferry from Horta, the number of visiting yachts is increasing. With its striking mountain peak, the obvious excursion here is to the top with spectacular views. The Island also has whaling museums and wineries.

Position 38° 32'11"N, 28° 32'06"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

Visiting yachts usually anchor in the outer part of the harbour, as the docking space is limited and used by ferries and fishing boats.

There is a small floating dock where dinghies can be tied up in the inner harbour.

Last updated December 2015.
