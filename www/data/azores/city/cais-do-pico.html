Cais do Pico - Profile

Location

Located on the north east coast of the island, this is a commercial and fishing port. It has an interesting whaling museum.

Position 38° 31'44"N, 28° 19'02"W

Docking

Visiting yachts usually anchor, as the docking space is limited and used by ferries, fishing boats and, at Cais do Pico, also by container ships.

The harbour is open to the east.

Last updated December 2015.
