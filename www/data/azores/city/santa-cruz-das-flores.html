Santa Cruz das Flores - Profile

Location

Located on the eastern coast, on a very rugged part of the island.

Position 39° 27'N, 31° 07'22"W (outside the port)

Clearance

Yachts are being redirected to Lajes for clearance and discouraged from entering Santa Cruz, partially because of unsafe conditions.

Whilst Lajes is not an official port of entry, the Maritime Police and National Guard based there will check visiting yachts in as an accommodation. They will not stamp passports however and clearing into Europe for immigration purposes will have to be done at the next island or port of call.

Last updated December 2015.
