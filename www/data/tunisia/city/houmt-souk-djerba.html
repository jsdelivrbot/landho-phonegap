Houmt Souk (Djerba) - Profile

Location

Houmt Souk is the capital of the Isle of Djerba, which in the SE of Tunisia, in the Golf of Gabes. The town lies on the northern coast of the island. A relaxed place, with a polynesian feel and surrounded by fine sandy beaches.

Position 33° 53'27"N, 10° 51'24"E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Docking

Berth in the marina or there is an anchorage on west side of the harbour.

Last updated February 2013.
