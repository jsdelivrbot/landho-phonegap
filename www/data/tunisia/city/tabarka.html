Tabarka - Profile

Location

This port is on the north coast, close to the Algerian border and is usually the first landfall for yachts coming from the north. It is classified as a C yachting harbour, and borders the Montazah/Tabarka resort area. It is a useful stop if wishing to visit the underground Roman Villas at Bulla Regia, about 30 miles south of the port.

Position 36° 57' 33"N, 08° 45'52"E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

Docking

Some yachting facilities here.

There are banks, pharmacies, shops and restaurants in the town.

Last updated February 2013.

A picturesque harbour with limited space for visiting yachts who may find space stern-to in the SW corner by the Capitainerie building. There is a fuel pump on the fishing quay. There is a 110 ton travelift and a yard servicing the local fishing fleet.
