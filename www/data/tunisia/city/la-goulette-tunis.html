La Goulette (Tunis) - Profile

Location

This is a busy commercial port and yachts are not able to stop here.

Position 36° 48'N, 10° 18'E.

Docking

Marina La Goulette
Office des Ports Nationaux Tunisiens , 2060 La Goulette , Tunisia
Tel:(+34) 902 091 582 ,VHF Channel 16
This marina is now administered by the Club Nautique de la Marine Tunisienne and access to it by non-members is strictly prohibited. The Port Captain advises arriving yachts to proceed to either Sidi Bou Said or Yasmin Hammamet as even emergency stops are no longer tolerated.
