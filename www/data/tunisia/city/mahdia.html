Mahdia - Profile

Location

Mahdia is a major fishing port with excellent shelter. It lies about 120nm due west of Malta and has an attractive 'Old Quarter'.

Position 35° 29'59"N, 11° 04'24"E (harbour)

Clearance

See Tunisia Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of the country.

This is a Port of Entry and there is a Customs post here. Note however that Customs are not based at the port, they have a large building within the town limits.

It is likely you will be asked to moor alongside the police dock - the port-hand pier in the inner harbour.

They will alert Customs of your arrival who will come and visit the boat.

Officials reported to be polite and efficient.

Last updated June 2016.

Docking

Extensive work on the construction of the new Marina Mahdia has been on-going here since 2013, with no end in sight. It is being built in a lagoon just outside the town and will be an impressive facility if/when finished.

Currently the only berthing option here is alongside the police dock.

Last updated June 2016.
