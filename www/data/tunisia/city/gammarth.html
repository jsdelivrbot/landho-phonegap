Gammarth - Profile

Location

Starting life as a small fishing village, Gammarth, located about 10nm north of Tunis, is now a select tourist resort.

It is a good alternative to Bizerte or Sidi Bou Said.

Position: 36˚55’12”N,  10˚18’42”E (marina entrance)

Clearance

This is port of Entry, so Customs and Police are available. It is recommended as a good place to enter Tunisia.

See Tunisia Formalities for full details on clearing into and out of the country.

A 'gift' may be requested on departure.

Last updated July 2016.

Docking

There is a new marina here and it is reported to be very welcoming and a pleasant place to visit.
