Ben Khiar - Profile

Location

Beni Khiar, a small friendly fishing port noted for its weavers of carpets and woollen fabrics. It is a useful stop between Kelibia and Monastir.

Position 36°27' N, 10°47' 45"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

There are a few berths which usually involve rafting up. The port captain likes to be boss, so agree with his wishes (while he is there!). 3m at the entrance.
