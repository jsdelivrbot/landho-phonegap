Zarzis - Profile

Location

Zarzis is just south of the Island of Djerba and is an ideal place from which to explore the southern part of Tunisia and in particular the timeless sands of the Sahara.

Position 33° 28'36"N, 11° 07'42"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

This is an expanding commercial port with 60 berths for yachts. Water, electricity, fuel and repairs are available.
