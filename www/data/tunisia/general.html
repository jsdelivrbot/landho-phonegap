Tunisia - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +1. Summer time UTC +2, May to October.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Tunisian dinar (D) of 1000 millimes (M). In spring 2006 the exchage rate was approximately €1 = 1.60 D. The import and export of dinars is illegal. One should only change as much as needed because it is only possible to change back 30% on departure. Exchange receipts should be kept in order to be able to do this.

Communications

The International Dialing code for Tunisia is +216.

The country is covered by two GSM 900 mobile networks.

There is widespread Internet coverage with Internet cafes in the main cities and towns.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Tunisia

Emergencies

Police - Dial 197
Firefighters – Dial 198
Ambulance - Dial 190
Free emergency call - Dial 112
