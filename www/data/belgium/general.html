Belgium - General Info

Time Zone

UTC + 1, summer time UTC + 2 April to October.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Note: a C style plug can be used with E and F receptacles. All double-insulated appliances are indeed fitted with a C plug, and can be used in any compatible receptacle (C E F and narrow L). Type C receptacles are prohibited in Belgium

Money

Euro

Communications

International dialing code for Belgium is +32.

rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone. Acquiring a OneSimCard global SIM card before arriving in Belgium is most efficient method.

Emergencies

Emergency telephone numbers

Medical and Fire Services - 100

Police - 101

European emergency number - 112 (calls are free from any mobile cellular of fixed-line telephone)
