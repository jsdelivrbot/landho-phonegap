Nieuwpoort - Profile

Location

The port is located one and half miles up the River Yser, roughly midway between Dunkirk and Oostende. It is a fishing port, an industrial centre, and also a beach resort.

Position 51° 09'28"N, 002° 42'54"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

Although the entrance is fairly narrow, is it accessible in all weathers.

There are now three yacht basins,Ljzervaarders, Vlotkom and Novus Portus.

The modern marinas here offer all facilities and the port also gives access the the Belgian Inland Waterway network for those boats prepared to de-mast.

Last updated April 2016.
