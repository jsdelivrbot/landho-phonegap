Brussels - Profile

Location

This city is best known in Europe as the hub of the European Union as many of its institutions are located here.

Position 50° 53'05"N, 004° 22'55"E (marina)

Docking

It is possible for yachts to reach Brussels via the canal network from Amsterdam without having to lower the mast.
