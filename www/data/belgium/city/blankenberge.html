Blankenberge - Profile

Location

Blankenberge lies less than 4nm west of the large port of Zeebrugge. Originally a small fishing port, it is now a popular holiday resort.

Position 51° 19'N, 03° 06'18"E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

This is a port of entry from early April to late September.

Call the Harbour Master on +32 50 417 536 for information.

Last updated February 2015.

Docking

The shelter is good in the harbour here, but the approach can be dangerous in on-shore winds above F5

There are some visitors' pontoons are split between the Old Yacht Harbour, which is to port, past the fishing vessels, and the new harbour and marina is to sta'bd.

The berthing is controlled by the 3 Yacht Clubs.

Harbour Master : Use Marina/Y.C. Telephone numbers.

For boat emergencies, call Belgium Coastguard on VHF 16 or 67. They will contact Vrijwillige Blankenbergse Zeereddingsdienst (VBZR), the local volunteer search and rescue organisation.

Last updated February 2015.
