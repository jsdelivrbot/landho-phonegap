Oyster Pond - Profile

Location

A very protected harbour on the east coast of the island. The border between Sint Maarten (to the south) and St Martin (to the north) runs along the coast round the bay. So the town is in St Martin and the marina and bay itself is in Sint Maarten.

Position 18° 03'13"N, 63° 00'47"W. (entrance to the bay)

Docking

It is possible to anchor in the bay.

Last updated December 2014.
