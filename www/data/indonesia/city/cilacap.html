Cilacap - Profile

Location

This one of the few places on the south coast of Java offering good shelter from the monsoons. Unfortunately, it is rather industrial and so does not provide the most attractive of surroundings.

Position 007° 43.54' S, 108° 59.79' E

Docking

The approach channel is well marked and well lit but keep in the channel as there are shoals one each side.

If just a brief stop is required, there is an anchorage just behind theTanjung Karangbolong Peninsula.

There are several anchorages off the town and up the rivers. Be sure to keep clear of the channels.

There is a dinghy dock off the town.

Last updated February 2015.
