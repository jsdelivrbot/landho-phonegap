Maumere - Profile

Location

Flores is an island arc extending east from Java. Maumere is the largest town in Flores. It lies towards the eastern end of the the north coast. This island is one of the few places where the Komodo dragon can be found in the wild.

Location: 08°37′S, 121°08′E.

In 1992 an earthquake measuring 7.8 on the Richter scale killed 2,500 people in and around Maumere. In 2013 a volcanic erruption on Palue, 40nm NW of Maumere killed 6 people.

Docking

Much of the bay is deep (40m+) but there is shallower water closer to the concrete dock.

There is a good anchorage just west of the ship dock in about 10m. It is open to the south and can get very rolly/uncomfortable.

An alternative, is an anchorage off the Sea World Resort, about 5 miles east of Maumere. Depths are 10 to 20m. Holding is suspect but just off the river mouth is good.
