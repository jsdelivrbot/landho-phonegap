Gili Gede Island - Profile

Location

Gili Gede is an island located in a big sheltered bay close to the coastal town of Tembowong, approx. 15nm West of the harbour town of Lembar in South West Lombok. It is one of 12 islands clustered together, very sheltered and surrounded by hills (photo courtesy of Marina Del Ray).

There are no motorized vehicles on the island, so to move around you take the coastal path, a horse drawn cart or a taxi boat.

It appears that the planned new modern marina at the southern end of the island still just consists of a few mooring buoys and no other facilities (see comment posted below April 2016).

Position 08° 45' 45"S, 115° 56'01"E (marina moorings)

Clearance

There is no clearance here. It is not a Port of Entry.

Last updated March 2016.

Docking

Currently the area provides a sheltered anchorage, 12m deep with good holding in the sand bottom. There are a few moorings.

There are resorts and a couple of bars on Gili Gede island, plus a surfing and diving business, but very limited provisioning.

Last updated April 2016.
