Pemangkat - Profile

Location

Pemangkat is a small, relatively sleepy fishing town in the far west of Kalimantan. It is located northwest of the provincial capital of Pontianak.

Pemangkat has a famous temple built as the worship place to the God that Chinese locals believe protects fishermen when sailing out to sea. This small town still holds many traditions of Chinese culture that are rarely seen elsewhere.

Position 01° 11'08"N, 108° 57'48"E

Clearance

This is not an official Port of Entry.

Docking

There is secure anchoring inside the river in 17 feet, mud.

Shallow depths and fish traps across the river make it advisable to use a dinghy to reach the town, although with local knowledge it is likely passable.

The local Navy post can provided a great deal of friendly assistance to cruisers.

Other than the Harbour Master, very little English is spoken here.
