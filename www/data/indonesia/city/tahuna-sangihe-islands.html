Tahuna (Sangihe Islands) - Profile

Location

Tahuna is the chief town and port for the Sangihe Islands within the province of North Sulawesi. It is located on the island of Sangihe Besar. A convenient entry port into Indonesia if coming from the southern Philippines. The only airport in the islands is located here - Naha airport.

Position 03° 36'19"N, 125° 30'03"E.

Clearance

Whilst this is not an official port of entry for Indonesia, there is a CIQP office here and there is support from central government to make this a clearance port for yachts coming from the north.

It is probable that you are able to complete all clearance formalities except for securing a visa on arrival.

Last updated February 2016.

Docking

Details on the anchorage here will be coming soon. A dinghy pontoon is planned. There are some internet cafés in the centre of town but it's easier to buy a "Wifi Internet Voucher", which you can use on your boat. Only Rp100,000 for 250Mb, valid 30 days.
