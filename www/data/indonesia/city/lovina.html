Lovina - Profile

Location

Lovina is a great stop for cruisers and is an official stop for the Sail Indonesia Rally. This small town on the North coast of Bali lined with bars and restaurants is becoming a popular place for those who want to get away from the more crowded tourist spots in the South.

Position 08° 09'36"S, 115° 01'22"E

Docking

This open bay would be a roadstead in the wrong conditions, but is benign during the cruising season.

Provisioning is easier here with a local market and a number of smaller stores with imported goods, catering to a resident expat population.
