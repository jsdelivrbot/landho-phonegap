Tanimbar Islands - Profile

Location

These islands are often frequented by Australian boats. There are extensive reefs around the coasts and most anchorages are in mangrove bays.

Beware of pirates which operate on the west coast.

Docking

Saumlaki

Anchor between the two jetties in 18m in sand/mud, or in deeper water N of the new jetty.

Good fish market and general market. Hardware store near the hotel (open mornings only). Fuel from the fuel station 1km N or the town centre.

Lutur Harbour

Good shelter from all directions. The the holding is mud in about 7m. Approach from north towards the green beacon (may be lit) marking the entrance. Beware the reefs on either side of the entrance, also a line of piles from an old jetty reaching half way across the bay from Lutur town, some isolated groups of piles in the bay and large areas of fishing nets.

There is a market and some shops here.

Bulu Village

Anchorage N of the village at 07° 14.9’S, 131° 28.1’E

Quiet anchorage in 9m in mud. Exposed to the south. Reefs each side of the entrance.
