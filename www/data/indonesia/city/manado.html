Manado - Profile

Location

Manado lies on the extreme eastern end of the most northerly of the 'arms' of the island of Sulawesi. It is the provincial capital of the area and has an ethnically very diverse population.

Position 01° 30’N, 124° 50’E.

Clearance

This is not an official Port of Entry.

'Lucky' is the self appointed yacht service man. He can help with check in, arranging diesel, water, laundry, rubbish disposal and tours.

Last updated February 2013.

Docking

It is possible to land a dinghy in the harbour but there are no suitable yacht moorings.

The prevailing wind is on-shore but there is reasonable holding in mud/sand 20 meters.

Last updated February 2013.
