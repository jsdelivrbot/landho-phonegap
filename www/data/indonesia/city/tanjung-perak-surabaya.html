Tanjung Perak (Surabaya) - Profile

Location

Located at the eastern end of the north shore of Java, this is Indonesia's second largest city. It is also the a home port for the Indonesian navy. It is a major shopping destination as well as large commercial port.

Position 7°12’S 112°44’E.

Docking

This not a comfortable place to anchor and there does not appear to be any facilities for yachts.
