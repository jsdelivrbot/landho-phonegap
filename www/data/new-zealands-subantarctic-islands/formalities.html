New Zealand's Subantarctic Islands - Formalities

Clearance

There are strictly enforced impact restrictions and codes as well as anti-biodiversity precautions to be taken when visiting here.

A hull inspection is also required.

Entry to the islands is by permit only, issued by the DOC.

There is a landing fee per day per person. This covers quarantine and processing costs as well as including the new Subantarctic Guidebook.

There is an annual quota of tourists allowed to visit each site. All visitors, including those on yachts, must be accompanied by an official DOC guide.

See this link www.doc.govt.nz/parks-and-recreation/places-to-go/southland/places/subantarctic-islands/ for more information.
