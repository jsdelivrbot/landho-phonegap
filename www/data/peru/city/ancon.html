Location

Las Colinas is the north facing bay about 25nm north of Lima where the Ancon yacht club is based.

Clearance

The yacht club at Ancon welcomes visitors. Cruising boats should go directly to the yacht club dock. The club will contact the authorities for clearance. The new yacht club has very good facilities including a heavy-duty travelift, fuel and water on the main dock, and moorings with good security.
