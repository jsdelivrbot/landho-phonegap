Chimbote - Profile

Location

Chimbote lies approximately 220nm north of Lima and is principally a commercial port but the south facing bay is protected to the west by the island of Isla Blanca and a chain of other, smaller islands, providing a very sheltered anchorage.

Position 09° 07.35'S, 78° 37.13'W.
