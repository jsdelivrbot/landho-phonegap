Cap Mole St. Nicolas - Profile

Location

An attractive town and harbour located on the extreme north west corner of the island.

Position 19° 48.45"N, 73° 25.02"W.

Clearance

It is possible to clear into Haiti here.

Last updated October 2014.

Docking

The anchorage off the town is about 7m and can be exposed in northerly winds. Further into the inlet is very secure and sheltered  but is much further from the town and it is not wise to leave the boat unattended here.

An alternative anchorage which is sheltered from the north can be found off the very welcoming fishing village of Bombardopolis. It lies about 10 miles south of Cap Mole.

Last updated October 2014.
