Algiers (Alger/El Djazair) - Profile

Location

Algiers is the capital city of Algeria. It lies on the western side of the Bay of Algiers.

Position 36° 45'30"N, 03° 05'26"E (harbour)

Clearance

The best advice from a consul based in Algeria, is do not try and enter Algiers port - you are likely to be arrested! (Dated August 2008)
