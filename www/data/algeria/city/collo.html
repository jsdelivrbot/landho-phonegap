Location

A small, friendly fishing port about 30nm west of Skikda.

Clearance

Coastguard/police will attract your attention when entering and then deal with the formalities.

Last updated July 2014.

Docking

You will be found a berth among the fishing boats. The Harbour Master is very helpful.

In the port you will be a curiosity and people will stand and look at your boat. Talk to them in French!
