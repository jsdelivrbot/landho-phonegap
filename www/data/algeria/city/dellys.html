Dellys - Profile

Location

This port is a useful overnight stop midway between Bejaia and Algiers.

Position 36.9°N, 3.9°E.

Clearance

The coastguard/police are likely to attract your attention on entering the harbour and berth you at their wharf. The officials are very friendly and helpful.

Last updated August 2014.

Docking

Berthing here is safe, in spite of there likely to be small terrorist groups in the mountains outside the town.

For safety, the officials will drive you into the town for provisions. leaving the port area on your own is not permitted.

Last updated August 2014.
