Cherchell - Profile

Location

This friendly fishing port lies about 50nm west of Algiers.

Position 36°36′36″N, 2°11′48″E.

Clearance

The officials will attract your attention on entering the port and will deal with formalities.

Last updated August 2014.

Docking

Yachts are berthed in front of the police/coastguard building where there is a water tap.

For your first visit into the town, a policemen in civilian clothes will accompany you to act as a guide. After that you are free to visit on your own but it is important to advice them of your plans.

Provisioning is good and the roman ruins worth visiting.

Last updated August 2014.
