Sidi Fredj - Profile

Location

This modern resort lies on a peninsula about 15nm west of Algiers. The marina lies on the western side, south of the town. It is the best port of call from which to visit Algiers.

Position 36° 45'47"N, 02° 50'59"E (marina entrance)

Clearance

This is the only marina in Algeria. Berth at the fuel pontoon to await for the officials. Completing the paperwork here can take some time both clearing in and also out.

Last updated August 2014.

Docking

The entrance is tricky in on shore winds due to being shallow. The marina is subject to silting but was dredged in 2013 to minimum depth of about 2.5m.

Facilities are basic and the marina is in poor state of repair with water and electricity points destroyed. There are some repair facilities in Sidi Fredj, which has a number of locally owned yachts. There is a 16-ton travelift as well as fuel and water (via a local yacht owner) in the marina.

Theft from boats left unattended has been reported so it is not advisable to leave a boat unattended for any length of time unless arrangements have been made to have it watched.

Last updated August 2014.
