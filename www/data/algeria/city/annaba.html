Annaba - Profile

Location

The first significant city if sailing west from Tunisia. It is a large commercial harbour.

Position 36°54'06"N, 07°46′55"E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

This is a useful port of entry when coming from Tunsia. Ensure you call the Coastguard on entering Algerian waters and fly the courtesy flag.

Call the Port Authorities on VHF channel 16 or 14 before entering the harbour.. You will receive visits from all the officials.

See Noonsite/Countries/Algeria/Formalities for more information.

Last updated July 2014

Docking

You are likely to be berthed outside the police station, among the Coastguard boats.

For water and fuel,  ask Harbourmaster. They both will have to be delivered.

You are allowed to freely walk the town without an escort.

Last updated July 2014.
