Tenes - Profile

Location

This small port is sited on an old Roman town and lies about 70nm west of Cherchell.

Position 36°30′44″N, 1°18′16″E.

Clearance

It is important to call the Authorities on VHF channel 16 or 14 before entering the harbour. They will visit the boat at the entrance to complete formalities. Refuse to give any 'gifts'.

Last updated August 2014.

Docking

Ask the Harbourmaster about water and fuel.

Provisions are in the main town about 1.5km away.

Last updated August 2014.
