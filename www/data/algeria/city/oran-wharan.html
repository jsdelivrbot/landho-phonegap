Oran (Wharan) - Profile

Location

This major port is the second largest city in Algeria and lies about 150nm east of the border with Morocco. It is an attractive city with many restored colonial buildings.

Position 35°41′49″N, 0°37′59″W.

Clearance

Call the Authorities on VHF channel 16 or 14 before entering the port.

The coastguard will board the boat at the entrance.

Coastguard, Police, Harbour Master and Immigration will visit the boat to complete formalities. If entering or leaving Algeria insist on getting a visit from Customs. It can take some time to organise this.

Politely decline to give any 'gifts'.

Last updated August 2014.

Docking

Visiting yachts are now directed to berth near the ferry terminal (not in the marina).

The Harbour Master can arrange water and also fuel, but this is expensive as it has to be delivered by tanker.

It is necessary to take a taxi to the town (around 200dh) as officials do not like you walking by yourself.

Last updated August 2014.
