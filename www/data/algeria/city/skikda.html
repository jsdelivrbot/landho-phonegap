Skikda - Profile

Location

Skikda lies about 90nm west of Annaba, in the Gulf of Stora. It is a gas and oil commercial port.

The best reason for stopping here is to visit Constantine.

Position 36°52′N, 6°54′E.

Clearance

Call the Port Authorities on VHF channel 16 or 14 before entering the harbour. You will receive visits from all the officials.

See Noonsite/Countries/Algeria/Formalities for more information.

Last updated July 2014

Docking

You are likely to be berthed away from the big ships, but it means that it is quite a walk to the port gate.
