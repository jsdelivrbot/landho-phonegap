Mostaganem (Mestghanem) - Profile

Location

This port lies about 50nm east of Oran on the eastern side of the Gulf of Arzew.

Position 35°56′N, 0°5′E.

Clearance

Call the Port Authorities on VHF channel 16 or 14 on approach. They will visit the boat at the entrance to complete formalities. Decline to give any 'gifts'.

Last updated August 2014.

Docking

For water and fuel, ask the police/coastguard.

When leaving the port, you will be escorted by a policemen in civilian clothes who will help you to find what you need.

A taxi to the town costs about 60 to 80dh.

Last updated August 2014.
