Facts

  • Bosnia-Herzegovina has a very short piece of coastline just 25km long. It divides the better-known Croatian cruising ground into its two parts, the east and west coastal zones.
  • This small coastal strip contains one port - Neum, a seaside resort. One should be aware of the many mussel farms lying just off the coast.
  • The currency is the Bosnian marka but the Croatian kuna and Euro are also accepted.

Weather

Bosnia has long, warm summers and short, mild winters.
