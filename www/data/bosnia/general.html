Bosnia - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +1

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Opening Hours

Banking hours are from 08:00 to 19:00 Mondays to Fridays.

Shopping hours are normally from 08:00 to 20:00, although the days of opening vary from area to area.

Post office hours are generally 09:00-17:00 Mondays to Fridays.

Money

The currency of Bosnia and Herzegovina is the Bosnia and Herzegovina Konvertibilna Marka (KM), with one Marka equalling 100 Pfening.

Most transactions in Bosnia are in cash.

ATM's are now readily available in towns and cities.

Traveller's cheques are not widely accepted and it can prove difficult to exchange them.

Communications

International dialing code for Bosnia and Herzegovina is +387

Internet cafes are available throughout Bosnia but broadband availability is limited.

Post offices can be found in almost all villages, towns and cities.

Mobile phone coverage is reasonable in major cities and towns.
