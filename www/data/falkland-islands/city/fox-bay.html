Fox Bay - Profile

Location

This is the second largest settlement in the Falkland Islands and is consists of Fox Bay East and Fox Bay West. It lies on the south east coast of West Falkland.

Position 51° 58'15"S, 60° 00'50.6"W.

Clearance

No Clearance is possible here.

Docking

There is a shop and post office here.
