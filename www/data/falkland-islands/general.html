Falkland Islands - General Info

Time Zone

UTC -4 (all year)

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 240 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Falklands pound (£). This is not legal tender in the UK, but pounds sterling can be used in the Falklands. Money can be exchanged at the Standard Chartered Bank, Ross Road, Port Stanley. Falklands £s cannot be changed for sterling or other currencies outside of the islands.

Many retail outlets accept credit cards and you can obtain cash advances on credit and debit cards at the bank (but not American Express or Diners Club etc..)

Communications

International Dialing Code for Falkland Islands is +500.

There is no internet café, however, several of the hotels and guesthouses in Stanley, the Tourist Information Jetty Centre and some shops offer internet facilities. There is only limited mobile phone coverage in the Falklands which presently only covers the top half of East Falkland.

Cellphone coverage is expanding around the islands and now includes basic data capability via GPRS EDGE technology. If you phone is not locked to a network you can buy a local SIM card.

Emergencies

Emergencies - call 999

By Ewen Southby-Tailyour
Publisher: Imray - Edition: 1st (1985, Supp 1st 1996)
