Bonaire - General Info

Time Zone

UTC -4

Electrical Supply

120 volts.

Money

US dollars

Euros are widely accepted.

All major credit cards are accepted.

Communications

International dialing code for Bonaire is +599

There are several mobile phone service providers.

Digicel update December 2014:
Anyone cruising through the islands using Digicel should be aware that you cannot top up a sim from one island in another as used to be able to. So if for example you want to use the sim bought here while cruising through other islands, you should either put on a large enough sum to cover your needs, or you can set up an account and do it online, but you cannot get it topped up in another island.
You can top-up Digicel at https://online-top-up.digicelgroup.com/

There is an internet cafe at the Harbourside Mall in Kralendijk as well as various hot-spots around the town and in several restaurants.

Transport

There is little Public transport, but hire cars are available.

Driving is on the right.

Diplomatic Missions

There are no foreign embassies located on Bonaire. Make sure to visit your national embassy's website for information about travel to the Caribbean.

Bonaire Police Emergency Numbers - Call 911 first. This number is manned by one person, so if no one answers, call 717-8000.
