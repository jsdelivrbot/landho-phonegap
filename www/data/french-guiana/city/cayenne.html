Cayenne - Profile

Location

The capital is no longer used as a commercial port since the Cayenne River entrance and harbour have silted up in recent years, making it unsafe for boats of over 4-foot draft to enter.

The port at Dégrad des Cannes on the Mahury River (just SE of Cayenne) is now the seaport.

Clearance

It is possible to clear in at the Customs office at the Marina de Degrad des Cannes on the Mahoury River, just south of Cayenne.

The office has quite limited hours (open until 13:00 Mon-Fri, and 15:00-18:00 Tues & Thurs.). They no longer stamp passports.

It may be necessary to visit Immigration at the airport to get the passports stamped.

Last updated September 2014.

Docking

Visiting yachts, as well as some local yachts, moor at the marina at Degrad des Cannes which is on the northern shore past the Navel base and the commercial port.

Boats also anchor further up the Mahoury River, off the village of Stouppan. The river is well buoyed.

Last updated September 2014.
