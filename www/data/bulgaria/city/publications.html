Bulgaria - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2. Summer time UTC +3. April 1 to September 31.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Note - Outlets are reported as type F, though both type C and F plugs may be encountered.

Money

Lev (plural: leva). Import/export of leva is prohibited.

Communications

International dialing code for Bulgaria is +359.

WiFi access is said to be very good.

rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone.

Emergencies

National Emergency Numbers

Ambulance Tel: 150
Fire/Rescue Tel: 160
Police Tel: 166
BULSAR (Bulgarian Black Sea Search & Rescue) Tel: 088 161
Car Breakdown Service - Sofia Tel: 1286
Car Breakdown Service - outside Sofia Tel: 146
Pharmacy On-Duty Tel: 178






If any inccident occures to crew or boat, immediately report to MRCC Varna on VHF radio channel 16.
