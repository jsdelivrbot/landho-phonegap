Location

This newly created township is situated just north of Auckland, on the Whangaparaoa Peninsula. It has a world-class golf course as well as being one of New Zealand's largest marinas.

Docking

There is a daily ferry to downtown Auckland and also an airport shuttle service available from the marina.
