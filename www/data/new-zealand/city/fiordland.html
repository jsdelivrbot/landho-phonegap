Fiordland - Profile

Location

This is an area on the south west coast of South Island comprising of magnificient fiords. The most notable being Milford Sound and Doubtful Sound. Most of the area is a National Park and there are several off shore Marine Reserves where fishing is restricted. The area is practically uninhabited, so it is important to be very self-sufficient when cruising here.
