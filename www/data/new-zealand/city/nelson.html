Nelson - Profile

Location

Located amid the stunning landscape at the top northwest corner of the South Island. Port Nelson is the biggest fishing port in Australasia and enjoys the highest hours of sunshine in New Zealand.

The climate, and proximity to various activities, plus the great cruising grounds locally, make this area a good stopover/re-fit location.

Position 41º 15.40'S, 173º 16.56'E.

Clearance

Contact Customs 48 hours before ETA by SSB radio 4125Khz or email.

Contact to be made with the Harbour Control to confirm the draft at the time of your arrival as there is 1.8m (chart datum) are the Customs/Super yacht berth.
