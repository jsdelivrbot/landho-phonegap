Whangaroa - Profile

Location

Whangaroa harbour lies about 35nm north of the Bay of Islands and is a useful stopover when exploring the northern tip of New Zealand's North Island; an area known as The Northlands. It is in an area well-known for its big game fishing.

Location 35°.03' S, 173°.44.5' E.

Clearance

This is not a port of entry. The nearest such port is Opua in the Bay of Islands.

Docking

Whangaroa (pronounced fong-ga-ro-a) is a large natural, sheltered  harbour. The entrance is narrow and through which the tide runs hard.

Last updated February 2013.
