Whitianga - Profile

Location

Whitianga is situated on the east coast of the Coromandel Peninsula, across the Hauraki Gulf from Auckland. This striking peninsula has towering mountain ranges covered in dense forest, and is fringed with sandy beaches. It is the gateway to the stunning Mercury Island Group.

Docking

There is a town wharf and a 200 berth marina as well a plenty of protected anchorages in the area.

Last updated June 2013.
