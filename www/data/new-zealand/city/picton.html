Picton - Profile

Location

Picton is a small seaport at the head of the Queen Charlotte Sound at the northern tip of New Zealand's South Island. It is in an area known as the Marlborough Sounds.

Docking

There are numerous marina berths in Picton harbour.

Just a mile north east of Picton, lies the large modern marina of Waikawa. The bay here also provides plenty of sheltered anchorages.

Although only about 15km directly west of Picton, Havlock is very many miles by sea, but it also provides excellent shelter in the marina there.

The Marlborough Sounds is a large area and has many splendid anchorages.

Marlborough Marine Industries Association was established late 2013 and comprises a group of marine related businesses keen to offer their services to the world cruising community as well as local sailors. The local port authority is involved and will arrange marina berths and customs too.

Last updated January 2014.
