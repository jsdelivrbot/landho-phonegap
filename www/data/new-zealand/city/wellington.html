Docking

Chaffers Marina
Chaffers Bay , PO Box 27555 , Wellington
Tel:+64 (0)4 382 9300 Fax:+64 (0)4 382 9300
‎ , chaffers@wnc.quik.co.nz
Internationally classed floating marina adjacent to Te Papa (Museum of Zealand) in Wellingtons inner harbour with 185 berths. The marina is protected by a floating breakwater and is attached to the Overseas Passenger Terminal wharf. Full service marina, 40 ton travelift, chandlery, sailmaker and other repair services. Supermarket, banks, post office, bars, cafes and restaurants and yacht club close by. 5km from the airport.

Mana Marina is the closest North Island Marina to the Marlborough Sounds with the entrance to Queen Charlotte Sound situated due west approximately 18 miles from the Marina. Full service marina with a marina village (supermarket, cafes, restaurants, chandlery, medical facilities) 5 minutes walk. 35 ton travelift.
