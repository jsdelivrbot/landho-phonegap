Skopun - Profile

Location

Skopun is a village located on the northern coast of Sandoy, Faroe Islands.

Location: 61° 54' 10" N,  6° 52' 45" W.

Docking

This is a busy, well sheltered harbour with three basins, each approached through a narrow gate. Beware of the strong surge which occurs at these gates.

There is usually room for a yacht in the middle, fishing boat, basin and there may also be room on the west quay in the inner basin.

Skopun is a large village with a reasonable range of shops.

Water is available on the quay.
