Sandur - Profile

Location

Situated on the west coast of Sandoy, this is a well sheltered harbour.

The church here is one of the most attractive in the Faroes.

Location: 61° 49' 44" N,  6° 48' 33" W.

Docking

There is also a very pleasant anchorage, with good holding, off the large sandy beach at the head of the bay. There is a small boat landing just below the church.

Water and showers are both available in the harbour, and the village has a shop.
