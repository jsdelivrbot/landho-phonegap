Klaksvik - Profile

Location

This is the second largest town in the Faroes and is a busy commercial harbour. It also houses the ship-building industry.

Location: 62°13'45"N, 6°35'23"W

Docking

It is a designated winter harbour.

It is best to call the Harbour office on VHF channel 16 to obtain advice on berthing as the harbour is very crowded.
