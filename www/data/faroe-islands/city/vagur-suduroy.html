Vagur (Suduroy) - Profile

Location

This large, protected harbour is classed as a winter harbour.

Location: 61°28'16" N,  6°48'41" W.

Clearance

There is a customs post here.

Docking

Berth as convenient, avoiding the ferry berths on the outside of the outer quay.

The anchorage at the head of the fjord is subject ot strong winds and the holding is poor due to weed.

Showers are available at the small hotel by the harbour.
