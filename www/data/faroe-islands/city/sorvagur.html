Sorvagur - Profile

Location

Location: 65°4'12" N, 7°19'22" W.

Docking

This village is approached via a long fjord, boardered by magnificient waterfalls. Beware of unlit fish farms in the fjord.

The best berth is on the semi-derelict quay at the entrance to the small boat harbour, by the salmon processing plant. This is a mile form the main village.

The Harbour Office is at the western end of the processing plant.

There is water on the quay. Reasonable shops and a bank in the village.

The airport is a mile and a half from the village - uphill.
