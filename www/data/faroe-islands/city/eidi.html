Eidi - Profile

Location

This small town provides the only secure harbour in the north of the islands. It is situated at the northern end of the fjord of Sundini.

Location: 62°17'48"N, 7°5'29"W

Docking

The main quays are in the NW of the harbour, but are exposed to the south. A better berth may be found on the small boat pontoons, with local assistance.

There is water on the quay and a supermarket close to the harbour.
