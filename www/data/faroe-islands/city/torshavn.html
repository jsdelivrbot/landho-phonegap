Torshavn - Profile

Location

Torshavn is the capital of the Faroe Islands and lies on the south east coast of Streymoy.

The oldest part of the town still has its wooden houses with turf roofs.

Position 62° 00'13"N, 006° 46'12"W (harbour entrance)

Clearance

Call Thorshavn Harbour Master on VHF Channel 12

For clearance, report to the Customs office behind the Harbour Master's office which is in the commercial port.

Last updated March 2016.

Docking

The approach to the harbour is straightforward.

Visiting yachts berth in the Vestaravag yacht harbour in the north west part of the harbour.

An alternative, quieter spot may be found to the east on the island of Nolsoy.
