Tvoroyri - Profile

Location

Suduroy is the most convenient landfall for boats arriving from the south. Tvoroyri lies on a long inlet on the eastern side of the island.

Position 61° 33'18"N, 006° 49'01"W (harbour)

Clearance

Suduroy Radio should be contacted on VHF Channel 16. The Customs office overlooks the harbour at Tveraa (Tvoroyri) and formalities should be completed there. The Harbourmaster also requests that yachts contact him on departure.

Docking

The harbour is very secure, but also very busy.

Yachts should berth alongside the wooden jetty to the west of the fishing boat harbour. Depth is 3m

There is also an anchorage further up the fjord, off the village of Trongisvagur. The holding is excellent in thick mud.

Another anchorage may be found  to the west of Hvidenaes, in about 10m. The bottom is mud.

Showers and launderette are available at the Harbourmaster's office.
