Location

This volcanic island is about 6 miles across and is the political centre of the Lau Group. The main village is Tubou and lies near the only well-protected anchorage in the lagoon. There are 6 other villages spread around the coast.

Docking

The channel through the reef off Tubou is very narrow (23 m wide), is only roughly marked and very challenging. About half a mile insite the reef, a channel leads to a long jetty, beyond which, is a narrow basin in which to anchor. Here there is about 6m over sand. Several anchors will be needed to ensure remaining centred in this small anchorage.

There is an airport with a weekly flight to Suva.

There are a few basic shops and a clinic.
