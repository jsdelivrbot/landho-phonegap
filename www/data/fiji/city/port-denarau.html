Port Denarau - Profile

Location

This resort lies on the western side of Nadi Bay, about 15nm south of Lautoka.

Clearance

It may be possible to obtain final clearance out of Fiji from here, if one pays the taxi fare and a fee to have officials come to Port Denarau to complete all paperwork, including delivery of firearms. The costs could be split among several yachts.
