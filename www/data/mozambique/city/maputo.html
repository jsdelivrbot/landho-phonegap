Maputo - Profile

Location

Maputo is the capital of Mozambique and lies about 70nm north of the border with South Africa. It is a major commercial port.

Richards Bay is approximately a 200 miles sail away.

Position 21°0'S,  032°38'E (off the harbour entrance)

Clearance

This is an official port of entry

Port control maintain continuous watch on VHF Channel 16. It is possible to come alongside one of the wharfs for clearance formalities.

If not planning to step ashore, there is no requirement to to officially clear in.

Last updated January 2016.

Docking

The harbour is approached through two main channels, the south channel being recommended only for small vessels and those with local knowledge. The buoyage into Maputo is all well maintained, well lit at night, and easy to follow.

If just making a brief stop here to wait out strong winds, then there is a useful anchorage at approximately 25° 57.6'S, 032° 31.4'E.

Last updated January 2016.
