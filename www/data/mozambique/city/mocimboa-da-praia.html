Mocimboa da Praia - Profile

Location

This is the northern most port in Mozambique and lies just 40nm from the border with Tanzania.

Position 11°21′S,40°20′E.

Clearance

It is a Port of Entry and yachts can clear into Mozambique here.

However, see Noonsite/Mozambique/formalities page for the details.
