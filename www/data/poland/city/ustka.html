Ustka - Profile

Location

Ustka is an attractive seaside and a natural therapy resort as well as a fishing harbour. There are medical mud, sulphur and other mineral baths. Also the town has preserved many examples of XIX century domestic architecture.

The town is located at the mouth of Słupia river about 15NM east of the German border.

Position 54°35' 33"N, 16°51' E (harbour entrance)

Docking

Entering in strong on shore winds can be dangerous.

The harbour is well sheltered, althogh some swell enters in strong northerlies.

Yachts can berth on the east side of the harbour, or in the fishing harbour on the west side with the yachts belonging to the yacht club.

The key for the facilities is obtained from the Harbour Master.

The town is 2km away.

Last updated July 2011.
