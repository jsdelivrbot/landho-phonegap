Gorki Zachodnie - Profile

Location

Gorki Zachodnie lies on the west bank of the Vistula River delta, about a mile from the entrance. On the opposite side is the other fishing village of Gorki Wschodnie. This delta is 7NM east of the entrance to Gdansk. It offers a choice of low cost berths for winter storage, in or out of the water. As a summer destination, it may not be quite so attractive as it is quite remote and the surrounding reed beds provide a good breeding ground for mosquitoes.

Position 54°22' 28"N, 18°46'34" E (river entrance)

Docking

There are several yacht clubs here with small, basic marinas and also the new National Sailing Centre with a marina.
