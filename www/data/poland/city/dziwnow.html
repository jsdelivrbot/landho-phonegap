Dziwnow - Profile

Location

Dziwnow Harbour is 17m East of Swinoujscie and 30m West of Kolobrzeg.

Position 54° 01'26"N, 14° 43'29"E (river entrance)

Clearance

Call VHF channel 71 for clearance. Customs office is the white building on the dock.

Docking

This is a small fishing and holiday town and the mouth of the river Dziwnow. There are 3 small marinas here. Polmax marina lies close to the entrance and the other two are just beyond the bridge in Basen Zimowy.

Warnings - watch out for fishing nets on approach. In winds from the W to SW up to force 5B the entrance is not difficult but in strong winds from W to NW the entrance is dangerous from force 6B.

Updated January 2009.

The marina is located on the western side of the Dziwnow river opposite the Harbour master & passport control office. Berths for up to 20 yachts with maximum draught of 2.5m. Mooring at the wharf or at mooring buoys. Services include showers, water, electricity, laundry and Internet access (free WiFi). There is a fuel station nearby. Open May - end of October. Fuel, petrol and diesel, is available upriver just beyond the bridge (VHF Ch10) which opens on demond on the even hours, 24 hours. It lifts to a maximum of 75m, when entering keep to the Eastern side. Bridge does not open in wind force 6B and above.
