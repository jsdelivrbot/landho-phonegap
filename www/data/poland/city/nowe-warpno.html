Nowe Warpno - Profile

Location

Nowe Warpno is a small fishing harbour and summer resort on the border zone of Szczecin Lagoon to which it is connected by a shallow (1.8m) channel.

Position  53° 43'36"N, 14°16'56" E (dock)

Clearance

Close to the ferry wharf there is a Customs and Immigration office.

Docking

Yachts berth on the quay.
