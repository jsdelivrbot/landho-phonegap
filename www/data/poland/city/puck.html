Puck - Profile

Location

Located on the mainland approximately 15nm north of Gdynia, Puck is an old town and fishing port. It makes interesting stop in this part of the Gulf of Gdansk.

Position 54°43′27"N, 18°25′04"E (marina)

Puck Market Square : ©http://en.wikipedia.org

Docking

There are two harbours which can be used by yachts. The southern one contains the Municipial marina and yacht club. The northern one is the old fishing harbour, used by the scouts' yachts and visitors. It has one pontoon with fingers berths.

Puck Marina : ©http://en.wikipedia.org

The marina has all facilities, a restaurant and a fuel berth on the jetty. The yacht club can arrange repairs.

If entering the Old fishing harbour, beware of the sandbank lying between the to harbours. The approach has lit beacons, and by day you can use the transit towers, but most characteristic point is  the brick church and its tower next to harbour. There is electricity on a jetties. The toilets and harbourmaster's office are in a new building (go upstairs, open only during the day) close to the pontoon.
