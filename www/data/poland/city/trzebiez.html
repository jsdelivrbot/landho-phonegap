Trzebiez - Profile

Location

Trzebiez is situated in the southwestern part of Zalew Szczeciski.

Position 53° 39'46"N, 14° 30'56"E (marina)

Clearance

Trzebiez is a Port of Entry for border and Customs clearance (particularly useful for those clearing out of Germany on the island route via the Peenestrom and Stettiner Haff).

Customs post in the wooden office on the quay.

Docking

The harbour is located behind a 600m wall, entry is well buoyed from either the northwest or southeast There are two parts to the harbour

Mooring - the northwest part is owned by the Polish Sailing Association and has accommodation for 80 yachts in two basins. Basin A takes up to 18m with a draught of 3m and Basin B takes yachts up to 12 m with a draught of 1.5m. Customs clearance in a small wooden office on the quayside (open 24 hours).

The southeast part of the harbour is the Fishing Harbour. It has excellent visitors moorings for yachts. Two harbour walls available for visitors to moor alongside with plenty of depth. The Harbour Office (manned 24 hours) is located next to the visitors moorings. This is an attractive small fishing harbour on the southern shore of the inland water, Zalew Szcecinshi.

The harbour is protected from the northeast by a large breakwater island. Yachts berth in the yacht basin north of the fishing harbour.
