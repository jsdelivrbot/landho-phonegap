Szczecin - Profile

Location

This city lies on the Oder River. Although some miles from the open sea through the Szczecin Lagoon, it is a major sea port.

Position 53° 25'57"N, 14° 38'12"E (approach channel)

Clearance

Szcezecin Port Control
Tel:+91 433 0697 / 440 3510 ,VHF Channel 16, 69

Docking

Marina Porta Hotele (Marina Marco)and Pogon marina both lie on the south shore of Jezioro Dabie, a few miles east of the city. This is where yachts lower their masts before continuing south on the Odra River to the German inland waterways. Marina Goclaw is located north of the city.

Harbour Master
Tel:+91 433 6657/ 440 3596
Opening hours: 08:30-15:00LT(Mon-Fri)
His office is well marked, in English, on the main pier and it is possible to briefly stop here.
Marina Goclaw
53° 28.45'N 14° 36.0E.
Tel:Harbour Master +91 423 0656
On the W bank of the channel, about 2NM N of the city centre. Frequent tram service to the city. A quiet and sheltered spot.
Water, electricity, showers, toilets, chandlery and restaurant.

Located at the southern end of Jezioro Dabie.
Depths range from 2.5m to 4m. Mainly residential. Water, electricity, showers and toilets, laundry. Mast crane. Winter storage.
Pogon (Zielona) Marina
Location 53° 24'N 14° 37.2E
Tel:+48(0)91 462 46 57
sejk@inet.com.pl
This marina lies at the junction of The Dabska Struga and Regalica Odra, about 0.5nm west of Marina Porta Hotele.
All main facilities including washing machine and restaurant. Winter storage possible. Safe in all weather conditions.
Szczecin Yacht Club
Location 53° 04'.0N, 14° 37'.7E ,
Ul Przestrzenna 9 , 70-800 Szczecin
Tel:+48 (0)91 461 27 34 Fax:+48(0)91 461 47 39
Located in the southwest part of Dabskie Lake, in an inlet sheltered from the wind.
Several visitors' moorings available all year round with additional spaces in the summer. Mooring bows on with stern rope tied to buoy also some alongside mooring. Marina manager will allocate a berth.
Over-wintering on hard standing or undercover in a hanger by arrangement with Yacht Club.
