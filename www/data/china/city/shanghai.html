Shanghai - Profile

Location

Shanghai, is one of the world’s largest ports and is situated on a very busy river in the middle of China’s east coastline. It is a useful port for provisioning if exploring the Zhou Shan Islands in the Eastern China Sea.

Position 31° 15'3"N, 121° 29'59"E (berth for foreign yachts)

Clearance

All yachts must use the services of a local agent for all formalities (Customs,Immigration, Quarantine and Harbour Master).

All yachts must use a pilot. The pilot station is located at buoy S7 in the South channel in Changjiangkou. Contact them on VHF channel 69.

Docking

There is now a berth available for foreign yachts located in the Huangpu river off the Yangzi river, at 31.15'3"N, 121.29'59"E. There is a maximum height of 49 metres. It is opposite the Oriental Tower and 10 min to the Bund area by taxi.

Fresh water, electricity, provisions and fuel are available.
