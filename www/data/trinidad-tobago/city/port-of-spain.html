Port of Spain - Profile

Location

Port of Spain is a commercial port, and as such, is not open to yachts. I lies on at the northern end of the Gulf of Paria on the west side of the island.

It lies just 10nm from Chaguaramas and has many useful services.

Position 10° 39.1'N, 61° 32.8'W.

Clearance

Arriving yachts should clear at Chaguaramas. Port of Spain is a commercial port, and yachts may only stop there in real emergencies.

Last updated January 2013
