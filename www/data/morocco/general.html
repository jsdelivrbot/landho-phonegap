Morocco - General Info

Time Zone

UTC, Summer time UTC +1, from 1 April to 1 August (Ramaddan).

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 127/220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Dirham (DH) of 100 centimes.

It is illegal to take DH in or out of the country. It is also difficult to change DH back into foreign currency on departure although it is possible if exchange receipts are kept.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Morocco is +212.

Mobile phone: Roaming agreements exist with international mobile phone companies. Coverage is good in towns and cities, and across the west of Morocco, but a little more erratic east and further south.

Internet: Access is unrestricted and is widely available in business centres, hotels and in Internet cafés. A one months prepaid unlimited internet access card for laptops, iPhones and smartphones is available from Maroc Telecom.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Morocco

Events

Ramadan started 11th of August in 2010 and in 2011 it starts 1st of August. It lasts for 29-30 days and is good to know about if you like dining out during daytime.

Emergencies

Police - Dial 19
Fire and Ambulance - Dial 15
