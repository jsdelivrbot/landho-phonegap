Al Hoceima - Profile

Location

This newly rebuild harbour is on the Mediterranean coast, due south of Malaga in Spain.

Position 35° 14.9'N, 03° 55.1'W.

Clearance

Clearance can be completed here, but not that the berths outside the Customs building are for impounded vessels only.

Docking

Yachts can dock on the south quay. There is good shelter here.

Some provisions available.
