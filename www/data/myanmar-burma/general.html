Time Zone

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Kyat (K) of 100 pyas.

Import/export of local currency prohibited. Foreign currency must be declared on arrival; the amounts will be checked on departure, as well as any exchange receipts.

It is necessary to bring sufficient cash cover expenses for the duration of your visit, since traveller's cheques, credit cards, and ATM cards are not accepted in Burma. US dollars are best.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Myanmar is +95.

Internet access is available at internet cafes but only in the larger cities.

Public free web-based emails such as yahoo or hotmail email accounts are not available within Myanmar.

Mobile phones will be held by Customs until departure.

There are no international GSM roaming agreements with Myanmar.
