Gustavia - Profile

Location

Gustavia is on the west coast of the island. The town surrounds the harbour.

Position 17° 54'N, 62° 51'12"W (harbour entrance)

Clearance

See the main Noonsite/StBarts/Formalities page for full details.

The Port Authority should be contacted on Channel 12 two hours prior to arrival. Yachts anchor or moor in the pleasure port in the centre of town, separate from the commercial harbour. Proceed ashore to the Port Captain's office with papers and passports. Third party insurance is occasionally requested.

If staying 24hrs, clearing in and out can be completed at the same time.

Last updated October 2015.

Docking

This is a well procteded harbour, but in winter can be subject to some swell.

It is possible to anchor in the outer harbour, or you may find a mooring buoy available in the inner harbour.

Boats also come stern-to the two yacht quays (electricity available and most expensive option).

There is water, showers, toilets and garbage disposal at the yacht dock. This is an expensive place to berth, but worth a visit.
