El Colony - Profile

Location

The small round island of Isla de la Juuventud (Isle of Pines) lies off the western end of Cuba's southern coast.

The small resort hotel and marina is on the west side, in a bay formed by the Cayos los Indios chain of reefs and islands stretching away to the NW.

Position 21° 50'N, 83° 08'W. (entrance to the bay)

Clearance

There is a small marina and a coast guard station here, but it is not an official port of entry.

Docking

The small marina here caters mainly for visitors wishing to enjoy the diving and snorkelling available in this area.

Last updated December 2014.
