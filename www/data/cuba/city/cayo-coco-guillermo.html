Cayo Coco-Guillermo - Profile

Location

Cayo Guillermo is a tiny island of thirteen square km, part of the "Jardines del Rey" island group. Located off the Atlantic coast of Cuba this island group is part of the Sabana-Camaguey archipelago. Find out more at Cayo Guillermo.com

Position 22°33’ N, 78°38'W

Clearance

This is no longer a Port of Entry, only an exit port.  Patience and some Spanish will definitely help in dealing with officials here.

Last updated September 2014.

Docking

The pass through the reef into marina Guillermo is shallow, but if the marina is called, an official will be sent out to pilot you in when there is sufficient rise of tide.
