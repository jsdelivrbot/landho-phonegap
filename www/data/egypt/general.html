Egypt - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2

Egypt operates Daylight-Saving Time between the last Friday in April and the last Thursday in September (UTC +3).

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Egyptian pound (E£) of 100 piastres. No more than E£100 can be taken out of the country, but unused currency can be changed back before departure. A foreign currency declaration must be made on arrival and exchange receipts should be kept.

Communications

International dialing code for Egypt is +20

Internet available.

Mobile phone - Call your cell phone provider to make sure that they offer phone coverage in Egypt. Ask to be upgraded to international roaming.

Consider renting a cell phone or SIM card to use with your personal cell phone if your cellular company does not offer coverage in Egypt. rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone.
