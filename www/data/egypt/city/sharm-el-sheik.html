Location

This is a vibrant resort town on the southern tip on the Sinai peninsula. Diving activities abound here due to the proximity of the many spectacular reefs in the Red Sea.

Docking

The port, in Sharem-al-Maya bay, has became the city's main yacht and service port. This is a commercial port and not very comfortable for yachts. Mooring fees are very high.

Travco Marina is only for Egyptian boats.
