Ismailia - Profile

Location

Situated approximately halfway along the Suez Canal, on the western shore of Lake Timsah. It is the overnight stop for yachts transiting the canal.

The town, which is about 1km from the marina, has some quite attractive areas with parks and many old colonial houses (including Ferdinand de Lessep’s).

Position 30° 34'58"N, 32° 16'53"E

Docking

There are guards at the gate and they generally check passports every time. They might also expect a "gift" when bringing anything in to the marina (groceries, fuel etc.), although this is not always the so.

See This report for price increases at the yacht club in June 2009.

There are some mooring buoys, otherwise use your anchor for stern-to. Alternatively, you can anchor off at no charge.

Last updated October 2013.
