Puerto Puyuhuapi - Profile

Location

Puyuhuapi is one of the main thermal resorts in Chile and a spectacular tourist centre. Its architecture reflects the roots of its original German settlers. It is close to the San Raphael glacier, the most northerly one in the southern hemisphere.

Position 44° 20'S, 72° 34'W (marina)

Docking

There is a modern marina here (44deg 20'S, 72deg 34'W) with the usual facilities.
