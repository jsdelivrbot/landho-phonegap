Algarrobo - Profile

Location

This is a popular summer resort in central Chile. It lies about 25nm south of Valparaiso.

Position 33°23′28″S, 71°41′34″W.

Docking

The sheltered marina here makes this port a useful stop when cruising between San Antonio and Valparaiso.

It is a large town with the usual resources: large supermarkets, banks, ATM's, laundry services, hardware stores.

Last updated October 2015.
