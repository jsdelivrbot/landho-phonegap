Talcahuano - Profile

Location

Talcahuano is in central Chile, on Concepción Bay. It is the port for the city of Concepción. This harbour is one of the finest along the Pacific coast of South America. It serves large ships as well as being the site of the principal Chilean naval base. It was severly damaged in the tsunami of 2010.

The city is connected by rail to Concepción, Santiago, and Valdivia.

Position 36° 42'S, 73° 05'W.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry. See Noonsite/Chile/Formalities for information.

Docking

The marina is reported to be operational.

The yacht club was destroyed by the tsunami in 2010 and its current state is not known.
