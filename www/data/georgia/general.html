Georgia - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +4

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Lari (GEL).

The import and export of local currency is prohibited. Foreign currency can only be exported up to the amount declared on arrival.

ATMs accept major credit cards.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Georgia is +995.

Mobile phone - Call your cell phone provider to make sure that they offer phone coverage in Georgia. Ask to be upgraded to international roaming.

Consider renting a cell phone or SIM card to use with your personal cell phone if your cellular company does not offer coverage in Georgia. rangeRoamer provides short-term cell phone service for international travelers. Buy, Rent, or use your own cell phone.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Georgia

Emergencies

Police - dial 022
Ambulance - dial 03, 009, 901
Fire Department - dial 01
