Haifa - Profile

Location

The northern most port in Israel, it is a busy oil and passenger terminal. Its main attraction is the religious sites on Mount Carmel.

Position 32°49.2' N,35° 1.9'E.

Clearance

This is a convenient port of entry for yachtsman arriving from Turkey and Cyprus.

Yachts should clear inside the Kishon harbour (Shavit Port). New arrivals are met by a Navy boat who will alert the officials. Yachts should dock at the visitor's pontoon provided and officials will board there.

Haifa Port Police, VHF 16, Tel. +972 4-8686425.

Docking

Haifa-Kishon Marina is a new marina situated in the old Kishon fishing harbour. It has about 250 berths. Due to its land-locked position (inside Haifa Harbour) it is considered the safest marina in Israel, although the facilities are fairly minimal.

Reports are that the marina is generally full.
