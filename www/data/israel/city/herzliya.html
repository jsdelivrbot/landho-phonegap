Herzliya - Profile

Location

Situated 40nm south of Haifa and 10 miles North of Tel Aviv, Herzliya is a modern town with a marina village which includes the Ritz Carlton, shops and restaurants.

This is a pleasent sailing destination and a safe place to leave your boat whilst you travel inland.

Be sure to email the marina: info@herzliya-marina.co.il, and the Israeli Navy: shipping@idf.gov.il in advance.

Clearance

The best port at which to clear in if coming from the north.

Herzliya Marina is a check-in port with a customs and immigration office in the marina building. Be careful not to arrive during the Shabat (Friday afternoon through Saturday night), as there is a substantial surcharge.

Last updated July 2014.

Docking

Herzliya Marina is a fully developed marina with 800 berths and all facilities. It is the largest marina in Israel, providing full haul out and yard services, yachting chandlers and good restaurants in the marina as well as a shopping mall.
