Ashdod - Profile

Location

This city is located about 30 miles south of Tel Aviv and is a fairly grim concrete metropolis.

Clearance

Not a Port of Entry for yachts.

The marina is just south of the commercial harbour (31° 47 N 34° 37 E) and is very protected. Toilets, showers, laundry. Electricity, water. 40 ton travel hoist. Sore storage. Town 15 minute walk. Good security and helpful staff. Surroundings are uninspiring.
