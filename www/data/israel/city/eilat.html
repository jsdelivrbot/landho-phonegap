Eilat - Profile

Location

Eilat lies on the Red Sea, and is Israel’s southernmost city. It can also be approached via the Suez Canal. Eilat is a tourist centre and has some of the world’s most beautiful diving sites.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry. Arrange entry in advance (see comment below from cruising yacht that visited January 2015).
