Touho - Profile

Location

This small town lies on the north eastern side of the main island.

Clearance

All clearance should be done in Noumea.

Docking

There is a small marina here, however the anchorage is very sheltered. Dinghies can be landed at the marina and it's a 10 minute walk into town.

Last updated June 2016.
