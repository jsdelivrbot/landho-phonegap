Lifou Island (We) - Profile

Location

Lifou is the principal island of the Loyalty Group, and is a nice stopover on the route from Port Vila to Noumea.

We, the island's capital is on the East coast, and is most convenient for boats coming from that direction and those on course from Vanuatu to New Caledonia. There is a new marina here.

The North and West sides offer beautiful sandy beaches and good snorkeling. The local people are friendly and make you feel welcome.

Position 20° 56.5’S, 167° 12.8’E

Clearance

NOTE: As of September 2012 there are no longer Quarantine (Biosecurity) officials stationed here. This means that clearance is only possible here if you pre-arrange it with a Clearance Agent.

If you are planning to visit with a group, the fee can be shared amongst yachts.

Last updated April 2016.

Docking

Yachts need to clear in to Noumea before coming to Lifou and should contact the Marina Manager (Capitaine de Port) on arrival to arrange berthing inside the small marina. The marina at Port We is very well sheltered. There are also several protected anchorages on the north and west coasts of the island.

There is WiFi in the harbour included in the harbour fee.

Last updated June 2016.
