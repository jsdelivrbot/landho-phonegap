Location

Ua Huka is much more arid than the other Marquesian islands and has a very different look to it, as a brochure says, its the wild West of the Marquesas with its dry grasslands and dozens of wild horses and goats dotting the sky line.

Clearance

Not a port of entry. Report to the Gendarmerie.

Docking

Baie de Vaipae: narrow anchorage between high cliffs (can be a death trap with SE winds!).

If the Aranui cargo ship is due, it will tie to both cliffs and block you in or out of the harbor for the day.

If space is available, boats may come alongside the quay.
