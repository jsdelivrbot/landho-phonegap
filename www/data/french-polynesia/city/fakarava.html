Location

This is the second biggest atoll of the Tuamotu archipelago. Fakarava is an interesting atoll to visit.

Scuba diving at both passes is excellent and the channel between the passes is well marked so the very few coral heads are easily avoided. This atoll was classified as a biosphere reserve by UNESCO.

Pass Garuae (north) is very wide and easy and is near the town of Rotaova. Pass Tumakohua (25nm further south) is a little more tricky to negotiate but well marked.

Clearance

This is not a Port of Entry and so no official clearance is possible here.

However, it is important to report to the local gendarmerie.

Docking

Public dock available in Rotoava, the main town in the North for loading/unloading.

Check schedule of local freighters before mooring here.
