Mangareva - Profile

Location

The Gambier Islands lie about 1000NM southeast of Tahiti and consists of 14 small, mountainous islands. The largest and most populated is Mangareva whose main village is Rikitea with its impressive cathedral. Most of the population live in this village.

Clearance

Mangareva is the Port of Entry.

It is NO LONGER compulsory for all yachts visiting French Polynesia to visit Papeete Customs and Immigration to complete the clearing-in process.

Yachts can now clear in or out of FP with the “gendarmes” offices (French Police) at the ports of entry in the other islands.

The Gendarmerie Nationale in Mangareva can deal with Immigration and Customs formalities.

To reach the Gendarmerie, head south west from the dock. Their office is clearly marked and on the way into town, just past the Municipal Building. You will find the officers polite, friendly and easy to deal with.

For full details on clearance into and out of FP go to French Polynesia Formalities.

Last updated July 2014.

Docking

A buoyed channel leads into the anchorage inside the reef, off the main settlement at Rikitea. This anchorage offers good protection.
