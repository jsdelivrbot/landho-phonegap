Time Zone

UTC +2, +3 April to September.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Dinar (JD) of 1000 fils.

Import/export of foreign currency is unrestricted provided it is declared. Export of JD limited to JD300. Israeli currency is prohibited.

Communications

International Dialing code for Jordan is +962.

A prepaid Jordan SIM card with an international cell phone offers convenient and affordable while visiting Jordan. All incoming calls while in Jordan are FREE, regardless of where they originate, pay the local rate for local calls and use your cellular phone.
