Puerto Cortes - Profile

Location

Located on the Caribbean coast of Honduras about 20nm NE of the border with Guatemala. Puerto Cortez is a large and important container port, easy for provisioning.

Clearance

See Honduras Formalities for full details.

Yachts anchor off the naval base.

Checking in at Puerto Cortez is simple, professional and free. The captain must go into town to clear with the port captain and immigration. It´s a 30 limpera cab ride to the Port Captain and Immigration is walking distance nearby.

Officials are in the centre of town so it´s easy to do shopping as well. Prices here are much lower than in the Bay Islands and it is a recommended port by cruisers for checking-in and re-provisioning.

Checking out costs are $5 per passport to immigration, about $0.40 for copies about $1.75 for an international zarpe.

Last updated May 2013.

Docking

A new marina/haulout facility/5 star hotel is planned for Puerto Cortes.

In March 2015 a charter yacht was boarded by pirates not far from here, en-route from Belize to Roatan (see report adjacent). Care should be taken to avoid remote stretches of coastline here as a lone yacht.
