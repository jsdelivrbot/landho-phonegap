Canada - General Info

Time Zone

Canada has six time zones, with Newfoundland at UTC -3.5 to the Pacific coast at UTC -8. Most places add one hour for daylight saving from the 2nd Sunday in March to the 1st Sunday in November.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 120 V

Frequency: 60 Hz

Money

Canadian dollar of 100 cents (Can$).

Communications

International dialing code for Canada is +1 plus area/city code.

A prepaid Canada SIM card with an international cell phone is the most convenient and economical solution for staying in touch while in Canada. Pay the local rate for local calls and use a cellular phone in Canada like you do at home.
