Location

Sidney is a major boating centre on the east coast of Vancouver Island, approximately 30nm north of Victoria. There is an extensive range of boating supplies and facilities in this area.

Clearance

There are customs reporting stations at Canoe Cove Marina, Van Isle Marina and Port Sidney Marina.

At Canoe Cove, the customs dock is at the end of C dock. Stay between the yellow marker and the dock.

At Port Sidney, the Customs Post is in a tent at the end of F dock. During the summer it is staffed by Customs Officials, but for the rest of the year, telephone reporting facilities only, are provided.

For more details see Noonsite/Canada/Clearance.
