Charlottetown - Profile

Location

Located on the northern side of Hillsborough Bay on the southern coast of Prince Edward Island, Charlottetown lies at the confluence of the Hillsborough, the West and the North Rivers. It is the capital and largest city on the island and is noted for being the birthplace of the Canadian Confederation.

Position 46°14'N, 63°09'W.

Docking

Berths are available at the yacht club. There is also berthing at Quatermaster Marine at Peakes Wharf, which is the 2nd marina to port as you approach the harbour.

Last updated December 2011.
