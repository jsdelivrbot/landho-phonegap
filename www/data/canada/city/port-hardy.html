Port Hardy - Profile

Location

Located on the northern tip of Vancouver Island, it is the ferry port for ships travelling up the Inside Passage to Prince Rupert. It is also a useful starting point for visiting the Queen Charlotte Islands.

Position 50° 42.5'N, 127° 29.2'W.

Clearance

The harbour at Port Hardy is not an official Port of Entry for boats. The airport is, but it is unknown if it is possible for cruisers to clear in here. Calling Tel: 1-888-226-7277 or 204-983-3500 or 506-636-5064 will advise the skipper on the correct course of action.

Last updated October 2015.
