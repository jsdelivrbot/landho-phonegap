Kitimat - Profile

Location

This is about as far into the interior of Northern B.C as you can get in a boat! It is due west of the Queen Charlotte Islands and about 70nm inland.

Location 54° 01.3'N, 128° 37.04'W.

Docking

There is a government dock at Kitimat village, but MK Bay Marina is 7 miles from the town. There is a taxi.
