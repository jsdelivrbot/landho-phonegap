Nanaimo - Profile

Location

One of the principal ferry ports between Vancouver Island and the mainland.

Position 49° 10'04"N, 123° 55'35"W (southern entrance to Newcastle Channel)

Clearance

Customs post for Port of Entry clearance is based at the Nanaimo Port Authority marina on 'E' dock (next to the fuel barge).

For more details see Noonsite/Canada/Clearance.
