Lund - Profile

Location

Lund is a small port on the west coast of the Malaspina Peninsula which is on the mainland on the eastern coast of the Georgia Strait. It lies about 20nm north of Powell River, one of the main ferry crossings to Vancouver Island.

In the season, it is popular with hikers, sailors and other watersport enthusiasts.

Position 49° 58'53"N, 124° 45'51"W

Docking

This is a sheltered harbour with 2 pontoons for yachts. Boats can moor to the eastern side of the wave breaks but there is no shore access , power or water.

The hotel dock is more exposed to swell and has no power or water.

Last updated April 2015.
