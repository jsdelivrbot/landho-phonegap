Cortes Bay - Profile

Location

A well protected harbour of the southern side of Cortes Island. This island lies at the northern end of Georgia Strait, opposite Campbell River.

Position 50° 03'46"N, 124° 55'18"W (entrance to the bay)

Docking

There is a public dock as well as two yacht club out stations (Royal Vancouver YC and Seattle YC) here.

The bay is an excellent anchorage with good holding in 6 to 10m over mud and sand..

Beware of the frequent float plane activity.

Last updated April 2015.
