LaHave - Profile

Location

Lying at the entrance of the LaHave river on Nova Scotia’s south-eastern shore, this town is only about 7 miles (as the crow flies) south of Lunenburg although nearer 25nm by sea.

It is an attractive town with its historic area having many restored 18th century buildings.

Position 44° 18.4’N, 64° 24.3' W.

Docking

The yacht club here provides sheltered berthing in the river.
