Pender Island (summer only) - Profile

Location

Pender Island consists of two islands separated by a narrow canal. North Pender is the more populated one, while south Pender is very rural.

Bedwell harbour is on the south west coast of South Pender Island.

Position 48°44’56″N, 123°13’33″W. (Poets Cove marina).

Clearance

Customs officers are available at the marina at Poets Cove, Bedwell Harbour, from May to September. Their office is clearly marked.

Outside this time, a NEXUS pass is required.

Last updated December 2013.

Docking

There is plenty of anchorage space in both Port Browning and Bedwell harbours.

Last updated December 2013.
