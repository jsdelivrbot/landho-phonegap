Refuge Cove - Profile

Location

The most useful stop in this area for fuel, provisions etc if visiting Desolation Sound Marine Park.

Position 50°07.19'N, 124°51.34'W (entrance to the cove)

Docking

There is a public wharf here and two good anchorages. One anchorage is west of the public wharf in about 10-12m. The other, which is very sheltered, but shallower, is at the head on the cove in 2 to 6m over mud. Beware of the rocks at the entrance to this little bay.

There is a refuge barge based here during the high season.

Last updated May 2015.

Refuge Cove Public Wharf
Visitor's moorings. Open year round. Water, power (limited), fuel dock, WiFi, showers, laundry, provisions.
