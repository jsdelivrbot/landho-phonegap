Toronto - Profile

Docking

There are a large number of marinas or boat docks along the lake front and on the off-lying islands opposite Toronto. This group of islands form the southern boundary of the Inner Harbour.

Alexandra Yacht Club
2 Stadium Road , Toronto , Ontario M5V 2R9
Tel:+1 416 260 8690
http://www.ayc.on.ca , info@ayc.on.ca
Situated in the Western Channel, opposite Island Airport. Visitors' berths, and for members of clubs with reciprocal arrangements, the first two nights are free. Showers and washrooms in the clubhouse, pump-out facilities and fuel. Close to Downtown.
Bluffers Park Marina
on Lake Ontario , 7 Brimley Road S , Toronto ON M1M 1C1, Canada
Tel:+1 416 266 4556 Fax:+1 416 266 7808
http://www.bluffersparkmarina.ca , admin@bluffsparkmarina.com
Well marked harbour entrance (lighthouse) Facilities - Deep water harbour, 400 slip, full service marina, Land storage for trailered boats, Fuel dock, 40 ton mobile lift, Accommodation for boats to 70', Mast crane & mast storage. Restaurant, Pub and Snack Bar, Banquet facilities. Showers, washrooms, Laundry facilities. Paddle boats, Sailing School. (December 2008)

on Lake Ontario , 264 Queens Quay West, Suite 504 , Toronto M5J 1B5, Ontario Canada
Tel:001 416 203 2582 Fax:001 416 203 8492

The marina is on the western side of Mugg's Island, 5 minutes by tender from Downtown. A family club set in lovely treed and lawned surroundings. Visitors' berths, 25 ton travel hoist, repair facilities, restaurant and fuel.
