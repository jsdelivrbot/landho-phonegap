Gaspe - Profile

Location

Loacated on the southern shore at the entrance to the St Lawrence Seaway, Gaspe is a long established small port at the head of a deep inlet.

Postition 48°49.3'N, 64°26.2'W.

Docking

The marina is at the entrance to the York river which is just west of the commercial port. There is a bridge spanning the river from the marina on the south bank to the town on the north side.

Last updated August 2009.
