Campbell River - Profile

Location

Located halfway up the eastern coast of Vancouver Island, this is a popular tourist destination. It provides a useful jumping off point for exploring Desolation Sound.

Position 50°02 10"'N, 125°14'29"W (Discovery marina)

Clearance

There are clearance facilities at Discovery Harbour marina for CANPASS boaters.

Last updated October 2015.

Docking

There are several marinas in the heart of downton Campbell River as well as smaller docks both north and south of the town as well as across on the other side of the Georgia Strait.

Most supplies and services can be found here.

Last updated May 2015.
