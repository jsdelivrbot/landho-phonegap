Sao Tome and Principe - General Info

Time Zone

UTC

Electrical Supply

220V / 50Hz.

Money

dobras

Euros and US dollars are also accepted.

There are no ATMs.

Credit cards are accepted in a very few tourist places.

Communications

The international dialing code  is +239

Transport

In São Tomé town there are shared taxis but no other public transport anywhere.
