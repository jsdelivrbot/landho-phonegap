Saint Gilles les Bains - Profile

Location

This small port lies about 10nm south west of Le Port. It is a popular seaside resort.

Position 21° 03'16"S, 55° 13'19"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

Almost all spaces in the part are taken up by local sport fishing boats and visiting yachts may not find a berth.

Last updated August 2016.
