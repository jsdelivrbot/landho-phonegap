Location

This atoll in the Phoenix Group of Islands is a former US satellite tracking station. Only a handful of families live there and only three supply ships call a year.

This is one of the islands where climate change is having a severe impact.

See comment at bottom of page from September 2014. Permission is now required to visit here.

Clearance

When approaching the island, call Kanton Radio on VHF 16. On arrival the officials will visit the boat to carry out clearance procedures and collect fees.

Last updated October 2009.

Docking

The dredged ship pass should be entered near slack water and the anchorage is inside the dredged turning basin. The natural pass is not recommended.

There is also a mooring not far from the wharf.

The village is three miles away; officials will visit the yacht.

Last updated December 2009.
