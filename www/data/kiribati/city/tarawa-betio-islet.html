Tarawa (Betio Islet) - Profile

Location

Tarawa is the capital of Kiribati and lies in the group of atolls forming the northern Gilberts.

Pilotage into the lagoon here is straightforward. It is a wide deep pass used by ships, and is currently buoyed at each end of the channel. Take care with the entry waypoints on the Kiribati website (yachting information), as these take you very close to the port side of the channel on entry.
