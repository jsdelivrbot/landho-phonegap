La Rochelle - Profile

Location

La Rochelle is a beautiful Medieval City with an imposing fortified old harbour dating back to the Middle Ages. Due to the high tides on this coast, the old harbour has lock gates, the confinement however is worth it as the old harbour is right in the heart of the City. Cruisers may find it easier to use La Rochelle as a base from which to visit other ports and towns nearby by hire car.

Position 48°08' 51"N, 01°10' 37"W

Docking

Beware that occasional dredging takes place in the channel. Take particular care if arriving in the dark as there are also unlit buoys and pole markers.

If wishing to moor in the Old Port (max. length 14m), it is advisable to make arrangements in advance, especially during the high season.

The huge Port des Minimes is some way from the city centre and is to sta'bd at the start of the approach channel to the Vieux Port.

La Rochelle Vieux Port

Tel:+33 (0)5 46 41 32 05 / (0)6 03 54 00 57 Fax:+33 (0)5 46 41 38 04 ,VHF Channel 09
http://www.portlarochelle.com , vieuxport@portlarochelle.com
Opening hours: 8:00-12:00 / 14:00-17:45(low season), 06:00 / 21:00 (high season)
There are 2 berthing possibilities in the Old Port. Firstly in the inner basin where there are 40 berths for visitors. Access, through a lock, is possible from 2 hours before high water to 30 minutes after and the minimum draft is 4m. Secondly in the outer basin where there are also 40 visitors' berths but minimum draft is 1.3m. Both basins are close to the centre of the town.
Port des Minimes
Capitainerie, Mole Central , 17025 La Rochelle Cedex 1 , France
Tel:+33 (0)5 46 44 41 20 Fax:+33 (0)5 46 44 36 49 ,VHF Channel 09
http://www.portlarochelle.com , capitainerie@portlarochelle.com
A huge marina with 5000 berths. Access is limited by the depth in the approach channel which dredged to .5m below CD. Visitors berth on the RH side of the arrivals pontoon and on pontoons 1 to 7.
All facilities, fuel (24hrs), laundry, bike rental. Boatyard with 3 travel hoists with capacities of 10 to 50 tons.
This marina is about 2.5 kilometres from the city centre.
