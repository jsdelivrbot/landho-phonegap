Bastia - Profile

Location

Bastia is one of the two largest cities in Corsica and is on the NE coast of the island. Its port was, and is, the source of its prosperity.

The narrow inlet of the Old-Port is one of the most lively areas of Bastia and is right in the heart of it.

Position 42° 42.5' N, 09° 27.4' E.

Docking

There are berths both on the quay and on buoys (mostly occupied by local craft). When berthing, beware of rocks within 2 or 3 m of the quay walls.

In calm weather you can anchor just south of the walled city over a sand bottom, close to a small beach below the main road. From the anchorage it is a short dinghy ride into the harbour where you can tie up.
