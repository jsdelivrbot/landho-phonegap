Port la Nouvelle - Profile

Location

A commercial port and seaside resort which prides itself on its miles of clean, sandy beaches. This port offers the shortest route to the sea if approaching the Mediterranean via the Canal du Midi.

Position 43° 00'46"'N, 03° 04'14"'E. (Harbour entrance)

Docking

Berths are at the far end of the channel, beyond the commercial port. Access to the Canal de la Robine is marked by a low bridge at the end of the Port de Plaisance.

Last updated March 2015.
