Docking

Call the Harbourmaster on arrival on VHF channel 09 for instructions on berthing.

It is advised to avoid arriving between 11am and 12:30 and 17:30 to 18:30 as the harbour is particularly busy then.

The arrivals/visitors' berths at Port de St.Tropez are on the inside of the northern breakwater, and opposite, on the quay in front of the capitainerie.

During the summer, do not be surprised if no place is available in the old port if your yacht is less than 22m. Advance booking is essential.

There are several alternatives to the marina at St Tropez itself, especially if any repairs or nautical hardware are required. Cogolin and Port Grimaud are both close by.

There is a good, quiet anchorage in Bay de Canebiers. Anchor west of rocks "La Croisette". There is a pleasent sandy beach and good walks in the nature researve here.
