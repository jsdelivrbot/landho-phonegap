Port-Vendres - Profile

Location

This attractive deep-water port is just 10nm from the Spanish border. It is both an important fishing and commercial harbour. The old town is interesting and there is also a 13th century castle.

Position 42°31′08″N, 3°06′21″E.

Docking

There are only a few moorings for visitors, so booking ahead is advisable in high season.

The visitors pontoon is immediately ahead when entering the inner harbour and is well sheltered in all winds. Do not enter the harbour if three lights are flashing RED.

No anchoring is permitted in the harbour.

Last updated March 2015.
