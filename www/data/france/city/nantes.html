Nantes - Profile

Location

Nantes lies on the river Loire about 30km up river from St Nazaire. It is one of Fance's largest cities. Approximately 1km beyond the Pont de Cheviré road bridge, the river divides. The downtown area has some interesting timber-framed medieval houses.

Position 47°11′52″N, 01°34′39″W.

Docking

It is best to take the whole flood tide to reach the city from St Nazaire.

The left-hand branch of the river leads to the Belon Jetty which is on the port side. The tide run fast here, but mooring is straightforward. There are showers and toilets and security. It is close to the city centre.

There are no longer any moorings for private boats on the island side. This is reserved for tour boats only.
