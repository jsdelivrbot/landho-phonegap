Gruissan - Profile

Location

Just 30nm southwest of Sete, this sheltered port is a practical alternative as a place to over-winter or to haul out to do work on the boat. It lies just 5 miles from Narbonne. This once small fishing village is now a tourist resort.

Position 42° 52' N, 3° 03' E.

Docking

This marina is easy to enter day or night.

As well as berths in the marina, there are moorings belonging to Nautiland (see boatyards). Their yard is in the industial section and is very clean and a good place to work on your boat. See this report for reviews by cruisers: South of France, Gruissan: Winter Storage at Nautiland.

Last updated March 2015.
