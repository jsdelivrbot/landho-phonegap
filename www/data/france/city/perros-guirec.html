Perros Guirec - Profile

Location

This is a useful port of call on the pink granite coast of northern Brittany. It is linked with the attractive little port of Plumanac'h on the other side of the headland.

Position 48° 48'09"N, 03° 26'19"W (marina entrance)

Docking

The marina here is well sheltered and accessed through a very narrow gate. Access opens when tidal rise is +7m. At some neap tides they may be no access in or out for up to 4 days.

There are 5 while visitor's buoys and an anchorage in 3m with good holding just east of Pointe du Chateau. It is exposed in north easterlies.

The centre of the town is a stiff climb up the hill, but there is a chandlery, shops and restaurants close to the marina.

Last updated April 2016.
