Antibes - Profile

Location

This old, attractive town is situated midway between Nice and Cannes and has a large number of visitors' berths, mostly in Port Gallice.

Position 43° 35'32"N, 07° 07'53"E. (Harbour entrance)

Docking

Port Vauban-Antibes is close to the centre of the town. Port Gallice is on the opposite side of the Cap D'Antibes peninsular. Across the bay from there is the marina at Port Camille Rayon. Just 3km east of Antibes, is the port of Baie des Anges.

It is advisable to book any of these marinas in advance.

Last updated March 2015.
