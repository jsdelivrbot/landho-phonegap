Port St Louis du Rhone - Profile

Location

A useful port, being one of the major entrances to Europe's system of inland waterways. It is approximately 40NM west of Marseilles.

It is just north of the marina, Port Napoleon, which is more suited for its boatyards as it is quite a distance from the town.

Position 43° 23'29"N, 04° 52'23"E. (entrance channel mark)
