Port Joinville (L’Ile d’Yeu) - Profile

Location

The of port Joinville is on the north east coast of this small island which lies about 10nm west of mainland France and approximately 50nm south of Saint Nazaire. The port is an important fishing harbour and consists of several basins, one of which is the marina. The island itself is an interesting place to explore by bike as there are practically no cars.

Position 46° 43' 45" N, 02° 20' 45"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

The marina provides very good shelter. It gets very busy in the season and you are likely to be met on entry and escorted to a berth.

New port added January 2013.
