Ile Rousse - Profile

Location

Located on the north west coast of Corsica, Ile Rousse is about 15nm NE of Calvi.

Position 42°38’ 5'N, 08°56’ 3'E.

Docking

This harbour provides excellent shelter, including from northerly winds.

It may be possible to moor or anchor in the bay, but check with the Harbour Master. Be aware of the large ferries which arrive there.

Last updated March 2015.
