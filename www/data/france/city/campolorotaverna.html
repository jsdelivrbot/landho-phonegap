CampoloroTaverna - Profile

Location

This port, on the east coast, is near the towns of San Nicoloa and Cervione. It is adjacent to a wetland area and mosquitoes can be a problem.

Position 42°20.47' N, 09°32.50' E.

Docking

It is easy to enter in most but NE winds.

No anchoring is allowed in the harbour. Turn immediately to port once through the entrance, and the reception pontoon is ahead.

Last updated March 2015.
