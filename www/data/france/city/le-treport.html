Le Treport - Profile

Location

A useful port of call on the Channel coast between Dieppe and Calais. A small fishing port with an illuminated calvery at the entrance.

Position 50° 04'N, 01° 22'E (harbour approach)

Docking

Difficult to enter in strong onshore winds. Approach dries half a mile out from pierheads. Beware of cross currents.

Keep to the western side of the channel. The marina is entered through a lock which opens HW +/-4.

Last updated June 2016.
