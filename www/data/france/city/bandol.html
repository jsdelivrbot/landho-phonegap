Bandol - Profile

Location

The old town and attractive town of Bandol enjoys a permanent sunny climate and is famous for its wine. It is situated in the heart of Provence between Marseilles and Toulon.

Location 43° 08'0 N, 005° 45'5.E

Clearance

The Customs and Port Authority offices are on the seaward side of the eastern quay, near the beach.

Customs
Quai Port , 83150 Bandol , France
Tel:+33 (0)8 99 02 21 78 Fax:+33 (0)8 99 02 21 78

Docking

Once through the entrance, turn immediately to starboard and berth stern-to on the Accueil pontoon. This is just beyond the fuel dock. Then report to the Capitainerie.

Good shelter but if busy (e.g. expecting regatta) you could be evicted regardless of weather.

The best facilities block is at the west end of the marina.

Daily market by the dock.
