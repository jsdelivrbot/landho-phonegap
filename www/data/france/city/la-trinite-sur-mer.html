La Trinite-sur-Mer - Profile

Location

This town is a major yachting port and has excellent sailing facilities. It has a flourishing oyster industry. As a holiday town, also, it is well provided with shops and restaurants.

It lies on the northern shore of the Quiberon Bay.

Position 47°33' 55"N, 03°00' 41"W (river entrance)

Docking

The shelter in the marina is good, but more exposed at high tide.

There is a good neap-tide anchorage in the Saint-Pilbert river, just to the east of the town, and another, more exposed, west of Pointe St-Colomban.
