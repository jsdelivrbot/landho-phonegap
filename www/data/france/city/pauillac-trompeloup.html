Pauillac-Trompeloup - Profile

Location

Situated on the west bank of the Gironde River, this town is in the heart of the wine growing district of Bordeaux.

Position 45° 11' 50"N, 0° 44' 40"W (marina)

Docking

If travelling up the Gironde to Bordeaux, the the marina at Pauillac is a useful stop. The entrance to the marina is shallow (<1m) at low tide, but ther are 2 white waiting buoys near the entrance.

Last updated November 2015.

It is advisable to anchor just N of the marina (or pick up the waiting buoy) to wait for slack water before entering. The tide runs very strongly through the berths here.
Visitors are on the first two pontoons. Max depth 2m. It has all facilities, fuel, WiFi. 1 ton mast crane, boatyard with 8 ton boat hoist, some repairs. Restaurants and laundrette close by.
