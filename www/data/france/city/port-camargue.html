Port Camargue - Profile

Location

This is a huge man-made port/marina and is flanked by wonderful beaches.

Position 43°31' 18"N, 04°07' 19"E

Clearance

Port Police
Tel:+33 (0)4 66 93 21 15.

Docking

The port here  provides an extensive range of boat repair facilities and services.

Visitors berth between posts in the area to port, just inside the inner part of the marina. A useful plan of the port area.

A helpful capitainerie and a useful place to over-winter.

Advisable to call in advance to be allocated a berth.

Last updated March 2015.

Large marina with 400 visitor's berths. All facilities, fuel, pump-out, WiFi, laundry, chandlery, provisions. Yacht club with clubhouse and restaurant. Boatyard with 5, 16 and 50 ton hoists and 80 ton crane, scrubbing grid. Wide range of repair services. Security.
