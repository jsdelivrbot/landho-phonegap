Calvi - Profile

Location

Located on the north west coast of Corsica, this ancient town is a popular tourist destination. It is well marked by the citadel on the headland.

Position 42°33' 54"N, 08°45' 38"E

Docking

Arrivals should initially berth on the end of the quay to sta'board on entering the port. Alternatively, pick up a mooring just off the harbour wall. The mooring buoys are by the town during the summer and are managed separately from the marina. They are an economic alternative to the marina. In addition, the moorings enjoy excellent shelter from prevailing winds and it is only a short dinghy trip to the dock.

As this port can be rather chaotic in the busy season, it is best to arrive in the morning if possible.

There is no anchoring in the harbour, but a good anchorage can be found in 3 to 10m south east of the port.
