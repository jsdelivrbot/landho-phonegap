Le Havre - Profile

Location

This is a busy port and although not having the charm of Honfleur across the river, it is a useful stop, being a major yachting centre.

Position 49° 29'24"N, 00° 04'39"E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

If arriving from outside the EU, contact the Port Control on VHF channel 12 or 20 on approach for instructions.

Last updated July 2016.

Docking

This marina is well sheltered and accessible at any tide, both day and night.

The marina is to port, round the short breakwater and the visitors' berths are to sta'bd round the second breakwater.

Last updated April 2016.
