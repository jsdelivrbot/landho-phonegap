Piriac-sur-mer - Profile

Location

This useful port of call lies approximately 8nm south of the entrance to the Villaine river on the Guerande Peninsula. It is a pleasant place to stop if coast hopping through the Bay of Biscay. It prides itself on being 'kid friendly'.

Position 47° 22.5'  N, 2° 32.3'  W.

Docking

There is a drying cill into the marina and beware of the flow of the tide when entering.

There are good bus connections to La Baule and Guerande.

Last updated December 2012.
