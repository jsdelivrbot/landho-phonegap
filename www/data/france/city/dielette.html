Dielette - Profile

Location

Located on the west coast of Cotentin Peninsula and just 25nm east of Guernsey, this is an attractive little port surrounded by sandy beaches.

Position 49° 33'15"N, 01° 51'54"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

This port has tidal access with a cill into the inner harbour/marina. Access is at chart datum +5m.

Last updated April 2016.

All facilities, fuel dock, pump-out, laundry. Boatyard with 40 ton hoist, mast crane, dry storage.
