La Baule(Pornichet) - Profile

Location

This large holiday town sits at the eastern end of the 5 mile long sandy beach which stretches along this coast from la Baule.

Position 47°15.3'N, 002°21.1'W (marina entrance).

Docking

The large marina is straight forward to enter by day or night and at any state of tide. The entrace is rather narrow.

It is about half a mile from the town, which has all the facilities of a holiday resort.

Last updated December 2012.
