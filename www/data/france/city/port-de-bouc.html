Port de Bouc - Profile

Location

A small convenient port in a rather industrialized area about 20nm north west of Marseille and across the bay from Port Saint Louis du Rhone.

Position 43° 23'36"N, 04° 59'E (harbour entrance)

Docking

The Port du Plaisance is located in the north western corner of the harbour.

Call the port Captain for advice on where to berth.
