St Denis d’Oleron - Profile

Location

This small town is located on the eastern side of the northern tip of the Isle d'Oleron. It is approximately 15nm south west of La Rochelle. It has excellent beaches on the west coast of the island.

Position 46° 02'02"N, 01° 21'58"W (marina)

Docking

Access to the marina here is limited to 2 hours each side of HW.

The arrivals pontoon in front of the capitainiere with the tide guage in the foreground.

There is a anchorage outside the harbour, but it is exposed to winds from the north and east.

Added as a new port January 2013.
