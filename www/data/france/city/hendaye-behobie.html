Hendaye-Behobie - Profile

Location

Hendaye is a sprawling town right on France’s Atlantic border with Spain. The old medieval Basque sits on a hill over looking the Bay of Chingoudy and the river Bidassoa estuary, and with impressive views of the foothills of the Pyrenees Mountains to the west. Down by the shore is the more modern town and the marina.

Position 43° 22'53"N, 001° 47'08"W (river entrance)

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry.

As both countries are part of the Schengen Area, locating the necessary officials if arriving from outside the EU, might not be easy.

Docking

This is a 'port of refuge' and can be entered at any time and in any weather.

This French marina is to port, past the Spanish marina on the sta'bd side.

See Hondarribia for details of the Spanish marina.

It is also possible to anchor  among the moorings close to the fishing harbour on the Spanish side of the river, or among those further up beyond the marinas.
