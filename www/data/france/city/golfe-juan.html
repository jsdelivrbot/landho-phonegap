Golfe Juan - Profile

Location

Situated approximately halfway between Nice and Antibes, the port here offers facilities for large yachts and super-yachts.

Position 43° 33'46" N, 07° 04'30" E. (harbour entrance)

Docking

On entering the harbour, Port Camille Rayon is straight ahead, and Port de Golfe Juan is through the narrow entrance immediately to port, round the Quai Sud.

Last updated March 2015.
