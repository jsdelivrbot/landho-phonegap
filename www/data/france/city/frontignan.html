Frontignan - Profile

Location

This town in the Languedoc region is famous for the production of muscat wine. It lies just 3nm NE of Sete.

Position 43° 25'46"N, 03° 46'36"E (entrance to the channel)

Clearance

Customs (Sete)
Tel: +33 (0)467 186490

Docking

The arrivals quay is to port in the narrow channel between the outer and inner parts of the harbour.

Do not attempt to enter in easterly winds of F7 or more.

Last updated March 2015.
