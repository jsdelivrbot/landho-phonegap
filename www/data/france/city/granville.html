Granville - Profile

Location

An attractive town to visit. Approaches are shallow and dangerous in strong on-shore winds.

Position 48° 49'54"N, 01° 35'48"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

In settled weather it is possible to anchor just north of the harbour entrance off the fine sandy beach. The marina offers complete shelter but being completely surrounded with concrete it can be very hot in summer.

There is a chandlery and restaurants adjacent to the marina and shops not far away.

Last updated April 2016.
