Benodet - Profile

Location

Sitting near the mouth of the river Odet, Benodet is a principal yachting centre. The town is a pleasant holiday resort with good restaurants and a large supermarket, as well as a good beach. On the opposite bank is the much quieter village of Sainte Marine, with just a few shops and restaurants.

Position 47° 51'40"N, 04° 06'24"W (river entrance)

Docking

In addition to the two marinas, there are river mooring buoys (marked V). There are also anchorages off Anse du Trez at Benodet and, in off shore winds, to the W of Pointe de  Combrit
