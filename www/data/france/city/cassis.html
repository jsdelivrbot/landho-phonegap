Cassis - Profile

Location

This pleasant town is on a very attractive part of the coast, about 20 miles east of Marseille.

Position 43° 12'45"N, 05° 32'E (harbour entrance)

Clearance

Customs
32 Place Mirabeau, Cassis
Tel: +33 (0)442 017732
This is situated off the NE corner of the harbour.


Docking

The arrivals pontoon is on the southern side of the harbour, at the root of the fuel quay. The marina is often very full, but it is worth visiting. La Ciotat is a useful alternative.

Last updated February 2015.
