Quimper - Profile

Location

The idyllic town of Quimper is spread around the junction of the Steir and Odet Rivers on the western edge of Brittany. It has its medieval character intact with old buildings overhanging narrow lanes and footbridges.

Position 47° 58'52"N, 04° 07'W (bridge below the town)

Docking

Masted boats need to anchor 1/2 mile below the town and complete their journey up the beautiful Odet river, by dinghy.
