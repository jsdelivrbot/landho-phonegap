Concarneau - Profile

Location

This is an interesting, walled medieval town and the port is its heart. It is still a busy fishing centre and together with tourism, forms the main activity here.

Position 47° 51'58"N, 03° 54'50"W

Docking

Within the Baie de la Foret are two popular sailing destinations, Port de la Foret and the old town of Concarneau.

La Foret has one of the largest mainas in Brittany and is a safe place to leave a yacht.

Concarneau is a more bustling place and a busy fishing port. Some areas of the port are shallow, so care should be taken when finding a berth.

Last updated April 2016.
