St Nazaire - Profile

Location

Best known for its modern shipbuilding and the 2nd World war submarine pens. The town was completely destroyed during the war and has been rebuild in a rather nondescript style.

Position 45° 15'56"N, 02° 12' 14"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

The large outer harbour is for cruise ships, and yachts use the lock to the east to pass into Bassin de St Nazaire.

Bassin de St Nazaire
Tel:+33 (0)2 40 00 45 20
Berth alongside the wall at the S end of the basin. The pontoons on the west side are officially for organised events. Long way from the town and limited repair services for yachts.
