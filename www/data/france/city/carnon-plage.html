Carnon-Plage - Profile

Location

A popular seaside resort about 15nm east of Sete. Just 5 miles inland is the attractive city of Montpelier.

Position 43° 32′ 49″ N, 3° 58′ 45″ E.

Docking

This is a very large marina.  Visitors should first berth near the port office (to sta'bd past the fish quay).

Last updated September 2014.
