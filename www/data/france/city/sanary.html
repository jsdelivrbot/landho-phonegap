Sanary - Profile

Location

This is a delightful and charming little town and port. It is just 5nm south east of Bandol.

Harbour at Sanary sur mer :©S/Y ChaliVentures III

Position 43°07′N, 5°48′E.

Docking

It is possible to anchor off in the bay, but only in settled weather as the holding is suspect.

Call on VHF channel 09 or telephone in advance of arrival. Possible to book a berth 48 hours in advance but stays are limited to 2 days in high season.

The visitors' berths are on pontoon A, directly opposite the entrance.

Last updated October 2014.
