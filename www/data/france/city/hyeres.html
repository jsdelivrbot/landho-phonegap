Hyeres - Profile

Location

The Port of Hyeres is separated from the city of Hyeres by the airport, which is, therefore, several miles further inland.

Position 43° 04'39"N, 06° 09'28"E. (Harbour entrance)

Clearance

Customs, based in the port, Tel: +33 (0)494 576032

Docking

The Port de Plaisance is very large, having 3 main basins. Visitors enter round the southern-most arm of the breakwater and berth on the arrivals pontoon. This is now to sta'bd (not to port) and a long way from the office and facilities.

In settled weather it is possible to anchor south of the entrance.

Last updated October 2014.
