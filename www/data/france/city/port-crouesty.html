Port Crouesty - Profile

Location

This port is a useful base from which to explore both the Bay of Quiberon and the Golf of Morbihen.

Position 47°32'27"N, 02°54'07"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

This extensive marina has all facilities. Access is possible at all states of the tide, but care must be taken to keep to the marked approach channel at low tide, as it is bounded by some rather unfriendly rocks.

In addition to all boating services, the marina has many shops and restaurants, as well as a good supermarket. The near-by village of Boug d'Arzon has a baker, butcher and small provision shops and an open-air market on Monday mornings (in season).

Last updated October 2015.
