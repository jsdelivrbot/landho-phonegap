Saint-Valery-sur-Somme - Profile

Location

Adjacent to the Baie de la Somme and at the mouth of the Somme river, this port is an access point for the Northern France inland waterway. With its Medieval charm, the town and surrounding area is popular with tourists.

Position 50° 13'57"N, 01° 28'49"E (river entrance)

Docking

Located at the mouth of the Somme Ship Canal and the bottom of the Baie de Somme. The marina has 250 berths and can accommodate boats up to 16m LOA and 2.3m draft. Access is 2.5 hours either side of high tide. Visitor berths are at the end of the pontoons. Facilities include toilets, showers, laundry and WiFi.
