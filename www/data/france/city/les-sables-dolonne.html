Les Sables d'Olonne - Profile

Location

This attractive port in the North Eastern sector of the Bay of Biscay has become better known now through its hosting of the Vendee Globe Round the World yacht race.

The town is an interesting mix of holiday resort, fishing port and yacht harbour. Some of the world's best sea salt is produced here, using traditional methods.

Position 46°29' 16"N, 01°47' 23"W (port entrance)

Docking

Report to the captainerie pontoon (shown below) at the entrance to Port Olona.

This huge marina is the main mooring for visiting yachts and provides all possible services.

There is now also new, small  marina on the S side of the Bassin de Peche, which is the first basin to sta'bd after the harbour entrance. This is much closer to the town and the beaches.
