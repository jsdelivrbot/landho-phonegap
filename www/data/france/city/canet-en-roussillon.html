Canet-en-Roussillon - Profile

Location

This small port lies about 20nm north of the Spanish border.

Position 42° 42'11" N, 03° 02'35" E. (harbour entrance)

Docking

Entry is not advisable in very strong NE winds.

Approach - the twin pillars of the lighthouse are not obvious coming from the North until quite close.

The arrivals/visitors berths are on the first pontoon to port on entering the first basin of the inner harbour. A prior call on VHF usually results in a quick reply and help with berthing if necessary.

The shore storage area and comprehensive repair facilities are on the north side of the harbour. There is a ferry service across the basin.

Last updated March 2015.
