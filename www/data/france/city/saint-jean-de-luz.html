Saint-Jean-de-Luz - Profile

Location

Saint Jean de Luz is a charmingly attractive resort with a distinct Basque identity. It is just 8nm north east of the border with Spain.

It has a natural harbour and is an active port and important base for sardine, anchovy and tuna fishing. The town lies on the north bank of the river, with Cibourne on the south.

Position 43° 24'05"N, 01° 40'21"W (harbour entrance)

Docking

The outer harbour is well protected with breakwaters and rocks. The inner harbour, with the marina on the Ciboure side, is totally secure.

The fishing harbour is to port on entering the inner harbour.

There is a small anchorage just inside the western breakwater and another, larger area, off the beach opposite St Jean de Luz. There are also 20 mooring buoys for yachts on passage.

Last updated June 2016.
