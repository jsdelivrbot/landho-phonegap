Capbreton - Profile

Location

A useful port of call is making short hops down the Bay of Biscay coast. This is a holiday town about 30nm north of Biarritz.

Position 43° 39'18"N, 01° 26'55"W (port entrance)

Docking

The entrance to the port is narrow and probably not safe to enter in strong on shore winds or in any swell.

There is a marina here.

New port added February 2013.
