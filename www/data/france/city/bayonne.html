Bayonne - Profile

Location

Bayonne is a beautifully preserved cathedral city, offering narrow streets lined with half-timbered houses, between the mountains and the sea a few miles up the coast from Biarritz. It is a busy port and has an interesting medieval town centre with ramparts and quays and lively markets and festivals.

Position 43° 31'45"N, 001° 31'10"W (river entrance).

Docking

Although principally a commercial port, there is a yacht marina on the southern bank of the Ardour river, opposite the first part of the port.

Safe to enter is any weather and well lit at night. Beware of fishing nets/lobster pots around the entrance.

There is a good yacht club  here.

Last updated August 2015.
