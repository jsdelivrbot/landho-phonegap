Bonifacio - Profile

Location

Situated on the southern tip of the island, this town is regarded as one of the most attractive on Corsica. It is however reported to be very expensive, with over-zealous Customs (see comment below).

Position 41°23'N, 09°09'E (Harbour entrance)

Clearance

Customs
quai Banda del Ferro
Tel:+33 (0)495 517151/730353

Docking

It has a large number of visitors' berths but can get very crowded in the season.

Beware of the ground chains in the anchorage.

Good marina and recommended as the best harbour in Corsica.

Last updated March 2015.
