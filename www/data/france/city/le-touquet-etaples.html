Le Touquet-Etaples - Profile

Location

Etaples is a working fishing port at the mouth of the river Canche. It is a dynamic little place which has survived many a fire and invasion.

Position 50° 32'40"N, 01° 32'30"E (safe water mark)

Docking

The estuary dries a long way out and the buoyed channel constantly shifts. In even moderate south westerly winds the seas break heavily well out to sea. Aim for the safe water mark and the current channel markers should be seen.

Shelter is good except in strong north westerlies.

Beware of the strong current in the river. The marina is down stream of the low bridge.

Last updated June 2016.
