La Ciotat - Profile

Location

This port is situated midway between Marseille and Toulon and is very popular with superyachts making space tight.

Position 43°10′37″N, 5°36′31″E.

Docking

Visitors can berth in the Bassin Berouard (bassin Sud), the entrance to which is at the southern end of the breakwater. These berths can be rather exposed in strong easterly winds.

An alternative berth may be found in La Ciotat Vieux Port on the Quai General de Gaulle which is to sta'bd beyond the commercial docks. There is good protection here.
