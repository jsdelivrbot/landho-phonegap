Porto-Vecchio - Profile

Location

Porto-Vecchio is a ferry port at the southern end of Corsica's est coast.

This is an attractive port with an interesting 'Old Town' with narrow, cobbled streets full of bars, restaurants and historic buildings.

It is a 10 minute (steep) walk up the hill from the marina. There is also an alternative longer, but less testing route up to the town.

Position 41° 35.4'N, 9° 17.1'E.

Docking

In addition to mooring in the marina, it is possible to anchor, out of the channel, close to the commercial docks, where it is very sheltered.

Another anchorage can be found east of the port (41° 35.512'N, 009° 18.072'E) with good holding. You are allowed to tie the dinghy up to the pontoon closest to the marina office.

Baie Stagnoli has good holding but is reported to be often noisy with wet bikes from the afternoon until well into the evening.
