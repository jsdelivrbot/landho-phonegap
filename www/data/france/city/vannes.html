Vannes - Profile

Location

If cruising in the Gulf of Morbihan, then a visit to the medieval city of Vannes is a must. Although it's at the head of the Gulf, up a mile-long channel, the city is charming, with beautiful gardens and historic buildings.

Position 47° 38'21"N, 02° 45' 39"W (lock)

Docking

The lock gate into Vannes opens for 2 1/2 hrs each side of local HW between 0800-2200 in the summer. The road bridge opens on the hour and half hour when the lock is open. There is usually a harbour launch to direct you to a berth.

Last updated June 2016.
