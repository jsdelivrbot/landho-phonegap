Port-Leucate - Profile

Location

Leucate is known for its huge beach and its excellent oysters. This port is located about 45nm north of the Spanish border.

Position 42° 52'28"N, 03° 03'15"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

The visitors' berths are well inside the port, in Bassin Central, which is to port approximately halfway down the Anse de Correge. Beware that the depths at the entrance might be shallower than indicated.

Last updated March 2015.
