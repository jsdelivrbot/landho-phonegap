Ajaccio - Profile

Location

Situated on the west coast of the island, Ajaccio occupies a sheltered position at the foot of wooded hills. This is the birthplace of Napoleon Bonaparte, Emperor of France, and his association with the town are everywhere.

Position 41°55' 59"N, 08°44' 40"E (marina entrance)
