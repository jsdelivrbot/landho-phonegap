Nice - Profile

Location

This is one of the largest cities in France. It lies about 15nm west of the Italian border. It is a popular tourist destination.

Position 43°42′N, 7°17′E (marina).

Docking

The old port of Nice is called Port Lympia and lies on the eastern side of the city. Yachts of any size can berth in the marina if an advance request is made.

Last updated March 2015.
