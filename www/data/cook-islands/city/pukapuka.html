Pukapuka - Profile

Location

Because of its isolated location, a warm welcome awaits any visiting yacht. There are three villages, as well as a hospital.

The island was severely damaged by a cyclone in 2005, but these islanders are tough, and have rebuilt their community.

Position 10° 51'73"S, 165° 51'25"W

Clearance

Whilst this is a clearance port of sorts, yachts wanting to clear at Pukapuka must pay for transporting the Customs officer by air or sea. Not a very practical option unless there is a large group of yachts.

See Cook Islands Formalities for complete clearance information.
