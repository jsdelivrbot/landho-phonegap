Location

Ebeye has a population of about 14,000 and provides access to some of the world's best wreck diving. Kwajalein lagoon has numerous WW2 and earlier wrecks including the famous Prinz Eugen, the escort ship to Germany's Bismark.

The neighbouring island of Kwajalein is off limits to all but US military personnel and their permitted visitors.

Clearance

On arrival, contact officials on Channel 16 for instructions where to come alongside for clearance. If there is no space, it may be necessary to anchor.

See Marshall Islands Clearance page for details.

Last updated April 2014.

Docking

Most yachts anchor on the east side of the T-pier, between the Mobil tanks and the old dock.
