Marshall Islands - General Info

Time Zone

Majuro UTC +12, Kwajalein UTC -12

Opening Hours

Shops and businesses are closed on Sundays.

Money

United States dollar (US$)

Credit Cards accepted by many merchants in Majuro.

ATMs in in Uliga and Delap.

Communications

International Dialing Code for Marshall Islands is +692.

Mobile Phones - International roaming is available in Majuro and Ebeye

National Telecommunications Authority (NTA) provides:  Mobile phones, SIM cards, and WIFI hotspots around town, including Internet service to the north and south mooring fields in Majuro.

Prepaid unlimited WIFI internet hookup for laptop is $35/month.

Unlimited WIFI for smart phone or tablet is $15/month.

The Mieco Beach Yacht Club runs Monday to Saturday VHF net at 7:30am on VHF 71 and a daily SSB net at 7:45am on 6.224 USB.  The latter is called the Yokwe Net (Yokwe means hello).

Mail

The islands are part of the US postal service and enjoy a U.S. Postal Code (Zip code: 96960).

Mail sent from the U.S. arrives within (usually) 6-10 business days by U.S. Priority Mail.  No duty is usually paid by vessels in transit. It’s recommended you use USPS Priority Mail as it is cost effective. The address to use is:-
Name of Yacht
First and Last Name
General Delivery
Majuro, MH 96960

Fed-Ex, DHL and UPS are all available with only DHL providing outgoing freight.

Transport

United Airlines has several weekly flights to and from Hawaii and Guam.

Our Airline, the Nauru-owned airline, provides flights to and from Fiji and Australia.

Air Marshall Islands is the government-owned airline. It services the inhabited outer islands
