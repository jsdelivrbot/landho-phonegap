Facts

  • St Pierre and Miquelon is a territory of France, located in the North Atlantic Ocean 15 miles off the south coast of Newfoundland.
  • The main islands of the small archipelago are St Pierre, Miquelon (Grande Miquelon), and Langlade (Petite Miquelon), the latter two connected by a sandy isthmus.
  • The majority of the population live on St Pierre and this island has the principle port and most repair services.
