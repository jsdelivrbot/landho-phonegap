St Pierre & Miquelon - General Info

Time Zone

UTC -3, UTC -2 April to October.

Electrical Supply

Voltage is 220v, 50 Hz.

The sockets are Standard French.

Opening Hours

Most businesses are open Monday to Friday, from 9:00 am to 12:00 pm and from 1:30 pm to 6:00 pm.
Most shops are closed Saturday afternoons and Sundays.

Money

Euro

US and Canadian dollars are widely accepted, as are credit cards and travellers cheques.

Displayed prices include taxes.

There are ATMs in banks and several businesses in Saint-Pierre, one ATM is available at the Banque des îles in Miquelon.

Communications

International Dialing Code for St Pierre Miquelon is +508.

Purchase a new cell phone and SIM card for St. Pierre & Miquelon (France) and take advantage of the low rates that the local cellular phone users are paying, regardless of the length of your trip.
