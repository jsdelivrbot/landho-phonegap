Miquelon - Profile

Location

The tiny harbour of Miquelon lies on the eastern side of the 2 mile long peninsula which extends northwards from the top of the main part of the island.

Position 47°06' 13"N, 56°22'30"W (harbour entrance)

Clearance

The customs office is at the root of the harbour mole.

Last updated September 2015.

Docking

The harbour here has been refurbished and there are now 60 berth for yachts. Min. depth 3m. Nothing more is known about facilities.

In settled weather there are possible anchorages either side of the harbour.
