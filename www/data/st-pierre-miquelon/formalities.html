Immigration

All visitors require a valid passport expect Canadians who can enter for stays of less than 90 days using a photo ID.

As an overseas territory of France,  St Pierre and Miquelon comes under French immigration rules, but it is neither a member of the EU nor included in the Schengen Area Agreement. However, the rules for visitors are much the same.

If a visa is required, note that the visa application must specify "France + St Pierre and Miquelon".
