Puerto Deseado - Profile

Location

Lying on the northern bank of the Ria Deseado, this important fishing port is in an area known for its huge range of wildlife.

Position 47° 45.4'S, 65° 51.4'W.

Clearance

For full details on clearing into and out of Argentina, see noonsite's Argentina Formalities.

The Prefectura can deal with all the clearance formalities.

Docking

Beware of the fast tidal stream at the entrance.

The channel is well marked. The small bay on the northern bank, formed by a peninsular of land to the east and a breakwater to the west, offers the best protection. Take note of the tidal range as a large part of this bay dries at LW springs.

It may be possible to find an anchorage further up the Ria. Comments below re. other docking options from a cruiser who visited here in November 2015 are also helpful.
