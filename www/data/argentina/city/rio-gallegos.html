Rio Gallegos - Profile

Location

This city lies at the southern tip of Argentina, close to the Chilian border. It sits on the southern bank of the Gallegos river and is an important military base. It is about 50nm north of the Beagle Channel.

Position 51° 35.4'S, 68° 57.2'W.
