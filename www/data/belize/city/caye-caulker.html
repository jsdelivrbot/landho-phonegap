Caye Caulker - Profile

Location

Caye Caulker is an island in the north of Belize, located approximately one mile west of the Belize Barrier Reef. The island is approximately five miles long.

Position 17° 45'N, 088° 01'W (middle of the island)

Clearance

It is now possible to also clear in at the marina here, but there is the additional cost of bringing out the officials from Belize City.

Ensure all clearance is done within 24hrs of arrival.

Last updated September 2010.
