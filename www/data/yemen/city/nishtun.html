Nishtun - Profile

Location

A small fishing port.

Position 15°49.1'N, 52°11.73E.

Clearance

Not an official Port of Entry, but officials will come to the boat and check passports and papers.

Crew not permitted to leave the port area.

Docking

Will be required to moor to a concrete wall in the small harbour if staying overnight and will likely to be watched over by armed guards.
