Yemen - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +3

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 220/230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

The local currency is the Yemeni rial. The exchange rate is stable at 215 YER for 1USD.

US dollars is the best currency to carry.

Communications

International Dialing Code is +967

Yemen City Area Telephone Codes

+ 967 3 al-Hudaydah
+ 967 5 al- Mukalla



Yemen Mobile Telephone Codes/Operators
Sabafon Yemen +967 71
SpaceTel Yemen +967 73


A Yemen prepaid SIM card with a GSM international cell phone is an affordable solution for staying in touch while in Yemen. All incoming calls, while in Yemen are FREE, regardless of where they originate. Pay the local rate for local calls and use a cellular phone in Yemen like you do at home. And unlike home, your Yemen SIM card and cell phone service is prepaid so there is no need for a contract.
