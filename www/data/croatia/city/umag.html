Umag - Profile

Location

This attractive old Medieval town is the closest major settlement to Croatia's northern border with Slovenia.

Position 45°26.3'N,13°31.2'E.

Clearance

This is an all year Port of Entry.

During the summer, clearance can be completed at the eastern breakwater in the marina.

Docking

The addition of a new breakwater and marina ensures this harbour is a very sheltered spot, but the town quay is exposed to winds from the northern sector.

Berth in the marina as directed or stern-to on the town quay west of the Customs pier.

Pick up one of the visitors' buoys in the centre of the harbour or anchor north of the moorings in 3 to 4m in mud, good holding.

Last updated March 2010.
