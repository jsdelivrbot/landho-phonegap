Opatija - Profile

Location

This is busy tourist resort and is at the northern point of the Rijecki Zaijev.

Position 45°19'N, 14°18'E.

Docking

The small harbour at Opatija is usually full of local boats, so either of the two marinas near the town offer the best berthing options.

Both marinas are sheltered, although Marina Opatija is more exposed to the NE.

Fuel berth is by the HM office.

Last updated February 2014.
