Zut (Kornati Islands) - Profile

Location

This 6 mile long island is just east of Kornat, the largest of the Kornati islands. It is part of a national park and Park regulations apply. See Croatia/Restrictions. It is a quiet place with very few permanent residents. The island covered with olives, figs and vines.

Position 43°53'N, 15°18.6'E.

Docking

The Podražanj Cove, on the NE coast of the island, has a marina on the west side of this bay.

There are also two anchorages in inlets on the southern shore of this cove.

Another anchorage can be found at Uvala Hiljaca.

Last updated February 2014.
