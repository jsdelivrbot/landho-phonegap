Nerezine (Losinj) - Profile

Location

This small harbour is on the east coast of Otoka Losinj, about 3nm south of the bridge linking the island to Cres.

Position 44°39.6'N, 14°24.2'E.

Docking

Generally well sheltered, and protected from the bora by a high breakwater, although strong NE winds can cause problems here.

Berh to one on the pontoons or alongside the quay in the inner part of the harbour. Beware of obstructions alone parts of the quay wall.

Last updated March 2010.
