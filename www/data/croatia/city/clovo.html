Clovo - Profile

Location

This island forms the south western side of the bay, Kastelanski Zaljev, and is linked at its western end by a low bridge to the mainland and the town of Trogir.

Position 43°30.2'N, 16°20.3'E (Slatine).

Docking

The harbour at Slatine is on the N coast of the island, facing the bay. The village istself is someway from the harbour. Berth alongside the wall. Depths range from 4m at the outer end to 1m near the shore.

There are several sheltered anchorages around the island, Uvala Razetinovac being an especially peaceful one.

Last updated February 2010.
