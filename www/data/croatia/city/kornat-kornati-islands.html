Kornat (Kornati Islands) - Profile

Location

This is the largest of the Kornati Islands and is about 13 miles long. It is part of a national park and Park regulations apply. See Croatia/Restrictions

Vrulje is a small village in a bay midway down the SW coast of the island.

Position 43°48.6'N, 15°18.5'E (Vrulje).

Docking

The bay provides good shelter. There are several visitors' buoys here or anchor in about 4m, taking a line ashore.

If the anchorage and buoys are full, there is an alternative anchorage in the next bay to the north. Here there is good holding in sand and a depth of 7 to 10m.

There is a National Park reception centre here.

Last updated February 2010.
