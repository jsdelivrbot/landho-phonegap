Prvic - Profile

Location

This tiny island lies just off the coast opposite Sibenik.

Position 43°43.4'N, 15°48.2'E.

Docking

Prvic Luka is an attractive village on the SE side of the island.

Berth to the breakwater where the depth is 3m, or anchor NW of this in 6m. the holding is good.

Last updated February 2010.
