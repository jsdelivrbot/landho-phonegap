Luka Peles - Profile

Location

This t-shaped inlet is just 2NM south of Primosten. The northern arm is now a marina.

Position 43°33.8'N, 15°56.5'E.

Clearance

Clearance procedures can be carried out at the marina here.

Docking

Moor where directed in the marina.

Although anchoring or berthing to the quay in the southern arm is permitted for the day, remaining overnight is not.

Generally good shelter, although the marina is subjec to some surge in strong S or SW winds.

Last updated February 2010.
