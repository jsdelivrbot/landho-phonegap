Sibenik - Profile

Location

The city of Sibenik is on the eastern bank of the large, land-locked bay of the same name. It is approached via the narrow channel, Kanal Sv. Ante. This large commercial port lies about 40NM NW of Split.

Position 43°43.9'N, 15°53.7'E.

Clearance

This is a year-round Port of Entry.

Clearance can be arrange by the Madalina marina.

Last updated February 2014.

Docking

Berth at the visitors' quay NW of the fuel berth or at the Mandalina marina in the southern arm of the bay.

There are several anchorages in various inlets off the Kanal Sv Ante but be aware that the wind funnels down this channel.

Located 6nm south of Sibenik is the Solaris Holiday Resort and marina. It provides excellent all round protection.
