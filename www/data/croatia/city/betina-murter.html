Betina (Murter) - Profile

Location

The village of Betina is at the northeastern corner of the island of Murter. The marina is on the other side of the promontory from the village.

Position 43°49'06"N, 15°36'04"E. (marina).

Docking

See the Noonsite report regarding the marina/boatyard here.

Berth at the marina where directed.

If berthing in the harbour at the village, tie stern-to on the eastern side of the wall dividing the harbour.

There is an anchorage east of the harbour in 5m, in mud.

Last updated February 2010.
