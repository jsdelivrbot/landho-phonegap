Trpanj - Profile

Location

A busy holiday town on the north coast of the Peljesac peninsula.

Position 43°0.7'N, 17°16.2'E.

Docking

Moor to the quay west of the ferry berth or to the breakwater, although from here you will require a dinghy to get ashore.

Anchoring outside the harbour exposes the yacht to the bora or sirocco wimds.

The Harbour Master's office is on the western side of the harbour.

Last updated February 2010.
