Marina Agana - Profile

Location

This village sits at the western end of the bay of Zaljev Marina and is about 6nm from Trogir. It is an attractive small port to visit.

Position 43°30.8'N, 16°07'E.

Marina :©www.adriagate.com/

Docking

The facilities here have been much improved by the contruction of a breakwater and the installation of some floating pontoons to form a marina.

The harbour provides good shelter.

Last updated February 2014.
