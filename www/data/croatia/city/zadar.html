Zadar - Profile

Location

This is a busy ferry port and commercial centre. The old town lies on a peninsula and is an ancient walled town of Roman origins. There are many attractive churches and interesting museums here.

Position 44°07.3'N,15°13.7'E.

Docking

There are several marinas here. Boriki and Vitenjak are to the north of the main port and are quieter.

Marina Zardar is in the heart of the port, in an inlet on the north eastern side. Berth where directed.

All the marinas have good shelter.
