Trget (River Rasa) - Profile

Location

This is a small fishing village on the east bank of the Raski Zaljev, a long, wide fjord. There is a small commercial dock just north of the village and the bay where there is a sheltered anchorage.

Position 45° 01'N, 14° 03'24"E.

Docking

There is a substantial stone pier here where it is possible to dock and a large sheltered anchorage.
