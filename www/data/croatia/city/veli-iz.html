Veli Iz - Profile

Location

Lying just to the west of Ugljan, this little island is just half the size of its neighbour to the east. The main interest here is the marina at Veli.

Position 44°03.2'N,15°07'E (Veli)

Docking

Berth in the marina where directed. The whole harbour is designated as a marina.

Generally good shelter, although strong NE winds cause an uncomfortable swell in the harbour.

Last updated February 2010.
