Pomer - Profile

Location

This little village, along with Medulin, are at the head of the sheltered waters of Medulinski Zaljev. It is an attractive area and popular with water-loving tourists.

Position 44°49.4'N,13°54.4'E.

Docking

The approaches to this area requires some care, not least because of the number of people in and on the water.

Berth at the marina where directed.

There are achorages both north and south of the village of Medulin, in about 4m in mud.

Both the sirocco and the bora blow stongly here, but the marina offers good shelter.

Last updated February 2014.
