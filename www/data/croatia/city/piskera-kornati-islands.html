Piskera (Kornati Islands) - Profile

Location

This tiny island is just off the SW coast of Kornat, about 3 miles from Vrulje. It is part of a national park and Park regulations apply. See Croatia/Restrictions

Position 43° 45,6'N, 15° 21,2'E (marina).

Docking

If just requiring to visit the National park office, it is possible to moor to the first concrete pier to briefly go ashore.

The marina here gets very full, so an early arrival may be necessary to secure a berth. Some areas in the marina are quite shallow (1.5m).

Last updated February 2010.

This marina lies between the islands of Piskera and Panitula Vela, on the northern coast of Panitula Vela Island. Max. draft of 1.5 - 3.5m. Max. length 15m. Open from mid March to mid October.
Electricity (supplied by generators and only available at certain times of day and night). Water limited. Showers available at certain times.
