Podgora - Profile

Location

On the mainland opposite the island of Hvar, about 25NM NW of Ploce. It is a busy tourist town.

Position 43°14.5'N, 17°04.9'E.

Docking

The harbour has been reconstructed and there are berths for yachts on pontoons here.

It is well sheltered although it can be subject to surge in SW winds.

Last updated February 2010.
