Dugi Otok - Profile

Location

This island, which is twice as long and twice as wide as Kornati, lies immediatley to the north of this island. It is not part of the Kornati National Park.

Position 43°56.2'N, 15°10.3'E (Sali).

Clearance

Sali and Bozava are both summer Ports of Entry.

Docking

Veli Rat
There is a very sheltered marina here on the west coast at the northern end of the island.

Sali
This is a fishing port on the SE coast. There are visitors' berths on the east and southwest quays in the harbour.

Bozava
This popular port is on the NE coast. Berth to the inner side of the pier or anchor close to the north shore at the harbour entrance and take lines ashore.

Luka Telascica
There are a number of visitors' buoys in this inlet at the southern end of the island.

Uvala Lucina
There are several mooring buoys here for visitors. It is also possible to anchor in 3 to 10m. Holding is reasonably good in sand.

Brbinj
This is an attractive cove on the NE coast of Dugi Otok. There are several options here:-
Berth to the quay in the NW part of the bay.
Pick up a mooring buoy on the eastern side.
Anchor either opposite the quay in 6 to 10m or in the inlet in the SW corner in 4m in mud.

This island provides many possible anchorages. The ones mentioned are just a few of them.
