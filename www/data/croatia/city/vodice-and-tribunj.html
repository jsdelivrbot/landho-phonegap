Vodice and Tribunj - Profile

Location

The small town of Vodice is a tourist resort about 5nm NW of the entrance channel leading to Sibenik.

The attractive fishing village of Tribunj is just 2nm to the west.

Position 43°45.4'N, 15°46.8'E (Vodice).

Clearance

This is not a Port of Entry.

Docking

Vodice
The marina is to sta'bd on approach to the harbour. When approaching the marina entrance, beware of a rocky shoal marked by an unlit beacon. Berth as directed. Beware of the concrete blocks on the seabed.

It is also possible to anchor SE of the marina breakwater in about 5m over sand.

Tribunj
Berth in the marina or tie to the quay on the western side of the village. Facilities for super-yachts (max. length 30m)

Anchor to the W or SW of the village in about 3 to 6m.

Last updated February 2014.
