Otok Iz - Profile

Location

Otok Iz is a 6 mile long island lying between the islands of Dugi Otok and Ugljan, opposite the mainland port of Zadar. It is a quiet place with limited facilities.

Position 44°59.75'N, 15°22.03'E.

Docking

Veli Iz is the main yachting port on the island. It lies on the east cost, about 2nm north of the ferry port of Mali Iz.

Veli Iz : ©nikola_pu

This is an interesting port which still has a traditional wooden boat building yard.

Mali Iz is the only other main settlement on the island and is the  ferry port with connections to Zadar on the mainland.

Last updated February 2014.
