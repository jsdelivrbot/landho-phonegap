Hramina (Murter) - Profile

Location

Hramina sits at the head of the large bay at the northern end of Murter. It is a popular spot to lay up for the winter.

Position 43°49.6'N, 15°35.6E.

Docking

The marina is on the eastern shore of the bay. There is good all-round shelter here. Being a popular spot, shore storage space for the winter is best booked well in advance.

Manoeuvring in the marina is not easy due to restricted space between berths.

Berth where directed in the marina.

There is an anchorge just east of the harbour in 5m and mud.

Last updated February 2010.
