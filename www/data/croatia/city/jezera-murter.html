Jezera (Murter) - Profile

Location

This small village is on the SE coast of the small island of Murter.

Position 43°47'N,15°39'E.

Docking

This is a sheltered, natural harbour.

Berth where directed in the marina.

There is an anchorage about 1 NM SE of Jezera.

Last updated February 2010.
