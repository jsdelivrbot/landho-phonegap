Solta - Profile

Location

This small, rocky island is just west of Brac.

Position 43°23.7'N, 16°18.4'E (Rogac).

Docking

Rogac is the main port on the island. It lies on the NE coast.

Berth stern-to on the quay. It is possible to anchor but the holding is reported to be unreliable.

There are several sheltered anchorages to be found around this island. One of the best sheltered is at Uvala Sesula at the west end of the island.

There are visitors' berths on the quay at the small harbour of Maslinica, also aon the west end. They have water and electricity laid on and there is a supermarket in the village there.

Last updated February 2010.
