Vrsar - Profile

Location

This pleasent, tourist town is about 5nm north of Rovinj. It is close to a large archipelago of 18 small islands covered with Mediterranean plants.

Position 45°09'N,13°36.1'E.

Docking

There is a marina in the harbour at Vrsar and another about 2nm south of the town, although this may not be everyone's choice as it is part of a nudist resort ( Koversada nudist camp - the largest in Europe!).

In addition to the marina, there are berths on the quay, NE of the mole in Vrsar harbour.

Good shelter, although some swell can penetrate.

Funtana: Just 1.5nm north of Vrsar is a modern marina with haulout facilities.
