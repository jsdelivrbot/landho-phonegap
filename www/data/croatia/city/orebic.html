Orebic - Profile

Location

A useful port on the south west coast of the Peljesac Peninsula.

Position 42°58.4'N, 17°10'E.

Docking

There are berths on the pontoons in the marina or berth stern-to on the mole.

Possible to anchor west of the harbour entrance but the holding is not good and a boat is vulnerable at night as an anchor light is difficult to see against the town lights.

Good shelter but subject to some swell from the west.

Last updated February 2010.
