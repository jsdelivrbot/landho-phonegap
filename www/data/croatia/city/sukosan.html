Sukosan - Profile

Location

Sukosan is just 6nm south of Zadar and has the largest marina in Croatia. It is a tourist resort on a very attractive part of the coast.

Position 44°03'N, 15°18.4'E.

Sukosan Marina :©www.yacht-chartercroatia.com

Docking

The huge marina here is the principal reason for visiting this village.

Berth in the marina where directd. It is advisable to use a dinghy to reach the harbour office and other services as it is a very long way round from the visitors' berths.

It is possible to anchor in the bay to the SE of the marina, or in the harbour by the village (to the east of the ruins), both in about 2m.

Last updated February 2014.
