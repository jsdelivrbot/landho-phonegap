Skradin - Profile

Location

This small, pretty, inland town, is situated in the Krka National Park, approximately 10 miles beyond the town of Sibenik, through the Prukijansko Jezero. The waterfalls, further up the Krka river, is the main attraction when visiting here.

Position 43°49'N, 15°55.5'E.

Docking

Berth in the marina as directed.

There are several bays opposite the town where it is possible to anchor during the day, in about 2 to 5m. The holding is good in the mud. Overnight anchoring is not permitted.

Last updated February 2010.
