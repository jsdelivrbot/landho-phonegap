Simuni (Pag) - Profile

Location

Otok Pag is a large, irregular shaped island and lies close to the mainland opposite Karlobag. Simuni is roughly halfway down the west coast.

Position 44°28'N,14°57.8'E (Simuni).

Docking

The marina is in the NW arm of the bay.

Berth in the marina where directed or at the head of the inlet, near the fishing boats.

Good shelter.

Last updated February 2014.
