Supetarska Draga (Rab) - Profile

Location

The large bay of Supetarska Draga is on the northern tip of the island of Rab.

Position 44°48.6'N,14°43'E.

Docking

The marina is in the SE corner of the inlet.

Berth in the marina as directed.

There are several anchorages at the northern end of the west side of the bay. All the anchorages are in about 3m and the holding is good in the sand. Care needs to be taken to avoid the rocks and rocky ledges in this area.

There is good shelter here from all but north westerly winds, although the marina does provide some shelter even from this direction.

Last updated February 2014.
