Falmouth Harbour - Profile

Location

This large harbour lies on the southern coast of Antigua, immediately west of English Harbour.

Position 17° 00'45"N, 61° 46'44"W.

Clearance

See the main Noonsite/Antigua/Formalities page for all the details.

Remember you will still need to clear into Antigua in person at the Customs Office before proceeding to the marina or fuel dock.

Clearance will need to be completed at the Customs & Immigration office at Nelson’s Dockyard, English Harbour.

Harbour dues/park fees are payable when at anchor and in addition to marina fees. See comments at bottom of page with latest costs reported.

Last updated October 2015.

Docking

It is possible to anchor anywhere in the harbour, clear of the channels and the exclusion area in front of the Antigua Yacht Club and Falmouth Harbour marinas.

There is an anchoring charge in this harbour as well as various other fees (see comment at bottom of page with latest fees reported).

Water is available at the various marinas and will have to be purchased from them. Currently no petrol stations operating.

Last updated March 2015.
