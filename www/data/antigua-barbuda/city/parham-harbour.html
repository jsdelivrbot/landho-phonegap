Parham Harbour - Profile

Location

Located on the north coast of Antigua. There a marked channel leading into the harbour from just off Maiden Island. Not advisable to either enter or leave except in daylight. Originally this was the island's principal harbour.

Position 17° 08'N, 61° 46'W.

Clearance

There is a Customs post and and Immigration office here.

See the main Noonsite/Antigua/Formalities page for full details.

Last updated October 2015.

Docking

This is a well sheltered anchorage. There is a shallow (5ft) berth at the jetty just east of Myers Cove and a hurricane hole in the mangroves on the south eastern side of the harbour with a 2m dredged entrance.

The best hurricane storage is at North Sound Marine services which has dedicated tie-downs.

Last updated August 2015.
