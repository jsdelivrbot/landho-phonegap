LifeHarbour Limanu - Profile

Location

LifeHarbour Limanu is located in the Mangalia estuary, 3 NM west from the entrance to Mangalia Harbour.

Before entering the harbour you must keep clear of the SE pier where the water is very shallow. Follow the left channel marked with red and green buoys, as the right one leads into Marina Mangalia.You need to go past the Daewoo Shipyard which will be on your left, then you will pass under a bridge with a 20m clearance.

LifeHarbour Marina (photo from Ioan Cobzarenco)

Clearance

Before entering the LifeHarbour marina you need to clear in with  Marina Mangalia. To do this, contact the Harbour Master, VHF Channel 16, 67 or by telephone at +40 241 751088 or +40 382 416237 and they will guide you.

The Police Border and Custom will visit you at the berth in the marina.

Last updated September 2015.

Docking

LifeHarbour is a private marina, with 70 berths for boats up to 24m.

Water and electricity is provided at each berth.

Being well up the estuary, this marina is safe in all weather conditions regardless of the wind’s direction.

In terms of amenities, there is a hotel and a restaurant in the marina but no fuel station or place to buy groceries or other supplies.

Photo from Ioan Cobzarenco

You will have to ask the staff for a taxi in order to visit Mangalia for supply.

In the marina there is a small chandlery and limited repairs are possible.

At weekends there may be a party with loud music until early in the morning.

Last updated September 2015.
