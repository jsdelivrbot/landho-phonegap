Romania - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2. Summer time UTC +3 from early April until the end of September.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 230 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Money

Leu (plural lei) divided into 100 bani

The import/export of lei is prohibited. Foreign currency must be declared on arrival and exchange receipts kept to show customs on departure.

ATMs are called "Bankomats"

Cash is widely used due to the risk of credit card fraud. Euros is the best foreign currency to use.

Communications

International dialing Code for Romania is +40.

Mobile Phones - Notify your cell phone provider that you will be traveling to Romania, and ask to be upgraded to international roaming, or Rent a cell phone or SIM card to use with your personal cell phone.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Romania

Emergencies

All emergencies - Dial 112
Ambulance - Dial 961
Fire Department - Dial 981
Police - Dial 955
