Lebanon - General Info

Time Zone

UTC +2, summer time UTC +3.

Electrical Supply

Voltage: 110/220 V

Frequency: 50 Hz

Opening Hours

Shops and businesses are typically open Monday through Saturday, 9:00-18:00.

Hours vary, and in summer many establishments close early.

Restaurant hours vary, and many restaurants, especially in Beirut, are open late.

Banking hours are Monday through Saturday, 8:30-12:30.

Working hours for government offices and post offices are typically 8:00-14:00.

Money

Lebanese pound of 100 piastres.

Communications

International dialing code for Lebanon is +961.

Most Lebanese use mobile phones, and coverage extends throughout the country.

There are Internet cafés available throughout Lebanon, and many larger hotels now offer high-speed and wireless Internet access for free or for a small fee.

Diplomatic Missions

Foreign Embassies in Lebanon

Emergencies

Police - dial 112
