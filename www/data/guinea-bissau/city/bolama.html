Bolama - Profile

Location

This very small port is about 25nm due south of Bissau on the east coast of the island of Ponta Doce Mancanha.

Position 11° 34.4' N, 15° 28.2'W.

Docking

There is just one commercial jetty here. Not a recommended berth for yachts.

No other information.

Last updated January 2012.
