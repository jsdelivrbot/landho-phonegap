Bissau - Profile

Location

Located on the north bank of the Geba River. It is the principal port in Guinea-Bassau.

Position 11° 51.3' N,15° 34.3' W.

Clearance

Port Authority
Direccao Nacional de Marinha e Portos , CP 25, Capitania dos Portos , Bissau, Guinea-Bissau
Tel:+245 201984 Fax:245 201984

Docking

There is good holding in the anchorage in the river in 5 to 6m. The tidal range is about 2m.
