Cacheu - Profile

Location

This town lies on the southern bank of the Cacheu River, the entrance of which is just 20nm fron the border with Senegal. The port is about 15nm from the entrance. It is the site of the earliest Portuguese settlement in Guinea-Bissau and in the seventeenth and eighteenth centuries was the centre of the slave trade.

Position 12°16.5'N, 16°09.5'W.

Clearance

This is a Port of Entry and yachts can clear in here.
