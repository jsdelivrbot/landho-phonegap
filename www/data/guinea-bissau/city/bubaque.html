Bubaque - Profile

Location

Bubaque lies on the northern tip if the Isla Bubaque, in the centre of the Bijagos Archipelago.

Position 11°18.1'N, 15°49.5'W.

Clearance

Yachts can clear in here and it is the best place to do so. Administration and procedures are complicated and take time and patience to negotiate fees, but the officials are nice if you are pleasant.

Last updated January 2012.
