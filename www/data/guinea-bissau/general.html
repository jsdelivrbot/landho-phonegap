Money

The unit of currency is the West African CFA franc.

ATMs in Guinea-Bissau are very rare and credit cards are not generally accepted anywhere.

Euros are the easiest currency to exchange.

Transport

Shared taxis are cheap and plentiful. Stop a taxi and tell them where you want to go. Prices are more or less fixed, and you'll normally not get cheated.

The only international airport is at Bissau which has flights to Europe 3 times per week with the Portuguese airline TAP.
