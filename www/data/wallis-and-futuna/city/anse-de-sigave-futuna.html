Anse de Sigave (Futuna) - Profile

Location

Anse de Sigave at Leava, lies on the west coast of Futuna. It forms a natural harbour.

Position 14° 17'45"S, 178° 09'39" (Leava wharf)

Clearance

See the main Wallis and Futuna Formalities page for the details.

Land by dinghy at the dock (wharf) and check in with Customs first (open from 730am till 130pm Mon-Fri, in the French Delegation building complex) and at the local gendarmerie (Immigration). Both are located at walking distance, south of the wharf.

Ensure to visit both offices before leaving the island.

Last updated October 2016.

Docking

The small harbour of Leava offers shelter in all winds except SE-SW, although the swell is constant. Several boats have come to grief on the coral reef in the harbour so one has to be careful anchoring with plenty of scope and swinging room is important.

The alternate anchorage is off Alofi island, in the channel.

Last updated August 2016.
