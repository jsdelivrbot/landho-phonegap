Alif Dhaal Atoll - Profile

Location

There are several islands within this atoll. The principal island is Maamingili. The other islands to the west are mainly resort islands.

Position 03° 28'50"N, 72° 50'41"E (harbour entrance)

Docking

There is a deep water harbour at the eastern end Maamgili island. Capable of berthing boats up to 20m.

Due to the local boat building there are some repair facilities available as well as the usual provisioning and fuel from the busy town here.
