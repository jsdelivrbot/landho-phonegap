Dhonakulhi - Profile

Location

This heavily wooded island is an exclusive holiday resort, but with a new marina on the deep water side of this crescent-shaped island, it provides a useful, but limited port of call. Visiting yachtsmen have no access to the beach or shore facilities of the resort. You must give them at least one day's advance notice before arrival.

Position 06° 50'29"N, 73° 02'55"E (marina)

Docking

A sheltered anchorage is available in the south part of the channel.
