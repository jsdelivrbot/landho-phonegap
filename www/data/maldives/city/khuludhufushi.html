Khuludhufushi - Profile

Location

Kulhudhuffushi is the capital of Haa Dhaalu Atoll administrative division and geographically part of Thiladhummathi Atoll in the north of the Maldives. It is one of the four international ports where foreign cruising crew can go ashore and is located at the southern tip of the island.

Position 06° 36.9' N, 73° 03.97' E  (inside the harbour).

This is a Port of Entry, however not one that is used to clearing pleasure yachts. Whilst all foreign yachts can get inward and outward clearance here, prices are likely to be higher.

Docking

This is not a busy port and so a comfortable berth for yachts.

There is a fee of US$12 for a 24 hour stay in the port and it is possible to fill up with water there for a small fee.

The town of Khuludhufushi has quite a few shops with basic provisioning and some restaurants. A café on the main street has Wi Fi, however electricity here can be variable.
