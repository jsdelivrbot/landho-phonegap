Rangali Island - Profile

Location

This island lies towards the south western side of the Ari Atoll. It actually consists of two islands connected by a walkway.

Position 03° 36'35"N, 72° 43'42E (entrance to the pass)

Docking

Enter the lagoon by the South pass and sail eastwards. There are some channel markers.

The resort welcomes cruisers, but do contact them in advance. There is an anchorage off the resort and a jetty.

Rihineli North
There is a big area between Olhuveli resort and Rihineli resort with a calm, quiet anchorage in 25/30m sand.

Last updated April 2016.
